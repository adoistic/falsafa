### Paragraph 4

He, Lord Amitābha, resides in the West. Red is his color. She, Lady Pāṇḍaravāsinī, is his `devī`. Hrīḥ is his `mantra`. His `jñāna` is `pratyavekṣaṇa`. His `mudrā` is the `dhyāna`. The `nāgapāśa` is his implement. Sukhāvatī is his heaven. He, Lord Vajradharma, Lord Vajratīkṣṇa, Lord Vajraketu, Lord Vajrabhāsa, are those who accompany him. Now, as for his residence in the body, the inside of the liver is his abode.
