### Paragraph 3

He, Lord Ratnasambhava, resides in the South. Yellow is his color. She, Lady Māmakī, is his `devī`. Trāṃ is his `mantra`. His `jñāna` is `ākāśamata`. The `kapāla` is his implement. The inside of the spleen is his abode.
