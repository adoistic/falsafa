### Paragraph 3

sira bhaṭāra ratnasambhava, muṅguh riṅ dakṣiṇa, pīta varṇanira, sira bharālī māmakī devīnira, tra.m mantranira, ākāśamata jñānanira, kapāla sañjatanira, riṅ jro niṅ limpa pinakasthānanira.
