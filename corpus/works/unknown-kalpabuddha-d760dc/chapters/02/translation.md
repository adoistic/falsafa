### Paragraph 2

He, Lord Akṣobhya, resides in the East. Black is his color. She, Lady Locanā, is his `devī`. Hūṃ is his `mantra`. His `mudrā` is the `bhūsparśa`. His `jñāna` is `ādarśana`. The `vajra` is his implement. Abhirati is the name of his heaven. An elephant is his mount. Lord Vajrarāja, Lord Vajrarāga, the divine Vajrasādhu, Lord Vajradharādi, are those who accompany him. Now, as for his residence in the body, the inside of the lungs is his abode.
