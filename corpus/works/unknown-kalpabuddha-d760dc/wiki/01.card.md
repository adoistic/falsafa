## Closes
> [p-e1c8df] This is the royal command of the Lord, the divine Buddha, this great instruction must be heeded, listen to all the `mantra`s. Lord Buddha was in the great dissolution (`mahāpralaya`), his body came into being, he then became the Lord Pañcatathāgata. Their bodies and names are distinct, each has a counterpart in form. His `mudrā` is the `bodhyagrī`, his `jñāna` is `śāśvata`, his implement is the `cakra`. Sahāvatī is the name of his dominion, a lion is his mount. She, Lady Sattvavajrī, she, Lady Dharmavajrī, she, Lady Karmavajrī, they are the `devī`s accompanying him. Now, as for his residence in the body, the inside of the heart is his abode.
