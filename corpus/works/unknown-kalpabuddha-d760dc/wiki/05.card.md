## Closes
> [p-8f8def] He, Lord Amoghasiddhi, resides in the North. Variegated is his color. She, Lady Tārā, is his `devī`. Aḥ is his `mantra`. His `mudrā` is the `abhaya`. His `jñāna` is `kṛtyānuṣṭhāna`. Kusumita is the name of his heaven. A `garuḍa` is his mount. He, Lord Vajrakarma, Lord Vajrarakṣa, [Lord Vajrayakṣa,] Lord Vajrasandhi are those who accompany him. Now, as for his residence in the body, the inside of the gall bladder is his abode.
