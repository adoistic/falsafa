Hwaet! We gefrunan     on fyrndagum twelfe under tunglum     tireadige haeleth, theodnes thegnas.     No hira thrym alaeg campraedenne thonne cumbol hneotan,
syththan hie gedaeldon,     swa him dryhten sylf, heofona heahcyning, hlyt getaehte. thaet waeron maere     men ofer eorthan, frome folctogan     ond fyrdhwate, rofe rincas,     thonne rond ond hand
on herefelda     helm ealgodon, on meotudwange.     Waes hira Matheus sum, se mid Iudeum ongan     godspell aerest wordum writan     wundorcraefte. tham halig god     hlyt geteode
ut on thaet igland     thaer aenig tha git elltheodigra     ethles ne mihte blaedes brucan.     Oft him bonena hand on herefelda     hearde gesceode. Eal waes thaet mearcland     morthre bewunden,
feondes facne,     folcstede gumena, haeletha ethel.     Naes thaer hlafes wist werum on tham wonge,     ne waeteres drync to bruconne,     ah hie blod ond fel, fira flaescheoman,     feorrancumenra,
thegon geond tha theode.     Swelc waes theaw hira thaet hie aeghwylcne     elltheodigra dydan him to mose     metethearfendum, thara the thaet ealand     utan sohte. Swylc waes thaes folces     freotholeas tacen,
unlaedra eafoth,     thaet hie eagena gesihth, hettend heorogrimme, heafodgimmas agetton gealgmode     gara ordum. Syththan him geblendan bitere tosomne, dryas thurh dwolcraeft,     drync unheorne,
se onwende gewit,     wera ingethanc, heortan on hrethre,     (hyge waes oncyrred), thaet hie ne murndan     aefter mandreame, haeleth heorograedige,     ac hie hig ond gaers for meteleaste     methe gedrehte.
tha waes Matheus     to thaere maeran byrig cumen in tha ceastre.     thaer waes cirm micel geond Mermedonia,     manfulra hloth, fordenera gedraeg,     syththan deofles thegnas geascodon     aethelinges sith.
Eodon him tha togenes,     garum gehyrsted, lungre under linde,     (nalas late waeron), eorre aescberend,     to tham orlege. Hie tham halgan thaer     handa gebundon ond faestnodon     feondes craefte,
haeleth hellfuse,     ond his heafdes segl abreoton mid billes ecge.     Hwaethre he in breostum tha git herede in heortan     heofonrices weard, theah the he atres drync     atulne onfenge. Eadig ond onmod,     he mid elne forth
wyrthode wordum     wuldres aldor, heofonrices weard,     halgan stefne, of carcerne.     Him waes Cristes lof on fyrhthlocan     faeste bewunden. He tha wepende     weregum tearum
his sigedryhten     sargan reorde grette, gumena brego,     geomran stefne, weoruda wilgeofan,     ond thus wordum cwaeth: "Hu me eltheodige     inwitwrasne searonet seowath! A ic symles waes
on wega gehwam     willan thines georn on mode;     nu thurh geohtha sceal daede fremman     swa tha dumban neat. thu ana canst     ealra gehygdo, meotud mancynnes,     mod in hrethre.
Gif thin willa sie,     wuldres aldor, thaet me waerlogan     waepna ecgum, sweordum, aswebban,     ic beo sona gearu to adreoganne     thaet thu, drihten min, engla eadgifa,     ethelleasum,
dugetha daedfruma,     deman wille. Forgif me to are,     aelmihtig god, leoht on thissum life,     thy laes ic lungre scyle, ablended in burgum,     aefter billhete thurh hearmcwide     heorugraedigra,
lathra leodsceathena,     leng throwian edwitspræce.     Ic to anum the, middangeardes weard,     mod statholige, faeste fyrhthlufan,     ond the, faeder engla, beorht blaedgifa,     biddan wille
thaet thu me ne gescyrige     mid scyldhetum, werigum wrohtsmihtum,     on thone wyrrestan, dugotha demend,     death ofer eorthan." aefter thyssum wordum     com wuldres tacen halig of heofenum,     swylce hadre segl
to tham carcerne.     thaer gecythed wearth thaet halig god     helpe gefremede, tha wearth gehyred     heofoncyninges stefn wraetlic under wolcnum,     wordhleothres sweg maeres theodnes.     He his maguthegne
under hearmlocan     haelo ond frofre beadurofum abead     beorhtan stefne: "Ic the, Matheus,     mine sylle sybbe under swegle.     Ne beo thu on sefan to forht, ne on mode ne murn.     Ic the mid wunige
ond the alyse     of thyssum leothubendum, ond ealle tha menigo     the the mid wuniath on nearonedum.     the is neorxnawang, blaeda beorhtost,     boldwela faegrost, hama hyhtlicost,     halegum mihtum
torht ontyned.     thaer thu tyres most, to widan feore     willan brucan. Gethola theoda threa!     Nis seo thrah micel thaet the waerlogan     witebendum, synnige thurh searocraeft,     swencan motan.
Ic the Andreas     aedre onsende to hleo ond to hrothre     in thas haethenan burg. He the alyseth     of thyssum leodhete. Is to thaere tide     taelmet hwile emne mid sothe     seofon ond twentig
nihtgerimes,     thaet thu of nede most, sorgum geswenced,     sigore gewyrthod, hweorfan of henthum     in gehyld godes." Gewat him tha se halga     helm aelwihta, engla scyppend,     to tham uplican
ethelrice.     He is on riht cyning, statholfaest styrend,     in stowa gehwam. tha waes Matheus     miclum onbryrded niwan stefne.     Nihthelm toglad, lungre leorde.     Leoht aefter com,
daegredwoma.     Duguth samnade, haethne hildfrecan,     heapum thrungon, (guthsearo gullon,     garas hrysedon), bolgenmode,     under bordhreothan. Woldon cunnian     hwaether cwice lifdon
tha the on carcerne     clommum faeste hleoleasan wic     hwile wunedon, hwylcne hie to aete     aerest mihton aefter fyrstmearce     feores beraedan. Haefdon hie on rune     ond on rimcraefte
awriten, waelgraedige,     wera endestaef, hwaenne hie to mose     metethearfendum on thaere wertheode     weorthan sceoldon. Cirmdon caldheorte,     (corthor othrum getang), rethe raesboran.     Rihtes ne gimdon,
meotudes mildse.     Oft hira mod onwod under dimscuan     deofles larum, thonne hie unlaedra eaefethum gelyfdon. Hie tha gemetton     modes glawne, haligne haele,     under heolstorlocan
bidan beadurofne thaes him beorht cyning, engla ordfruma,     unnan wolde. tha waes first agan     frumraedenne thinggemearces     butan thrim nihtum, swa hit waelwulfas     awriten haefdon
thaet hie banhringas     abrecan thohton, lungre tolysan     lic ond sawle, ond thonne todaelan     duguthe ond geogothe, werum to wiste     ond to wilthege, faeges flaescheoman.     Feorh ne bemurndan,
graedige guthrincas,     hu thaes gastes sith aefter swyltcwale     geseted wurde. Swa hie symble ymb thritig     thing gehedon nihtgerimes;     waes him neod micel thaet hie tobrugdon     blodigum ceaflum
fira flaescheoman     him to foddorthege. tha waes gemyndig,     se the middangeard gestathelode     strangum mihtum, hu he in elltheodigum     yrmthum wunode, belocen leothubendum,     the oft his lufan adreg
for Ebreum     ond Israhelum; swylce he Iudea     galdorcraeftum withstod stranglice.     tha sio stefn gewearth gehered of heofenum,     thaer se halga wer in Achaia,     Andreas, waes,
(leode laerde     on lifes weg), tha him cirebaldum     cininga wuldor, meotud mancynnes,     modhord onleac, weoruda drihten,     ond thus wordum cwaeth: "thu scealt feran     ond frith laedan,
sithe gesecan,     thaer sylfaetan eard weardigath,     ethel healdath morthorcraeftum.     Swa is thaere menigo theaw thaet hie uncuthra     aengum ne willath on tham folcstede     feores geunnan
syththan manfulle     on Mermedonia onfindath feasceaftne.     thaer sceall feorhgedal, earmlic ylda cwealm,     aefter wyrthan. thaer ic seomian wat     thinne sigebrothor mid tham burgwarum     bendum faestne.
Nu bith fore threo niht     thaet he on thaere theode sceal fore haethenra     handgewinne thurh gares gripe     gast onsendan, ellorfusne,     butan thu aer cyme." aedre him Andreas     agef andsware:
"Hu maeg ic, dryhten min,     ofer deop gelad fore gefremman     on feorne weg swa hraedlice,     heofona scyppend, wuldres waldend,     swa thu worde becwist? thaet maeg engel thin     eath geferan,
halig of heofenum     con him holma begang, sealte saestreamas ond swanrade, warothfarutha gewinn     ond waeterbrogan, wegas ofer widland.     Ne synt me winas cuthe, eorlas eltheodige,     ne thaer aeniges wat
haeletha gehygdo,     ne me herestraeta ofer cald waeter     cuthe sindon." Him tha ondswarude     ece dryhten: "Eala, Andreas,     thaet thu a woldest thaes sithfaetes     saene weorthan!
Nis thaet uneathe     eallwealdan gode to gefremmanne     on foldwege, thaet sio ceaster hider     on thas cneorisse under swegles gang     aseted wyrthe, breogostol breme,     mid tham burgwarum,
gif hit worde becwith     wuldres agend. Ne meaht thu thaes sithfaetes     saene weorthan, ne on gewitte to wac,     gif thu wel thencest with thinne waldend     waere gehealdan, treowe tacen.     Beo thu on tid gearu;
ne maeg thaes aerendes     ylding wyrthan. thu scealt tha fore geferan     ond thin feorh beran in gramra gripe,     thaer the guthgewinn thurh haethenra     hildewoman, beorna beaducraeft,     geboden wyrtheth.
Scealtu aeninga     mid aerdaege, emne to morgene,     aet meres ende ceol gestigan     ond on cald waeter brecan ofer baethweg.     Hafa bletsunge ofer middangeard     mine, thaer thu fere!"
Gewat him tha se halga     healdend ond wealdend, upengla fruma,     ethel secan, middangeardes weard,     thone maeran ham, thaer sothfaestra     sawla moton aefter lices hryre     lifes brucan.
tha waes aerende     ethelum cempan aboden in burgum,     ne waes him bleath hyge, ah he waes anraed     ellenweorces, heard ond higerof,     nalas hildlata, gearo, guthe fram,     to godes campe.
Gewat him tha on uhtan     mid aerdaege ofer sandhleothu     to saes faruthe, thriste on gethance,     ond his thegnas mid, gangan on greote.     Garsecg hlynede, beoton brimstreamas.     Se beorn waes on hyhte,
syththan he on waruthe     widfaethme scip modig gemette.     tha com morgentorht beacna beorhtost     ofer breomo sneowan, halig of heolstre.     Heofoncandel blac ofer lagoflodas.     He thaer lidweardas,
thrymlice thry     thegnas gemette, modiglice menn,     on merebate sittan sithfrome,     swylce hie ofer sae comon. thaet waes drihten sylf,     dugetha wealdend, ece aelmihtig,     mid his englum twam.
Waeron hie on gescirplan     scipferendum, eorlas onlice     ealithendum, thonne hie on flodes faethm     ofer feorne weg on cald waeter     ceolum lacath. Hie tha gegrette,     se the on greote stod,
fus on farothe, faegn reordade: "Hwanon comon ge     ceolum lithan, macraeftige menn,     on merethissan, ane aegflotan?     Hwanon eagorstream ofer ytha gewealc     eowic brohte?"
Him tha ondswarode     aelmihti god, swa thaet ne wiste,     se the thaes wordes bad, hwaet se manna waes     methelhegendra, the he thaer on warothe     withthingode: "We of Marmedonia     maegthe syndon
feorran geferede.     Us mid flode baer on hranrade     heahstefn naca, snellic saemearh,     snude bewunden, oththaet we thissa leoda     land gesohton, waere bewrecene,     swa us wind fordraf."
Him tha Andreas     eathmod oncwaeth: "Wolde ic the biddan,     theh ic the beaga lyt, sincweorthunga,     syllan meahte, thaet thu us gebrohte     brante ceole, hea hornscipe,     ofer hwaeles ethel
on thaere maegthe.     Bith the meorth with god, thaet thu us on lade     lithe weorthe." Eft him ondswarode     aethelinga helm of ythlide,     engla scippend: "Ne magon thaer gewunian     widferende,
ne thaer eltheodige     eardes brucath, ah in thaere ceastre     cwealm throwiath, tha the feorran thyder     feorh gelaedath, ond thu wilnast nu     ofer widne mere thaet thu on tha faegthe     thine feore spilde."
Him tha Andreas     agef ondsware: "Usic lust hweteth     on tha leodmearce, mycel modes hiht,     to thaere maeran byrig, theoden leofesta,     gif thu us thine wilt on merefarothe     miltse gecythan."
Him ondswarode     engla theoden, neregend fira,     of nacan stefne: "We the estlice     mid us willath ferigan freolice     ofer fisces baeth efne to tham lande     thaer the lust myneth
to gesecanne,     syththan ge eowre gafulraedenne     agifen habbath, sceattas gescrifene,     swa eow scipweardas, aras ofer ythbord,     unnan willath." Him tha ofstlice     Andreas with,
winethearfende,     wordum maelde: "Naebbe ic faeted gold     ne feohgestreon, welan ne wiste     ne wira gespann, landes ne locenra beaga,     thaet ic the maege lust ahwettan, willan in worulde,     swa thu worde becwist."
Him tha beorna breogo,     thaer he on bolcan saet, ofer warotha geweorp     withthingode: "Hu gewearth the thaes,     wine leofesta, thaet thu saebeorgas     secan woldes, merestreama gemet,     mathmum bedaeled,
ofer cald cleofu     ceoles neosan? Nafast the to frofre     on farothstraete hlafes wiste     ne hlutterne drync to dugothe.     Is se drohtath strang tham the lagolade     lange cunnath."
tha him Andreas     thurh ondsware, wis on gewitte,     wordhord onleac: "Ne gedafenath the,     nu the dryhten geaf welan ond wiste     ond woruldspede, thaet thu ondsware     mid oferhygdum,
sece sarcwide.     Selre bith aeghwam thaet he eathmedum     ellorfusne oncnawe cuthlice,     swa thaet Crist bebead, theoden thrymfaest.     We his thegnas synd gecoren to cempum.     He is cyning on riht,
wealdend ond wyrhta     wuldorthrymmes, an ece god     eallra gesceafta, swa he ealle befehth     anes craefte, hefon ond eorthan,     halgum mihtum, sigora selost.     He thaet sylfa cwaeth,
faeder folca gehwaes,     ond us feran het geond ginne grund     gasta streonan: 'Farath nu geond ealle     eorthan sceatas emne swa wide     swa waeter bebugeth, oththe stedewangas     straete gelicgath.
Bodiath aefter burgum     beorhtne geleafan ofer foldan faethm.     Ic eow freotho healde. Ne thurfan ge on tha fore     fraetwe laedan, gold ne seolfor.     Ic eow goda gehwaes on eowerne agenne dom     est ahwette.'
Nu thu seolfa miht     sith userne gehyran hygethancol.     Ic sceal hrathe cunnan hwaet thu us to duguthum gedon wille." Him tha ondswarode ece dryhten: "Gif ge syndon thegnas     thaes the thrym ahof
ofer middangeard,     swa ge me secgath, ond ge geheoldon thaet eow     se halga bead, thonne ic eow mid gefean     ferian wille ofer brimstreamas,     swa ge benan sint." tha in ceol stigon     collenfyrhthe,
ellenrofe,     aeghwylcum wearth on merefarothe     mod geblissod. tha ofer ytha geswing     Andreas ongann merelithendum     miltsa biddan wuldres aldor,     ond thus wordum cwaeth:
"Forgife the dryhten     domweorthunga, willan in worulde     ond in wuldre blaed, meotud manncynnes,     swa thu me hafast on thyssum sithfaete     sybbe gecythed!" Gesaet him tha se halga helmwearde neah,
aethele be aethelum.     aefre ic ne hyrde thon cymlicor     ceol gehladenne heahgestreonum.     Haeleth in saeton, theodnas thrymfulle,     thegnas wlitige. tha reordode     rice theoden,
ece aelmihtig,     heht his engel gan, maerne maguthegn,     ond mete syllan, frefran feasceafte ofer flodes wylm, thaet hie the eath mihton     ofer ytha gethring drohtath adreogan.     tha gedrefed wearth,
onhrered hwaelmere.     Hornfisc plegode, glad geond garsecg,     ond se graega maew waelgifre wand.     Wedercandel swearc, windas weoxon,     waegas grundon, streamas styredon,     strengas gurron,
waedo gewaette.     Waeteregsa stod threata thrythum.     thegnas wurdon acolmode.     aenig ne wende thaet he lifgende     land begete, thara the mid Andreas     on eagorstream
ceol gesohte.     Naes him cuth tha gyt hwa tham saeflotan     sund wisode. Him tha se halga     on holmwege ofer argeblond,     Andreas tha git, thegn theodenhold,     thanc gesaegde,
ricum raesboran,     tha he gereordod waes: "the thissa swaesenda     sothfaest meotud, lifes leohtfruma,     lean forgilde, weoruda waldend,     ond the wist gife, heofonlicne hlaf,     swa thu hyldo with me
ofer firigendstream     freode gecythdest! Nu synt gethreade     thegnas mine, geonge guthrincas.     Garsecg hlymmeth, geofon geotende.     Grund is onhrered, deope gedrefed, duguth is geswenced,
modigra maegen     myclum gebysgod." Him of helman oncwaeth     haeletha scyppend: "Laet nu geferian     flotan userne, lid to lande     ofer lagufaesten, ond thonne gebidan     beornas thine,
aras on earde,     hwaenne thu eft cyme." Edre him tha eorlas     agefan ondsware, thegnas throhthearde,     thafigan ne woldon thaet hie forleton     aet lides stefnan leofne lareow     ond him land curon:
"Hwider hweorfath we     hlafordlease, geomormode,     gode orfeorme, synnum wunde,     gif we swicath the? We bioth lathe     on landa gehwam, folcum fracothe,     thonne fira bearn,
ellenrofe,     aeht besittath, hwylc hira selost     symle gelaeste hlaforde aet hilde,     thonne hand ond rond on beaduwange     billum forgrunden aet nithplegan     nearu throwedon."
tha reordade     rice theoden, waerfaest cining,     word stunde ahof: "Gif thu thegn sie     thrymsittendes, wuldorcyninges,     swa thu worde becwist, rece tha gerynu,     hu he reordberend
laerde under lyfte.     Lang is thes sithfaet ofer fealuwne flod;     frefra thine maecgas on mode.     Mycel is nu gena lad ofer lagustream,     land swithe feorr to gesecanne. Sund is geblonden,
grund with greote.     God eathe maeg heatholithendum     helpe gefremman." Ongan tha gleawlice     gingran sine, wuldorspedige weras     wordum trymman: "Ge thaet gehogodon,     tha ge on holm stigon,
thaet ge on fara folc     feorh gelaeddon, ond for dryhtnes lufan     death throwodon, on aelmyrcna     ethelrice sawle gesealdon.     Ic thaet sylfa wat, thaet us gescyldeth     scyppend engla,
weoruda dryhten.     Waeteregsa sceal, gethyd ond gethreatod     thurh thrythcining, lagu lacende,     lithra wyrthan. Swa gesaelde iu,     thaet we on saebate ofer waruthgewinn     waeda cunnedan,
farothridende.     Frecne thuhton egle ealada.     Eagorstreamas beoton bordstaethu,     brim oft oncwaeth, yth otherre.     Hwilum upp astod of brimes bosme     on bates faethm
egesa ofer ythlid.     aelmihtig thaer, meotud mancynnes,     on merethyssan beorht basnode.     Beornas wurdon forhte on mode,     frithes wilnedon, miltsa to maerum.     tha seo menigo ongan
clypian on ceole,     cyning sona aras, engla eadgifa,     ythum stilde, waeteres waelmum.     Windas threade, sae sessade,     smylte wurdon merestreama gemeotu.     tha ure mod ahloh
syththan we gesegon     under swegles gang windas ond waegas     ond waeterbrogan forhte gewordne     for frean egesan. Forthan ic eow to sothe     secgan wille, thaet naefre forlaeteth     lifgende god
eorl on eorthan,     gif his ellen deah." Swa hleothrode     halig cempa, theawum gethancul.     thegnas laerde eadig oreta,     eorlas trymede, oththaet hie semninga     slaep ofereode,
methe be maeste.     Mere sweotherade, ytha ongin     eft oncyrde, hreoh holmthracu.     tha tham halgan wearth aefter gryrehwile     gast geblissod. Ongan tha reordigan     raedum snottor,
wis on gewitte,     wordlocan onspeonn: "Naefre ic saelidan     selran mette, macraeftigran,     thaes the me thynceth, rowend rofran,     raedsnotterran, wordes wisran.     Ic wille the,
eorl unforcuth,     anre nu gena bene biddan,     theah ic the beaga lyt, sincweorthunga,     syllan mihte, faetedsinces.     Wolde ic freondscipe, theoden thrymfaest, thinne, gif ic mehte,
begitan godne.     thaes thu gife hleotest, haligne hyht     on heofonthrymme, gif thu lidwerigum     larna thinra este wyrthest.     Wolde ic anes to the, cynerof haeleth,     craeftes neosan,
thaet thu me getaehte,     nu the tir cyning ond miht forgeaf,     manna scyppend, hu thu waegflotan     waere bestemdon, saehengeste,     sund wisige. Ic waes on gifethe     iu ond nu tha
syxtyne sithum     on saebate, mere hrerendum     mundum freorig, eagorstreamas,     (is thys ane ma), swa ic aefre ne geseah     aenigne mann, thrythbearn haeletha, the gelicne,
steoran ofer staefnan.     Streamwelm hwileth, beateth brimstaetho.     Is thes bat ful scrid, faereth famigheals,     fugole gelicost glideth on geofone.     Ic georne wat thaet ic aefre ne geseah     ofer ythlade
on saeleodan     syllicran craeft. Is thon geliccost     swa he on landsceare stille stande,     thaer hine storm ne maeg, wind awecgan,     ne waeterflodas brecan brondstaefne,     hwaethere on brim snoweth
snel under segle.     thu eart seolfa geong, wigendra hleo,     nalas wintrum frod, hafast the on fyrhthe,     farothlacende, eorles ondsware.     aeghwylces canst worda for worulde     wislic andgit."
Him ondswarode     ece dryhten: "Oft thaet gesaeleth,     thaet we on saelade, scipum under scealcum,     thonne sceor cymeth, brecath ofer baethweg,     brimhengestum. Hwilum us on ythum     earfothlice
