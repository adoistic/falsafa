Sarguzasht-e-Adam
(Sarguzasht-e-Adam)

Source: https://www.allamaiqbal.com/works/poetry/urdu/bang/text/part01/38.htm
Collection: Bang-e-Dara
Part: part01

sune
koi meri ghurbat ki dastaan mujh se
bhulaya
qissa-e-paimaan-e-awwaleen mein ne
lagi
na meri tabiyat riyaz-e-jannat mein
piya
shaoor ka jab jaam-e-aatishin mein ne
rahi
haqeeqat-e-alam ki justuju mujh ko
dikhaya
auj-e-khayal falak nashin mein ne
mila
mizaj-e-taghayyur pasand kuch aisa
kiya
qarar na zer-e-falak kahin mein ne
nikala
Kaabe se pathar ki mooraton ko kabhi
kabhi
buton ko banaya haram nashin mein ne
kabhi
main zauq-e-takallum mein Toor par pahuncha
chhupaya
noor-e-azal zer-e-aasteen mein ne
kabhi
saleeb pe apnon ne mujh ko latkaya
kiya
falak ko safar, chhod kar zameen mein ne
kabhi
main ghaar-e-Hira mein chhupa raha barson
diya
jahan ko kabhi jaam-e-aakhreen mein ne
sunaya
Hind mein aa kar sarood-e-rabbani
pasand
ki kabhi Yunan ki sar zameen mein ne
deyar
Hind ne jis dam meri sada na suni
basaya
khitta-e-Japan o mulk-e-Cheen mein ne
banaya
zarron ki tarkeeb se kabhi aalam
khilaf
maani-e-taleem-e-ehl-e-deen mein ne
lahu
se laal kiya sainkron zameenon ko
jahan
mein chhed ke paikaar-e-aql-o-deen mein ne
samajh
mein aayi haqeeqat na jab sitaron ki
usi
khayal mein raatein guzaar din mein ne
dara
sakeen na kaleesa ki mujh ko talwarein
sikhaya
masla-e-gardish-e-zameen mein ne
kashish
ka raaz huweda kiya zamane par
laga
ke aaina-e-aql-e-door-been mein ne
kiya
aseer shuaaon ko, barq-e-muztar ko
bana di
ghairat-e-jannat yeh sar zameen mein ne
magar
khabar na mili aah! raaz-e-hasti ki
kiya
khirad se jahan ko teh-e-nageen mein ne
hui
jo chashm-e-mazahir parast vaa aakhir
toh
paaya khana-e-dil mein use makeen mein ne
