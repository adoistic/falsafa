Majnoon ne sheher chhora to sehra bhi chhor de

Majnoon
ne sheher chhora to sehra bhi chhor de
Nazzare
ki hawas ho to Laila bhi chhor de
Waaiz!
kamaal-e-tark se milti hai yaan muraad
Duniya
jo chhor di hai to uqba bhi chhor de
Taqleed
ki rawish se to behtar hai khudkushi
Rasta
bhi dhoondh, Khizr ka sauda bhi chhor de
Maanind
khaama teri zubaan par hai harf-e-ghair
Begaana
shay pe naazish-e-beja bhi chhor de
Lutf
kalaam kya jo na ho dil mein dard-e-ishq
Bismil
nahin hai to tu tadapna bhi chhor de
Shabnam
ki tarah phoolon pe ro, aur chaman se chal
Is
baagh mein qayaam ka sauda bhi chhor de
Hai
aashiqi mein rasm alag sab se baithna
But
khaana bhi, Haram bhi, Kaleesa bhi chhor de
Saudagari
nahin, yeh ibaadat Khuda ki hai
Aye
be-khabar! jaza ki tamanna bhi chhor de
Achha
hai dil ke saath rahe paasbaan-e-aql
Lekin
kabhi kabhi usey tanha bhi chhor de
Jeena
woh kya jo ho nafs-e-ghair par madaar
Shohrat
ki zindagi ka bharosa bhi chhor de
Shokhi
si hai sawaal-e-mukarrar mein aye Kaleem
!
Shart
raza yeh hai ke taqaaza bhi chhor de
Waaiz
saboot laaye jo mai ke jawaaz mein
Iqbal
ko yeh zid hai ke peena bhi chhor de
