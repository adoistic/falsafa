Kya kahun apne chaman se main juda kyonkar hua
Aur aseer-e-halqa-e-daam-e-hawa kyonkar hua

Jaaye hairat hai bura saare zamaane ka hoon main
Mujh ko yeh khil'at sharaafat ka ata kyonkar hua

Kuch dikhane dekhne ka tha taqaza Tur par
Kya khabar hai tujh ko ae dil faisla kyonkar hua

Hai talab be-mudda'a hone ki bhi ik mudda'a
Murgh-e-dil daam-e-tamanna se riha kyonkar hua

Dekhne waale yahan bhi dekh lete hain tujhe
Phir yeh waada-e-hashr ka sabr-aazma kyonkar hua

Husn-e-kaamil hi na ho is be-hijaabi ka sabab
Woh jo tha pardon mein pinhaan, khud-numa kyonkar hua

Maut ka nuskha abhi baaqi hai ae dard-e-firaaq!
Chaaragar deewana hai, main la-dawa kyonkar hua

Tu ne dekha hai kabhi ae deeda-e-ibrat ke gul
Ho ke paida khaak se rangeen-qaba kyonkar hua

Pursish-e-aamaal se maqsad tha ruswai meri
Warna zaahir tha sabhi kuch, kya hua, kyonkar hua

Mere mitne ka tamasha dekhne ki cheez thi
Kya bataun un ka mera saamna kyonkar hua
