Bacha aur Shama

Kaisi
hairani hai yeh ae tiflak-e-parwana khoo
!
Shama
ke sholon ko ghadiyon dekhta rehta hai tu
Yeh
meri aaghosh mein baithe hue junbish hai kya
Roshni
se kya baghal-geeri hai tera mudda'a?
Iss
nazare se tera nanha sa dil hairaan hai
Yeh
kisi dekhi hui shai ki magar pehchaan hai
Shama
ik shola hai lekin tu sarapa noor hai
Aah
!
is mehfil mein yeh uryaan hai tu mastoor hai
Dast-e
qudrat ne ise kya jaane kyun uryaan kiya
!
tujh ko khaak-e-teera ke fanoos mein pinhaan kiya
Noor
tera chhip gaya zer-e-naqaab-e-aagahi
Hai
ghubaar-e-deeda-e-beena hijaab-e-aagahi
Zindagani
jis ko kehte hain faramoshi hai yeh
Khwaab
hai, ghaflat hai, sarmasti hai, be-hoshi hai yeh
Mehfil-e
qudrat hai ik darya-e-be-payaan-e-husn
Aankh
agar dekhe to har qatre mein hai toofan-e-husn
Husn
, kohistaan ki haibat naak khamoshi mein hai
Mehr
ki zau-gustari, shab ki siyah-poshi mein hai
Aasmaan-e
subah ki aaina-poshi mein hai yeh
Shaam
ki zulmat, shafaq ki gul-faroshi mein hai yeh
Azmat-e
dereena ke mitte hue aasaar mein
Tiflak-e
na-aashna ki koshish-e-guftaar mein
Saakinan-e
sehn-e-gulshan ki hum-aawazi mein hai
Nanhe
nanhe taairon ki aashiyan-saazi mein hai
Chashma-e
kohsaar mein, darya ki aazadi mein husn
Sheher
mein, sehra mein, veeranay mein, aabadi mein husn
Rooh
ko lekin kisi gum-gashta shai ki hai hawas
Varna
is sehra mein kyun naalaan hai yeh misl-e-jaras
!
Husn
ke is aam jalway mein bhi yeh be-taab hai
Zindagi
is ki misaal maahi-e-be-aab hai
