Jinhein main dhoondta tha aasmanon mein zameenon mein

Jinhein main dhoondta tha aasmanon mein zameenon mein
Woh nikle mere zulmat-khana-e-dil ke makeenon mein
Haqeeqat apni aankhon par numayan jab hui apni
Makaan nikla hamare khana-e-dil ke makeenon mein
Agar kuch aashna hota mazaq-e-jabeha-saai se
Toh sang-e-aastan-e-Kaaba ja milta jabeenon mein
Kabhi apna bhi nazara kiya hai tu ne ae Majnun
Ke Laila ki tarah tu khud bhi hai mehmil-nasheenon mein
Maheene vasl ke ghariyon ki surat udte jaate hain
Magar ghariyan judaai ki guzarti hain maheenon mein
Mujhe roke ga tu ae nakhuda kya gharq hone se
Ke jin ko doobna ho, doob jaate hain safeenon mein
Chupaya husn ko apne Kaleem-Ullah se jisne
Wohi naaz-afreen hai jalwa-pera naazneenon mein
Jala sakti hai shama-e-kushta ko mauj-e-nafas un ki
Ilahi! kya chhupa hota hai ehl-e-dil ke seenon mein
Tamanna-e-dard-e-dil ki ho toh kar khidmat faqeeron ki
Nahin milta yeh gauhar badshahon ke khazeenon mein
Na pooch in khirqa-poshon ki, iradat ho toh dekh un ko
Yad-e-baiza liye baithe hain apni aasteenon mein
Tarasti hai nigaah-e-na-rasa jis ke nazare ko
Woh raunaq-e-anjuman ki hai inhi khalwat-guzinon mein
Kisi aise sharar se phoonk apne khirman-e-dil ko
Ke khursheed-e-qayamat bhi ho tere khosha-cheenon mein
Mohabbat ke liye dil dhoond koi tootne wala
Yeh woh mai hai jise rakhte hain naazuk aabgeenon mein
Sarapa husn ban jaata hai jis ke husn ka aashiq
Bhala ae dil haseen aisa bhi hai koi haseenon mein
Phadak utha koi teri adaa-e-'Ma Arafna' par
Tera rutba raha barh charh ke sab naaz-afreenon mein
Numayan ho ke dikhla de kabhi un ko jamaal apna
Bahut muddat se charche hain tere bareek-beenon mein
Khamosh ae dil!, bhari mehfil mein chillana nahin achha
Adab pehla qareena hai mohabbat ke qareenon mein
Bura samjhun unhein mujh se toh aisa ho nahin sakta
Ke main khud bhi toh hoon 'Iqbal' apne nukta-cheenon mein
