Hindustani Bachon Ka Qaumi Geet
(Hindustani Bachon Ka Qaumi Geet)

Chishti ne jis zameen mein paigham-e-haq sunaya
Nanak ne jis chaman mein wahdat ka geet gaya
Tatariyon ne jis ko apna watan banaya
Jis ne Hijaziyon se dasht-e-Arab chhudaya
Mera watan wohi hai, mera watan wohi hai

Yunanion ko jis ne hairaan kar diya tha
Saare jahan ko jis ne ilm-o-hunar diya tha
Mitti ko jis ki haq ne zar ka asar diya tha
Turkon ka jis ne daaman heeron se bhar diya tha
Mera watan wohi hai, mera watan wohi hai

Toote thay jo sitaare Faras ke aasman se
Phir taab de ke jis ne chamkaye kehkashan se
Wahdat ki lay suni thi duniya ne jis makaan se
Meer-e-Arab ko aayi thandi hawa jahan se
Mera watan wohi hai, mera watan wohi hai

Bande Kaleem jis ke, parbat jahan ke Seena
Nuh Nabi ka aa kar thehra jahan safeena
Rifat hai jis zameen ki baam-e-falak ka zeena
Jannat ki zindagi hai jis ki fiza mein jeena
Mera watan wohi hai, mera watan wohi hai
