Zuhd aur Rindi
(Zuhd aur Rindi)

Zuhd 
      aur
Rindi
Maulvi sahab ki sunata hoon kahani
Tezi
nahin manzoor tabiyat ki dikhani
Shohra
tha bahut aap ki soofi-munshi ka
Karte
the adab un ka a'ali o adani
Kehte
the ke pinhan hai tasawwuf mein shariat
Tarah ke alfaaz mein muzmar hon ma'ani
Labrez
mai-e-zuhd se thi dil ki surahi
Thi
teh mein kahin dard-e-khayal-e-hama-dani
Karte
the bayan aap karamat ka apni
Manzoor
thi ta'daad muridon ki barhani
Muddat
se raha karte the hamsaye mein mere
Thi
rind se zahid ki mulaqat purani
Hazrat
ne mere ek shanasa se yeh poocha
Iqbal
, ke hai qumri-e-shamshad-e-ma'ani
Pabandi
ahkaam-e-shariat mein hai kaisa?
She'r mein hai rashk-e-Kaleem-e-Hamadani
Sunta
hoon ke kafir nahin Hindu ko samajhta
Aisa aqidah asar-e-falsafa-dani
Us ki tabiyat mein tashayyu' bhi zara sa
Tafzeel
-e-Ali hum ne suni us ki zabani
Samjha
hai ke hai raag ibadaat mein dakhil
Maqsood
hai mazhab ki magar khaak urani
Kuch
aar ise husn-faroshon se nahin hai
Aadat
yeh hamare shu'ara ki hai purani
Gana
jo hai shab ko to seher ko hai tilawat
Is ramz ke ab tak na khule hum pe ma'ani
Lekin
yeh suna apne muridon se hai maine
Daagh hai manind-e-seher us ki jawani
Majmu'a
-e-azdaad hai, Iqbal nahin hai
Daftar-e-hikmat hai, tabiyat khafqani
Rindi
se bhi aagah shariat se bhi waqif
Poocho
jo tasawwuf ki to Mansoor ka saani
Is shakhs ki hum par to haqeeqat nahin khulti
Hoga yeh kisi aur hi Islam ka baani
Al-qissa
bahut tool diya wa'az ko apne
Der rahi aap ki yeh naghz-bayani
Sheher mein jo baat ho urr jaati hai sab mein
Main
ne bhi suni apne ahbaab ki zabani
Din jo sar-e-raah mile Hazrat-e-Zahid
Phir
chhirr gayi baaton mein wohi baat purani
Farmaya
, shikayat woh muhabbat ke sabab thi
Tha
farz mera raah-e-shariat ki dikhani
Main
ne yeh kaha koi gila mujh ko nahin hai
Aap ka haq tha ze-rah-e-qurb-e-makani
Hai sar-e-tasleem mera aap ke aage
Peeri
hai tawazo ke sabab meri jawani
Aap ko maloom nahin meri haqeeqat
Paida
nahin kuch is se qusoor-e-hama-dani
Main
khud bhi nahin apni haqeeqat ka shanasa
Gehra
hai mere behr-e-khayalaat ka paani
Mujh
ko bhi tamanna hai ke 'Iqbal' ko dekhun
Us ki judai mein bahut ashk-fishani
Iqbal
bhi 'Iqbal' se aagah nahin hai
Kuch
is mein tamaskhur nahin, Wallah nahin hai
