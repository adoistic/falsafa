Shama-o-Parwana
(Shama-o-Parwana)

Parwana
Tujh se karta hai ae shama pyaar kyun
Yeh
jaan beqarar hai tujh par nisaar kyun
Seemab
waar rakhti hai teri ada ise
Aadaab
ishq tu ne sikhaye hain kya ise?
Karta
hai yeh tawaf teri jalwa-gah ka
Phoonka
hua hai kya teri barq-e-nigah ka?
Aazaar
maut mein ise aaram-e-jaan hai kya?
Sho'le
mein tere zindagi-e-jawidaan hai kya?
Gham
khana-e-jahan mein jo teri ziya na ho
Is
tafta dil ka nakhl-e-tamanna hara na ho
Girna
tere huzoor mein is ki namaz hai
Nanhe
se dil mein lazzat-e-soz-o-gudaaz hai
Kuch
is mein josh-e-aashiq-e-husn-e-qadeem hai
Chhota
sa Toor to yeh zara sa Kaleem hai
Parwana
, aur zauq-e-tamasha-e-roshni
Keera
zara sa, aur tamanna-e-roshni
!
