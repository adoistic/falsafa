Sach keh doon ae Brahman! gar tu bura na maane
Tere sanam-kadon ke but ho gaye purane
Apnon se bair rakhna tu ne buton se seekha
Jang-o-jadal sikhaya waaiz ko bhi Khuda ne
Tang aa ke main ne aakhir dair-o-haram ko chhora
Waaiz ka waaz chhora, chhore tere fasaane
Patthar ki mooraton mein samjha hai tu Khuda hai
Khaak-e-watan ka mujh ko har zarra devta hai
Aa, ghairiyat ke parde ik baar phir utha dein
Bichhdon ko phir mila dein, naqsh-e-dui mita dein
Sooni padi hui hai muddat se dil ki basti
Aa, ik naya shivala is des mein bana dein
Duniya ke teerthon se ooncha ho apna teerath
Damaan-e-aasmaan se is ka kalas mila dein
Har subah uth ke gaayein mantra woh meethe meethe
Saare pujariyon ko mai preet ki pila dein
Shakti bhi shaanti bhi bhagton ke geet mein hai
Dharti ke baasiyon ki mukti preet mein hai
