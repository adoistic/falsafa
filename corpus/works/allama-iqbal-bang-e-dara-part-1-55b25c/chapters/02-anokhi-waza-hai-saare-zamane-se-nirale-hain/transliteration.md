Anokhi waza hai, saare zamane se nirale hain
Yeh aashiq kaun si basti ke ya Rab rehne wale hain
Ilaaj-e-dard mein bhi dard ki lazzat pe marta hoon
Jo thay chhalon mein kaante, nok-e-sozan se nikale hain
Phala phoola rahe ya Rab! Chaman meri umeedon ka
Jigar ka khoon de de kar yeh boote maine paale hain
Rulati hai mujhe raaton ko khamoshi sitaron ki
Nirala ishq hai mera, nirale mere naale hain
Na poochho mujh se lazzat-e-khanumaan barbaad rehne ki
Nasheman sainkron maine bana kar phoonk daale hain
Nahin begaanagi achhi rafeeq-e-raah-e-manzil se
Thehar ja ae sharar, hum bhi to aakhir mitne wale hain
Umeed-e-hoor ne sab kuch sikha rakha hai waaiz ko
Yeh Hazrat dekhne mein seedhe saadhe, bhole bhale hain
Mere ashaar ae Iqbal kyun pyaare na hon mujh ko
Mere toote hue dil ke yeh dard angez naale hain
