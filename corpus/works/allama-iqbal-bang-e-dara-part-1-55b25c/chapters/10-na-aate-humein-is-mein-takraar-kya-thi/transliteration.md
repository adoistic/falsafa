Na aate, humein is mein takraar kya thi
Magar waada karte hue aar kya thi

Tumhaare payaami ne sab raaz khola
Khata is mein bande ki sarkaar kya thi

Bhari bazm mein apne aashiq ko taada
Teri aankh masti mein hoshiyaar kya thi!

Taammul to tha un ko aane mein qaasid
Magar yeh bata tarz-e-inkaar kya thi

Khinche khud ba khud jaanib-e-Toor-e-Musa
Kashish teri ae shauq-e-deedar kya thi!

Kahin zikr rehta hai Iqbal tera
Fusoon tha koi, teri guftaar kya thi
