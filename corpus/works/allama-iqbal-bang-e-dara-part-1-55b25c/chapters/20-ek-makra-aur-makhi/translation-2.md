Ek Makra aur Makhi
(Ek Makra aur Makhi)

Ek
din kisi makkhi se yeh kehne laga makra
Is
raah se hota hai guzar roz tumhara
Lekin
meri kutia ki na jaagi kabhi qismat
Bhoole
se kabhi tum ne yahan paaon na rakha
Ghairon
se na miliye to koi baat nahin hai
Apnon
se magar chahiye yun khinch ke na rehna
Aao
jo mere ghar mein toh izzat hai yeh meri
Woh
saamne seedhi hai jo manzoor ho aana
Makkhi
ne suni baat jo makray ki to boli
Hazrat!
kisi nadaan ko deejiye ga yeh dhoka
Is
jaal mein makkhi kabhi aane ki nahin hai
Jo
aap ki seedhi pe charha, phir nahin utra
Makray
ne kaha wah! farebi mujhe samjhe
Tum
sa koi nadaan zamanay mein na ho ga
Manzoor
tumhari mujhe khaatir thi wagarna
Kuch
fayeda apna to mera is mein nahin tha
Udti
hui aayi ho Khuda jaane kahan se
Thehro
jo mere ghar mein to hai is mein bura kya!
Is
ghar mein kayi tum ko dikhane ki hain cheezein
Bahar
se nazar aata hai chhoti si yeh kutia
Latke
huye darwazon pe bareek hain parday
Deewaron
ko aainon se hai main ne sajaya
Mehmaanon
ke aaraam ko haazir hain bichhaunay
Har
shakhs ko samaan yeh mayassar nahin hota
Makkhi
ne kaha khair, yeh sab theek hai lekin
Main
aap ke ghar aaun, yeh umeed na rakhna
In
narm bichhaunon se Khuda mujh ko bachaye
So
jaye koi in pe to phir uth nahin sakta
Makray
ne kaha dil mein suni baat jo us ki
Phaansun
ise kis tarah yeh kam-bakht hai daana
Sau
kaam khushamad se nikalte hain jahan mein
Dekho
jise duniya mein khushamad ka hai banda
Yeh
soch ke makkhi se kaha us ne Badi Bi!
Allah
ne bakhsha hai bada aap ko rutba
Hoti
hai use aap ki surat se mohabbat
Ho
jis ne kabhi ek nazar aap ko dekha
Aankhein
hain ke heeray ki chamakti hui kaniyan
Sar
aap ka Allah ne kalghi se sajaya
Yeh
husn, yeh poshaak, yeh khoobi, yeh safai
Phir
is pe qayamat hai yeh udte hue gaana
Makkhi
ne suni jab yeh khushamad to paseeji
Boli
ke nahin aap se mujh ko koi khatka
Inkaar
ki aadat ko samajhti hoon bura main
Sach
yeh hai ke dil todna achha nahin hota
Yeh
baat kahi aur udi apni jagah se
Paas
aayi to makray ne uchhal kar use pakda
Bhooka
tha kayi roz se ab haath jo aayi
Aaraam
se ghar baith ke makkhi ko uraya
