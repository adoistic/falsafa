Laaon woh tinke kahin se aashiyane ke liye

Laaon
woh tinke kahin se aashiyane ke liye
Bijliyan
be-taab hon jin ko jalaane ke liye
Waaye
naakaami, falak ne taak kar toda use
Main
ne jis daali ko taada aashiyane ke liye
Aankh
mil jaati hai haftaad-o-do millat se teri
Ek
paimaana tera saare zamaane ke liye
Dil
mein koi is tarah ki aarzoo paida karoon
Laut
jaaye aasmaan mere mitaane ke liye
Jama
kar khirman toh pehle daana daana chun ke tu
Aa
hi nikle gi koi bijli jalaane ke liye
Paas
tha naakaami-e-sayyad ka ae hum-safeer
Varna
main, aur ud ke aata ek daane ke liye
!
Iss
chaman mein murgh-e-dil gaaye na aazaadi ka geet
Aah
yeh gulshan nahin aise taraane ke liye
