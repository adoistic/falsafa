Bachay Ki Dua

lab pe aati hai dua ban ke tamanna meri
zindagi shama ki surat ho Khudaya meri
door duniya ka mere dam se andhera ho jaye
har jagah mere chamakne se ujala ho jaye
ho mere dam se yunhi mere watan ki zeenat
jis tarah phool se hoti hai chaman ki zeenat
zindagi ho meri parwane ki surat Ya Rab
ilm ki shama se ho mujh ko mohabbat Ya Rab
ho mera kaam ghareebon ki himayat karna
dardmandon se zaeefon se mohabbat karna
mere Allah! burai se bachana mujh ko
nek jo raah ho uss reh pe chalana mujh ko
