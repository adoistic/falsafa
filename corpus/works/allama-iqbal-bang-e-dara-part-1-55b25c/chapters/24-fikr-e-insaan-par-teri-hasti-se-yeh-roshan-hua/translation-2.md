Fikr-e-insaan par teri hasti se yeh roshan hua
Hai par-e-murgh-e-takhaiyul ki rasaai ta kuja
Tha sarapa rooh tu, bazm-e-sukhan paikar tera
Zaib-e-mehfil bhi raha, mehfil se pinhaan bhi raha
Deed teri aankh ko uss husn ki manzoor hai
Ban ke soz-e-zindagi har shai mein jo mastoor hai

Mehfil-e-hasti teri barbat se hai sarmaya-daar
Jis tarah nadi ke naghmon se sukoot-e-kohsaar
Tere firdaus-e-takhaiyul se hai qudrat ki bahaar
Teri kisht-e-fikr se ugte hain aalam sabza-waar
Zindagi muzmir hai teri shokhi-e-tehreer mein
Taab-e-goyai se jumbish hai lab-e-tasveer mein

Nutq ko sau naaz hain tere lab-e-eijaz par
Mehv-e-hairat hai surayya rif'at-e-parwaaz par
Shaahid-e-mazmoon tasadduq hai tere andaaz par
Khanda-zan hai ghuncha-e-Dilli gul-e-Shiraz par
Aah!
Tu ujri hui Dilli mein aaramida hai
Gulshan-e-Weimar mein tera hum-nawa khwabida hai

Lutf-e-goyai mein teri hum-sari mumkin nahin
Ho takhaiyul ka na jab tak fikr-e-kaamil hum-nasheen
Haaye!
Ab kya ho gayi Hindustan ki sar-zameen
Aah! Ae nazara-amoz nigaah-e-nukta-been
Gaisu-e-Urdu abhi minnat-pazeer-e-shana hai
Shama yeh saudai-e-dil-sozi-e-parwana hai

Ae Jahanabad, ae gehwara-e-ilm-o-hunar
Hain sarapa naala-e-khamosh tere baam-o-dar
Zarre zarre mein tere khwabida hain shams-o-qamar
Yun toh posheeda hain teri khaak mein laakhon gauhar
Dafn tujh mein koi fakhr-e-rozgaar aisa bhi hai?
Tujh mein pinhaan koi moti aabdaar aisa bhi hai?
