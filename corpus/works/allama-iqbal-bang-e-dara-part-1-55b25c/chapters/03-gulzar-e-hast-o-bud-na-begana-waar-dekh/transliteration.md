Gulzar
hast-o-bud na begana-waar dekh
Hai
dekhne ki cheez ise baar baar dekh
Aaya
hai tu jahaan mein misaal-e-sharaar dekh
Dam
de na jaaye hasti-e-na-paidaar dekh
Maana
ke teri deed ke qaabil nahin hoon main
Tu
mera shauq dekh, mera intezaar dekh
Kholi
hain zauq-e-deed ne aankhein teri agar
Har
reh-guzar mein naqsh-e-kaf-e-paa-e-yaar dekh
