Kushaada dast-e-karam jab woh beniyaaz kare
Niyaazmand na kyun aajizi pe naaz kare
Bitha ke arsh pe rakkha hai tu ne ae waaiz!
Khuda woh kya hai jo bandon se ehtiraaz kare
Meri nigaah mein woh rind hi nahin saaqi
Jo hoshiyaari o masti mein imtiyaaz kare
Madaam gosh ba-dil reh, yeh saaz hai aisa
Jo ho shikasta toh paida nawaa-e-raaz kare
Koi yeh poochhe ke waaiz ka kya bigadta hai
Jo be-amal pe bhi rehmat woh beniyaaz kare
Sukhan mein soz, Ilaahi kahaan se aata hai
Yeh cheez woh hai ke patthar ko bhi gudaaz kare
Tameez-e-laala-o-gul se hai naala-e-bulbul
Jahaan mein waa na koi chashm-e-imtiyaaz kare
Ghuroor-e-zohd ne sikhla diya hai waaiz ko
Ke bandgaan-e-Khuda par zabaan daraaz kare
Hawa ho aisi ke Hindustaan se ae Iqbal
Udaa ke mujh ko ghubaar-e-rah-e-Hijaaz kare
