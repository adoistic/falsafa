(Shair)

Source: https://www.allamaiqbal.com/works/poetry/urdu/bang/text/part01/29.htm
Collection: Bang-e-Dara
Part: part01

Qaum
goya jism hai, afraad hain aaza-e-qaum
Manzil
san'at ke reh-paima hain dast-o-paa-e-qaum
Mehfil
nazm-e-hukumat, chehra-e-zeba-e-qaum
Shair
rangeen nawa hai deeda-e-beena-e-qaum
Mubtala-e
dard koi uzv ho roti hai aankh
Kis
qadar hamdard saare jism ki hoti hai aankh
