The Poet

The Nation is a body, a living frame,
Its people are the limbs that bear its name.

Its hands and feet tread paths of craft and trade,
Its government, a graceful face displayed.

The Poet is the Nation’s seeing eye;
When one part aches, it cannot help but cry.
