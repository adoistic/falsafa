Ehd-e-Tifli

Thay
dayaar-e-nau, zameen o aasmaan meray liye
Vus'at-e-
aaghosh-e-maadar ik jahaan meray liye
Thi
har ik jumbish nishaan-e-lutf-e-jaan meray liye
Harf-e-
be-matlab thi khud meri zubaan meray liye
Dard
, tifli mein agar koi rulaata tha mujhe
Shorish-e-
zanjeer-e-dar mein lutf aata tha mujhe
Taktay
rehna haaye! woh pehron talak soo-e-qamar
Woh
phatay baadal mein be-aawaaz pa uss ka safar
Poochna
reh reh ke uss ke koh-o-sahra ki khabar
Aur
woh hairat-e-darogh-e-maslihat-aamez par
Aankh
waqf-e-deed thi, lab maa'il-e-guftaar tha
Dil
na tha mera, saraapa zauq-e-istifsaar tha
