Kahoon kya aarzoo-e be-dili mujh ko kahan tak hai

Kahoon
kya aarzoo-e be-dili mujh ko kahan tak hai
Mere
bazaar ki raunaq hi sauda-e ziyaan tak hai
Woh
mai-kash hoon farogh-e-mai se khud gulzaar ban jaaoon
Hawaa-e
gul firaaq-e saaqi-e na-mehrbaan tak hai
Chaman
afroz hai sayyaad meri khush-nawaai tak
Rahi
bijli ki be-taabi, so mere aashiyaan tak hai
Woh
musht-e-khaak hoon, faiz-e-pareshaani se sehra hoon
Na
poocho meri vus'at ki, zameen se aasmaan tak hai
Jaras
hoon, naala-e-khwaabeeda hai mere har rag-o-pai mein
Yeh
khaamoshi meri waqt-e-raheel-e-kaarvaan tak hai
Sukoon
dil se saamaan-e-kushood-e-kaar paida kar
Keh
uqda-e-khaatir-e-girdaab ka aab-e-rawaan tak hai
Chaman
zaar-e-mohabbat mein khaamoshi maut hai bulbul
!
Yahan
ki zindagi paabandi-e-rasm-e-faghaan tak hai
Jawaani
hai toh zauq-e-deed bhi, lutf-e-tamanna bhi
Hamaare
ghar ki aabaadi qiyaam-e-mehmaan tak hai
Zamaane
bhar mein ruswa hoon magar ae vaaye naadaani
!
Samajhta
hoon keh mera ishq mere raazdaan tak hai
