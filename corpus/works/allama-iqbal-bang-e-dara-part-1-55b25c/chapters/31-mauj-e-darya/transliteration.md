hasti hai tarap soorat-e-seemab mujhe

hai naam mera, behr hai payaab mujhe

na zanjeer kabhi halqa-e-girdab mujhe
Aab
mein misl-e-hawa jaata hai tausan mera
Khaar

Mein
uchhalti hoon kabhi jazb-e-mah-e-kaamil se

mein sar ko patakti hoon kabhi saahil se
