Tere ishq ki inteha chahta hoon

Tere
ishq ki inteha chahta hoon
Meri
saadgi dekh kya chahta hoon
Sitam
ho ke ho waada-e-be-hijaabi
Koi
baat sabr-aazma chahta hoon
Yeh
jannat mubarak rahe zahidon ko
Ke
main aap ka saamna chahta hoon
Zara
sa to dil hoon, magar shokh itna
Wohi
lan taraani sunna chahta hoon
Koi
dam ka mehmaan hoon ae ahl-e-mehfil
Chiragh
-e-seher hoon, bujha chahta hoon
Bhari
bazm mein raaz ki baat keh di
Bada
be-adab hoon, saza chahta hoon
