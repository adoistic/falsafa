Dil
(Dil)

Daar-o-rasan baazi-e-tiflaana-e-dil
Iltija-e
Arini'
      surkhi-e-afsaana-e-dil
 Rab is saaghar-e-labrez ki mai kya ho gi
Jalwa-e-
mulk-e-baqa hai khat-e-paimana-e-dil
Abr-e-
rehmat tha ke thi ishq ki bijli, Ya Rab!
 Jal gayi mazra-e-hasti to uga daana-e-dil
Husn
ka ganj-e-giraan-maaya tujhe mil jaata
 Aye Farhaad! na khoda kabhi veeraana-e-dil
Arsh
ka hai kabhi Kaabe ka hai dhoka us par
Kis ki manzil hai Ilahi! mera kaashaana-e-dil
 Sab ko apna hai junoon aur mujhe sauda apna
 Kisi aur ka deewaana, main deewaana-e-dil
 Samajhta nahin aye zaahid-e-naadaan is ko
 Rashk-e-
sad sajda hai ik laghzish-e-mastaana-e-dil
 Khaak
ke dher ko ikseer bana deti hai
 Ye asar rakhti hai khaakistar-e-parwaana-e-dil
 Ishq
ke daam mein phans kar yeh riha hota hai
 Barq
girti hai to yeh nakhl hara hota hai
