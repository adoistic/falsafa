Maa ka Khwab
(Maa Ka Khwab)

Main
Soi jo ik shab to dekha yeh khwab
Badha
Aur jis se mera iztiraab
Yeh
Dekha ke main ja rahi hoon kahin
Andhera
Hai aur raah milti nahin
Larzata
Tha darr se mera baal baal
Qadam
Ka tha dehshat se uthna muhaal
Jo
Kuch hausla pa ke aage badhi
To
Dekha qitaar ek ladkon ki thi
Zumurrud
Si poshaak pehne huey
Diye
Sab ke haathon mein jalte huey
Woh
Chup chaap the aage peechhe rawaan
Khuda
Jaane jaana tha un ko kahan
Isi
Soch mein thi ke mera pisar
Mujhe
Us jamaat mein aaya nazar
Woh
Peechhe tha aur tez chalta na tha
Diya
Us ke haathon mein jalta na tha
Kaha
Main ne pehchaan kar, meri jaan
!
Mujhe
Chhod kar aa gaye tum kahan
!
Judaai
Mein rehti hoon main be-qaraar
Paroti
Hoon har roz ashkon ke haar
Na
Parwa hamari zara tum ne ki
Gaye
Chhod, achhi wafa tum ne ki
Jo
Bachay ne dekha mera paich-o-taab
Diya
Us ne munh pher kar yun jawab
Rulaati
Hai tujh ko judaai meri
Nahin
Is mein kuch bhi bhalaai meri
Yeh
Keh kar woh kuch der tak chup raha
Diya
Phir dikha kar yeh kehne laga
Samajhti
Hai tu ho gaya kya isse?
Tere
