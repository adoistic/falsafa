...I am harsh upon my heart; of all else, heedless I am.
...ah, how well it is said: a tyrant I am, an ignorant one I am.
...the falsehood that vanishes when the Truth appears, I am.
...alas, my deprivation! A gatherer of worthless shards on the shore, I am.
...that heedless one for whose neglect the very angels weep, I am.
...you are but an image of the Assembly, while the Assembly itself, I am.

I wander, searching for my own self, O Iqbal;
For I am the traveler, and the destination I am.
