Sakhtiyan karta hoon dil par, ghair se ghafil hoon main
Haaye! Kya achhi kahi, zaalim hoon main, jaahil hoon main

Main jabhi tak tha ke teri jalwa pairaai na thi
Jo namood-e-haq se mit jaata hai, woh baatil hoon main

Ilm ke darya se nikle ghota-zan gohar badast
Waaye mehroomi! khazaf cheen-e-lab-e-saahil hoon main

Hai meri zillat hi kuch meri sharafat ki daleel
Jis ki ghaflat ko malak rote hain, woh ghafil hoon main

Bazm-e-hasti! apni aaraish pe tu naazaan na ho
Tu toh ek tasveer hai mehfil ki aur mehfil hoon main

Dhoondta phirta hoon aye Iqbal! apne aap ko
Aap hi goya musafir, aap hi manzil hoon main
