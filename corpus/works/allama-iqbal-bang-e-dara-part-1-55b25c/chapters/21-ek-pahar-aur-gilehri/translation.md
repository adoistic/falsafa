Ek Pahar aur Gilehri
(Ek Pahar aur Gilehri)

Koi
Pahar yeh kehta tha ik gilehri se
Tujhe
ho sharam to paani mein ja ke doob mare
Zara
si cheez hai, is par ghuroor, kya kehna
Yeh
aql aur yeh samajh, yeh shaoor, kya kehna
!

Khuda
ki shaan hai naacheez cheez ban baithein
Jo
be shaoor hon yoon baatameez ban baithein
Teri
bisaat hai kya meri shaan ke aage
Zameen
hai past meri aan baan ke aage
Jo
baat mujh mein hai, tujh ko woh hai naseeb kahan
Bhala
pahar kahan jaanwar ghareeb kahan
!

Kaha
yeh sun ke gilehri ne, munh sambhaal zara
Yeh
kachi baatein hain dil se unhein nikaal zara
Jo
main badi nahin teri tarah to kya parwa
Nahin
hai tu bhi to aakhir meri tarah chhota
Har
ek cheez se paida khuda ki qudrat hai
Koi
bada, koi chhota, yeh us ki hikmat hai
Bada
jahan mein tujh ko bana diya us ne
Mujhe
darakht pe charhna sikha diya us ne
Qadam
uthane ki taaqat nahin zara tujh mein
Niri
badai hai, khoobi hai aur kya tujh mein
Jo
tu bada hai to mujh sa hunar dikha mujh ko
Yeh
chhaaliya hi zara tod kar dikha mujh ko
Nahin
hai cheez nikammi koi zamaane mein
Koi
bura nahin qudrat ke kaarkhane mein
