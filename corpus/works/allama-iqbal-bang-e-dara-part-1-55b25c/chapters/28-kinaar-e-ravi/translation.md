Kinaar-e-Ravi
(Kinaar-e-Ravi)

Sukoot-e-shaam mein mehv-e-surood hai Ravi
Na pooch mujh se jo hai kaifiyat mere dil ki

Payaam-e-sajde ka yeh zer-o-bam hua mujh ko
Jahaan tamaam sawaad-e-haram hua mujh ko

Sar-e-kinaara-e-aab-e-rawaan khada hoon main
Khabar nahin mujhe lekin kahan khada hoon main

Sharaab-e-surkh se rangeen hua hai daaman-e-shaam
Liye hai peer-e-falak dast-e-rasha-daar mein jaam

Adam ko qaafila-e-roz tez-gaam chala
Shafaq nahin hai, yeh sooraj ke phool hain goya

Khade hain door woh azmat-faza-e-tanhaai
Manaar-e-khwaabgaah-e-shehsawar-e-Chughtai

Fasana-e-sitam-e-inqilaab hai yeh mehel
Koi zamaan-e-salaf ki kitaab hai yeh mehel

Maqaam kya hai? Sarood-e-khamosh hai goya
Shajar, yeh anjuman-e-be-kharosh hai goya

Rawaan hai seena-e-darya pe ik safeena-e-tez
Hua hai mauj se mallah jis ka garm-e-sitez

Subuk-rawi mein hai misl-e-nigaah yeh kashti
Nikal ke halqa-e-hadd-e-nazar se door gayi

Jahaaz-e-zindagi-e-aadmi rawaan hai yoonhi
Abad ke behr mein paida yoonhi, nihaan hai yoonhi

Shikast se yeh kabhi aashna nahin hota
Nazar se chhupta hai lekin fana nahin hota
