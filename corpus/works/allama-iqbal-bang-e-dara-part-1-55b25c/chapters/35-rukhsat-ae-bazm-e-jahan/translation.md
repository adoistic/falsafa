Rukhsat Ae Bazm-e-Jahan
(Rukhsat Ae Bazm-e-Jahan)

Rukhsat
Ae bazm-e-jahan! soo-e-watan jaata hoon main
Aah!
Is aabaad veeraane mein ghabraata hoon main
Bas-ke main afsurda dil hoon, darkhur-e-mehfil nahin
Tu mere qaabil nahin hai, main tere qaabil nahin
Qaid hai, darbaar-e-sultan o shabistaan-e-wazeer
Tod kar niklega zanjeer-e-tilaai ka aseer
Go badi lazzat teri hangama-aaraai mein hai
Ajnabiyat si magar teri shanasai mein hai
Muddaton tere khud-araaon se hum-suhbat raha
Muddaton be-taab mauj-e-behr ki soorat raha
Muddaton baitha tere hangama-e-ishrat mein main
Roshni ki justujoo karta raha zulmat mein main
Muddaton dhoonda kiya nazara-e-gul khaar mein
Aah, woh Yusuf na haath aaya tere bazaar mein
Chashm-e-hairaan dhoondti ab aur nazaare ko hai
Aarzoo saahil ki mujh toofan ke maare ko hai
Chhod kar maanind-e-boo tera chaman jaata hoon main
Rukhsat ae bazm-e-jahan! soo-e-watan jaata hoon main
Ghar banaya hai sukoot-e-daaman-e-kohsaar mein
Aah!
Yeh lazzat kahan mausiqi-e-guftaar mein
Hum-nasheen-e-nargis-e-shehla, rafeeq-e-gul hoon main
Hai chaman mera watan, hamsaaya-e-bulbul hoon main
Shaam ko aawaz chashmon ki sulaati hai mujhe
Subah farsh-e-sabz se koyal jagaati hai mujhe
Bazm-e-hasti mein hai sab ko mehfil-aaraai pasand
Hai dil-e-shaayar ko lekin kunj-e-tanhaai pasand
Hai junoon mujh ko ke ghabraata hoon aabaadi mein main
Dhoondta phirta hoon kis ko koh ki waadi mein main?
Shauq kis ka sabza-zaaron mein phiraata hai mujhe
Aur chashmon ke kinaare par sulaata hai mujhe?
Ta'ana-zan hai tu ke shaida kunj-e-uzlat ka hoon main
Dekh ae ghaafil! payaami-e-bazm-e-qudrat ka hoon main
Hum-watan shamshaad ka, qumri ka main hum-raaz hoon
Is chaman ki khaamoshi mein gosh-bar-aawaz hoon
Kuch jo sunta hoon to auron ko sunaane ke liye
Dekhta hoon kuch to auron ko dikhaane ke liye
Aashiq-e-uzlat hai dil, naazaan hoon apne ghar pe main
Khanda-zan hoon masnad-e-Dara-o-Iskandar pe main
Letna zer-e-shajar rakhta hai jaadoo ka asar
Shaam ke taare pe jab padti ho reh reh kar nazar
Ilm ke hairat-kade mein hai kahan is ki namood!
Gul ki patti mein nazar aata hai raaz-e-hast-o-bood
