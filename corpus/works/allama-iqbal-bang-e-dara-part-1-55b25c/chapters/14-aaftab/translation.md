Aaftab

Ae
Aaftab! Rooh-o-rawaan-e-jahan hai tu
Shiraza
band-e-daftar-e-kaun-o-makaan hai tu
Baais
hai tu wajood-o-adam ki namood ka
Hai
sabz tere dam se chaman-e-hast-o-bood ka
Qayam
yeh anasiron ka tamasha tujhi se hai
Har
shay mein zindagi ka taqaza tujhi se hai
Har
shay ko teri jalwa-gari se sabaat hai
Tera
yeh soz-o-saaz sarapa hayat hai
Woh
Aaftab jis se zamaane mein noor hai
Dil
hai, khirad hai, rooh-e-rawaan hai, shaoor hai
Ae
Aaftab, hum ko ziya-e-shaoor de
Chashm-e
khirad ko apni tajalli se noor de
Hai
mehfil-e-wajood ka saamaan taraaz tu
Yazdaan-e
saakinan-e-nasheb-o-faraaz tu
Tera
kamaal-e-hasti har jaandaar mein
Teri
namood silsila-e-kohsaar mein
Har
cheez ki hayat ka parwardigaar tu
Zaaidgaan-e
noor ka hai taajdaar tu
Ne
ibtida koi na koi inteha teri
Azaad-e
qaid-e-awwal-o-aakhir ziya teri
