1

Reverence to Shri Ganesha. The compassionate Viharacharya creates this Nyaya Tilaka [Crown of Logic] for the good of students. The categories [padartha] are: substance [dravya], quality [guna], action [karma], universal [samanya], particular [vishesha], inherence [samavaya]—these are existents [bhava]. Non-existence [abhava] forms the seventh category. Among these, substance is the substrate of qualities and actions. Examples include: earth possessing fragrance, cool water, luminous fire, tangible air, intangible space [akasha], time as the cause of past-future distinctions, direction [dik] as the cause of east-west distinctions, the knowing self [atma], and mind [manas] as the instrument of knowledge. These substances are ninefold. Among these, earth, water, fire, and air exist as both eternal atoms [paramanu] and non-eternal composite forms like bodies. Space, time, direction, self, and mind are eternal. Composite forms like pots are produced from eternal atoms of earth. The elements undergo transformations...
