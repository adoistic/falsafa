The vibhāga encompasses these vākya-avayavas: pratijñā expressing sādhya-viśiṣṭa-pakṣa, hetu expressing madhya-viśiṣṭa-pakṣa, udāharaṇa expressing vyāpti-viśiṣṭa-sapakṣa, upanaya expressing vyāpti-smaraṇa through viśeṣa-viśiṣṭa configurations, nigamana expressing the final viśeṣa-viśiṣṭa determination.

Each vākya performs specific bodha-generation: pratijñā produces sādhya-pakṣa awareness, hetu produces madhya-consciousness, udāharaṇa produces vyāpti-understanding. When doṣas arise, these generate hetvābhāsas rather than valid pramāṇa-niṣṭha knowledge.

The principal hetvābhāsas: sandigdha, viruddha, satpratipakṣa, sādhyābhāvavat, āśrayāsiddha, svarūpāsiddha, and others that vitiate the anumāna-process...
