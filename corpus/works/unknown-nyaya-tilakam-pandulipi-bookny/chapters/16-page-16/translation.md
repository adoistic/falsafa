Upamāna thus generates svataḥ-pṛthag-jñāna regarding śabda-artha-saṃbandha that other pramāṇas cannot produce. The cognitive process involves smaraṇa-pratyakṣa-synthesis creating unique epistemological content.

Vākya-artha understanding operates through yogyatā ensuring meaningful word-combinations, āsatti maintaining proper sequential connection, tātparya resolving ambiguity through prakaraṇa and contextual indicators.

Śakti-graha occurs through vyavahāra recognition: hearing "ghaṭo nīla-h" activates conventional śabda-artha relationships established through community usage and verified through successful communication.
