Kisi be-kas ko ae bidaad-gar maara toh kya maara
Jo aap hi mar raha ho uss ko gar maara toh kya maara

Na maara aap ko jo khaak ho akseer ban jaata
Agar paare ko ae akseer-gar maara toh kya maara

Bade moozi ko maara nafs-e-ammara ko gar maara
Nehang o azhdaha o sher-e-nar maara toh kya maara

Khata toh dil ki thi qaabil bohot si maar khaane ke
Teri zulfon ne mushkein baandh kar maara toh kya maara

Nahin woh qaul ka sachcha hamesha qaul de de kar
Jo uss ne haath mere haath par maara toh kya maara

Tufang o teer toh zaahir na tha kuch paas qaatil ke
Ilaahi uss ne dil ko taak kar maara toh kya maara

Hansi ke saath yaan rona hai misl-e-qulqul-e-meena
Kisi ne qahqaha ae be-khabar maara toh kya maara

Mere aansu hamesha hain ba-rang-e-laal-e-gharq-e-khoon
Jo ghota aab mein tu ne gauhar maara toh kya maara

Jigar dil donon pehlu mein hain zakhmi uss ne kya jaane
Idhar maara toh kya maara udhar maara toh kya maara

Gaya shaitaan maara ek sajda ke na karne mein
Agar laakhon baras sajde mein sar maara toh kya maara

Dil-e-sangeen-e-khusrau par bhi zarb ae koh-kan pahunchi
Agar tesha sar-e-kohsaar par maara toh kya maara

Dil-e-bad-khwaah mein tha maarna ya chashm-e-bad-been mein
Falak par Zauq teer-e-aah gar maara toh kya maara
