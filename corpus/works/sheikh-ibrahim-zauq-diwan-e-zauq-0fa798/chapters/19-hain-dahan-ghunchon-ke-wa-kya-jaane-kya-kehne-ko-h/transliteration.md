Hain dahan ghunchon ke wa kya jaane kya kehne ko hain
Shaayad us ko dekh kar sall-e-ala kehne ko hain

Wasf-e-chashm o wasf-e-lab us yaar ka kehne ko hain
Aaj hum dars-e-isharaat-o-shifa kehne ko hain

Aaj un se muddai kuchh muddaa kehne ko hain
Par nahin maaloom kya kahwein ge kya kehne ko hain

Jo woh qad-o-qaamat sada kehne ko hain
Aur aashiq wasf-e-qad-o-qaamat tera kehne ko hain

Poochh aa qaatil se toh woh kab karega hum ko qatl
Aaj hum taareekh-e-marg aap ae qazaa kehne ko hain

Main tere haathon ke qurbaan waah kya maare hain teer
Sab dahan-e-zakhm munh se marhaba kehne ko hain

Woh janaaze par mere kis waqt aaye dekhna
Jab ke izn-e-aam mere aqraba kehne ko hain
