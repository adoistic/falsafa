Aankh us pur-jafa se ladti hai
Jaan kashti qaza se ladti hai

Shola bhadke na kyun ke mehfil mein
Shama tujh bin hawa se ladti hai

Qismat us but se ja ladi apni
Dekho ahmaq Khuda se ladti hai

Shor-e-qulqul yeh kyun hai dukhtar-e-raz
Kya kisi paarsa se ladti hai

Nahin mizhgaan ki do safein goya
Ik bala ik bala se ladti hai

Nigaah-e-naaz us ki aashiq se
Chhot kis kis ada se ladti hai

Tere beemar ke sar-e-baaleen
Maut kya kya shifa se ladti hai

Zaal-e-duniya ne sulah ki kis din
Yeh ladaaka sada se ladti hai

Waah kya kya tabiyat apni bhi
Ishq mein ibtida se ladti hai

Dekh us chashm-e-mast ki shokhi
Jab kisi paarsa se ladti hai

Teri shamsheer-e-khoon ke chheenton se
Chheente aab-e-baqa se ladti hai

Sach hai Al-harbu khid'atun aye Zauq
Nigaah us ki dagha se ladti hai
