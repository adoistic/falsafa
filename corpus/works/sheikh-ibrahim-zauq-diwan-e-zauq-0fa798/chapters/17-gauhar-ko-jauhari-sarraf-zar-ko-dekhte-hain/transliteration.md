Gauhar ko jauhari sarraf zar ko dekhte hain
Bashar ke hain jo mubassir bashar ko dekhte hain

Na khoob o zisth na aib-o-hunar ko dekhte hain
Yeh cheez kya hai bashar hum bashar ko dekhte hain

Woh dekhein bazm mein pehle kidhar ko dekhte hain
Muhabbat aaj tere hum asar ko dekhte hain

Woh apni burrish-e-tegh-e-nazar ko dekhte hain
Hum un ko dekhte hain aur jigar ko dekhte hain

Jab apne girya o soz-e-jigar ko dekhte hain
Sulagti aag mein hum khushk-o-tar ko dekhte hain

Rafeeq jab mere zakhm-e-jigar ko dekhte hain
Toh chara-gar unhein woh chara-gar ko dekhte hain

Na tamtaraaq ko na karr-o-farr ko dekhte hain
Hum aadmi ke sifaat o siyar ko dekhte hain

Jo raat khwaab mein us fitna-gar ko dekhte hain
Na poochh hum jo qayamat seher ko dekhte hain

Woh roz hum ko guzarta hai jaise eid ka din
Kabhi jo shakl tumhari seher ko dekhte hain

Jahan ke aainon se dil ka aaina hai juda
Us aaine mein hum aaina-gar ko dekhte hain

Bana ke aaina dekhe hai pehle aaina-gar
Hunar-war apne hi aib-o-hunar ko dekhte hain
