Nigah ka waar tha dil par phadakne jaan lagi
Chali thi barchhi kisi par kisi ke aan lagi

Tera zubaan se milaana zubaan jo yaad aaya
Na haaye haaye mein taalu se phir zubaan lagi

Kisi ke dil ka suno haal dil laga kar tum
Jo howe dil ko tumhaare bhi meherbaan lagi

Tu woh hai maah-jabeen misl-e-deeda-e-anjum
Rahe hai teri taraf chashm-e-yak-jahaan lagi

Khuda kare kahe tujh se yeh kuch Khuda-lagti
Ke zulf ae but-e-badkeshaan tere kaan lagi

Udaayi hirs ne aa kar jahaan mein sab ki khaak
Nahi hai kis ko hawa zer-e-aasmaan lagi

Kisi ki kaawish-e-muzhgaan se aaj saari raat
Nahi palak se palak meri ek aan lagi

Tabaah bahr-e-jahaan mein thi apni kashti-e-umr
So toot-phoot ke baare kinaare aan lagi

Tumhaare haath se seene mein dil se ta-ba-jigar
Sinaan o khanjar o paikaan ki hai dukaan lagi

Khadang-e-yaar mere dil se kis tarah nikle
Ke is ke saath hai ae Zauq meri jaan lagi
