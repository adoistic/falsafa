Lete hi dil jo aashiq-e-dil-soz ka chale
Tum aag lene aaye the kya aaye kya chale

Tum chashm-e-surmagin ko jo apni dikha chale
Baithe bithaye khaak mein hum ko mila chale

Deewana aa ke aur bhi dil ko bana chale
Ek dam toh thehro aur bhi kya aaye kya chale

Hum lutf-e-sair-e-bagh-e-jahan khaak uda chale
Shauq-e-visaal dil mein liye yaar ka chale

Ghairon ke saath chhod ke tum naqsh-e-paa chale
Kya khoob phool gor pe meri chadha chale

Dikhla ke mujh ko nargis-e-beemar kya chale
Aawara misl-e-aahu-e-sahra bana chale

Ae gham mujhe tamaam shab-e-hijr mein na khaa
Rehne de kuch ke subah ka bhi nashta chale

Bal-be ghuroor-e-husn zameen par na rakhe paaon
Maanind-e-aaftaab woh be-naqsh-e-paa chale

Kya le chale gali se teri hum ko joon naseem
Aaye the sar pe khaak udaane uda chale

Afsos hai ke saaya-e-murgh-e-hawa ki tarah
Hum jis ke saath saath chalein woh juda chale

Kya dekhta hai haath mera chhod de tabeeb
Yaan jaan hi badan mein nahin nabz kya chale

Qaatil jo tere dil mein rukawat na ho toh kyun
Ruk ruk ke mere halq pe khanjar tera chale

Le jaayein tere kushte ko jannat mein bhi agar
Phir phir ke tere ghar ki taraf dekhta chale

Aalooda chashm mein na hui surme se nigaah
Dekha jahan se saaf hi ahl-e-safa chale

Roz-e-azal se zulf-e-muambar ka hai aseer
Kya ud ke tujh se taair-e-nikhat bhala chale

Saath apne le ke tosan-e-umr-e-rawaan ko aah
Hum is saraaye-e-dehr mein kya aaye kya chale

Suljhaayein zulfein kya lab-e-dariya pe aap ne
Har mauj misl-e-maar-e-siyah tum bana chale

Duniya mein jab se aaye raha ishq-e-gul-rukhaan
Hum is jahan mein misl-e-saba khaak uda chale

Qaatil se dakhl kya hai ke jaan-bar ho apna hosh
Gar ud ke misl-e-taair-e-rang-e-hina chale

Fikr-e-qana'at un ko mayassar hui kahan
Duniya se dil mein le ke jo hirs-o-hawa chale

Is roo-e-aatishin ke tasavvur mein yaad-e-zulf
Yaani ghazab hai aag lage aur hawa chale

Ae Zauq hai ghazab nigaah-e-yaar al-hafeez
Woh kya bache ke jis pe yeh teer-e-qaza chale
