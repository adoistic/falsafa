Jo kuch ke hai duniya mein woh insaan ke liye hai
Aarasta yeh ghar usi mehmaan ke liye hai

Zulfein teri kaafir unhein dil se mere kya kaam
Dil Kaaba hai aur Kaaba Musalmaan ke liye hai

Ho qaid-e-tafakkur se kab azaad sukhan-war
Manzoor qafas murgh-e-khush-ilhaan ke liye hai

Apnon se na mil apne hain sab apnon ke dushman
Har nai mein bhari aag neestaan ke liye hai

Kuch bakht se mere jo siwa hai woh siyahi
Baaqi hai toh meri shab-e-hijraan ke liye hai

Deewana hoon main bhi woh tamasha ke mera zikr
Goya sabaq atfaal-e-dabistaan ke liye hai

Hai baada-kashon ke liye ik ghaib se taa'eed
Zaahid jo dua maangta baaraan ke liye hai

Chungal mein hai muzi ke dil iss chashm ke haathon
Ghera yeh ghazab panja-e-mizhgaan ke liye hai

Main kis ki nigaahon ka hoon vehshi ke meri khaak
Ik kohl-e-basar chashm-e-ghazalaan ke liye hai

Dil bhi hai bala qaabil-e-mashq-e-sitam-o-naaz
Jo teer hai uss tooda-e-toofaan ke liye hai

Nikle koi kya qaid-e-alaa'iq se ke ae Zauq
Dar hi nahi is khaana-e-zindaan ke liye hai
