Laayi hayaat aaye qaza le chali chale
Apni khushi na aaye na apni khushi chale

Ho umr-e-Khizr bhi to ho maloom waqt-e-marg
Hum kya rahe yahan abhi aaye abhi chale

Hum se bhi is bisaat pe kam honge bad-qimaar
Jo chaal hum chale so nihaayat buri chale

Behtar to hai yehi ke na duniya se dil lage
Par kya karein jo kaam na be-dil-lagi chale

Laila ka naqa dasht mein taseer-e-ishq se
Sun kar fighaan-e-Qais bajaaye-hudi chale

Naazaan na ho khirad pe jo hona hai ho wahi
Daanish teri na kuch meri daanish-wari chale

Duniya ne kis ka raah-e-fana mein diya hai saath
Tum bhi chale chalo yunhi jab tak chali chale

Jaate hawaa-e-shauq mein hain is chaman se 'Zauq'
Apni bala se baad-e-saba ab kabhi chale
