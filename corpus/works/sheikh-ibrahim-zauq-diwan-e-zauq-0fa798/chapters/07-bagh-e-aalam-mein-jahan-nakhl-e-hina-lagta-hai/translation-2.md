Bagh-e-aalam mein jahan nakhl-e-hina lagta hai
Dil-e-pur-khun ka wahan haath pata lagta hai

Kya tadapna dil-e-bismil ka bhala lagta hai
Ke jab uchhlay hai tere seene se ja lagta hai

Dil kahan sair tamashe pe mera lagta hai
Jee ke lag jaane se jeena bhi bura lagta hai

Jo hawadis se zamaane ke gira phir na utha
Nakhl aandhi ka kahin ukhda hua lagta hai

Dil lagaane ka maza hai ke gazak mein us ke
Sab kababon se namak dil pe siwa lagta hai

Na shab-e-hijr mein lagti hai zubaan taalu se
Aur na pehlu mera bistar se zara lagta hai

Haaye mohtaj hua marham-e-zangaar ka tu
Zakhm-e-dil zeher mujhe hansna tera lagta hai

Aab-e-khanjar jo hai zehraab wafadari ko
Paani shayad ke sar-e-mulk-e-fana lagta hai

Qad-e-majnu koi pehle se chhadi baid ki hai
Jab zara jhukta hai qad paaon se ja lagta hai

Zard zahid hai toh kya khot abhi hai dil mein
Zauq uss zar ko kasauti par kasa lagta hai
