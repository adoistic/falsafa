Sab ko duniya ki hawas khwaar liye phirti hai
Kaun phirta hai yeh murdaar liye phirti hai

Ghar se baahar na nikalta kabhi apne khursheed
Hawas-e-garmi-e-bazaar liye phirti hai

Woh mere akhtar-e-taala' ki hai waazhon' gardish
Ke falak ko bhi nigun-saar liye phirti hai

Kar diya kya tere abru ne ishaara qaatil
Ke qaza haath mein talwaar liye phirti hai

Jaa ke ek baar na phirna tha jahaan waan' mujh ko
Be-qaraari hai ke sau baar liye phirti hai
