Na karta zabt main naala to phir aisa dhuaan hota
Ke neeche aasmaan ke aur paida aasmaan hota
Kahe hai murgh-e-dil ae kaash main zaagh-e-kamaan hota
Ke ta shaakh-e-kamaan par uss ki mera aashiyan hota
Abhi kya sard qaatil yeh shaheed-e-tafta-jaan hota
Koi dam shama-e-murda mein bhi hai baaqi dhuaan hota
Na hoti dil mein kaavish gar kisi ki nok-e-mizhgaan ki
To kyun haq mein mere har moo-e-tan misl-e-sinaan hota
Aza-daari mein hai kis ki yeh charkh-e-maatami-jaama
Ke habeeb-e-chaak ki soorat hai khat-e-kehkashaan hota
Bagola gar na hota vaadi-e-vahshat mein ae Majnoon
To gumbad hum se sargashton ki turbat par kahan hota
Jo rota khol kar dil tang-naaye-dehr mein aashiq
To joo-e-kehkashaan mein bhi falak par khoon rawaan hota
Na rakhta gar na rakhta munh pe yeh daana mareez-e-gham
Magar tera mayassar bosa-e-khaal-e-dahaan hota
Tere khooneen jigar ki khaak par hota agar sabza
To mizhgaan ki tarah uss se bhi paiham khoon rawaan hota
Rukaavat dil ki uss kaafir ke waqt-e-zibah zaahir hai
Ke khanjar meri gardan par hai ruk ruk kar rawaan hota
Na karta zabt main girya to ae Zauq ik ghadi bhar mein
Katore ki tarah ghadiyaal ke gharq aasmaan hota
