Haath seena pe mere rakh ke kidhar dekhte ho
Ik nazar dil se idhar dekh lo gar dekhte ho
Hai dam-e-baaz-paseen dekh lo gar dekhte ho
Aaina munh pe mere rakh ke kidhar dekhte ho
Naatavaani ka meri mujh se na poochho ahwaal
Ho mujhe dekhte ya apni kamar dekhte ho
Par-e-parwana pade hain shajar-e-shama ke gird
Barg-rezi-e-mohabbat ka samar dekhte ho
Baid-e-majnoon ko ho jab dekhte ae ahl-e-nazar
Kisi majnoon ko bhi aashufta-basar dekhte ho
Shauq-e-deedar meri naash pe aa kar bola
Kis ki ho dekhte raah aur kidhar dekhte ho
Lazzat-e-naavak-e-gham Zauq se ho poochhte kya
Lab pade chaat'te hain zakhm-e-jigar dekhte ho
