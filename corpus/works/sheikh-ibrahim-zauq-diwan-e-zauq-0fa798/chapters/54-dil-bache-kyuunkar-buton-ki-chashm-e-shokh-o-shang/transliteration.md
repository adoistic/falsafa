dil bache kyuunkar buton ki chashm-e-shokh-o-shang se
apna ghar to soojhta hai saikdon farsang se

ek bhi nikle na meri si faghan-e-dil-kharaash
garchah khoon tapke galu-e-murgh-e-khush-aahang se

chhup ke baithega kahan tu hum se ae rangeen-nawa
hoga tu jis rang mein mil jaayenge us rang se

bal be-baariki ke goya har tera taar-e-sukhan
jantari mein khinch ke nikla hai dahan-e-tang se

ae taghaaful-kesh jaldi aa ke tu waaqif nahin
is dil-e-betaab o jaan-e-muztarib ke dhang se

josh-e-girya se rahi barsaat barson par kabhi
us ki tegh-e-tez aalooda na dekhi zang se

mere girye ke asar se ho gaye patthar bhi aab
jharte hain jaa-e-sharar paani ke qatre sang se

pehle yeh niyyat wuzu ki hai namaaz-e-ishq mein
dil se keh deejiye ke dhowe haath naam o nang se

Zauq zeba hai jo ho reesh-e-safeid-e-shaikh par
wasma aab-e-bang se mehndi mai-e-gul-rang se
