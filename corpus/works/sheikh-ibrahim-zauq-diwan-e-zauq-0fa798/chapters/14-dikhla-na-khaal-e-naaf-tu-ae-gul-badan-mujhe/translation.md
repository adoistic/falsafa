Dikhla na khaal-e-naaf tu ae gul-badan mujhe
Har laala yaan hai naafa-e-mushk-e-khutan mujhe

Humdum wabaal-e-dosh na kar pairahan mujhe
Kaanta sa hai khatakta mera tan badan mujhe

Phirta liye chaman mein hai deewana-pan mujhe
Zanjeer-e-paa hai mauj-e-naseem-e-chaman mujhe

Tasbeeh-e-daur-e-bazm mein dekho imam ko
Bakhshi hai haq ne zeb-e-sar-e-anjuman mujhe

Ae mere yasmin tere dandaan-e-aabdaar
Gulshan mein hain rulaate gul-e-yasmin mujhe

Mehraab-e-Kaaba jab se hai tera kham-e-kamaan
Said-e-haram samajhte hain naawak-fagan mujhe

Hai tan mein resha-haaye-naye khushk istikhwaan
Kyun kheenchta hai kaanton mein ae zo'f-e-tan mujhe

Ae lab missi ko phenk ke neelam hai kam-baha
Yaaqoot de ya de koi la'al-e-Yaman mujhe

Hoon shama ya ke shola khabar kuch nahin magar
Faanoos ho raha hai mera pairahan mujhe

Ik sarzameen-e-laala bahaar o khizaan mein hoon
Yaksaan hai daagh-e-taaza o daagh-e-kuhan mujhe

Khusrau se tesha bola jo chaatoon na tera khoon
Sheerin na hove khoon-e-sar-e-koh-kan mujhe

Rukh par tumhare daam jo daala hai sabze ne
Aata nazar hai deeda-e-anqa-dahan mujhe

Yeh dil woh hai ke kar de zameen aasmaan ko khaak
Ik dam ko barq de jo pinha pairahan mujhe

Kooche mein tere kaun tha leta bhala khabar
Shab chaandni ne aa ke pehnaya kafan mujhe

Dikhlaata aasmaan se hai roo-e-zameen ki sair
Ae rashk-e-maah teri jabeen ka shikan mujhe

Rakhta hai chashm-e-lutf pe kis kis adaa ke saath
Deta hai jaam saaqi-e-paimaan-shikan mujhe

Hai jazb-e-dil durust to chaah-e-firaaq se
Kheenche-gi teri zulf shikan-dar-shikan mujhe

Dikhlaata ik adaa mein hai sau sau tarah banao
Iss saada-pan ke saath tera baankpan mujhe

Jaise kuen mein ho koi taara chamak raha
Dil soojhta hai yoon teh-e-chaah-e-zaqan mujhe

Aa kar use bhi do kabhi aankhein zara dikha
Aankhein dikha raha hai ghazaal-e-Khutan mujhe

Aa ae mere chaman ke hawa mein teri hawa
Sehra-e-dil hua hai chaman-dar-chaman mujhe

Ya Rab yeh dil hai ya ke hai aaina-e-nazar
Dikhla raha hai sair-o-safar dar watan mujhe

Aaya hoon noor le ke main bazm-e-sukhan mein Zauq
Aankhon pe sab bithaaenge ahl-e-sukhan mujhe
