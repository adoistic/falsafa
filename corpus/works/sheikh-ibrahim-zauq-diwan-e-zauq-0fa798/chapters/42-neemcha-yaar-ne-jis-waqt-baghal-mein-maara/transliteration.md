Neemcha yaar ne jis waqt baghal mein maara
Jo chadha munh use maidaan-e-ajal mein maara

Maal jab us ne bohot radd-o-badal mein maara
Hum ne dil apna utha apni baghal mein maara

Is lab-o-chashm se hai zindagi-o-maut apni
Ke kabhi pal mein jalaya kabhi pal mein maara

Khench kar ishq-e-sitam-pesha ne shamsheer-e-jafa
Pehle ik haath mujhi par tha azal mein maara

Charkh-e-bad-been ki kabhi aankh na phooti sau baar
Teer naale ne mere chashm-e-zuhal mein maara

Ajal aayi na shab-e-hijr mein aur hum ko falak
Be-ajal tu ne tamanna-e-ajal mein maara

Ishq ke haath se ne Qais na Farhad bacha
Us ko gar dasht mein toh us ko jabal mein maara

Dil ko us kaakul-e-pechaan se na bal karna tha
Yeh siyah-bakht gaya apne hi bal mein maara

Kaun faryaad sune zulf mein dil ki tu ne
Hai musalmaan ko kaafir ke amal mein maara

Urs ki shab bhi meri gor pe do phool na laaye
Patthar ik gumbad-e-turbat ke kanwal mein maara

Aankh se aankh hai ladti mujhe dar hai dil ka
Kahin yeh jaaye na is jang-o-jadal mein maara

Hum ne jaana tha jabhi ishq ne maara us ko
Tesha Farhad ne jis waqt jabal mein maara

Na hua par na hua Meer ka andaaz naseeb
Zauq yaaron ne bohot zor ghazal mein maara
