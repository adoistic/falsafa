baadam do jo bheje hain batwe mein daal kar
imaan yeh hai ke bhej de aankhein nikaal kar

dil seene mein kahan hai na tu dekh-bhaal kar
ae aah keh de teer ka naama nikaal kar

utrega ek jaam bhi poora na chaak se
khaak-e-dil-shikasta na sarf-e-kulaal kar

le kar buton ne jaan jab imaan pe daala haath
dil kya kinaare ho gaya sab ko sambhaal kar

tasveer unki hazrat-e-dil kheench leejiye gar
rakh denge hum bhi paaon pe aankhein nikaal kar

qaatil hai kis maze se namak-paash-e-zakhm-e-dil
bismil zara tadap ke namak to halaal kar

dil ko rafeeq-e-ishq mein apna samajh na Zauq
tal jaayega yeh apni bala tujh pe taal kar
