Aate hi tu ne ghar ke phir jaane ki sunai
Reh jaaun sun na kyonkar yeh toh buri sunai

Majnun o koh-kan ke sunte the yaar qisse
Jab tak kahani hum ne apni na thi sunai

Shikwa kiya jo hum ne gaali ka aaj us se
Shikwe ke saath us ne ik aur bhi sunai

Kuch keh raha hai naaseh kya jaane kya kahega
Deta nahi mujhe toh ae be-khudi sunai

Kehne na paaye us se saari haqeeqat ik din
Aadhi kabhi sunai aadhi kabhi sunai

Soorat dikhaye apni dekhein woh kis tarah se
Awaaz bhi na hum ko jis ne kabhi sunai

Qeemat mein jins-e-dil ki maanga jo Zauq bosa
Kya kya na us ne hum ko khoti-khari sunai
