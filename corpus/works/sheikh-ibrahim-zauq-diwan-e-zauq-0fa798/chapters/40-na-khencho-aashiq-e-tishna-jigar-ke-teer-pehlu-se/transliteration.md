Na khencho aashiq-e-tishna-jigar ke teer pehlu se
Nikaale par hai misl-e-maahi-e-tasveer pehlu se

Na le ae naavak-afgan dil ko mere cheer pehlu se
Ke woh toh ja chuka saath aah ke joon teer pehlu se

Dil-e-seepara ko le taank taaveezon mein haikal ke
Na sarka yeh hamaail ae but-e-be-peer pehlu se

Woh hoon be-dast-o-pa bismil rasaai jab na haath aayi
Kiya ta paaye-qaatil az tah-e-shamsheer pehlu se

Aseer-e-zulf deewane hain dekh ae paasbaan shab ko
Daba kar baith unke paaon ki zanjeer pehlu se

Musavvir Laila o Majnun ki naakami pe hairaan hain
Kabhi baitha na mil kar pehlu-e-tasveer pehlu se

Yeh dil lab-e-tishna tegh-e-yaar ka hai raat bhar karta
Sada-e-al-atash joon naala-e-shab-geer pehlu se

Ajab hasrat ka aalam tha ke Majnun kehta tha paiham
Chhutte pehlu mere mehmil ka ya taqdeer pehlu se

Na kehna istikhwan unko yeh aalam laaghri ka hai
Ke hai dikhla raha mera dil-e-dilgeer pehlu se

Khayal-e-abru-e-jaanan nahin ab bhoolta ik dam
Sipahi hai juda karta nahin shamsheer pehlu se

Tamaam ahl-e-sukhan bazm-e-sukhan mein Zauq hairaan hain
Mila jo qaafiya tu ne kiya tahreer pehlu se
