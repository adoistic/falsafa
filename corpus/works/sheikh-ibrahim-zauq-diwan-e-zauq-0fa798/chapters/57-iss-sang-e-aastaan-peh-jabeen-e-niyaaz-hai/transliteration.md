iss sang-e-aastaan peh jabeen-e-niyaaz hai
woh apni jaanamaz hai aur yeh namaz hai

naasaaz hai jo hum se usi se yeh saaz hai
kya khoob dil hai waah hamein jis peh naaz hai

pahuncha hai shab kamand laga kar wahan raqeeb
sach hai haraam-zaade ki rassi daraaz hai

iss but peh gar Khuda bhi ho aashiq toh aaye rashk
harchand jaanta hoon keh woh paakbaaz hai

maddah-e-khaal-e-roo-e-butaan hoon mujhe Khuda
bakhshe toh kya ajab keh woh nukta-nawaaz hai

darta hoon khanjar uss ka na beh jaaye ho ke aab
mere gale mein naala-e-aahan-gudaaz hai

darwaza mai-kade ka na kar band muhtasib
zaalim Khuda se darr keh dar-e-tauba baaz hai

khaana-kharaabiyaan dil-e-beemaar-e-gham ki dekh
woh hi dawa kharaab hai jo khaana-saaz hai

shabnam ki jaaye-gul se tapakti hain shokhiyaan
gulshan mein kis ki khaak-e-shaheedaan-e-naaz hai

aah o fughaan na kar jo khule 'Zauq' dil ka haal
har naala ik kaleed-e-dar-e-ganj-e-raaz hai
