Kal gaye the tum jise bimaar-e-hijraan chhod kar
Chal basa woh aaj sab hasti ka saamaan chhod kar
Tifl-e-ashk aisa gira daaman-e-mizhgaan chhod kar
Phir na utha koocha-e-chaak-e-garebaan chhod kar
Kyunke nikle tera us ka dil mein paikaan chhod kar
Jaaye baize ko kahaan yeh murgh-e-parraan chhod kar
Jis ne ho lazzat uthaayi zakhm-e-tegh-e-ishq ki
Kab woh marham-daan ko dhoonde namak-daan chhod kar
Said-e-dil ko kyunke chhode jab ke dikhla de na tu
Machhliyaan dast-e-hinaai mein meri jaan chhod kar
Sard-mehri se kisi ki aage hi jee sard hai
Yaan se hat ja dhoop ae abr-e-khiraamaan chhod kar
Dekhiye kya ho ke hai ab jaan ke peechhe padi
Dil ko ae kaafir teri zulf-e-pareshaan chhod kar
Ae dil us ke teer ke hum-raah seene se nikal
Warna pachhtaaye-ga tu yeh saath naadaan chhod kar
Kyun na ram kar jaayein aahoo aise vehshi se tere
Sher bhaagein jis ke naalon se neestaan chhod kar
Surkhi-e-paan dekh le zaahid jo dandaan par tere
Uth khada ho haath se tasbeeh-e-marjaan chhod kar
Pesh-khema le ke nikla gard-e-baad-e-daur-rau
Hai jo sargarm-e-safar tan ko meri jaan chhod kar
Gar khuda deve qana'at maah-e-do-hafta ki tarah
Daude saari ko kabhi aadhi na insaan chhod kar
Saaghar-e-dil bechta aaya hoon kho mat haath se
Chookta hai kyun yeh jins-e-dast-e-gardaan chhod kar
Kaam yeh tera hi tha rehmat ho ae abr-e-karam
Warna jaaye daagh-e-isyaan mera daamaan chhod kar
Padh ghazal ae Zauq koi garm si ab tu na ja
Jaanib-e-mazmoon tarz-e-tafta-jaanaan chhod kar
