Maar kar teer jo woh dilbar-e-jaani maange
Keh do hum se na koi de ke nishaani maange

Ae sanam dekh ke har dam ki teri kam-sukhni
Maut ghabra ke na kyun yeh khafaqaani maange

Khaak se tishna-e-deedar ke sabza jo uthe
Toh zabaan apni nikaale hue paani maange

Maar-e-pechaan toh bala hai ga magar tu ae zulf
Hai woh kaafir ke na kaata tera paani maange

Dahan-e-yaar ho aur maange kisi se dil ko
Woh jo maange toh ba-andaaz-e-nihaani maange

Dil mera bosa-ba-paighaam nahin hai humdum
Yaar leta hai toh le apni zabaani maange

Jalwa us aalam-e-ma'ani ka jo dekhe ae Zauq
Lutf-e-alfaaz na be-husn-e-ma'ani maange
