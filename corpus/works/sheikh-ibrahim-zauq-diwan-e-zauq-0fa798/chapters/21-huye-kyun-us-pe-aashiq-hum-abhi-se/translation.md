Huye kyun us pe aashiq hum abhi se
Lagaya jee ko nahaq gham abhi se

Dila rabt us se rakhna kam abhi se
Jata dete hain tujh ko hum abhi se

Tere beemar-e-gham ke hain jo gham-khwaar
Barasta un pe hai maatam abhi se

Ghazab aaya hilein gar us ki mizgaan
Saf-e-ushaaq hai barham abhi se

Agarchay der hai jaane mein tere
Nahin par apne dum mein dum abhi se

Bhigo rahwe-ga girya jaib-o-daaman
Rahe hai aasteen pur-nam abhi se

Tumhara mujh ko paas-e-aabru tha
Wagarna ashk jaate tham abhi se

Lage seesa pilaane mujh ko aansu
Ke ho buniyaad-e-gham mohkam abhi se

Kaha jaane ko kis ne meenh khule par
Ke chhaaya dil pe abr-e-gham abhi se

Nikalte hi dum uthwaate hain mujh ko
Huye be-zaar yun humdum abhi se

Abhi dil par jaraahat sau na do sau
Dhara hai dosto marham abhi se

Kiya hai waada-e-deedaar kis ne
Ke hai mushtaaq ik aalam abhi se

Mera jaana mujhe ghairon ne aye Zauq
Ke phirte hain khush-o-khurram abhi se
