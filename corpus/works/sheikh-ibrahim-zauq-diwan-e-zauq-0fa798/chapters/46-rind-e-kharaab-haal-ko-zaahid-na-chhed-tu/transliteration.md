Rind-e-kharaab-haal ko zaahid na chhed tu
Tujh ko paraai kya padi apni nabed tu

Naakhun khuda na de tujhe aye panja-e-junoon
De-ga tamaam aql ke bakhiye udhed tu

Is said-e-muztarib ko tahammul se zibah kar
Daamaan o aasteen na lahu mein lathed tu

Chhut'ta hai kaun mar ke giraftaar-e-daam-e-zulf
Turbat pe uss ki jaal ka paaye-ga ped tu

Aye zaahid-e-do-rang na peer aap ko bana
Maanind-e-subh-e-kaazib abhi hai udhed tu

Yeh tangnaaye-deher nahin manzil-e-faraagh
Ghaafil na paaon hirs ke phaila suked tu

Ho qat'a nakhl-e-ulfat agar phir bhi sabz ho
Kya ho jo phenke jad hi se uss ko ukhed tu

Jo soti bhed baais-e-ghaugha jagaaye phir
Darwaaza ghar ka uss sag-e-duniya pe bhed tu

Umr-e-rawaan ka tausan-e-chaalaak is liye
Tujh ko diya ke jald kare yaan se ed tu

Aawargi se ku-e-mohabbat ki haath utha
Aye Zauq yeh utha na sake-ga khakhed tu
