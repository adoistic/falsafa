Juda hoon yaar se hum aur na ho raqeeb juda
Hai apna apna muqaddar juda naseeb juda

Teri gali se nikalte hi apna dum nikla
Rahe hai kyun ke gulistan se andaleeb juda

Dikha de jalwa jo masjid mein woh but-e-kaafir
Toh cheekh uthe muazzin juda khateeb juda

Juda na dard-e-judai ho gar mere aaza
Huroof-e-dard ki soorat hoon ae tabeeb juda

Hai aur ilm-o-adab maktab-e-mohabbat mein
Ke hai wahan ka muallim juda adeeb juda

Hujoom-e-ashk ke hum-raah kyun na ho naala
Ke fauj se nahin hota kabhi naqeeb juda

Firaaq-e-khuld se gandum hai seena-chaak ab tak
Ilaahi ho na watan se koi ghareeb juda

Kiya habeeb ko mujh se juda falak ne magar
Na kar saka mere dil se gham-e-habeeb juda

Karein judai ka kis kis ki ranj hum ae Zauq
Ke hone waale hain hum sab se an-qareeb juda
