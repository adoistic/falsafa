Barq mera aashiyan kab ka jala kar le gayi
Kuch jo khakistar bacha aandhi uda kar le gayi

Us ke qadmon tak na betaabi badha kar le gayi
Haaye do palte diye aur phir hata kar le gayi

Na-tawaani hum ko haathon haath utha kar le gayi
Chyunti se chyunti daana chhuda kar le gayi

Subah-rukh se kaun shaam-e-zulf mein jaata tha aah
Ae dil-e-shamat-zada shamat laga kar le gayi

Khoon se Farhad ke rangeen hua daman-e-koh
Kyun na mauj-e-sheer yeh dhabba chhuda kar le gayi

Tum ne to chhora hi tha ae hum-rahaan-e-qafila
Lekin aawaaz-e-jaras hum ko jaga kar le gayi

Nok-e-mizhgaan jab hui seena-figaaron se do-chaar
Paara-haaye-dil se guldasta bana kar le gayi

Dekhi kuch dil ki kashish Laila ke naaqe ko tere
Soo-e-Majnun aakhirash rasta bhula kar le gayi

Waah ae soz-e-daroon kooche mein us ke barq-e-aah
Raat hum ko har qadam mash'al dikha kar le gayi

Woh gaye ghar ghair ke aur yaan hamein dam bhar ke baad
Badgumaani un ke ghar sau ghar phira kar le gayi

Jo shaheed-e-naaz kooche mein tumhare tha pada
Kya kahoon taqdeer use kyun kar utha kar le gayi

Dasht-e-wehshat mein bagola tha ke deewana tera
Rooh-e-Majnun behr-e-istaqbaal aa kar le gayi

Aag mein hai kaun gir padta magar parwaane ko
Aatish-e-soz-e-mohabbat thi jala kar le gayi

Ae pari pehlu se mere kya kahoon teri nigaah
Dil uda kar le gayi ya par laga kar le gayi

Zauq mar jaane ka to apne koi mauqa na tha
Koo-e-jaanan mein ajal na-haq laga kar le gayi
