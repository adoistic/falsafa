Tere aafat-zada jin dashton mein ud jaate hain
Sabr o taaqat ke wahan paaon ukhad jaate hain

Itne bigde hain woh mujhse ke agar naam unke
Khat bhi likhta hoon toh sab harf bigad jaate hain

Kyun na ladwayein unhein ghair ke karte hain yehi
Hum-nasheen jinke naseebe kahin lad jaate hain
