Mehfil mein shor-e-qulqul-e-meena-e-mul hua
La saaqiya piyaala ke tauba ka qul hua

Jin ki nazar charha tera rukhsar-e-aatishin
Un ka chiragh-e-gor na ta-hashr gul hua

Banda-nawaziyan toh yeh dekho ke aadmi
Juzv-e-zaeef mehram-e-asrar-e-kul hua

Darya-e-gham se mere guzarne ke waaste
Tegh-e-khameeda yaar ki lohe ka pul hua

Parwana bhi tha garm-e-tapish par khula na raaz
Bulbul ki tang-hauslagi thi ke ghul hua

Aayi tihi-daron ki na hargiz samajh mein baat
Aawaaza go buland misaal-e-dohol hua

Us bin raha chaman mein bhi main Zauq dil-kharaash
Naakhun se tez-tar mujhe har barg-e-gul hua
