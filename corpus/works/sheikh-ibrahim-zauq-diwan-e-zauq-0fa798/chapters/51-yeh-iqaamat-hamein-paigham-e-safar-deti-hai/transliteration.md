Yeh iqaamat hamein paigham-e-safar deti hai
Zindagi maut ke aane ki khabar deti hai

Zaal-e-duniya hai ajab tarah ki allama-e-dehr
Mard-e-deen-daar ko bhi dehriya kar deti hai

Badhti jaati hai jo mashq-e-sitam us zaalim ki
Kuch mohabbat meri islaah magar deti hai

Faayda de tere beemaar ko kya khaak dawa
Ab toh akseer bhi deeje toh zarar deti hai

Shama ghabra na shab-e-gham se ke koi dam mein
Tujh ko kafoor-e-safedi-e-seher deti hai

Ghuncha hansta hai tere aage jo gustaakhi se
Chatakhna munh pe wahin baad-e-seher deti hai

Shama bhi kam nahin kuch ishq mein parwaane se
Jaan deta hai agar woh toh yeh sar deti hai

Dam-ba-dam zakhm pe ik zakhm hai dam lene ki
Mujh ko fursat nahin woh tegh-e-nazar deti hai

Kehte sunte nahin kuch hum toh shab-e-hijr mein par
Naala-e-dil ka jawaab aah-e-jigar deti hai

Teera-bakhti meri karti hai pareshaan mujh ko
Tohmat us zulf-e-siyaah-faam pe dhar deti hai

Nakhl-e-mizhgaan se hai kya jaaniye kya chashm-e-samar
Chashm paani ki jagah khoon-e-jigar deti hai

Deti sharbat hai kisey zeher bhari aankh teri
Ain ehsaan hai woh zeher bhi gar deti hai

Koi ghammaz nahin meri taraf se ae Zauq
Kaan us ke meri faryaad hi bhar deti hai
