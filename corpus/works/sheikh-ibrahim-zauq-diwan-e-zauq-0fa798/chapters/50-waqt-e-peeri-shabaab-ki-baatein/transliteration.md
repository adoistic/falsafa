Waqt-e-peeri shabaab ki baatein
Aisi hain jaise khwaab ki baatein

Phir mujhe le chala udhar dekho
Dil-e-khaana-kharaab ki baatein

Waaiza chhod zikr-e-ne'mat-e-khuld
Keh sharaab-o-kabaab ki baatein

Meh-jabeen yaad hain ke bhool gaye
Woh shab-e-maahtaab ki baatein

Harf aaya jo aabru pe meri
Hain yeh chashm-e-pur-aab ki baatein

Sunte hain us ko chhed chhed ke hum
Kis maze se itaab ki baatein

Jaam-e-mai munh se tu laga apne
Chhod sharm-o-hijaab ki baatein

Mujh ko ruswa karengi khoob ae dil
Yeh teri iztiraab ki baatein

Jaao hota hai aur bhi khafqaan
Sun ke naaseh janaab ki baatein

Qissa-e-zulf-e-yaar dil ke liye
Hain ajab pech-o-taab ki baatein

Zikr kya josh-e-ishq mein ae Zauq
Hum se hon sabr-o-taab ki baatein
