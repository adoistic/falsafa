The downy line, the curls, the raven tresses all advanced,
In Beauty’s court, the darkest of her foes advanced.

My heart still shrinks from our post-quarrel, cold embrace;
It’s best that I step forth, and your forgiveness shows, advanced.

At first, my hair and nails were all that seemed to grow;
But soon a feral madness in my spirit grows, advanced.

My fiery love, those rivals who provoke you so,
They only wish to see our bitter woes advanced.

What use, O healer, if one sorrow you make slow,
When in its place a hundred others grow, advanced?

O Zauq, to greet the grief my love on me bestows,
Before the wail can rise, the tear already flows, advanced.
