Khat barha kaakul barhe zulfen barhein gesu barhe
Husn ki sarkaar mein jitne barhe Hindu barhe

Baad ranjish ke gale milte hue rukta hai ji
Ab munaasib hai yahi kuch mein barhun kuch tu barhe

Barhte barhte barh gayi vahshat vagarna pehle toh
Haath ke naakhun barhe sar ke hamare moo barhe

Tujh ko dushman waan shararat se jo bhadkaate hain roz
Chaahte hain aur shar ae shokh-e-aatish-kho barhe

Kuch tap-e-gham ko ghata kya faayda is se tabeeb
Roz nuskhe mein agar khurfa ghate kaahu barhe

Peshwaai ko gham-e-jaanan ki chashm-e-dil se Zauq
Jab barhe naala toh us se peshtar aansu barhe
