gayin yaaron se woh agli mulaqaton ki sab rasmein
pada jis din se dil bas mein tere aur dil ke hum bas mein

kabhi milna kabhi rehna alag maanind-e-mizhgaan ke
tamasha kaj-sarishton ka hai kuch ikhlaas ke bas mein

tawaqqo kya ho jeene ki tere bemaar-e-hijraan ke
na jumbish nabz mein jis ki na garmi jis ke malmas mein

dikhaaye cheera-dasti aah baala-dast gar apni
toh maare haath daaman-e-qayamat charkh-e-atlas mein

jo hai gosha-nasheen tere khayal-e-mast-e-abru mein
woh hai bait-us-sanam mein bhi toh hai bait-ul-muqaddas mein

kare lab-aashna harf-e-shikayat se kahan yeh dam
tere mahzoon-e-be-dam mein tere maftoon-e-bekas mein

hawa-e-koo-e-jaanan le ude isko ta'ajjub kya
tan-e-laaghar mein hai jaan is tarah jis tarah bu khas mein

mujhe ho kis tarah qaul-o-qasam ka aitbaar un ke
hazaaron de chuke woh qaul laakhon kha chuke qasmein

huye sab jama mazmoon Zauq deewan-e-do-aalam ke
hawaas-e-khamsa hain insaan ke woh band-e-mukhammas mein
