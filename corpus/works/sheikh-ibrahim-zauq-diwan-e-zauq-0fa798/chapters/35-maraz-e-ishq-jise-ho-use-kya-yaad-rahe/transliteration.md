Maraz-e-ishq jise ho use kya yaad rahe
Na dawa yaad rahe aur na dua yaad rahe

Tum jise yaad karo phir use kya yaad rahe
Na khudaai ki ho parwa na khuda yaad rahe

Laut'te saikdon nakhcheer hain kya yaad rahe
Cheer do seene mein dil ko ke pata yaad rahe

Raat ka waada hai bande se agar banda-nawaaz
Band mein de lo girah ta ke zara yaad rahe

Qasid-e-aashiq-e-sauda-zadah kya laaye jawaab
Jab na maaloom ho ghar aur na pata yaad rahe

Dekh bhi lena hamein raah mein aur kyun saahab
Hum se munh pher ke jaana yeh bhala yaad rahe

Tere madhosh se kya hosh-o-khirad ki ho umeed
Raat ka bhi na jise khaaya hua yaad rahe

Kushta-e-naaz ki gardan pe chhuri phero jab
Kaash us waqt tumhein naam-e-khuda yaad rahe

Khaak barbaad na karna meri is kooche mein
Tujh se keh deta hoon main baad-e-saba yaad rahe

Gor tak aaye toh chhaati pe qadam bhi rakh do
Koi bedil idhar aaye toh pata yaad rahe

Tera aashiq na ho aasooda ba-zer-e-tooba
Khuld mein bhi tere kooche ki hawa yaad rahe

Baaz aa jaaein jafa se jo kabhi aap toh phir
Yaad aashiq ko na keejiye_ga bhala yaad rahe

Daagh-e-dil par mere phaaha nahin hai angaara
Chaara-gar leejo na chutki se utha yaad rahe

Zakhm-e-dil bole mere dil ke namak-khwaaron se
Lo bhala kuch toh mohabbat ka maza yaad rahe

Hazrat-e-ishq ke maktab mein hai taaleem kuch aur
Yaan likha yaad rahe aur na padha yaad rahe

Gar haqeeqat mein hai rehna toh na rakh khud-beeni
Bhoole banda jo khudi ko toh khuda yaad rahe

Aalam-e-husn khudaai hai buton ki ae Zauq
Chal ke but-khaane mein baitho ke khuda yaad rahe
