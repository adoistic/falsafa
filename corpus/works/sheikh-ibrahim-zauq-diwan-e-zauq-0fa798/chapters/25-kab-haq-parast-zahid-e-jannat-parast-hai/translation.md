Kab haq-parast zahid-e-jannat-parast hai
Hooron pe mar raha hai yeh shahwat-parast hai

Dil saaf ho toh chahiye maani-parast ho
Aaina khaak saaf hai soorat-parast hai

Darvesh hai wohi jo riyazat mein chust ho
Taarik nahin faqir bhi rahat-parast hai

Juz zulf soojhta nahin ae murgh-e-dil tujhe
Khaffash tu nahin hai ke zulmat-parast hai

Daulat ki rakh na maar-e-sar-e-ganj se ummeed
Moozi woh de-ga kya ke jo daulat-parast hai

Anqa ne gum kiya hai nishaan naam ke liye
Gum-gashta kaun kehta hai shohrat-parast hai

Yeh Zauq mai-parast hai ya hai sanam-parast
Kuchh hai bala se lek mohabbat-parast hai
