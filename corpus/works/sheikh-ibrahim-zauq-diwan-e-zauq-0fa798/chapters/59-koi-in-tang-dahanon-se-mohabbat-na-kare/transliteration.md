koi in tang-dahanon se mohabbat na kare
aur jo yeh tang karein munh se shikayat na kare

ishq ke daagh ko dil mohr-e-nabuwat samjha
dar hai kafir kahin daawa-e-nabuwat na kare

hai jarahat ka meri sauda-e-almas ilaaj
faayda us ko kabhi sang-e-jarahat na kare

har qadam par mere ashkon se ravan hai dariya
kya kare jaada agar tark-e-rafaqat na kare

aaj tak khoon se mere tar hai zabaan-e-khanjar
kya kare jab ke talab koi shahadat na kare

hai yeh insaan bade ustaad ka shagird-e-rashid
kar sake kaun agar yeh bhi khilafat na kare

bin jale shama ke parwana nahin jal sakta
kya badhe ishq agar husn hi sabqat na kare

phir chala maqtal-e-ushaq ki janib qaatil
sar pe barpa kahin kushton ke qayamat na kare
