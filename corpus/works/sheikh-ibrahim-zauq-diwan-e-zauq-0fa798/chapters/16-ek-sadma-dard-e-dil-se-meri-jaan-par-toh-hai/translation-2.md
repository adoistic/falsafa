Ek sadma dard-e-dil se meri jaan par toh hai
Lekin bala se yaar ke zaanu pe sar toh hai

Aana hai unka aana qayamat ka dekhiye
Kab aayein lekin aane ki unke khabar toh hai

Hai sar shaheed-e-ishq ka zeb-e-sinan-e-yaar
Sad shukr baare nakhl-e-wafa barwar toh hai

Maanind-e-shama girya hai kya shughl-e-turfa-tar
Ho jaati raat iss mein bala se basar toh hai

Hai dard dil mein gar nahin humdard mere paas
Dil-soz koi gar nahin soz-e-jigar toh hai

Ae dil hujoom-e-dard-o-alam se na tang ho
Khaana-kharaab khush ho ke aabaad ghar toh hai

Turbat pe dil-jalon ki nahin gar charaagh o gul
Seene mein sozish-e-dil o daagh-e-jigar toh hai

Ghaayab mein jo kaha so kaha phir bhi hai yeh shukr
Khaamosh ho gaya woh mujhe dekh kar toh hai

Kashti-e-behr-e-gham hai mere haq mein tegh-e-yaar
Kar deti ek dam mein idhar se udhar toh hai

Woh dil ke jiss mein soz-e-mohabbat na hove 'Zauq'
Behtar hai sang uss se ke uss mein sharar toh hai
