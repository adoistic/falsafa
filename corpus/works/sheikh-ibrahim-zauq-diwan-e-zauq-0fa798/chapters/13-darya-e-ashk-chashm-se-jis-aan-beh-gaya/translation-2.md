Darya-e-ashk chashm se jis aan beh gaya
Sun lijiyo ke arsh ka aivaan beh gaya

Bal-e-be-gudaaz-e-ishq ke khoon ho ke dil ke saath
Seene se tere teer ka paikaan beh gaya

Zaahid sharaab peene se kaafir hua main kyun
Kya dedh chullu paani mein imaan beh gaya

Hai mauj-e-bahr-e-ishq woh toofaan ke al-hafeez
Bechaara musht-e-khaak tha insaan beh gaya

Darya-e-ashk se dam-e-tehreer haal-e-dil
Kashti ki tarah mera qalam-daan beh gaya

Yeh roye phoot phoot ke paani ke aablay
Naala sa ek soo-e-bayaabaan beh gaya

Tha tu baha mein besh par us lab ke saamne
Sab mol tera laal-e-badakhshaan beh gaya

Kashti sawaar-e-umr hoon bahr-e-fana mein Zauq
Jis dam baha ke le gaya toofaan beh gaya

Tha Zauq pehle Dehli mein Punjab ka sa husn
Par ab woh paani kehte hain Multan beh gaya
