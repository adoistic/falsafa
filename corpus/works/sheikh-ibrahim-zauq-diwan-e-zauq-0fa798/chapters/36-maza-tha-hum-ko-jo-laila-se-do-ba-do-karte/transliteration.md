Maza tha hum ko jo Laila se do-ba-do karte
Ke gul tumhaari bahaaron mein aarzu karte

Maze jo maut ke aashiq bayaan kabhu karte
Maseeh o Khizr bhi marne ki aarzu karte

Gharaz thi kya tere teeron ko aab-e-paikaan se
Magar ziyaarat-e-dil kyun ke be-wuzu karte

Ajab na tha ke zamaane ke inqilaab se hum
Tayammum aab se aur khaak se wuzu karte

Agar yeh jaante chun chun ke hum ko todenge
Toh gul kabhi na tamanna-e-rang-o-boo karte

Samajh yeh daar-o-rasan taar-o-sozan ae Mansoor
Ke chaak-e-parda haqeeqat ka hain rafoo karte

Yaqeen hai subh-e-qayamat ko bhi suboohi-kash
Uthenge khwaab se saaqi suboo suboo karte

Na rehti Yusuf-e-Kinaan ki garmi-e-bazaar
Muqable mein jo hum tujh ko rubaru karte

Chaman bhi dekhte gulzaar-e-aarzu ki bahaar
Tumhaari baad-e-bahaari mein aarzu karte

Suraagh-e-umr-e-guzishta ka kijiye gar Zauq
Tamaam umr guzar jaaye justuju karte
