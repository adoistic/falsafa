Chupke chupke gham ka khana koi hum se seekh jaaye
Jee hi jee mein tilmilana koi hum se seekh jaaye

Abr kya aansu bahana koi hum se seekh jaaye
Barq kya hai tilmilana koi hum se seekh jaaye

Zikr-e-shama-e-husn laana koi hum se seekh jaaye
Un ko darparda jalana koi hum se seekh jaaye

Jhoot-moot afiyun khaana koi hum se seekh jaaye
Un ko kaf laa kar darana koi hum se seekh jaaye

Sun ke aamad un ki az-khud-rafta ho jaate hain hum
Peshwa lene ko jaana koi hum se seekh jaaye

Hum ne awwal hi kaha tha tu karega hum ko qatl
Tewaron ka taad jaana koi hum se seekh jaaye

Lutf uthana hai agar manzoor us ke naaz ka
Pehle us ka naaz uthana koi hum se seekh jaaye

Jo sikhaya apni qismat ne wagarna us ko ghair
Kya sikhayega sikhana koi hum se seekh jaaye

Dekh kar qaatil ko bhar laaye kharash-e-dil mein khoon
Sach toh yeh hai muskurana koi hum se seekh jaaye

Teer-o-paikaan dil mein jitne the diye hum ne nikaal
Apne haathon ghar lutana koi hum se seekh jaaye

Keh do qaasid se ke jaaye kuch bahaane se wahan
Gar nahi aata bahana koi hum se seekh jaaye

Khat mein likhwa kar unhein bheja toh matla'a dard ka
Dard-e-dil apna jatana koi hum se seekh jaaye

Jab kaha marta hoon woh bole mera sar kaat kar
Jhoot ko sach kar dikhana koi hum se seekh jaaye

Waan hile abru yahan pheri gale par hum ne tegh
Baat ka eema se paana koi hum se seekh jaaye

Tegh toh ochi padi thi gir pade hum aap se
Dil ko qaatil ke badhana koi hum se seekh jaaye

Zakhm ko seete hain sab par sozan-e-almas se
Chaak seene ke silana koi hum se seekh jaaye

Poochhe mulla se jise karna ho sajda-e-sahu ka
Seekhe gar apna bhulana koi hum se seekh jaaye

Kya hua ae Zauq hain joon mardumak hum ru-siyaah
Lekin aankhon mein samana koi hum se seekh jaaye
