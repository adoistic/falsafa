Hum hain aur shughl-e-ishq-baazi hai
Kya haqeeqi hai kya majazi hai

Dukhtar-e-raz nikal ke meena se
Karti kya kya zubaan-daraazi hai

Khat ko kya dekhte ho aaine mein
Husn ki yeh ada-taraazi hai

Hindu-e-chashm taaq-e-abru mein
Kya bana aan kar namazi hai

Nazr dein nafs-kash ko dunya-daar
Waah kya teri be-niyaazi hai

But-e-tannaz hum se ho na-saaz
Kaarsaazon ki kaarsaazi hai

Sach kaha hai kisi ne yeh ae Zauq
Maal-e-moozi naseeb-e-ghaazi hai
