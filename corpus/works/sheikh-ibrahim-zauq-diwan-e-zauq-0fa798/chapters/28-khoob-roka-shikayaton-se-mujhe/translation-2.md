Khoob roka shikayaton se mujhe
Tu ne maara inayaton se mujhe

Wajib-ul-qatl us ne thehraya
Aayaton se riwayaton se mujhe

Kehte kya kya hain dekh to aghyaar
Yaar teri himayaton se mujhe

Kya ghazab hai ke dost tu samjhe
Dushmanon ki riayaton se mujhe

Dam-e-girya kami na kar ae chashm
Shauq kam hai kifayaton se mujhe

Kami-e-girya ne jala maara
Hua nuqsaan kifayaton se mujhe

Le gayi ishq ki hidayat Zauq
Un kane sab nihayaton se mujhe
