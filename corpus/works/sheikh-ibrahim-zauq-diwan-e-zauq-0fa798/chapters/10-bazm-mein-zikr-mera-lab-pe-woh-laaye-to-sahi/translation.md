Bazm mein zikr mera lab pe woh laaye to sahi
Waheen maaloom karun hont hilaaye to sahi

Sang par sang har ek koocha mein khaaye to sahi
Par bala se tere deewane khaaye to sahi

Go janaaze pe nahin qabr pe aaye woh meri
Shikwa kya keejay ghaneemat hai ke aaye to sahi

Kyonke deewar pe chadh jaaun koi kehta hai
Paaon kaatun_ga angutha woh jamaaye to sahi

Paara-e-mushaf-e-dil thay tere koocha mein pade
Aate paaon ke talay shukr ke paaye to sahi

Saaf be-parda nahin hota woh ghurfa mein na ho
Rozan-e-dar se kabhi aankh ladaaye to sahi

Geh badhaata hai gehay meh ko ghataata hai falak
Par shab-e-hijr ko hum dekhen ghataaye to sahi

Karun ek naale se main hashr mein barpa sau hashr
Shor-e-mahshar mujhe sotay se jagaaye to sahi

Gir pade thay kai is kooche mein dil ke tukde
Aate paaon ke talay shukr ke paaye to sahi

Thay tumheen nikle jo is daam-e-bala se aye Zauq
Warna thay paich mein is zulf ke aaye to sahi
