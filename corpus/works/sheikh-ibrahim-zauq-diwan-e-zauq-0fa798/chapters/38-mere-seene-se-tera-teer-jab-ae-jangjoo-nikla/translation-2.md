Mere seene se tera teer jab ae jangjoo nikla
Dahan-e-zakhm se khoon ho ke harf-e-aarzoo nikla
Mera ghar teri manzil-gaah ho aise kahan taale'
Khuda jaane kidhar ka chaand aaj ae maah-roo nikla
Phira gar aasmaan to shauq mein tere hai sargardaan
Agar khursheed nikla tera garm-e-justajoo nikla
Mai-e-ishrat talab karte the naahaq aasmaan se hum
Woh tha labrez-e-gham us khumkade se jo suboo nikla
Tere aate hi aate kaam aakhir ho gaya mera
Rahi hasrat ke dam mera na tere roobaroo nikla
Kahin tujh ko na paaya garche hum ne ik jahan dhoonda
Phir aakhir dil hi mein dekha baghal hi mein se tu nikla
Khajal apne gunahon se hoon main yaan tak ke jab roya
To jo aansu meri aankhon se nikla surkh-roo nikla
Ghise sab naakhun-e-tadbeer aur tooti sar-e-sozan
Magar tha dil mein jo kaanta na hargiz woh kabhu nikla
Use ayyaar paaya yaar samjhe 'Zauq' hum jis ko
Jise yaan dost apna humne jaana woh adoo nikla
