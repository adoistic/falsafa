Qasd jab teri ziyarat ka kabhu karte hain
Chashm-e-pur-aab se aaine wuzu karte hain

Karte izhaar hain dar-parda adawat apni
Woh mere aage jo taareef-e-adoo karte hain

Dil ka yeh haal hai phat jaaye hai sau ja se aur
Agar ek ja se hum isko rafu karte hain

Toden ek naale se is kaasa-e-gardoon ko magar
Nosh hum is mein kabhu dil ka lahoo karte hain

Qad-e-dil-joo ko tumhare nahin dekha shayad
Sarkashi itni jo sarv-e-lab-e-joo karte hain
