Chashm-e-qaatil humein kyonkar na bhala yaad rahe
Maut insaan ko laazim hai sadaa yaad rahe

Mera khoon hai tere kooche mein baha yaad rahe
Yeh bahaa woh nahin jis ka na bahaa yaad rahe

Kushta-e-zulf ke marqad pe tu ae Laila-vash
Beid-e-Majnoon hi lagaa taake pataa yaad rahe

Khaaksaari hai ajab vasf ke jyon jyon ho siwaa
Ho safaa aur dil-e-ahl-e-safaa yaad rahe

Ho yeh labbaik-e-haram ya yeh azaan-e-masjid
Mai-kasho qulqul-e-meena ki sadaa yaad rahe

Yaad us waada-faraamosh ne ghairon se badi
Yaad kuchh kam toh na thi aur siwaa yaad rahe

Khat bhi likhte hain toh lete hain khataai kaaghaz
Dekhiye kab tak unhein meri khataa yaad rahe

Do warq mein kaf-e-hasrat ke do aalam ka hai ilm
Sabaq-e-ishq agar tujh ko dilaa yaad rahe

Qatl-e-aashiq pe kamar baandhi hai ae dil us ne
Par khuda hai ke usey naam mera yaad rahe

Taair-e-qibla-numa ban ke kahaa dil ne mujhe
Ke tadap kar yoon hi mar jaayega jaa yaad rahe

Jab yeh deen-daar hain duniya ki namazein padhte
Kaash us waqt unhein naam-e-khuda yaad rahe

Hum pe sau baar jafaa ho toh rakho ek na yaad
Bhool kar bhi kabhi howe toh wafaa yaad rahe

Mehv itna bhi na ho ishq-e-butaan mein ae Zauq
Chahiye bande ko har waqt khuda yaad rahe
