Azeezo is ko na ghadiyal ki sada samjho
Yeh umr-e-rafta ki apni sada-e-paa samjho

Baja kahe jise aalam use baja samjho
Zaban-e-khalq ko naqqara-e-Khuda samjho

Na samjho dasht shifakhana-e-junoon hai yeh
Jo khaak si bhi pade phaankni dawa samjho

Samajh to kor-sawadon ko ho jo ilm na ho
Agar samajh bhi na ho kor-be-asa samjho

Pade kitab ke qisson mein kya karo dil saaf
Safa ho dil to beh-az Rauzat-us-Safa samjho

Hanse jo woh mere rone pe to saf-e-mizhgaan
Na samjho tum use deewar-e-qahqaha samjho

Nafas ki aamad-o-shud hai namaz-e-ahl-e-hayat
Jo yeh qaza ho to ae ghaafilo qaza samjho

Tumhari raah mein milte hain khaak mein laakhon
Is aarzoo mein ke tum apna khaak-e-paa samjho

Duaein dete hain hum dil se tegh-e-qaatil ko
Lab-e-jarahat-e-dil ko lab-e-dua samjho

Baha diya mera khoon uss ne apne kooche mein
Usi ko yaaro diyat samjho khoon-baha samjho

Samajh hai aur tumhari kahun main tum se kya
Tum apne dil mein Khuda jaane sun ke kya samjho

Tumhein hai naam se kya kaam misl-e-aaina
Jo roobaroo ho use surat-aashna samjho

Zehe-naseeb ke hangaam-e-mashq-e-teer-e-sitam
Hamare dher ko tum to woh khaak ka samjho

Nahin hai kam zar-e-khaalis se zardi-e-rukhsaar
Tum apne ishq ko ae Zauq keemiya samjho
