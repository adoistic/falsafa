Ab toh ghabra ke yeh kehte hain ke mar jaayenge
Mar ke bhi chain na paaya toh kidhar jaayenge
Tum ne thehraai agar ghair ke ghar jaane ki
Toh iraade yahan kuch aur theher jaayenge
Khaali ae chaara-garo honge bahut marham-daan
Par mere zakhm nahin aise ke bhar jaayenge
Pahunchenge rah-guzar-e-yaar talak kyon kar hum
Pehle jab tak na do aalam se guzar jaayenge
Shola-e-aah ko bijli ki tarah chamkaaun
Par mujhe dar hai ke woh dekh ke dar jaayenge
Hum nahin woh jo karein khoon ka daawa tujh par
Balki poochhega Khuda bhi toh mukar jaayenge
Aag dozakh ki bhi ho jaayegi paani paani
Jab yeh aasi arq-e-sharm se tar jaayenge
Nahin paayega nishaan koi hamara hargiz
Hum jahaan se ravish-e-teer-nazar jaayenge
Saamne chashm-e-gauhar-baar ke keh do dariya
Chadh ke gar aaye toh nazron se utar jaayenge
Laaye jo mast hain turbat pe gulaabi aankhein
Aur agar kuch nahin do phool toh dhar jaayenge
Rukh-e-roshan se naqaab apne ulat dekho tum
Mehr-o-mah nazron se yaaron ki utar jaayenge
Hum bhi dekhenge koi ahl-e-nazar hai ke nahin
Yaan se jab hum ravish-e-teer-nazar jaayenge
Zauq jo madrasay ke bigde hue hain mulla
Inko mai-khaane mein le aao sanwar jaayenge
