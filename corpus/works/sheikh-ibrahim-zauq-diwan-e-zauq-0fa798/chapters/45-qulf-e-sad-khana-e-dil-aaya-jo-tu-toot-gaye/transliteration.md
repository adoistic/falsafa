Qulf-e-sad-khana-e-dil aaya jo tu toot gaye
Jo tilismaat na toote the kabhu toot gaye

Saikdon kaasa-e-sar-e-dehr mein maanind-e-hubaab
Kabhu ae charkh bane tujh se kabhu toot gaye

Taanke kya jeeb ke phir baad-e-rafu toot gaye
Ho ke naakhun kabhi seene mein faruu toot gaye

Tu jo kehta hai ke de ghair ko bhi saaghar-e-mai
Haath kya us ke hain ae aaina-ru toot gaye

Kyunke bin kashti-e-mai keejiye sair-e-dariya
Mai-kasho zeer-e-baghal ab toh kadu toot gaye

Dekh kar surme ki tehreer teri aankhon mein
Kaafiron ke bhi hain zunnaar-e-galu toot gaye

Sadma-e-gham se tere jyun gul-e-baazi afsos
Saare a'aza mere ae arbada-ju toot gaye

Sang-e-ghairat se kai aaiine ae ehd-shikan
Dekh kar saaf tera roo-e-nekoo toot gaye

Teer dil se woh nikalte hain koi jazba-e-shauq
Nikle saufaar jo seene se guloo toot gaye

Dil shikasta hi raha baad-e-fana bhi main toh
Ke meri khaak se bante hi subu toot gaye

Shiddat-e-girya se tha raat yeh ashkon ka hujoom
Chashm-e-tar phir mere mizhgaan ke hain muu toot gaye

Gulshan-e-ishq mein Allah hai kya kasrat-e-baar
Bin hawa kitne hi nakhl-e-lab-e-juu toot gaye

Keh ba-tabdeel-e-qawaafi ghazal ik aur bhi Zauq
Dekhein bithlaaye hai kis tarah se tu toot gaye
