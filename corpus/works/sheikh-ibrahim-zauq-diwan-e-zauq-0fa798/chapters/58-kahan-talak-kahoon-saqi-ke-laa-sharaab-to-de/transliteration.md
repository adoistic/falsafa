kahan talak kahoon saqi ke laa sharaab to de
na de sharaab dubo kar koi kabaab to de

bujhega soz-e-dil ae girya pal mein aab to de
digar hai aag mein duniya yoon hi azaab to de

guzarne gar yeh mere sar se itna aab to de
ke sar pe charkh bhi dikhlaayi joon hubaab to de

hazaron tishna jigar kis se hoenge sairaab
khuda ke waaste tegh-e-sitam ko aab to de

tumhare matla-e-abru pe yeh kahe hai khaal
ke aisa nuqta koi waqt-e-intekhab to de

dar-e-qubool hai darbaan na band kar dar-e-yaar
dua-e-khair meri hone mustajaab to de

khule hai naaz se gulshan mein ghuncha-e-nargis
zara dikha to use chashm-e-neem-khwaab to de

bala se aap na aayein pe aadmi un ka
tasalli aa ke mujhe waqt-e-iztiraab to de

hawa bagole mein hai kushtagaan-e-zulf ki khaak
ke baad-e-marg bhi maloom pech-o-taab to de

bala se kam na ho girya se mera soz-e-jigar
bujha par un ki zara aatish-e-ataab to de

shaheed keejiyo qaatil abhi na kar jaldi
theherne mujh ko teh-e-tegh-e-iztiraab to de

shikaar-e-basta-e-fitraak ko tere maqdoor
hua na yeh bhi ke bosa sar-e-rikaab to de

dil-e-birishta ko mere na chhoro mai-khwaaro
jo lazzat is mein hai aisa maza kabaab to de

nashe mein hosh kise jo gine hisaab kare
jo tujh ko dena hain bose bila hisaab to de

jawaab-e-nama nahin gar to rakh do nama-e-yaar
jo poochhein qabr mein aashiq se kuch jawaab to de

rakhe hai hausla darya kab ahl-e-himmat ka
nahin yeh itna ke bhar kasa-e-hubaab to de

kahan bujhi hai teh-e-khaak meri aatish-e-dil
kaho hawa se hila daaman-e-sahaab to de

khunak dilon ki agar aah-e-sard dozakh mein
pade to waqai ik baar aag daab to de

karega qatl woh ae 'Zauq' tujh ko surme se
nigah ki tegh ko hone siyaah-taab to de

pahunch rahunga sar-e-manzil-e-fana ae 'Zauq'
misaal-e-naqsh-e-qadam karne pa-turaab to de
