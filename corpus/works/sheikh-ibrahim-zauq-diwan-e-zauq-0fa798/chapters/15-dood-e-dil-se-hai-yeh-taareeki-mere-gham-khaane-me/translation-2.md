Dood-e-dil se hai yeh taareeki mere gham-khaane mein
Shama hai ik sozan-e-gum-gashta iss kaashaane mein

Main hoon woh khisht-e-kuhan muddat se iss veeraane mein
Barson masjid mein raha barson raha mai-khaane mein

Main woh kaifi hoon ke paani ho toh ban jaaye sharaab
Josh-e-kaifiyat se meri khaak ke paimaane mein

Barq-e-khirman-soz daanai hai na-fehmi teri
Warna kya kya lehlahaate khet hain har daane mein

Kis nazaakat se hai dekho ittehad-e-husn-o-ishq
Zulf waan shaane ne khenchi dard hai yaan shaane mein

Vahshat o na-aashnaai masti o begaangi
Ya teri aankhon mein dekhi ya tere deewane mein

Ishq ko nashonuma manzoor hai kab warna sabz
Tukhm-e-ashk-e-shama ho khaakistar-e-parwaane mein

Hosh ka daawa hai be-hoshon ko zer-e-aasmaan
Khum-nasheen misl-e-falatoon sab hain iss khum-khaane mein

Pattharon mein thokrein khaata hai nahaq sail-e-aab
Poocho kya le jaayega aa kar mere gham-khaane mein

Ek patthar poojne ko Shaikh-ji Kaabe gaye
Zauq har but qaabil-e-bosa hai iss but-khaane mein
