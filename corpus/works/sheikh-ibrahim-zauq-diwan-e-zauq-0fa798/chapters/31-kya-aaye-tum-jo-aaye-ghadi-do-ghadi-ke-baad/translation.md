Kya aaye tum jo aaye ghadi do ghadi ke baad
Seene mein hogi saans udi do ghadi ke baad

Kya roka apne girye ko hum ne ke lag gayi
Phir woh hi aansuon ki jhadi do ghadi ke baad

Koi ghadi agar woh mulaayam hue toh kya
Keh baithenge phir ek kadi do ghadi ke baad

Us laal-e-lab ke hum ne liye bose is qadar
Sab ud gayi missi ki dhadi do ghadi ke baad

Allah re zoaf-e-seena se har aah-e-be-asar
Lab tak jo pahunchi bhi toh chadhi do ghadi ke baad

Kal us se hum ne tark-e-mulaqat ki toh kya
Phir us baghair kal na padi do ghadi ke baad

The do ghadi se Sheikh ji shekhi baghaarte
Saari woh shekhi un ki jhadi do ghadi ke baad

Kehta raha kuch us se adu do ghadi talak
Ghammaz ne phir aur jadi do ghadi ke baad

Parwana gird shama ke shab do ghadi raha
Phir dekhi us ki khaak padi do ghadi ke baad

Tu do ghadi ka vaada na kar dekh jald aa
Aane mein hogi der badi do ghadi ke baad

Go do ghadi tak us ne na dekha idhar toh kya
Aakhir hamein se aankh ladi do ghadi ke baad

Kya jaane do ghadi woh rahe Zauq kis tarah
Phir toh na thehre paaon ghadi do ghadi ke baad
