> One should have the bull, yoked with the heifers, go in the northeastern direction. /
> He shall give a pair of garments to the officiating priest, and gold and bronze. || Vi_86.17 ||
>
> To the blacksmith is to be given a fee as desired by his mind. /
> And he shall feed the Brāhmaṇas a meal with much clarified butter. || Vi_86.18 ||
>
> In whatever body of water the released bull drinks, /
> That entire body of water goes to his ancestors. || Vi_86.19 ||

> Wherever, in his pride, he tears up the earth with his horn, /
> That becomes abundant food and drink for the ancestors. || Vi_86.20 ||
