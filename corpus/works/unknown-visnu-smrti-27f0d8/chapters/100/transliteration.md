Vi_100

dharmaśāstram idaṃ śreṣṭhaṃ svayaṃ devena bhāṣitam  /

ye dvijā dhārayiṣyanti teṣāṃ svarge gatiḥ parā  || Vi_100.1 ||

idaṃ pavitraṃ maṅgalyaṃ svargyam āyuṣyam eva ca  /

jñānaṃ caiva yaśasyaṃ ca dhanasaubhāgyavardhanam  || Vi_100.2 ||

adhyetavyaṃ dhāraṇīyaṃ śrāvyaṃ śrotavyam eva ca  /

śrāddheṣu śrāvaṇīyaṃ ca bhūtikāmair naraiḥ sadā  || Vi_100.3 ||

ya idaṃ paṭhate nityaṃ bhūtikāmo naraḥ sadā  /

idaṃ rahasyaṃ paramaṃ kathitaṃ ca dhare tava  || Vi_100.4 ||

mayā prasannena jagaddhitārthaṃ saubhāgyam etat paramaṃ yaśasyam  /

duḥsvapnanāśaṃ bahupuṇyayuktaṃ śivālayaṃ śāśvatadharmaśāstram  || Vi_100.5 ||

[End of Part 2 = end of Viṣṇu.]
