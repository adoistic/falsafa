**Vishnu Smriti 74**

On the *aṣṭakā* (अष्टका) days, having performed a *śrāddha* (श्राद्ध) with the rite for the gods performed first, with vegetables, meat, and cakes, and on the *anvaṣṭakā* (अन्वष्टका) days, having offered into the fire as on the *aṣṭakā* (अष्टका) days, with the rite for the gods performed first, he shall feed Brāhmaṇas for his mother, paternal grandmother, and paternal great-grandmother as before, and having honored them with gifts, he shall follow them and dismiss them. // Vi_74.1 //

Then he shall make *karṣūs* (कर्षूs) (ritual trenches). // Vi_74.2 //

At their base, having established a fire to the northeast, he shall perform the offering of *piṇḍas* (पिण्डs). // Vi_74.3 //

At the base of three *karṣūs* (कर्षूs) for the men, and at the base of three *karṣūs* (कर्षूs) for the women. // Vi_74.4 //

He shall fill the three *karṣūs* (कर्षूs) for the men with food and water. // Vi_74.5 //

The three *karṣūs* (कर्षूs) for the women with food and milk. // Vi_74.6 //

Each of the three *karṣūs* (कर्षूs) with curd, meat, and milk. // Vi_74.7 //

Having filled them, he shall mutter, "May this be imperishable for you (masculine) and for you (feminine)." // Vi_74.8 //
