**Vishnu Smriti 53**

Now, for illicit intercourse, wearing a garment of bark in the forest for a year, he shall perform the *prājāpatya* penance according to the rule of the *mahāvrata*. // Vi_53.1 //

And for intercourse with another's wife. // Vi_53.2 //

And for intercourse with a cow, the *govrata*. // Vi_53.3 //

For intercourse with a man, in an unnatural orifice, in the sky, in water, during the day, or in a cow-cart, he shall perform a bath with his clothes on. // Vi_53.4 //

For intercourse with a Caṇḍāla woman, he attains her state. // Vi_53.5 //

If done unknowingly, he shall perform two *cāndrāyaṇa* penances. // Vi_53.6 //

And for intercourse with a beast or a prostitute, the *prājāpatya* penance. // Vi_53.7 //

And a woman who has sinned once shall perform that same vow which is prescribed for a man for intercourse with another's wife. // Vi_53.8 //

> The sin which a twice-born man commits in one night by intercourse with a *vṛṣalī*, /
> He removes in three years by subsisting on alms and constantly muttering prayers. || Vi_53.9 ||
