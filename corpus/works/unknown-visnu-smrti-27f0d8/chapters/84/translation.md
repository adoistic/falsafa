**Vishnu Smriti 84**

One shall not perform a *śrāddha* (श्राद्ध) in a *mlecchadeśa* (म्लेच्छदेश) (land of barbarians). // Vi_84.1 //

One shall not go to a *mlecchadeśa* (म्लेच्छदेश). // Vi_84.2 //

By drinking water from the cisterns of others, one attains their state. // Vi_84.3 //

> The country where the system of the four *varṇas* is not established, /
> That is to be known as a *mlecchadeśa* (म्लेच्छदेश); *Āryāvarta* (आर्यावर्त) is beyond that. || Vi_84.4 ||
