**Vishnu Smriti 41**

Killing birds, aquatic animals, and those born in water. // Vi_41.1 //

And worms and insects. // Vi_41.2 //

Eating food associated with liquor. // Vi_41.3 //

These are defiling acts (*malāvaha*). // Vi_41.4 //

> For defiling acts, the *taptakṛcchra* is the purification, /
> Or the *kṛcchrātikṛcchra* is the purifying *prāyaścitta*. || Vi_41.5 ||
