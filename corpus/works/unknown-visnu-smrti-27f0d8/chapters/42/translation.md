**Vishnu Smriti 42**

What has not been mentioned is a miscellaneous sin (*prakīrṇaka*). // Vi_42.1 //

> In a miscellaneous sin, having ascertained its gravity or lightness, /
> A wise man should perform the *prāyaścitta* as approved by Brāhmaṇas. || Vi_42.2 ||
