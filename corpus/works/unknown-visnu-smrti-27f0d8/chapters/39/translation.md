**Vishnu Smriti 39**

Harming domestic and wild animals is a sin causing caste mixture (*saṃkarīkaraṇa*). // Vi_39.1 //

> Having committed a sin causing caste mixture, one shall eat barley for a month, /
> Or one should perform the *kṛcchra* or *atikṛcchra* as a *prāyaścitta* (penance). || Vi_39.2 ||
