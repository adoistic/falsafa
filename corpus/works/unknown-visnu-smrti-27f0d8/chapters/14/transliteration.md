Vi_14

atha kośaḥ // Vi_14.1 //

ugrān devān samabhyarcya tatsnānodakāt prasṛtitrayaṃ pibet // Vi_14.2 //

idaṃ mayā na kṛtam iti vadan sthāpitadevatābhimukhaḥ // Vi_14.3 //

yasya paśyed dvisaptāhāt trisaptāhād athāpi vā  /

rogo 'gnir jñātimaraṇaṃ rājātaṅkaṃ athāpi vā  || Vi_14.4 ||

tam aśuddhaṃ vijānīyāt tathā śuddhaṃ viparyaye  /

divye ca śuddhaṃ puruṣaṃ sat kuryād dhārmiko nṛpaḥ  || Vi_14.5 ||
