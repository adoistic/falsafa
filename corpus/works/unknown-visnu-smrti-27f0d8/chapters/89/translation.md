**Vishnu Smriti 89**

The month of *Kārttika* (कार्तिक) belongs to the deity Agni. // Vi_89.1 //

And Agni is the mouth of all the gods. // Vi_89.2 //

Therefore, he who bathes outside, is devoted to muttering the Gāyatrī mantra, and eats sacrificial food only once during the month of *Kārttika* (कार्तिक), is purified from the sin committed in a year. // Vi_89.3 //

> He who bathes daily for the entire month of *Kārttika* (कार्तिक), with his senses subdued, /
> Muttering prayers, eating sacrificial food, and being self-controlled, is freed from all sins. || Vi_89.4 ||
