**Vishnu Smriti 34**

Incest with one's mother, daughter, or daughter-in-law are heinous sins (*atipātaka*). // Vi_34.1 //

> Those who commit these heinous sins shall enter the fire; /
> For no other expiation for them is known in any way. || Vi_34.2 ||
