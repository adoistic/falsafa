**Vishnu Smriti 72**

He shall abide by self-control (*dama*). // Vi_72.1 //

And *dama* is declared to be the control of the senses. // Vi_72.2 //

For one who is self-controlled, this world and the next are his. // Vi_72.3 //

No action of one who is not self-controlled prospers. // Vi_72.4 //

> Self-control is the supreme purifier, self-control is the supreme good fortune; /
> By self-control one obtains everything, whatever one desires in one's mind. || Vi_72.5 ||
>
> He who travels in a chariot yoked with five horses, with the mind as the driver, following the path of the Āryas, /
> If the horses do not carry away that chariot, then the enemies do not overcome him who has thus gone. || Vi_72.6 ||
>
> Just as waters enter the ocean, which is ever being filled yet remains unmoved, /
> So he into whom all desires enter attains peace, not the desirer of desires.[^3] || Vi_72.7 ||
