**Vishnu Smriti 88**

Now, a cow in the act of giving birth becomes the earth. // Vi_88.1 //

Having given her, adorned, to a Brāhmaṇa, he obtains the fruit of giving the earth. // Vi_88.2 //

On this subject, there is a verse. // Vi_88.3 //

> Having given a cow with her calf, facing both ways, a man who has faith and is concentrated /
> Attains heaven for as many ages as there are hairs on her body. || Vi_88.4 ||
