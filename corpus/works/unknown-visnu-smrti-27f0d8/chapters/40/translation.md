**Vishnu Smriti 40**

Taking wealth from censured persons, commerce, living by usury, speaking untruth, and serving a Śūdra are sins making one unworthy of receiving gifts (*apātrīkaraṇa*). // Vi_40.1 //

> Having committed a sin making one unworthy of receiving gifts, one is purified by the *taptakṛcchra*, /
> Or again by the *śītakṛcchra*, or by the *mahāsāṃtapana*. || Vi_40.2 ||
