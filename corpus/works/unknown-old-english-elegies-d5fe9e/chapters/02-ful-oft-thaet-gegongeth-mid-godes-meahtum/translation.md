Ful oft thaet gegongeth,     mid godes meahtum,
thaette wer ond wif     in woruld cennath
bearn mid gebyrdum     ond mid bleom gyrwath,
tennath ond taetath,     oththaet seo tid cymeth,
gegaeth gearrimum,     thaet tha geongan leomu,
liffaestan leothu,     geloden weorthath.
Fergath swa ond fethath     faeder ond modor,
giefath ond gierwath.     God ana wat
hwaet him weaxendum     winter bringath!

Sumum thaet gegongeth     on geoguthfeore
thaet se endestaef     earfethmaecgum
wealic weortheth.     Sceal hine wulf etan,
har haethstapa;     hinsith thonne
modor bimurneth.     Ne bith swylc monnes geweald!

Sumne sceal hungor ahithan,     sumne sceal hreoh fordrifan,
sumne sceal gar agetan,     sumne guth abreotan.
Sum sceal leomena leas     lifes neotan,
folmum aetfeohtan,     sum on fethe lef,
seonobennum seoc,     sar cwanian,
murnan meotudgesceaft     mode gebysgad.

Sum sceal on holte     of hean beame
fitherleas feallan;     bith on flihte setheah,
laceth on lyfte,     oththaet lengre ne bith
westem wudubeames.     thonne he on wyrtruman
sigeth sworcenferth,     sawle bireafod,
falleth on foldan,     feorth bith on sithe.

Sum sceal on fethe     on feorwegas
nyde gongan     ond his nest beran,
tredan uriglast     eltheodigra,
frecne foldan;     ah he feormendra
lyt lifgendra,     lath bith aeghwaer
fore his wonsceaftum     wineleas haele.

Sum sceal on geapum     galgan ridan,
seomian aet swylte,     oththaet sawlhord,
bancofa blodig,     abrocen weortheth.
thaer him hrefn nimeth     heafodsyne,
sliteth salwigpad     sawelleasne;
nother he thy facne maeg     folmum biwergan,
lathum lyftsceathan,     bith his lif scaecen,
ond he feleleas,     feores orwena,
blac on beame     bideth wyrde,
bewegen waelmiste.     Bith him werig noma!

Sumne on baele sceal     brond aswencan,
fretan frecne lig faegne monnan;
thaer him lifgedal     lungre weortheth,
read rethe gled;     reoteth meowle,
seo hyre bearn gesihth     brondas theccan.

Sumum meces ecg     on meodubence
yrrum ealowosan     ealdor oththringeth,
were winsadum;     bith aer his worda to hraed.

Sum sceal on beore     thurh byreles hond
meodugal maecga;     thonne he gemet ne con
gemearcian his muthe     mode sine,
ac sceal ful earmlice     ealdre linnan,
dreogan dryhtenbealo     dreamum biscyred,
ond hine to sylfcwale     secgas nemnath,
maenath mid muthe     meodugales gedrinc.

Sum sceal on geoguthe     mid godes meahtum
his earfothsith     ealne forspildan,
ond on yldo eft     eadig weorthan,
wunian wyndagum     ond welan thicgan,
mathmas ond meoduful     maegburge on,
thaes the aenig fira maege forth gehealdan.

Swa missenlice     meahtig dryhten
geond eorthan sceat     eallum daeleth,
scyreth ond scrifeth     ond gesceapo healdeth,
sumum eadwelan,     sumum earfetha dael,
sumum geogothe glaed,     sumum guthe blaed,
gewealdenne wigplegan,     sumum wyrp oththe scyte,
torhtlicne tiir,     sumum taefle craeft,
bleobordes gebregd.     Sume boceras
weorthath wisfaeste.     Sumum wundorgiefe
thurh goldsmithe     gearwad weorthath;
ful oft he gehyrdeth     ond gehyrsteth wel,
brytencyninges beorn,     ond he him brad syleth
lond to leane.     He hit on lust thigeth.

Sum sceal on heape     haelethum cweman,
blissian aet beore     bencsittendum;
thaer bith drincendra     dream se micla.

Sum sceal mid hearpan     aet his hlafordes
fotum sittan,     feoh thicgan,
ond a snellice     snere wraestan,
laetan scralletan sceacol, se the hleapeth,
naegl neomegende; bith him neod micel.

Sum sceal wildne fugel     wloncne atemian,
heafoc on honda,     oththaet seo heoroswealwe
wynsum weortheth;     deth he wyrplas on,
fedeth swa on feterum     fithrum dealne,
lepeth lyftswiftne     lytlum gieflum,
oththaet se waelisca     waedum ond daedum
his aetgiefan     eathmod weortheth
ond to hagostealdes     honda gelaered.

Swa wraetlice     weoroda nergend
geond middangeard     monna craeftas
sceop ond scyrede     ond gesceapo ferede
aeghwylcum on eorthan     eormencynnes.
Forthon him nu ealles thonc     aeghwa secge,
thaes the he fore his miltsum     monnum scrifeth.
