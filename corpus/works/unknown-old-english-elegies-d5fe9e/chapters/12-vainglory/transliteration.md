Hwaet, me frod wita    on fyrndagum saegde, snottor ar,    sundorwundra fela. Wordhord onwreah witgan larum beorn boca gleaw,    bodan aercwide,
thaet ic sothlice    siththan meahte ongitan bi tham gealdre    godes agen bearn, wilgest on wicum,    ond thone wacran swa some, scyldum bescyredne,    on gescead witan. thaet maeg aeghwylc mon    eathe gethencan,
se the hine ne laeteth    on thas laenan tid amyrran his gemyndum    modes gaelsan ond on his daegrime druncen to rice, thonne monige beoth maethelhegendra, wlonce wigsmitas    winburgum in,
sittath aet symble,    sothgied wrecath, wordum wrixlath,    witan fundiath hwylc aescstede    inne in raecede mid werum wunige,    thonne win hweteth beornes breostsefan.    Breahtem stigeth,
cirm on corthre,    cwide scralletath missenlice.    Swa beoth modsefan dalum gedaeleD,    sindon dryhtguman ungelice.    Sum on oferhygdo thrymme thringeth, thrinteth him in innan
ungemedemad mod;    sindan to monige thaet! Bith thaet aefthonca    eal gefylled feondes fligepilum,    facensearwum; breodath he ond baelceth,    both his sylfes swithor micle    thonne se sella mon,
thenceth thaet his wise    welhwam thince eal unforcuth.    Bith thaes other swice, thonne he thaes facnes    fintan sceawath. Wrenceth he ond blenceth,    worn gethenceth hinderhoca,    hygegar leteth,
scurum sceoteth.    He tha scylde ne wat faehthe gefremede, feoth his betran eorl fore aefstum,    laeteth inwitflan brecan thone burgweal,    the him bebead meotud thaet he thaet wigsteal    wergan sceolde,
siteth symbelwlonc,    searwum laeteth wine gewaeged    word ut faran, thraefte thringan    thrymme gebyrmed, aefaestum onaeled,    oferhygda ful, nithum nearowrencum.    Nu thu cunnan meaht,
gif thu thyslicne    thegn gemittest wunian in wicum,    wite the be thissum feawum forthspellum    thaet thaet bith feondes bearn flaesce bifongen,    hafath fraete lif, grundfusne gaest    gode orfeormne,
wuldorcyninge.    thaet se witga song, gearowyrdig guma,    ond thaet gyd awraec: "Se the hine sylfne    in tha slithnan tid thurh oferhygda    up ahlaeneth, ahefeth heahmodne,    se sceal hean wesan
aefter neosithum    nither gebiged, wunian witum faest,    wyrmum bethrungen. thaet waes geara iu    in godes rice thaette mid englum    oferhygd astag, widmaere gewin.    Wroht ahofan,
heardne heresith,    heofon widledan, forsawan hyra sellan,    tha hi to swice thohton ond thrymcyning    theodenstoles ricne beryfan,    swa hit ryht ne waes, ond thonne gesettan    on hyra sylfra dom
wuldres wynlond.    thaet him wige forstod faeder frumsceafta;    wearth him seo feohte to grim. thonne bith tham othrum    ungelice se the her on eorthan    eathmod leofath, ond with gesibbra gehwone    simle healdeth
freode on folce    ond his feond lufath, theah the he him abylgnesse    oft gefremede willum in thisse worulde.    Se mot wuldres dream in haligra hyht    heonan astigan on engla eard.    Ne bith tham othrum swa,
se the on ofermedum    eargum daedum leofath in leahtrum,    ne beoth tha lean gelic mid wuldorcyning."    Wite the be thissum, gif thu eathmodne    eorl gemete, thegn on theode,    tham bith simle
gaest gegaederad    godes agen bearn wilsum in worlde,    gif me se witega ne leag. Forthon we sculon a hycgende    haelo raedes gemunan in mode    maela gehwylcum thone selestan    sigora waldend.
Amen.
