Maey ich be me sylfum      soth-yied wrekan, sithas seyan,      hu ich yeswinkdagum
earfothhwile      oft throwade, bitre breostcheare      yebiden haebbe,
    5  yekunnad in keole      chearselda fela, atol ytha yewealk,      thaer mek oft biyeat
nearo nihtwako      aet nakan stefnan, thonne he be klifum knossath.      Kalde yethrungen
waeron mine fet,      forste yebunden,
   10  kaldum klommum,      thaer tha cheare seofedun
hat ymb heortan;      hungor innan slat
mereweryes mod.      thaet se mon ne wat
the him on foldan      faegrost limpeth,
hu ich earmcheariy      ishealdne sae
   15  winter wunade      wraechan lastum,
winemaegum bidroren, bihongen hrimyikelum;      haegl shurum fleag.
thaer ich ne yehyrde      butan hlimman sae,
ishealdne waey.      Hwilum ylfete song
   20  dyde ich me to gomene,      ganetes hleothor
ond huilpan sweg      fore hleahtor wera,
maew singende      fore medodrinke.
Stormas thaer stanklifu beotan,      thaer him stearn onkwaeth
isiyfethera;      ful oft thaet earn biyeal,
   25  uriyfethra; ne aeniy hleomaega
feasheaftiy ferth frefran meahte.
Forthon him yelyfeth lyt,      se the ah lifes wyn
yebiden in burgum,      bealositha hwon,
wlonk ond wingal,      hu ich weriy oft
   30  in brimlade      bidan sheolde.
Nap nihtshua,      northan sniwde,
hrim hrusan bond,      haegl feol on eorthan,
korna kaldast.      Forthon knyssath nu
heortan yethohtas,      thaet ich hean streamas,
   35  sealtytha yelak      sylf kunniye;
monath modes lust      maela yehwylche
ferth to feran,      thaet ich feor heonan
eltheodigra      eard yeseke.
Forthon nis thaes modwlonk      mon ofer eorthan,
   40  ne his yifena thaes god,      ne in yoguthe to thaes hwaet,
ne in his daedum to thaes deor,      ne him his dryhten to thaes hold,
thaet he a his saefore      sorge naebbe,
to hwon hine dryhten      yedon wille.
Ne bith him to hearpan hyye      ne to hringtheye,
   45  ne to wife wyn      ne to worulde hyht,
ne ymbe owiht elles,      nefne ymb ytha yewealk,
ak a hafath longunge      se the on lagu fundath.
Bearwas blostmum nimath,      byrig faegriath,
wongas wlitigath, woruld onetteth;
   50  ealle tha yemon-iath      modes fusne
sefan to sithe,      tham the swa thencheth
on flodwegas      feor yewitan.
Swylche ye-ak monath      yeomran reorde,
singeth sumeres weard,      sorge beodeth
   55  bitter in breosthord.      thaet se beorn ne wat,
es-teadiy sej,      hwaet tha sume dreogath
the tha wraeklastas      widost lejath.
Forthon nu min hyye hweorfeth      ofer hretherlokan,
min modsefa      mid mereflode
   60  ofer hwaeles ethel      hweorfeth wide,
eorthan sheatas,      cymeth eft to me
yifre ond graedig,      yelleth anfloga,
hweteth on hwaelweg hrether unwearnum
ofer holma yelagu.      Forthon me hatran sind
   65  dryhtnes dreamas      thonne this deade lif,
laene on londe.      Ich yelyfe no
thaet him eorthwelan      eche stondath.
Simle threora sum      thinga yehwylche,
aer his tid aga,      to tweon weortheth;
   70  adl oththe yldo      oththe ejhete
faegum fromweardum      feorh oththringeth.
Forthon thaet bith eorla yehwam      aefterkwethendra
lof lifyendra      lastworda betst,
thaet he yewyrche,      aer he on weg shyle,
   75  fremum on foldan      with feonda nith,
deorum daedum      deofle toyeanes,
thaet hine aelda bearn      aefter heryen,
ond his lof siththan      lifye mid englum
awa to ealdre,      ecan lifes blaed,
   80  dream mid dugethum.      Dagas sind yewitene,
ealle onmedlan      eorthan riches;
naeron nu kyningas      ne kaeseras
ne goldyiefan      swylche yu waeron,
thonne hi maest mid him      maertha yefremedon
   85  ond on dryhtlicestum      dome lifdon.
Yedroren is theos duguth eal,      dreamas sind yewitene,
wuniath tha wacran      ond thas woruld healdath,
brukath thurh bisgo.      Blaed is yehnaeyed,
eorthan indryhto      ealdath ond searath,
   90  swa nu monna yehwylc      yeond middanyeard.
Yldo him on fareth,      onsyn blacath,
gomelfeax gnornath,      wat his yu-wine,
aethelinga bearn,      eorthan foryiefene.
Ne maey him thonne se flaeschhoma,      thonne him thaet feorg losath,
   95  ne swete forswelgan      ne sar yefelan,
ne hond onhreran      ne mid hyye thenchan.
theah the graef wille      golde stregan
brothor his yeborenum,      byrgan be deadum,
mathmum mislicum      thaet hine mid wille,
  100  ne maey thaere sawle      the bith synna ful
gold to yoce      for godes egsan,
thonne he hit aer hydeth      thenden he her leofath.
Mykel bith se meotudes egsa,      forthon hi seo molde onkyrreth;
se yestathelade      stithe grundas,
  105  eorthan sheatas      ond uprodor.
Dol bith se the him his dryhten ne ondraedeth;      cymeth him se death unthinged.
Eadiy bith se the eathmod leofath;      cymeth him seo ar of heofonum,
meotod him thaet mod yestathelath,      forthon he in his meahte yelyfeth.
Stieran mon sheal strongum mode,      ond thaet on stathelum healdan,
  110  ond yewis werum,      wisum klaene,
shyle monna yehwylc      mid yemete healdan
with leofne ond with lathne      bealo,
theah the he hine wille fyres      fulne
oththe on baele      forbaernedne
  115  his yeworhtne wine.      Wyrd bith swithre,
meotud meahtigra      thonne aenyes monnes yehygd.
Uton we hyjjan      hwaer we ham agen,
ond thonne yethenchan      hu we thider kumen,
ond we thonne eak tilien,      thaet we to moten
  120  in tha ecan      eadignesse,
thaer is lif yelong      in lufan dryhtnes,
hyht in heofonum.      thaes sy tham halgan thonk,
thaet he usich yeweorthade,      wuldres ealdor,
ece dryhten,      in ealle tid.
  125  Amen.
