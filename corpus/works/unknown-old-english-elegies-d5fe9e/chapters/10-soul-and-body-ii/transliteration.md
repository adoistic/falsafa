Soul and Body II
================

Huru, thas behofath      haletha aghwylc that he his sawle sith      sylfa bewitige, hu that bith deoplic      thonne se death cymeth, asundrath tha sibbe,      tha the ar somud waeron,
    5  lic ond sawle! Long bith siththan that se gast nimeth      at gode sylfum swa wite swa wuldor,      swa him in worulde ar efne that eorthfat      ar geworhte. Sheal se gast cuman      gehthum hremig,
   10  symle ymb seofon niht      sawle findan thone lichoman      the heo ar longe wag, threo hund wintra, butan ar wyrce      ece dryhten, almihtig god,      ende worlde.
   15  Cleopath thonne swa cearful      caldan reorde, spriceth grimlice      gast to tham duste: "Hwat, drug thu dreorga,      to hwon dreahtest thu me, eorthan fylnes      eal forweornast, lames gelicnes!      Lyt thu gethohtes
   20  to won thinre sawle sith      siththan wurde, siththan heo of lichoman      laded waere! Hwat, wite thu me, werga!      Hwat, thu huru wyrma gifl lyt gethohtes,      hu this is long hider, ond the thurh engel      ufan of roderum
   25  sawle onsende      thurh his sylfes hond, meotud almihtig,      of his magenthrymme, ond the tha gebohte      blode thy halgan, ond thu me thy heardan      hungre gebunde ond gehaftnadest      helle witum!
   30  Eardode ic the in innan.      No ic the of meahte, flasce bifongen,      ond me firenlustas thine gethrungon.      that me thuhte ful oft that waere thritig      thusend wintra to thinum deathdage.      Hwat, ic uncres gedales bad
   35  earfothlice.      Nis nu se ende to god! Waere thu the wiste wlonc      ond wines sad, thrymful thunedest,      ond ic ofthyrsted was godes lichoman,      gastes drinces. thar thu thonne hogode      her on life,
   40  thenden ic the in worulde      wunian sholde, that thu waere thurh flasc      ond thurh firenlustas stronge gestyred      ond gestathlad thurh mec, ond ic was gast on the      from gode sended, nafre thu me swa heardra      helle wita
   45  ned gearwode      thurh thinra neoda lust. Shealt thu nu hwathre minra geshenta      shome throwian on tham miclan dage,      thonne monna cynn se ancenda      ealle gegadrath. Ne eart thu nu thon leofre      nangum lifgendra,
   50  menn to gemaeccan,      ne medder ne fader, ne nangum gesibbra,      thonne se swearta hrefn, siththan ic ana of the      ut sithade thurh thas sylfes hond      the ic ar onsended was. Ne magon the nu heonan adon      hyrste tha readan,
   55  ne gold ne sylfor      ne thinra goda nan, ac her shulon abidan      ban bireafod, besliten seonwum,      ond the thin sawl sheal minum unwillan      oft gesecan, wemman mid wordum,      swa thu worhtest to me.
   60  Eart thu dumb ond deaf,      ne sindan thine dreamas wiht. Sheal ic the nihtes setheah      nyde gesecan, synnum gesargad,      ond eft sona from the hweorfan on honcred,      thonne halege menn gode lifgendum      lofsong doth,
   65  secan tha hamas      the thu me ar scrife, ond tha arleasan      eardungstowe, ond the shulon moldwyrmas      monige ceowan, seonowum beslitan      swearte wihte, gifre ond gradge.      Ne sindon thine geahthe wiht,
   70  tha thu her on moldan      monnum eawdest. Forthon the waere selle      swithe micle thonne the waeran ealle      eorthan spede, (butan thu hy gedalde      dryhtne sylfum), thar thu wurde at frumshafte fugel      oththe fisc on sa,
   75  oththe eorthan neat      ates tiolode, feldgongende      feoh butan snyttro, ge on westenne      wildra deora that grimmeste,      thar swa god wolde, ge theah thu waere wyrmcynna      that wyrreste,
   80  thonne thu afre on moldan      mon gewurde, oththe afre fulwihte      onfon sholde. thonne thu for unc bu      ondwyrdan shealt on tham miclan dage,      thonne eallum monnum beoth wunde onwrigene,      tha the in worulde ar
   85  firenfulle menn      fyrn geworhton, thonne wile dryhten sylf      dada gehyran, at ealra monna gehwam      muthes reorde wunde witherlean.      Ac hwat wilt thu thar on domdage      dryhtne secgan?
   90  thonne ne bith nanig to thas lytel lith      on lime geweaxen, that thu ne shyle for aghwylc      anra onsundran ryht agieldan,      thonne rethe bith dryhten at dome.      Ac hwat do wit unc, thonne he unc hafath geedbyrded      othre sithe?
   95  Shulon wit thonne atsomne      siththan brucan swylcra yrmtha,      swa thu unc ar scrife." Firenath thus that flashord,      sheal thonne feran on weg, secan helle grund,      nales heofondreamas, dadum gedrefed.      Ligeth dust thar hit was,
  100  ne mag him ondsware      anige secgan, ne thar edringe      ange gehatan gaste geomrum,      geoce oththe frofre. Bith that heafod tohliden,      honda tohleothode, geaflas toginene,      goman toslitene,
  105  seonwe beoth asogene,      sweora bicowen; rib reafiath      rethe wyrmas, drincath hlothum hra,      heolfres thurstge. Bith seo tunge totogen      on tyn healfe hungrum to hrothor.      Forthon heo ne mag horshlice
  110  wordum wrixlan      with thone wergan gast. Gifer hatte se wyrm,      tham tha geaflas beoth nadle sharpran.      Se genetheth to arest ealra      on tham eorthshrafe; he tha tungan totyhth      ond tha tothas thurhsmyhth,
  115  ond to aetwelan      othrum gerymeth, ond tha eagan thurhitheth      ufon on that heafod wyrmum to wiste,      thonne bith that werge lic acolad      that he longe ar werede mid wadum.      Bith thonne wyrmes giefl,
  120  at on eorthan.      that mag aghwylcum men to gemyndum      modsnotterra.
