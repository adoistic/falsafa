Me lifes onlah     se this leoht onwrah,
ond that torhte yeteoh,     tilliche onwrah.
Glad was ic gliwum,     glenged hiwum,
blissa bleoum,     blostma hiwum.
Secgas mec segon,     symbel ne alegon,
feoh-yiefe yefegon;     fratwed wagon
wigg ofer wongum     wennan gongum,
lisse mid longum     leoma yehongum.
tha was wastmum aweaht,     world onspreht,
under roderum areaht,     radmagne ofer-theaht.
Yiestas yengdon,     yershype mengdon,
lisse lengdon,     lustum glengdon.
Shrifen shrad glad     thurh yeshad in brad,
was on lagustreame lad,     thar me leothu ne biglad.
Hafde ic heanne had,     ne was me in healle gad,
that thar rof weord rad.     Oft thar rinc yebad,
that he in sele sage     sinc-yewage,
thegnum yethyhte.     thenden was me magen,
horshe mec heredon,     hilde yeneredon,
fagre feredon,     feondon biweredon.
Swa mec hyht-yiefu heold,     hygedryht befeold,
stathol-ahtum steold, stepegongum weold
swylce eorthe ol,     ahte ic ealdorstol,
galdorwordum gol. Gomen sibbe ne ofoll,
ac was yefest gear,     gellende sner,
wuniendo war     wilbec beshar.
Shalkas waron sharpe,     shyl was hearpe,
hlude hlynede,     hleothor dynede,
sweglrad swinsade,     swithe ne minsade.
Burgsele beofode,     beorht hlifade,
ellen eacnade,     ead beacnade,
freaum frodade,     fromum godade,
mod magnade,     mine fagnade,
treow telgade,     tir welgade,
blad blissade, gold yearwade,
gim hwearfade,     sinc searwade,
sib nearwade. From ic was in fratwum,
freolic in yeatwum;     was min dream dryhtlic,
drohtath hyhtlic.
Foldan ic freothode,     folcum ic leothode,
lif was min longe,     leodum in yemonge,
tirum yetonge,     teala yehonge.
Nu min hrether is hreoh, heofsithum sheoh,
nydbysgum neah;     yewiteth nihtes in fleah
se ar in dage was dyre.     Shritheth nu deop in feore
brondhord yeblowen,     breostum in forgrowen,
flyhtum toflowen.     Flah is yeblowen
miclum in yemynde;     modes yecynde
greteth ungrynde     grorn efenpynde,
bealofus byrneth,     bittre toyrneth.
Werig winneth,     widsith onginnet,
sar ne sinnith,     sorgum cinnith,
blad his blinnith,     blisse linnith,
listum linneth,     lustum ne tinneth.
Dreamas swa her yedreosath,     dryhtshype yehreosath,
lif her men forleosath,     leahtras oft yeceosath;
treow-thrag is to trag,     seo untrume yenag,
steapum eatole mis-thah,     ond eal stund yenag.
Swa nu world wendeth,     wyrde sendeth,
ond hetes henteth,     halethe shyndeth.
Wercyn yewiteth,     walgar sliteth,
flahmah fliteth,     flan mon hwiteth,
borgsorg biteth,     bald ald thwiteth,
wrac-fac writhath,     wrath ath smiteth,
singryn sidath, searofearo glideth,
gromtorn grafeth,     graft hafath,
searohwit solath,     sumurhat colath,
foldwela falleth,     feondshipe walleth,
eorth-magen ealdath,     ellen colath.
Me that wyrd yewaf,     ond yewyrht foryeaf,
that ic grofe graf,     ond that grimme graf
flean flashe ne mag,     thonne flanhred dag
nydgrapum nimeth,     thonne seo neaht becymeth
seo me etheles ofonn ond mec her eardes onconn.
thonne lichoma ligeth,     lima wyrm friteth,
ac him wenne yewigeth     ond tha wist yethygeth,
oth-that beoth tha ban     an, ond at nyhstan nan
nefne se neda tan balawun her yehloten.
Ne bith se hlisa adroren.
ar that eadig yethenceth,     he hine the oftor swenceth,
byrgeth him tha bitran synne,     hogath to thare betran wynne,
yemon mortha lisse, thar sindon miltsa blisse
hyhtlice in heofona riche.     Uton nu halgum yeliche
shyldum bishyrede     shyndan yenerede,
wommum biwerede,     wuldre yenerede,
thar moncyn mot     for meotude rot
sothne god yeseon,     ond aa in sibbe yefean.
