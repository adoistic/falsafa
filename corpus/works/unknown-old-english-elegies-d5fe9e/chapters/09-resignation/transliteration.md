Resignation
===========

Verse Indeterminate Saxon

Source: https://sacred-texts.com/neu/ascp/a03_25.htm
Lines: 24

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Age mec se aelmihta god, helpe min se halga dryhten!     thu gesceope heofon ond eorthan ond wundor eall,     min wundorcyning, the thaer on sindon,     ece dryhten,
   micel ond manigfeald.     Ic the, maere god, mine sawle bebeode     ond mines sylfes lic, ond min word ond min weorc,     witig dryhten, ond eal min leotho,     leohtes hyrde, ond tha manigfealdan     mine gethohtas.
   Getacna me,     tungla hyrde, thaer selast sy     sawle minre to gemearcenne     meotudes willan, thaet ic the getheo     thinga gehwylce, ond on me sylfum,     sothfaest cyning,
   raed araere.     Regntheof ne laet on sceade sceppan,     theah the ic scyppendum wuldorcyninge     waccor hyrde, ricum dryhtne,     thonne min raed waere. Forgif me to lisse,     lifgende god,
   bitre bealodaede.     Ic tha bote gemon, cyninga wuldor,     cume to, gif ic mot. Forgif thu me, min frea,     fierst ond ondgiet ond gethyld ond gemynd     thinga gehwylces thara thu me, sothfaest cyning,     sendan wylle
   to cunnunge.     Nu thu const on mec firendaeda fela,     feorma mec hwaethre, meotod, for thinre miltse,     theah aic ma fremede grimra gylta     thonne me god lyfde; haebbe ic thonne thearfe     thaet ic thine setheah,
   halges heofoncyninges,     hyldo getilge leorendum dagum,     lif aefter othrum geseo ond gesece,     thaet me siththan thaer unne arfaest god     ecan dreames, lif alyfe,     theah the laetlicor
   bette bealodaede     thonne bibodu waeron halgan heofonmaegnes.     Hwaet, thu me her fela ...... forgeafe.     Gesette minne hyht on thec, forhte forethoncas,     thaet hio faestlice stonde gestathelad.     Onstep minne hige,
   gaesta god cyning,     in gearone raed. Nu ic fundige to the,     faeder moncynnes, of thisse worulde,     nu ic wat thaet ic sceal, ful unfyr faca;     feorma me thonne, wyrda waldend,     in thinne wuldordream,
   ond mec geleoran laet,     leofra dryhten, geoca mines gaestes.     thonne is gromra to fela aefestum eaden,     haebbe ic thonne aet frean frofre,     theah the ic aer on fyrste lyt earnode arna.     Forlaet mec englas setheah
   geniman on thinne neawest,     nergende cyning, meotod, for thinre miltse.     theah the ic mana fela aefter dogrum dyde,     ne laet thu mec naefre deofol setheah thin lim laedan     on lathne sith, thy laes hi on thone forethonc     gefeon motan
   thy the hy him sylfum     sellan thuhten englas oferhydige     thonne ece Crist. Gelugon hy him aet tham geleafan;     forthon hy longe scul..., werge wihta,     wraece throwian. Forstond thu mec ond gestyr him,     thonne storm cyme
   minum gaeste ongegn;     geoca thonne, mihtig dryhten,     minre sawle, gefreotha hyre ond gefeorma hy,     faeder moncynnes, haedre gehogode,     hael, ece god, meotod meahtum swith.     Min is nu tha
   sefa synnum fah,     ond ic ymb sawle eom feam sithum forht,     theah thu me fela sealde arna on thisse eorthan.     the sie ealles thonc meorda ond miltsa,     thara thu me sealdest. No thaes earninga     aenige waeron mid;
   hwaethre ic me ealles thaes     ellen wylle habban ond hlyhhan     ond me hyhtan to, fraetwian mec on ferthweg     ond fundian sylf to tham sithe     the ic asettan sceal, gaest gearwian,     ond me thaet eal for gode tholian
   blithe mode,     nu ic gebunden eom faeste in minum ferthe.     Huru me frea witeteth sume thara synna     the ic me sylf ne conn ongietan gleawlice.     Gode ic haebbe abolgen, brego moncynnes;     forthon ic thus bittre wearth
   gewitnad for thisse worulde,     swa min gewyrhto waeron micle fore monnum,     thaet ic martirdom deopne adreoge.     Ne eom ic dema gleaw, wis fore weorude;     forthon ic thas word spraece fus on ferthe,     swa me on frymthe gelomp
   yrmthu ofer eorthan,     thaet ic a tholade geara gehwylce     (gode ealles thonc!) modearfotha     ma thonne on othrum, fyrhto in folce;     forthon ic afysed eom earm of minum ethle.     Ne maeg thaes anhoga,
   leodwynna leas,     leng drohtian, wineleas wraecca,     (is him wrath meotud), gnorath on his geoguthe, ond him aelce maele     men fullestath, ycath his yrmthu,     ond he thaet eal tholath,
   sarcwide secga,     ond him bith a sefa geomor, mod morgenseoc.     Ic bi me tylgust secge this sarspel     ond ymb sith spraece, longunge fus,     ond on lagu thence, nat min
   hwy ic gebycge     bat on saewe, fleot on farothe;     nah ic fela goldes ne huru thaes freondes,     the me gefylste to tham sithfate,     nu ic me sylf ne maeg fore minum wonaehtum     willan adreogan.
   Wudu mot him weaxan,     wyrde bidan, tanum laedan;     ic for taele ne maeg aenigne moncynnes     mode gelufian eorl on ethle.     Eala dryhten min, meahtig mundbora!     thaet ic eom mode seoc,
   bittre abolgen,     is seo bot aet the gelong aefter life. Ic on leohte ne maeg butan earfothum     aenge thinga feasceaft haele     foldan ...unian; thonne ic me to fremthum     freode haefde,
   cyththu gecwe...     me waes a cearu symle lufena to leane,     swa ic alifde nu. Giet bith thaet selast, thonne mon him sylf ne maeg wyrd onwendan,     thaet he thonne wel tholige.
