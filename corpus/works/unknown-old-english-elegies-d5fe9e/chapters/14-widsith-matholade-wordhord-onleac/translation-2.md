Widsith matholade,     wordhord onleac,
se the monna maest maegtha ofer eorthan,
folca geondferde;     oft he on flette gethah
mynelicne maththum. Him from Myrgingum
   aethele onwocon.     He mid Ealhhilde,
faelre freothuwebban,     forman sithe
Hrethcyninges     ham gesohte
eastan of Ongle,     Eormanrices,
wrathes waerlogan.     Ongon tha worn sprecan:
  "Fela ic monna gefraegn     maegthum wealdan!
Sceal theodna gehwylc     theawum lifgan,
eorl aefter othrum     ethle raedan,
se the his theodenstol     getheon wile.
thara waes Hwala hwile selast,
  ond Alexandreas     ealra ricost
monna cynnes,     ond he maest gethah
thara the ic ofer foldan     gefraegen haebbe.
aetla weold Hunum,     Eormanric Gotum,
Becca Baningum,     Burgendum Gifica.
  Casere weold Creacum     ond Celic Finnum,
Hagena Holmrygum ond Heoden Glommum.
Witta weold Swaefum,     Wada Haelsingum,
Meaca Myrgingum,     Mearchealf Hundingum.
theodric weold Froncum,     thyle Rondingum,
  Breoca Brondingum,     Billing Wernum.
Oswine weold Eowum     ond Ytum Gefwulf,
Fin Folcwalding     Fresna cynne.
Sigehere lengest     Saedenum weold,
Hnaef Hocingum,     Helm Wulfingum,
  Wald Woingum,     Wod thyringum,
Saeferth Sycgum,     Sweom Ongendtheow,
Sceafthere Ymbrum,     Sceafa Longbeardum,
Hun Haetwerum     ond Holen Wrosnum.
Hringweald waes haten     Herefarena cyning.
  Offa weold Ongle,     Alewih Denum;
se waes thara manna     modgast ealra,
no hwaethre he ofer Offan     eorlscype fremede,
ac Offa geslog     aerest monna,
cnihtwesende,     cynerica maest.
  Naenig efeneald him     eorlscipe maran
on orette.     Ane sweorde
merce gemaerde     with Myrgingum
bi Fifeldore;     heoldon forth siththan
Engle ond Swaefe,     swa hit Offa geslog.
  Hrothwulf ond Hrothgar     heoldon lengest
sibbe aetsomne     suhtorfaedran,
siththan hy forwraecon     wicinga cynn
ond Ingeldes     ord forbigdan,
forheowan aet Heorote     Heathobeardna thrym.
  Swa ic geondferde fela     fremdra londa
geond ginne grund.     Godes ond yfles
thaer ic cunnade     cnosle bidaeled,
freomaegum feor     folgade wide.
Forthon ic maeg singan     ond secgan spell,
  maenan fore mengo     in meoduhealle
hu me cynegode     cystum dohten.
Ic waes mid Hunum     ond mid Hrethgotum,
mid Sweom ond mid Geatum     ond mid Suthdenum.
Mid Wenlum ic waes ond mid Waernum     ond mid wicingum.
  Mid Gefthum ic waes ond mid Winedum     ond mid Gefflegum.
Mid Englum ic waes ond mid Swaefum     ond mid aenenum.
Mid Seaxum ic waes ond Sycgum     ond mid Sweordwerum.
Mid Hronum ic waes ond mid Deanum     ond mid Heathoreamum.
Mid thyringum ic waes     ond mid throwendum,
  ond mid Burgendum,     thaer ic beag gethah;
me thaer Guthhere forgeaf     glaedlicne maththum
songes to leane.     Naes thaet saene cyning!
Mid Froncum ic waes ond mid Frysum     ond mid Frumtingum.
Mid Rugum ic waes ond mid Glommum     ond mid Rumwalum.
  Swylce ic waes on Eatule     mid aelfwine,
se haefde moncynnes,     mine gefraege,
leohteste hond     lofes to wyrcenne,
heortan unhneaweste     hringa gedales,
beorhtra beaga,     bearn Eadwines.
  Mid Sercingum ic waes     ond mid Seringum;
mid Creacum ic waes ond mid Finnum     ond mid Casere,
se the winburga     geweald ahte,
wiolena ond wilna,     ond Wala rices.
Mid Scottum ic waes ond mid Peohtum     ond mid Scridefinnum;
  mid Lidwicingum ic waes ond mid Leonum     ond mid Longbeardum,
mid haethnum ond mid haelethum     ond mid Hundingum.
Mid Israhelum ic waes     ond mid Exsyringum,
mid Ebreum ond mid Indeum     ond mid Egyptum.
Mid Moidum ic waes ond mid Persum     ond mid Myrgingum,
  ond Mofdingum     ond ongend Myrgingum,
ond mid Amothingum.     Mid Eastthyringum ic waes
ond mid Eolum ond mid Istum     ond Idumingum.
Ond ic waes mid Eormanrice     ealle thrage,
thaer me Gotena cyning     gode dohte;
  se me beag forgeaf,     burgwarena fruma,
on tham siex hund waes     smaetes goldes,
gescyred sceatta     scillingrime;
thone ic Eadgilse     on aeht sealde,
minum hleodryhtne,     tha ic to ham bicwom,
  leofum to leane,     thaes the he me lond forgeaf,
mines faeder ethel,     frea Myrginga.
Ond me tha Ealhhild     otherne forgeaf,
dryhtcwen duguthe,     dohtor Eadwines.
Hyre lof lengde     geond londa fela,
  thonne ic be songe     secgan sceolde
hwaer ic under swegle selast wisse
goldhrodene cwen     giefe bryttian.
thonne wit Scilling     sciran reorde
for uncrum sigedryhtne     song ahofan,
  hlude bi hearpan     hleothor swinsade,
thonne monige men,     modum wlonce,
wordum sprecan,     tha the wel cuthan,
thaet hi naefre song     sellan ne hyrdon.
thonan ic ealne geondhwearf     ethel Gotena,
  sohte ic a gesitha tha selestan;
thaet waes innweorud     Earmanrices.
Hethcan sohte ic ond Beadecan     ond Herelingas,
Emercan sohte ic ond Fridlan     ond Eastgotan,
frodne ond godne     faeder Unwenes.
  Seccan sohte ic ond Beccan,     Seafolan ond theodric,
Heathoric ond Sifecan,     Hlithe ond Incgentheow.
Eadwine sohte ic ond Elsan,     aegelmund ond Hungar,
ond tha wloncan gedryht     Withmyrginga.
Wulfhere sohte ic ond Wyrmhere;     ful oft thaer wig ne alaeg,
  thonne Hraeda here     heardum sweordum
ymb Wistlawudu     wergan sceoldon
ealdne ethelstol     aetlan leodum.
Raedhere sohte ic ond Rondhere,     Rumstan ond Gislhere,
Withergield ond Freotheric,     Wudgan ond Haman;
  ne waeran thaet gesitha     tha saemestan,
theah the ic hy anihst     nemnan sceolde.
Ful oft of tham heape     hwinende fleag
giellende gar     on grome theode;
wraeccan thaer weoldan     wundnan golde
  werum ond wifum,     Wudga ond Hama.
Swa ic thaet symle onfond     on thaere feringe,
thaet se bith leofast     londbuendum
se the him god syleth     gumena rice
to gehealdenne,     thenden he her leofath."
  Swa scrithende     gesceapum hweorfath
gleomen gumena     geond grunda fela,
thearfe secgath,     thoncword sprecath,
simle suth oththe north     sumne gemetath
gydda gleawne,     geofum unhneawne,
  se the fore duguthe wile     dom araeran,
eorlscipe aefnan,     oththeat eal scaeceth,
leoht ond lif somod;     lof se gewyrceth,
hafath under heofonum     heahfaestne dom.
