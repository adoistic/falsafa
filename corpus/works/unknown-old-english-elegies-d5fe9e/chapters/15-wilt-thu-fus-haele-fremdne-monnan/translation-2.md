Wilt thu, fus haele,     fremdne monnan,
wisne wothboran     wordum gretan,
fricgan felageongne     ymb forthgesceaft,
biddan a gesecge     sidra gesceafta
  craeftas cyndelice     cwichrerende,
tha a dogra gehwam     thurh dom godes
bringe wundra fela     wera cneorissum!
Is thara anra gehwam     orgeate tacen,
tham thurh wisdom     woruld ealle con
  behabban on hrethre,     hycgende mon,
thaet geara iu,     gliwes craefte,
mid gieddingum     guman oft wrecan,
rincas raedfaeste;     cuthon ryht sprecan,
thaet a fricgende     fira cynnes
  ond secgende     searoruna gespon
a gemyndge maest     monna wiston.
Forthon scyle ascian,     se the on elne leofath,
deophydig mon,     dygelra gesceafta,
bewritan in gewitte     wordhordes craeft,
  faestnian ferthsefan,     thencan forth teala;
ne sceal thaes athreotan     thegn modigne,
thaet he wislice     woruld fulgonge.
Leorna thas lare.     Ic the lungre sceal
meotudes maegensped     maran gesecgan,
  thonne thu hygecraeftig     in hrethre maege
mode gegripan.     Is sin meaht forswith.
Nis thaet monnes gemet     moldhrerendra,
thaet he maege in hrethre     his heah geweorc
furthor aspyrgan thonne him frea sylle
  to ongietanne     godes agen bibod;
ac we sculon thoncian     theodne maerum
awa to ealdre,     thaes the us se eca cyning
on gaeste wlite     forgiefan wille
thaet we eathe magon     upcund rice
  forth gestigan,     gif us on ferthe geneah
ond we willath healdan     heofoncyninges bibod.
Gehyr nu this herespel     ond thinne hyge gefaestna.
Hwaet, on frymthe gescop     faeder aelmihtig,
heah hordes weard,     heofon ond eorthan,
  saes sidne grund,     sweotule gesceafte,
tha nu in tham thream     thurh theodnes hond
heath ond hebbath     thone halgan blaed.
Forthon eal swa teofanade,     se the teala cuthe,
aeghwylc with othrum;     sceoldon eal beran
  stithe stefnbyrd,     swa him se steora bibead
missenlice gemetu     thurh tha miclan gecynd.
Swa hi to worulde     wlite forth berath
dryhtnes duguthe     ond his daeda thrym,
lixende lof     in tha longan tid,
  fremmath faestlice     frean ece word
in tham frumstole     the him frea sette,
hluttor heofones weard,     healdath georne
mere gemaere;     meaht forth tihth
heofoncondelle     ond holmas mid,
  lathath ond laedeth     lifes agend
in his anes faethm     ealle gesceafta.
Swa him wideferh     wuldor stondeth,
ealra demena     tham gedefestan,
the us this lif gescop,     ond this leohte beorht
  cymeth morgna gehwam     ofer misthleothu
wadan ofer waegas     wundrum gegierwed,
ond mid aerdaege     eastan snoweth
wlitig ond wynsum     wera cneorissum;
lifgendra gehwam     leoht forth biereth
  bronda beorhtost,     ond his brucan mot
aeghwylc on eorthan,     the him eagna gesihth
sigora sothcyning     syllan wolde.
Gewiteth thonne mith thy wuldre     on westrodor
forthmære tungol     faran on heape,
  oththaet on aefenne     ut garsecges
grundas paetheth,     glom other cigth;
niht aefter cymeth,     healdeth nydbibod
halgan dryhtnes.     Heofontorht swegl
scir gescyndeth     in gesceaft godes
  under foldan faethm,     farende tungol.
Forthon naenig fira     thaes frod leofath
thaet his maege aespringe     thurh his aegne sped
witan, hu geond grund faereth     goldtorht sunne
in thaet wonne genip     under waetra gethring,
  oththe hwa thes leohtes     londbuende
brucan mote,     siththan heo ofer brim hweorfeth.
Forthon swa teofenede,     se the teala cuthe,
daeg with nihte,     deop with hean,
lyft with lagustream,     lond with waege,
  flod with flode,     fisc with ythum.
Ne waciath thas geweorc,     ac he hi wel healdeth;
stondath stithlice     bestrythed faeste
miclum meahtlocum     in tham maegenthrymme
mid tham sy ahefed     heofon ond eorthe.
  Beoth thonne eadge     tha thaer in wuniath,
hyhtlic is thaet heorthwerud. thaet is herga maest,
eadigra unrim,     engla threatas.
Hy geseoth symle     hyra sylfra cyning,
eagum on wlitath,     habbath aeghwaes genoh.
  Nis him wihte won,     tham the wuldres cyning
geseoth in swegle;     him is symbel ond dream
ece unhwylen     eadgum to frofre.
Forthon scyle mon gehycgan     thaet he meotude hyre;
aeghwylc aelda bearna     forlaete idle lustas,
  laene lifes wynne,     fundige him to lissa blisse,
forlaete hetenitha     gehwone sigan mid synna fyrnum,     fere him to tham sellan rice.
