thus frod faeder     freobearn laerde,
modsnottor mon, maga cystum eald,
wordum wisfaestum,     thaet he wel thunge:
"Do a thaette duge,     deag thin gewyrhtu;
god the bith symle     goda gehwylces
frea ond fultum,     feond tham othrum
wyrsan gewyrhta.     Wene thec thy betran,
efn elne this     a thenden thu lifge.
Faeder ond modor     freo thu mid heortan,
maga gehwylcne,     gif him sy meotud on lufan.
Wes thu thinum yldrum     arfaest symle,
faegerwyrde,     ond the in ferthe laet
thine lareowas     leofe in mode,
tha thec geornast     to gode trymmen."
Faeder eft his sunu     frod gegrette
othre sithe:     "Heald elne this!
Ne freme firene,     ne naefre freonde thinum,
maege man ne gethafa,     thy laes thec meotud oncunne,
thaet thu sy wommes gewita.     He the mid wite gieldeth,
swylce tham othrum     mid eadwelan."
thriddan sythe     thoncsnottor guma
breostgehygdum     his bearn laerde:
"Ne gewuna wyrsa,     widan feore,
aengum eahta,     ac thu the anne genim
to gesprecan symle     spella ond lara
raedhycgende.     Sy ymb rice swa hit maege."
Feorthan sithe     faeder eft laerde
modleofne magan,     thaet he gemunde this:
"Ne aswic sundorwine,     ac a symle geheald
ryhtum gerisnum.     Raefn elne this,
thaet thu naefre faecne weorthe freonde thinum."
Fiftan sithe     faeder eft ongon
breostgethoncum     his bearn laeran:
"Druncen beorg the     ond dollic word,
man on mode     ond in muthe lyge,
yrre ond aefeste     ond idese lufan.
Forthon sceal aewiscmod     oft sithian,
se the gewiteth     in wifes lufan,
fremdre meowlan.     thaer bith a firena wen,
lathlicre scome,     long nith with god,
geotende gielp.     Wes thu a giedda wis,
waer with willan,     worda hyrde."
Siextan sithe     swaes eft ongon
thurh blithne gethoht     his bearn laeran:
"Ongiet georne     hwaet sy god oththe yfel,
ond toscead simle     scearpe mode
in sefan thinum     ond the a thaet selle geceos.
A the bith gedaele;     gif the deah hyge,
wunath wisdom in,     ond thu wast geare
ondgit yfles,     heald the elne with,
feorma thu symle     in thinum ferthe god."
Seofethan sithe     his sunu laerde
faeder, frod guma,     saegde fela geongum:
"Seldan snottor guma     sorgleas blissath,
swylce dol seldon     drymeth sorgful
ymb his forthgesceaft,     nefne he faehthe wite.
Waerwyrde sceal     wisfaest haele
breostum hycgan,     nales breahtme hlud."
Eahtothan sithe     eald faeder ongon
his mago monian     mildum wordum:
"Leorna lare     laergedefe,
wene thec in wisdom,     weoruda scyppend
hafa the to hyhte,     haligra gemynd,
ond a soth to syge,     thonne thu secge hwaet."
Nigethan sithe     naegde se gomola,
eald uthwita     saegde eaforan worn:
"Nis nu fela folca     thaette fyrngewritu
healdan wille,     ac him hyge brosnath,
ellen colath,     idlath theodscype;
ne habbath wiht for thaet,     theah hi wom don
ofer meotudes bibod.     Monig sceal ongieldan
sawelsusles.     Ac laet thinne sefan healdan
forth fyrngewritu ond frean domas,
tha the her on maegthe gehwaere     men forlaetath
swither asigan,     thonne him sy sylfum ryht."
Teothan sithe     tornsorgna ful,
eald eft ongon     eaforan laeran:
"Snyttra bruceth     the fore sawle lufan
warnath him wommas     worda ond daeda
on sefan symle     ond soth fremeth;
bith him geofona gehwylc     gode geyced,
meahtum spedig,     thonne he mon flyhth.
Yrre ne laet the     aefre gewealdan,
heah in hrethre,     heoroworda grund
wylme bismitan,     ac him warnath thaet
on geheortum hyge.     Haele sceal wisfaest
ond gemetlice,     modes snottor,
gleaw in gehygdum,     georn wisdomes,
swa he with aelda maeg     eades hleotan.
Ne beo thu no to taelende,     ne to tweospraece,
ne the on mode laet     men to fracothe,
ac beo leofwende,     leoht on gehygdum
ber breostcofan.     Swa thu, min bearn, gemyne
frode faeder lare     ond thec a with firenum geheald."
