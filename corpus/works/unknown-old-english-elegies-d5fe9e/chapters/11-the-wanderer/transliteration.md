The Wanderer
============

Verse Indeterminate Saxon

Source: https://sacred-texts.com/neu/ascp/a03_06.htm
Lines: 24

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Oft him anhaga      are gebideth,
 metudes miltse,      theah the he modchearig
 geond lagulade      longe sholde
 hreran mid hondum      hrimkalde sae,
    5  wadan wraeclastas.      Wyrd bith ful ared!
 Swa cwaeth eardstapa,      earfetha gemyndig,
 wrathra waelsleahta,      winemaega hryre:
 "Oft ic sholde ana      uhtna gehwylce
 mine cheare cwithan.      Nis nu kwikra nan
   10  the ic him modsefan      minne durre
 sweotule asejjan.      Ic to sothe wat
 thaet bith in eorle      indryhten theaw,
 thaet he his ferthlocan      faeste binde,
 healde his hordcofan,      hyjje swa he wille.
   15  Ne maeg werig mod      wyrde withstondan,
 ne se hreo hyge      helpe gefremman.
 Forthon domgeorne      dreorigne oft
 in hyra breostcofan      bindath faeste;
 swa ic modsefan      minne sholde,
   20  oft earmchearig,      ethle bidaeled,
 freomaegum feor      feterum saelan,
 siththan geara iu      goldwine minne
 hrusan heolstre biwrah,      ond ic hean thonan
 wod winterchearig      ofer wathema gebind,
   25  sohte sele dreorig      sinces bryttan,
 hwaer ic feor oththe neah      findan meahte
 thone the in meduhealle      min mine wisse,
 oththe mec freondleasne      frefran wolde,
 weman mid wynnum.      Wat se the cunnath,
   30  hu slithen bith      sorg to geferan,
 tham the him lyt hafath      leofra geholena.
 Warath hine wraeclast,      nales wunden gold,
 ferthloca freorig,      nalaes foldan blaed.
 Gemon he selesejjas      ond sincthege,
   35  hu hine on geoguthe      his goldwine
 wenede to wiste.      Wyn eal gedreas!
 Forthon wat se the sceal      his winedryhtnes
 leofes larcwidum      longe fortholian,
 thonne sorg ond slaep      somod aetgaedre
   40  earmne anhogan      oft gebindath.
 thinceth him on mode      thaet he his mondryhten
 clyppe ond cysse,      ond on cneo lejje
 honda ond heafod,      swa he hwilum aer
 in geardagum      giefstolas breac.
   45  thonne onwaecneth eft      wineleas guma,
 gesihth him biforan      fealwe wegas,
 bathian brimfuglas,      braedan fethra,
 hreosan hrim ond snaw,      hagle gemenged.
 thonne beoth thy hefigran      heortan benne,
   50  sare aefter swaesne.      Sorg bith geniwad,
 thonne maga gemynd      mod geondhweorfeth;
 greteth gliwstafum,      georne geondsceawath
 secga geseldan.      Swimmath eft on weg!
 Fleotendra ferth      no thaer fela bringeth
   55  cuthra cwidegiedda.      Cearo bith geniwad
 tham the sendan sceal      swithe geneahhe
 ofer wathema gebind      werigne sefan.
 Forthon ic gethencan ne maeg      geond thas woruld
 for hwan modsefa min ne gesweorce,
   60  thonne ic eorla lif      eal geondthence,
 hu hi faerlice      flet ofgeafon,
 modge maguthegnas.      Swa thes middangeard
 ealra dogra gehwam      dreoseth ond falleth,
 forthon ne maeg weorthan wis      wer, aer he age
   65  wintra dael in woruldrice.      Wita sceal gethyldig,
 ne sceal no to hatheort      ne to hraedwyrde,
 ne to wac wiga      ne to wanhydig,
 ne to forht ne to faegen,      ne to feohgifre
 ne naefre gielpes to georn,      aer he geare cunne.
   70  Beorn sceal gebidan,      thonne he beot spriceth,
 oththaet collenferth      cunne gearwe
 hwider hrethra gehygd      hweorfan wille.
 Ongietan sceal gleaw haele      hu gaestlic bith,
 thonne ealre thisse worulde wela      weste stondeth,
   75  swa nu missenlice      geond thisne middangeard
 winde biwaune      weallas stondath,
 hrime bihrorene,      hrythge tha ederas.
 Woriath tha winsalo,      waldend licgath
 dreame bidrorene,      duguth eal gecrong,
   80  wlonc bi wealle.      Sume wig fornom,
 ferede in forthwege,      sumne fugel othbaer
 ofer heanne holm,      sumne se hara wulf
 deathe gedaelede,      sumne dreorighleor
 in eorthscraefe      eorl gehydde.
   85  Ythde swa thisne eardgeard      aelda scyppend
 oththaet burgwara      breahtma lease
 eald enta geweorc      idlu stodon.
 Se thonne thisne wealsteal      wise gethohte
 ond this deorce lif      deope geondthenceth,
   90  frod in ferthe,      feor oft gemon
 waelsleahta worn,      ond thas word acwith:
 "Hwaer cwom mearg? Hwaer cwom mago?      Hwaer cwom maththumgyfa?
 Hwaer cwom symbla gesetu?      Hwaer sindon seledreamas?
 Eala beorht bune!      Eala byrnwiga!
   95  Eala theodnes thrym!      Hu seo thrag gewat,
 genap under nihthelm,      swa heo no waere.
 Stondeth nu on laste      leofre duguthe
 weal wundrum heah,      wyrmlicum fah.
 Eorlas fornoman      asca thrythe,
  100  waepen waelgifru,      wyrd seo maere,
 ond thas stanhleothu      stormas cnyssath,
 hrith hreosende hrusan bindeth,
 wintres woma,      thonne won cymeth,
 nipeth nihtscua,      northan onsendeth
  105  hreo haeglfare      haelethum on andan.
 Eall is earfothlic      eorthan rice,
 onwendeth wyrda gesceaft      weoruld under heofonum.
 Her bith feoh laene,      her bith freond laene,
 her bith mon laene,      her bith maeg laene,
  110  eal this eorthan gesteal      idel weortheth!"
 Swa cwaeth snottor on mode,      gesaet him sundor aet rune.
 Til bith se the his treowe gehealdeth,      ne sceal naefre his torn to rycene
 beorn of his breostum acythan,      nemthe he aer tha bote cunne,
 eorl mid elne gefremman.      Wel bith tham the him are secheth,
  115  frofre to faeder on heofonum,      thaer us eal seo faestnung stondeth.
