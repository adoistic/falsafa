Leodum is minum    swylce him mon lac gife;
willath hy hine athecgan,    gif he on threat cymeth.
Ungelic is us.
Wulf is on iege,    ic on otherre.
Faest is thaet eglond,    fenne biworpen.
Sindon waelreowe    weras thaer on ige;
willath hy hine athecgan,    gif he on threat cymeth.
Ungelice is us.
Wulfes ic mines widlastum    wenum dogode;
thonne hit waes renig weder    ond ic reotugu saet,
thonne mec se beaducafa    bogum bilegde,
waes me wyn to thon,    waes me hwaethre eac lath.
Wulf, min Wulf,    wena me thine
seoce gedydon,    thine seldcymas,
murnende mod,    nales meteliste.
Gehyrest thu, Eadwacer?    Uncerne earne hwelp
bireth wulf to wuda.
thaet mon eathe tosliteth    thaette naefre gesomnad waes,
uncer giedd geador.
