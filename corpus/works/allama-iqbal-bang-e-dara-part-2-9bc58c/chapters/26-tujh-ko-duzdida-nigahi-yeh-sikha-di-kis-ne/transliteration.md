Tujh ko duzdida nigahi yeh sikha di kis ne
Ramz-e-aaghaz-e-mohabbat ki bata di kis ne
Har adaa se teri paida hai mohabbat kaisi
Neeli aankhon se tapakti hai zakawat kaisi
Dekhti hai kabhi un ko, kabhi sharmati hai
Kabhi uthti hai, kabhi let ke so jaati hai
Aankh teri sifat-e-aaina hairaan hai kya
Noor-e-aagahi se roshan teri pehchaan hai kya
Maarti hai unhein pahonchon se, ajab naaz hai yeh
Chher hai, ghussa hai ya pyaar ka andaaz hai yeh?
Shokh tu hogi to godi se utarenge tujhe
Gir gaya phool jo seene ka to maarenge tujhe
Kya tajassus hai tujhe, kis ki tamannai hai
Aah! Kya tu bhi usi cheez ki saudai hai
Khaas insaan se kuch husn ka ehsaas nahin
Soorat-e-dil hai yeh har cheez ke baatin mein makeen
Sheesha-e-dehr mein manind-e-maye-naab hai ishq
Rooh-e-khursheed hai, khoon-e-rag-e-mehtaab hai ishq
Dil har zarre mein posheeda kasak hai is ki
Noor yeh woh hai ke har shai mein jhalak hai is ki
Kahin saaman-e-musarrat, kahin saaz-e-gham hai
Kahin gohar hai, kahin ashk, kahin shabnam hai
