Visal
(Visal)

Justaju
Jis gul ki tadpati thi ae bulbul mujhe
Khoobi
Qismat se aakhir mil gaya woh gul mujhe
Khud
Tadpata tha, chaman waalon ko tadpata tha main
Tujh
Ko jab rangeen nawa paata tha, sharmata tha main
Mere
Pehlu mein dil-e-muztar na tha, seemab tha
Irtikaab
Jurm-e-ulfat ke liye be-taab tha
Na-muradi
Mehfil-e-gul mein meri mash'hoor thi
Subah
Meri aainadaar-e-shab-e-daijoor thi
Az
Nafs dar seena-e-khoon gashta nishtar dashtam
Zer
Khamoshi nihaan ghogha-e-mehshar dashtam
Ab
Ta'assur ke jahan mein woh pareshani nahin
Ahl
Gulshan par giraan meri ghazal khwani nahin
Ishq
Ki garmi se sholay ban gaye chhaale mere
Khulte
Hain bijliyon ke saath ab naale mere
Ghaza-e
Ulfat se yeh khaak-e-siyah aaina hai
Aur
Aaine mein aks-e-hamdam-e-dereena hai
Qaid
Mein aaya to haasil mujh ko azaadi hui
Dil
Ke lutt jaane se mere ghar ki aabadi hui
Zau
Se us khursheed ki akhtar mera taabinda hai
Chandni
Jis ke ghubaar-e-raah se sharminda hai
Yak
Nazar kardi o aadab-e-fana aamokhti
Ae
Khunak rozay ke khashaak mara wa-sokhti
