Yoon to ae bazm-e-jahan! dilkash thay hungamay teray

Yoon
to ae bazm-e-jahan! dilkash thay hungamay teray
Ik
zara afsurdagi teray tamashaon mein thi
Pa
gayi aasoodgi koo-e-mohabbat mein woh khaak
Muddaton
awara jo hikmat ke sehraon mein thi
Kis
qadar ae mai! tujhe rasm-e-hijab aayi pasand
Parda-e-
angoor se nikli to meenaon mein thi
Husn
ki taseer par ghalib na aa sakta tha ilm
Itni
nadani jahan ke saaray danaon mein thi
Main
ne ae Iqbal Europe mein usay dhoonda abas
Baat
jo Hindustan ke maah-seemaon mein thi
