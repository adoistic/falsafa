Aashiq Harjaai
(Aashiq-e-Harjayi)

Hai
Ajab majmua-e-azdaad ae Iqbal tu
Raunaq
Hangama-e-mehfil bhi hai, tanha bhi hai
Tere
Hangaamon se ae deewane rangeen-nawa
!
Zeenat
Gulshan bhi hai, aaraish-e-sehra bhi hai
Hum
Nasheen taaron ka hai tu rif'at-e-parwaaz se
Ae
Zameen-farsa, qadam tera falak-paima bhi hai
Ain
Shughl mein peshaani hai teri sajda-rez
Kuch
Tere maslak mein rang-e-mashrab-e-meena bhi hai
Misl
Bu-e-gul libaas-e-rang se uriyaan hai tu
Hai
Tu hikmat-aafreen, lekin tujhe sauda bhi hai
Jaanib
Manzil rawaan be-naqsh-e-pa maanind-e-mauj
Aur
Phir uftaada misl-e-sahil-e-darya bhi hai
Husn
Niswaani hai bijli teri fitrat ke liye
Phir
Ajab yeh hai ke tera ishq be-parwa bhi hai
Teri
Hasti ka hai aaeen-e-tafannun par madaar
Tu
Kabhi ek aastane par jabeen-farsa bhi hai?
Hai
Haseenon mein wafa-na-aashna tera khitaab
Ae
Talawwun-kesh! Tu mash'hoor bhi, ruswa bhi hai
Le
Ke aaya hai jahaan mein aadat-e-seemaab tu
Teri
Be-taabi ke sadqe, hai ajab be-taab tu
