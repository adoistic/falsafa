Zamana dekhe ga jab mere dil se mahshar uthe ga guftugu ka
Meri khamoshi nahin hai, goya mazaar hai harf-e-arzoo ka

Mauj-e-darya lagi yeh kehne, safar se qaaim hai shaan meri
Gohar yeh bola sadaf-nasheeni hai mujh ko saamaan-e-aabru ka

Ho tabiyat hi jin ki qaabil, woh tarbiyat se nahin sanwarte
Hua na sarsabz reh ke paani mein aks-e-sarv-e-kinaar-e-ju ka

Koi dil aisa nazar na aaya na jis mein khwaabeeda ho tamanna
Ilahi tera jahan kya hai? Nigaar-khana hai arzoo ka

Khula yeh mar kar ke zindagi apni thi tilism-e-hawas sarapa
Jise samajhte the jism-e-khaaki, ghubaar tha ku-e-arzoo ka

Agar koi shai nahin hai pinhaan to kyun sarapa talaash hoon main
Nigah ko nazaare ki tamanna hai, dil ko sauda hai justuju ka

Chaman mein gulchin se ghuncha kehta tha, itna bedard kyun hai insaan?
Teri nigaahon mein hai tabassum shikasta hona mere subu ka

Riyaaz-e-hasti ke zarre zarre se hai muhabbat ka jalwa paida
Haqeeqat-e-gul ko tu jo samjhe to yeh bhi paimaan hai rang-o-bu ka

Tamam mazmoon mere purane, kalaam mera khata sarapa
Hunar koi dekhta hai mujh mein to aib hai mere aib-ju ka

Sipaas shart-e-adab hai warna karam tera hai sitam se barh kar
Zara sa ek dil diya hai, woh bhi fareb-khurda hai arzoo ka

Kamaal-e-wahdat ayaan hai aisa ke nok-e-nishtar se tu jo chhere
Yaqeen hai mujh ko gire rag-e-gul se qatra insaan ke lahu ka

Gaya hai taqleed ka zamana, majaaz rakht-e-safar uthaaye
Hui haqeeqat hi jab numayan to kis ko yaara hai guftugu ka

Ghar se Iqbal door hoon main, to hun na mahzoon azeez mere
Misaal-e-gauhar watan ki furqat kamaal hai meri aabru ka
