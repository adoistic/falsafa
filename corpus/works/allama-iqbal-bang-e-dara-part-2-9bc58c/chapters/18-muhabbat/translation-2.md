Muhabbat
(Muhabbat)

Uroos-e-shab ki zulfein theen abhi na-aashna kham se
Sitaare aasmaan ke be-khabar thay lazzat-e-ram se
Qamar apne libaas-e-nau mein begaana sa lagta tha
Na tha waaqif abhi gardish ke aaeen-e-musallam se
Abhi imkaan ke zulmat-khaane se ubhri hi thi dunya
Mazaaq-e-zindagi posheeda tha pehnaaye aalam se
Kamaal-e-nazm-e-hasti ki abhi thi ibtida goya
Huwaida thi nageenay ki tamanna chashm-e-khaatam se
Suna hai aalam-e-baala mein koi keemiyagar tha
Safa thi jis ki khaak-e-paa mein barh kar saaghar-e-jam se
Likha tha arsh ke paaye pe ik akseer ka nuskha
Chhupaate thay farishte jis ko chashm-e-rooh-e-aadam se
Nigaahein taak mein rehti theen lekin keemiyagar ki
Woh is nuskhe ko barh kar jaanta tha ism-e-aazam se
Barha tasbeeh-khwaani ke bahaane arsh ki jaanib
Tamanna-e-dili aakhir bar aayi sa'i-e-paiham se
Phiraya fikr-e-ajza ne use maidaan-e-imkaan mein
Chhupe gi kya koi shai baargaah-e-haq ke mehram se
Chamak taare se maangi, chaand se daagh-e-jigar maanga
Uraayi teergi thori si shab ki zulf-e-barham se
Tadap bijli se paayi, hoor se pakeezgi paayi
Haraarat li nafsa-haaye Maseeh ibn-e-Maryam se
Zara si phir ruboobiyat se shaan-e-be-niyaazi li
Malak se aajizi, uftaadgi taqdeer-e-shabnam se
Phir in ajza ko ghola chashma-e-haiwaan ke paani mein
Murakkab ne muhabbat naam paaya Arsh-e-Aazam se
Mehavvas ne yeh paani hasti-e-nau-khez par chhirka
Girah kholi hunar ne us ke goya kaar-e-aalam se
Hui jumbish ayaan, zarron ne lutf-e-khwaab ko chhora
Gale milne lage uth uth ke apne apne humdam se
Khiraam-e-naaz paaya aaftaabon ne, sitaaron ne
Chatak ghunchon ne paayi, daagh paaye laala-zaaron ne
