Chaand aur Taare
(Chaand aur Taray)

Darte
darte dam-e-sehar se
Taare
kehne lage qamar se
Nazaare
rahe wohi falak par
Hum
thak bhi gaye chamak chamak kar
Kaam
apna hai subah o shaam chalna
Chalna
chalna, madaam chalna
Be
taab hai is jahan ki har shai
Kehte
hain jise sukoon, nahin hai
Rehte
hain sitam-kash-e-safar sab
Taare,
insaan, shajar, hajar sab
Hoga
kabhi khatm yeh safar kya
Manzil
kabhi aaye gi nazar kya
Kehne
laga chaand, hum-nasheeno
Ae
mazra-e-shab ke khosha-cheeno
!

Jumbish
se hai zindagi jahan ki
Yeh
rasm-e-qadeem hai yahan ki
Hai
daudta ashhab-e-zamana
Kha
kha ke talab ka taaziyana
Is
rah mein maqaam be-mahal hai
Posheeda
qaraar mein ajal hai
Chalne
waale nikal gaye hain
Jo
thehre zara, kuchal gaye hain
Anjaam
hai is khiraam ka husn
Aaghaaz
hai ishq, intiha husn
