Jalwa-e-Husn
(Jalwa-e-Husn)

Jalwa
Husn ke hai jis se tamanna be-taab
Paalta
hai jise aagosh-e-takhaiyyul mein shabaab
Abadi
banta hai yeh aalam-e-faani jis se
Ek
afsaana-e-rangeen hai jawaani jis se
Jo
sikhaata hai hamein sar-ba-garebaan hona
Manzar
aalam-e-haazir se gurezaan hona
Door
ho jaati hai idraak ki khaami jis se
Aql
karti hai taasur ki ghulami jis se
Aah!

Maujood bhi woh husn kaheen hai ke nahin
Khaatam
dehr mein ya Rabb woh nageen hai ke nahin
