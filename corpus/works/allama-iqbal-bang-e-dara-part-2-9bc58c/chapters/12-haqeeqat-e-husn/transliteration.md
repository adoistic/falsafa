Haqeeqat-e-Husn
(Haqeeqat-e-Husn)

Khuda se husn ne ik roz yeh sawaal kiya
Jahaan mein kyun na mujhe tu ne la-zawaal kiya

Mila jawaab keh tasveer-khaana hai duniya
Shab-e-daraaz adam ka fasaana hai duniya

Hui hai rang-e-taghayyur se jab namood iski
Wohi haseen hai haqeeqat zawaal hai jiski

Kaheen qareeb tha, yeh guftugu qamar ne suni
Falak pe aam hui, akhtar-e-sehar ne suni

Sehar ne taare se sun kar sunaai shabnam ko
Falak ki baat bata di zameen ke mehram ko

Bhar aaye phool ke aansu payaam-e-shabnam se
Kali ka nanha sa dil khoon ho gaya gham se

Chaman se rota hua mausam-e-bahaar gaya
Shabaab sair ko aaya tha, sogwaar gaya
