Chamak teri ayaan bijli mein, aatish mein, sharare mein

Chamak
Teri ayaan bijli mein, aatish mein, sharare mein
Jhalak
Teri huvaida chaand mein, suraj mein, taare mein
Bulandi
Aasmanon mein, zameenon mein teri pasti
Rawani
Behr mein, uftaadgi teri kinare mein
Shariat
Kyun garebaan-geer ho zauq-e-takallum ki
Chhupa
Jaata hoon apne dil ka matlab ista'aare mein
Jo
Hai bedaar insaan mein woh gehri neend sota hai
Shajar
Mein, phool mein, haiwaan mein, patthar mein, sitare mein
Mujhe
Phoonka hai soz-e-qatra-e-ashk-e-mohabbat ne
Ghazab
Ki aag thi paani ke chhote se sharare mein
Nahin
Jins-e-sawab-e-aakhirat ki aarzoo mujh ko
Woh
Saudagar hoon, main ne nafa dekha hai khasaare mein
Sukoon
Na-aashna rehna ise samaan-e-hasti hai
Tadap
Kis dil ki ya Rab chhup ke aa baithi hai paare mein
Sada-e
Lan-taraani sun ke ae Iqbal main chup hoon
Taqazon
Ki kahan taaqat hai mujh furqat ke maare mein
