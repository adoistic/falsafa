Ilahi aql-e-khajista-pai ko zara si deewangi sikha de
Use hai sauda-e-bakhiya-kaari, mujhe sar-e-pairahan nahin hai
Mila mohabbat ka soz mujh ko to bole subh-e-azal farishte
Misaal-e-shama-e-mazaar hai tu, teri koi anjuman nahin hai
Yahan kahan ham-nafas mayassar, yeh des na-aashna hai ae dil!
Woh cheez tu maangta hai mujh se ke zer-e-charkh-e-kuhan nahin hai
Nirala saare jahan se uss ko Arab ke me'maar ne banaya
Bana-e-hamare hisaar-e-millat, ittehad-e-watan nahin hai
Kahan ka aana, kahan ka jaana, fareb hai imtiyaz-e-uqba
Namood har shai mein hai hamari, kahin hamara watan nahin hai
Mudeer-e-'Makhzan' se koi Iqbal ja ke mera payaam keh de
Jo kaam kuchh kar rahi hain qaumein, unhein mazaaq-e-sukhan nahin hai
