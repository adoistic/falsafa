Husn o Ishq
(Husn-o-Ishq)

Jis
tarah doobti hai kashti-e-seemeen-e-qamar
Noor
khursheed ke toofan mein hangaam-e-sahar
Jise
ho jaata hai gum noor ka le kar aanchal
Chandni
raat mein mehtaab ka hum-rang kanwal
Jalwa-e-Toor mein jaise yad-e-baiza-e-kaleem
Mauja-e
nik'hat-e-gulzaar mein ghunche ki shameem
Hai
tere sail-e-mohabbat mein yunhi dil mera

Tu
jo mehfil hai to hangama-e-mehfil hoon main
Husn
ki barq hai to, ishq ka haasil hoon main
Tu
sahar hai to mere ashk hain shabnam teri
Shaam-e
ghurbat hoon agar main to shafaq tu meri
Mere
dil mein teri zulfon ki pareshani hai
Teri
tasveer se paida meri hairaani hai
Husn
kaamil hai tera, ishq hai kaamil mera

Hai
mere baagh-e-sukhan ke liye tu baad-e-bahaar
Mere
be-taab takhayyul ko diya tu ne qaraar
Jab
se aabaad tera ishq hua seene mein
Naye
jauhar huay paida mere aayine mein
Husn
se ishq ki fitrat ko hai tehreek-e-kamaal
Tujh
se sar-sabz huay meri umeedon ke nihaal
Qaafila
ho gaya aasooda-e-manzil mera
