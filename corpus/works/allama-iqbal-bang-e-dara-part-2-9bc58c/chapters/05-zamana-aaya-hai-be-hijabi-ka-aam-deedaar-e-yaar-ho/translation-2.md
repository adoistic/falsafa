Zamana aaya hai be-hijabi ka, aam deedaar-e-yaar hoga

Zamana aaya hai be-hijabi ka, aam deedaar-e-yaar hoga
Sukoot tha parda-daar jiska, woh raaz ab aashkaar hoga
Guzar gaya ab woh daur-e-saqi ke chhup ke peete the peene waale
Banega saara jahan maikhana, har koi baada-khwaar hoga
Kabhi jo aawara-e-junoon the, woh bastiyon mein phir aa basenge
Barahna-paai wohi rahegi magar naya khaarzaar hoga
Suna diya gosh-e-muntazir ko Hijaz ki khamoshi ne aakhir
Ahd sahraiyon se baandha gaya tha, phir ustawaar hoga
Nikal ke sehra se jis ne Rooma ki saltanat ko ulat diya tha
Suna hai yeh qudsiyon se main ne, woh sher phir hoshiyaar hoga
Kiya mera tazkira josaqi ne baada-khwaron ki anjuman mein
Peer-e-maikhana sun ke kehne laga ke munh-phat hai, khwaar hoga
Diyar-e-maghrib ke rehne waalo! Khuda ki basti dukaan nahin hai
Khara jise tum samajh rahe ho, woh ab zar-e-kam-ayaar hoga
Tumhari tehzeeb apne khanjar se aap hi khudkushi karegi
Jo shaakh-e-naazuk pe aashiyana banega, na-paaidaar hoga
Safeena-e-barg-e-gul bana lega qafla-e-mor-e-natawaan ka
Hazaar maujon ki ho kashakash magar yeh darya se paar hoga
Chaman mein laala dikhaata phirta hai daagh apna kali kali ko
Yeh jaanta hai ke is dikhaawe se dil jalon mein shumaar hoga
Ek tha ae nigaah tu ne hazaar kar ke humein dikhaya
Yehi agar kaifiyat hai teri toh phir kise aitbaar hoga
Kaha jo qumri se main ne ik din, yahan ke azaad pa-ba-gil hain
Toh ghunche kehne lage, hamare chaman ka yeh raazdaar hoga
Khuda ke aashiq toh hain hazaaron, banon mein phirte hain maare maare
Main iska banda banunga jisko Khuda ke bandon se pyaar hoga
Rasm-e-bazm-e-fana hai ae dil! Gunah hai jumbish-e-nazar bhi
Rahegi kya aabru hamari jo tu yahan be-qarar hoga
Main zulmat-e-shab mein le ke niklunga apne darmaanda kaarwaan ko
Sharar-fishaan hogi aah meri, nafas mera shola-baar hoga
Nahin hai ghair az namood kuch bhi jo mudda'a teri zindagi ka
Ik nafas mein jahan se mitna tujhe misaal-e-sharaar hoga
Poochh Iqbal ka thikana abhi wohi kaifiyat hai uski
Kahin sar-e-rahguzar baitha sitam-kash-e-intezaar hoga
