Abdul Qadir Ke Naam
(Abdul Qadir Ke Naam)

Uth
ke zulmat hui paida ufaq-e-khawar par
Bazm
mein shola-nawai se ujala kar dein
Ek
faryad hai manind-e-sipand apni bisat
Isi
hangame se mehfil teh-o-bala kar dein
Ahl-e-
mehfil ko dikha dein asar-e-saiqal-e-ishq
Sang-e-
imroz ko aaina-e-farda kar dein
Jalwa-e-
Yusuf-e-gumgashta dikha kar un ko
Tapish
aamadah-tar az khoon-e-Zuleikha kar dein
Is
chaman ko sabaq aaeen-e-numu ka de kar
Qatra-e-
shabnam-e-bemaya ko darya kar dein
Rakht-e-
jaan butkada-e-cheen se utha lein apna
Sab
ko mehv-e-rukh-e-Saadi-o-Saleemi kar dein
Dekh!
Yasrab mein hua naqa-e-Laila bekaar
Qais
ko aarzoo-e-nau se shinasa kar dein
Baada-e-
dereena ho aur garam ho aisa ke gudaaz
Jigar-e-
sheesha-o-paimana-o-meena kar dein
Garam
rakhta tha humein sardi-e-maghrib mein jo daagh
Cheer
kar seena use waqf-e-tamasha kar dein
Shama
ki tarah jiyein bazm-gah-e-aalam mein
Khud
jalein, deeda-e-aghyar ko beena kar dein
''
Har
cheh dar dil guzrad waqf-e-zuban darad shama
Sokhtan
neest khayale ke nihan darad shama
''
