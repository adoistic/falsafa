## Key passage (TextRank #1)
> [p-c34c82] Some years later, the same project was taken up again. Three ships were dispatched with a colony stronger than the first. The colonists were provided with arms, victuals, seeds, agricultural instruments, in short with all the objects necessary for their establishment. When they saw the labors to which they had to devote themselves to wrest from the earth products fit to serve them as food, they feared they would lack victuals, and they begged their commander to return to England to bring them some. He departed; but before his return, famine, diseases, and the savages had destroyed them all.
