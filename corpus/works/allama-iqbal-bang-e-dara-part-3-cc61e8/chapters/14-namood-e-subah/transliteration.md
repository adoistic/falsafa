Namood-e-Subah
(Namood-e-Subah)

Ho
rahi hai zer-e-damaan-e-ufaq se aashkaar
Subah
yaani dukhtar-e-dosheeza-e-lail-o-nahaar
Pa
chuka fursat durood-e-fasl-e-anjum se sipehr
Kisht
-e-khaavar mein hua hai aaftaab aaina-kaar
Aasmaan
ne aamad-e-khursheed ki pa kar khabar
Mehmil
-e-parvaaz-e-shab baandha sar-e-dosh-e-ghubaar
Shola-e-
khursheed goya haasil uss kheti ka hai
Boye
thay dehqaan-e-gardoon ne jo taaron ke sharaar
Hai
ravaan najm-e-sehar, jaise ibaadat-khaane se
Sab
se peechay jaaye koi aabid-e-shab-zinda-daar
Kya
samaan hai jis tarah aahista aahista koi
Kheenchta
ho miyaan ki zulmat se tegh-e-aab-daar
Matla-e-
khursheed mein muzmir hai yun mazmoon-e-subah
Jaise
khalvat-gaah-e-meena mein sharaab-e-khush-gawaar
Hai
teh-e-damaan-e-baad-e-ikhtilaat-angez-subah
Shorish-
e-naaqoos, aawaaz-e-azaan se hamkinaar
Jaage
koyal ki azaan se taairaan-e-naghma-sanj
Hai
tarannum-rez qanoon-e-sehar ka taar taar
