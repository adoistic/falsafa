Chaand
(Chaand)

Ae
Chaand! Husn tera fitrat ki aabru hai
Tauf
Hareem-e-khaaki teri qadeem khoo hai
Yeh
Daagh sa jo tere seene mein hai numayaan
Aashiq
Hai tu kisi ka, yeh daagh aarzoo hai?
Mein
Muztarib zameen par, betaab tu falak par
Tujh
Ko bhi justaju hai, mujh ko bhi justaju hai
Insaan
Hai shama jiss ki, mehfil wohi hai teri?
Mein
Jiss taraf rawaan hoon, manzil wohi hai teri?
Tu
Dhoondta hai jiss ko taaron ki khaamoshi mein
Posheeda
Hai woh shaayad ghogha-e-zindagi mein
Istaada
Sarv mein hai, sabze mein so raha hai
Bulbul
Mein naghma-zan hai, khaamosh hai kali mein
Aa
!
Mein tujhe dikhaaun rukhsaar-e-roshan uss ka
Nehron
Ke aainay mein shabnam ki aarsi mein
Sehra
o dasht o dar mein, kohsaar mein wohi hai
Insaan
Ke dil mein, tere rukhsaar mein wohi hai
