Tarana-e-Milli
(Tarana-e-Milli)

Cheen o Arab hamara, Hindustaan hamara
Muslim hain hum, watan hai saara jahan hamara
Tauheed ki amanat seenon mein hai hamare
Aasaan nahin mitana naam o nishaan hamara
Duniya ke but-kadon mein pehla woh ghar Khuda ka
Hum uss ke paasbaan hain, woh paasbaan hamara
Teghon ke saaye mein hum pal kar jawaan hue hain
Khanjar hilal ka hai qaumi nishaan hamara
Maghrib ki waadiyon mein goonji azaan hamari
Thamta na tha kisi se sail-e-rawaan hamara
Baatil se dabne waale ae aasmaan nahin hum
Sau baar kar chuka hai tu imtihaan hamara
Ae gulistan-e-Andalus! Woh din hain yaad tujh ko
Tha teri daaliyon par jab aashiyaan hamara
Ae mauj-e-Dajla! Tu bhi pehchanti hai hum ko
Ab tak hai tera darya afsana-khwaan hamara
Ae arz-e-paak! Teri hurmat pe kat mare hum
Hai khoon teri ragon mein ab tak rawaan hamara
Saalar-e-kaarwan hai Meer-e-Hijaaz apna
Is naam se hai baaqi aaraam-e-jaan hamara
Iqbal ka tarana Bang-e-Dara hai goya
Hota hai jaada-paima phir kaarwan hamara
