Tazmeen bar sher-e Aneesi Shamlu
(Tazmeen)

Hamesha
Soorat-e baad-e seher aawara rehta hoon
Mohabbat
Mein hai manzil se bhi khush-tar jaada paimaai
Dil
Be-taab ja pahuncha dayaar-e Peer-e Sanjar mein
Mayassar
Hai jahan darmaan-e dard-e na-shakeebaai
Abhi
Na-aashna-e labb tha harf-e aarzoo mera
Zubaan
Hone ko thi minnat-pazeer-e taab-e goyaai
Yeh
Marqad se sada aayi, Haram ke rehne walon ko
Shikayat
Tujh se hai ae taarik-e aaeen-e aabaai
!
Tera
Ae Qais kyun-kar ho gaya soz-e daroon thanda
Ke
Laila mein to hain ab tak wohi andaaz-e Lailaai
Na
Tukhm-e 'La Ilah' teri zameen-e shor se phoota
Zamaane
Bhar mein ruswa hai teri fitrat ki na-zaai
Tujhe
Maaloom hai ghaafil ke teri zindagi kya hai
Kunishti
Saaz, maamoor-e nawa haaye kaleesaai
Hui
Hai tarbiyat aaghosh-e Bait-Ullah mein teri
Dil
Shoreeda hai lekin sanam-khaane ka saudaai
''
Wafa
Aamokhti az ma, bakaar-e deegraan kar di
Raboodi
Gohar-e az ma nisaar-e deegraan kar di
''
