Ghurra-e-
Shawwal! Ae noor-e-nigah-e-roza-daar
Aa
Ke thay tere liye Muslim sarapa intezaar
Teri
Peshani pe tehreer-e-payaam-e-Eid hai
Shaam
Teri kya hai, subh-e-aish ki tamheed hai
Sarguzasht-e-
Millat-e-baiza ka tu aaina hai
Ae
Meh-e-nau! Hum ko tujh se ulfat-e-dereena hai
Jis
Alam ke saaye mein tegh-aazma hote thay hum
Dushmanon
Ke khoon se rangeen qaba hote thay hum
Teri
Qismat mein hum-aaghoshi usi raayat ki hai
Husn-e-
Roz-afzoon se tere aabru millat ki hai
Aashna
Parwar hai qaum apni, wafa aaeen tera
Hai
Mohabbat-khaiz yeh pairaahan-e-seemeen tera
Auj-e-
Gardoon se zara dunya ki basti dekh le
Apni
Rif'at se hamare ghar ki pasti dekh le
!
Qaafile
Dekh aur un ki barq-raftaari bhi dekh
Reh-rau-e
Darmaanda ki manzil se bezaari bhi dekh
Dekh
Kar tujh ko ufaq par hum lutate thay gohar
Ae
Tehi saaghar! Hamari aaj naadaari bhi dekh
Firqa
Aaraai ki zanjeeron mein hain Muslim aseer
Apni
Azaadi bhi dekh, un ki giraftaari bhi dekh
Dekh
Masjid mein shikast-e-rishta-e-tasbeeh-e-shaikh
But
Kaday mein Brahman ki pukhta zunnari bhi dekh
Kaafiron
Ki Muslim-aaeeni ka bhi nazaara kar
Aur
Apne Muslimon ki Muslim-aazaari bhi dekh
Baarish-e-
Sang-e-hawadis ka tamashai bhi ho
Ummat-e-
Marhoom ki aaina-deewari bhi dekh
Haan
, Tamalluq-peshgi dekh aabru walon ki tu
Aur
Jo be-aabru thay, un ki khud-daari bhi dekh
Jis
Ko hum ne aashna lutf-e-takallum se kiya
Us
Hareef-e-bezubaan ki garm-guftaari bhi dekh
Saaz-e-
Ishrat ki sada Maghrib ke aiwaanon mein sun
Aur
Iran mein zara maatam ki tayyari bhi dekh
Chaak
Kar di Turk-e-nadaan ne khilafat ki qaba
Saadgi
Muslim ki dekh, auron ki ayyari bhi dekh
Soorat-e-
Aaina sab kuch dekh aur khamosh reh
Shorish-e-
Imroz mein mehv-e-surood-e-dosh reh
