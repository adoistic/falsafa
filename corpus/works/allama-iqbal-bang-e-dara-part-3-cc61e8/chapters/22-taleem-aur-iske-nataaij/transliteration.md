Taleem aur iske Nataaij
(Taleem aur Nataaij)

Khush
toh hain hum bhi jawanon ki taraqqi se magar
Lab
khandaan se nikal jaati hai faryaad bhi saath
Hum
samajhte the ke laaye gi faraghat taaleem
Kya
khabar thi ke chala aaye ga ilhaad bhi saath
Ghar
mein Parvez ke Shireen toh hui jalwa-numa
Le
ke aayi hai magar tesha-e-Farhad bhi saath
"
Tukhm
deegar bakaf aareem o bakaareem ze nau
K'aanche kashteem ze khajlat natwaan kard daro
