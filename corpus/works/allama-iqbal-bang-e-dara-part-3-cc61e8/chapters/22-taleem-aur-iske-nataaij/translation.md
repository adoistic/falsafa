Education and Its Consequences

We too are pleased with the progress of our youth, but
from these smiling lips, a lament escapes as well.

We once believed education would bring solace and liberty;
who knew that godlessness would come along with it?

Shireen has, in splendor, graced the house of Parvez,
but brings with her Farhad's destructive adze.

*Let us bring another seed to hand and sow it anew,*
*For what we have already sown, we cannot reap for shame.*
