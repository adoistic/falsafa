The Chalice of Hind with Wine of Truth is crowned,
The West's philosophers are by its wisdom bound.

This is the sign of Hind's thought, scaling heaven's height,
Its lofty summit soars beyond the sky's own light.

In this domain, a thousand souls were heaven-born,
Whose fame today does India's great name adorn.

On Ram's existence, Hindustan can gaze with pride,
The wise of vision see him as Hind's Spiritual Guide.

This is the miracle of that great Lamp of Light:
The dusk of Hind is than the dawn itself more bright.

In valor with the sword, his courage was peerless shown;
In purity and loving fire, his soul was peerless known.
