Labrez
hai sharab-e-haqeeqat se jaam-e-Hind
Sab
falsafi hain khitta-e-maghrib ke Ram-e-Hind
Yeh
Hindiyon ke fikr-e-falak-ras ka hai asar
Rif'at
mein aasman se bhi ooncha hai baam-e-Hind
Iss
des mein hue hain hazaron malak-sarisht
Mashhoor
jin ke dam se hai duniya mein naam-e-Hind
Hai
Ram ke wajood pe Hindustaan ko naaz
Ahl-e-
nazar samajhte hain is ko Imam-e-Hind
Ejaz
iss chiragh-e-hidayat ka hai yahi
Roshan
tar az sehar hai zamaane mein shaam-e-Hind
Talwar
ka dhani tha, shuja'at mein fard tha
Pakeezgi
mein, josh-e-mohabbat mein fard tha
