Bilad-e-Islamiya
(Bilad-e-Islamiya)

Sarzameen
Dilli ki masjood dil-e-gham deeda hai
Zarre
Zarre mein lahu aslaaf ka khwabida hai
Paak
Is ujde gulistaan ki na ho kyun kar zameen
Khanqah-e
Azmat-e-Islam hai yeh sarzameen
Sote
Hain is khaak mein Khair-ul-Umam ke tajdaar
Nazm-e
Aalam ka raha jin ki hukumat par madaar
Dil
Ko tadpati hai ab tak garmi-e-mehfil ki yaad
Jal
Chuka hasil magar mehfooz hai hasil ki yaad
Hai
Ziyarat gaah-e-Muslim go Jahanabad bhi
Is
Karamat ka magar haq-daar hai Baghdad bhi
Yeh
Chaman woh hai ke tha jis ke liye saaman-e-naaz
Lala-e
Sehra jise kehte hain Tehzeeb-e-Hijaz
Khaak
Is basti ki ho kyun kar na hamdosh-e-Iram
Jis
Ne dekhe jaanasheenan-e-Paighambar ke qadam
Jis
Ke ghunche thay chaman saamaan, woh gulshan hai yahi
Kaanpta
Tha jin se Roma, un ka madfan hai yahi
Hai
Zameen-e-Qurtaba bhi deeda-e-Muslim ka noor
Zulmat-e
Maghrib mein jo raushan thi misl-e-shama-e-Toor
Bujh
Ke bazm-e-millat-e-baiza pareshaan kar gayi
Aur
Diya tehzeeb-e-haazir ka farozaan kar gayi
Qabr
Is tehzeeb ki yeh sarzameen-e-paak hai
Jis
Se taak-e-gulshan-e-Europe ki rag nam-naak hai
Khitta-e
Qustuntunia yaani Qaisar ka diyaar
Mehdi-e
Ummat ki satwat ka nishaan-e-payedaar
Soorat-e
Khaak-e-haram yeh sarzameen bhi paak hai
Aastaan-e
Masnad aaraye Shah-e-Laulaak hai
Nikhat-e
Gul ki tarah pakeeza hai is ki hawa
Turbat-e
Ayyub Ansari se aati hai sada
Ae
Musalman! Millat-e-Islam ka dil hai yeh sheher
Sainkdon
Sadiyon ki kisht-o-khoon ka hasil hai yeh sheher
Woh
Zameen hai tu magar ae khwab-gaah-e-Mustafa
Deed
Hai Kaabe ko teri Hajj-e-Akbar se siwa
Khaatam-e
Hasti mein tu taabaan hai maanind-e-nageen
Apni
Azmat ki wiladat-gaah thi teri zameen
Tujh
Mein raahat us shehenshah-e-moazzam ko mili
Jis
Ke daaman mein amaan aqwaam-e-aalam ko mili
Naam
Leva jis ke shahenshah-e-aalam ke hue
Jaansheen
Qaisar ke, waris-e-masnad-e-Jam ke hue
Hai
Agar qaumiyat-e-Islam paband-e-maqaam
Hind
Hi buniyad hai is ki, na Fars hai, na Shaam
Aah
Yasrab! Des hai Muslim ka tu, maawa hai tu
Nuqta-e
Jaazib-e-taasur ki shuaaon ka hai tu
Jab
Talak baaqi hai tu duniya mein, baaqi hum bhi hain
Subah
Hai to is chaman mein gauhar-e-shabnam bhi hain
