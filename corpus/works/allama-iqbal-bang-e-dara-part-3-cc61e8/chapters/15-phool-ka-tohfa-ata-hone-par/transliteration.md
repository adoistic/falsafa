Phool ka tohfa ata hone par
(Phool Ka Tohfa)

Voh
mast-e-naaz jo gulshan mein ja nikalti hai
Kali
kali ki zubaan se dua nikalti hai
''
Ilahi!
Phoolon mein voh intekhab mujh ko kare
Kali
se rashk-e-gul-e-aaftaab mujh ko kare
''
Tujhe
voh shaakh se torein! Zeh-e-naseeb teray
Tadaptay
reh gaye gulzar mein raqeeb teray
Utha
ke sadma-e-furqat visaal tak pohancha
Teri
hayaat ka jauhar kamaal tak pohancha
Mera
kanwal ke tasadduq hain jis pe ahl-e-nazar
Meray
shabab ke gulshan ko naaz hai jis par
Kabhi
yeh phool hum-aaghosh-e-mudda'a na hua
Kisi
ke daaman-e-rangeen se aashna na hua
Shagufta
kar na sake gi kabhi bahaar usay
Afsurda
rakhta hai gulcheen ka intezaar usay
