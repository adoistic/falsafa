Har nafas Iqbal tera aah mein mastoor hai
Seena-e-sozaan tera faryaad se ma'moor hai
Naghma-e-umeed teri barbat-e-dil mein nahin
Hum samajhte hain yeh Laila tere mehmil mein nahin
Gosh aawaz-e-sarood-e-rafta ka joya tera
Aur dil hangama-e-haazir se be-parwa tera
Qissa-e-gul hum nawayan-e-chaman sunte nahin
Ahl-e-mehfil tera paighaam-e-kuhan sunte nahin
Ae dara-e-kaarvaan-e-khufta paa! khaamosh reh
Hai bohot yaas aafreen teri sada, khaamosh reh
Zinda phir woh mehfil-e-dereena ho sakti nahin
Shama se roshan shab-e-dosheena ho sakti nahin
Hum nasheen! Muslim hoon main, Tauheed ka haamil hoon main
Iss sadaqat par azal se shaahid-e-aadil hoon main
Nabz-e-maujoodaat mein paida hararat is se hai
Aur Muslim ke takhayyul mein jasarat is se hai
Haq ne aalam is sadaqat ke liye paida kiya
Aur mujhe is ki hifazat ke liye paida kiya
Dehr mein gharat-gar-e-baatil parasti main hua
Haq toh yeh hai haafiz-e-naamoos-e-hasti main hua
Meri hasti perahan-e-uryaani-e-aalam ki hai
Mere mit jaane se ruswai bani aadam ki hai
Qismat-e-aalam ka Muslim kaukab-e-tabinda hai
Jis ki taabani se afsoon-e-seher sharminda hai
Aashkara hain meri aankhon pe asrar-e-hayat
Keh nahin sakte mujhe na-umeed-e-paikaar-e-hayat
Kab dara sakta hai gham ka aarzi manzar mujhe
Hai bharosa apni millat ke muqaddar par mujhe
Yaas ke unsur se hai azaad mera rozgaar
Fateh-e-kaamil ki khabar deta hai josh-e-kaarzar
Haan yeh sach hai chashm bar ehd-e-kuhan rehta hoon main
Ahl-e-mehfil se purani dastaan kehta hoon main
Yaad-e-ehd-e-rafta meri khaak ko akseer hai
Mera maazi mere istiqbaal ki tafseer hai
Saamne rakhta hoon is daur-e-nishaat afzaa ko main
Dekhta hoon dosh ke aaine mein farda ko main
