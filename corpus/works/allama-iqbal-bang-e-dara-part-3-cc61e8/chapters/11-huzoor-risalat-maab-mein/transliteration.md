Huzoor Risalat Ma'ab Mein
(Hazoor-e-Risalat)

Giran
Jo mujh pe yeh hangama-e-zamana hua
Jahan
Se baandh ke rakht-e-safar rawana hua
Qayood
Shaam-o-seher mein basar to ki lekin
Nizaam
Kohna-e-alam se aashna na hua
Farishte
Bazm-e-risalat mein le gaye mujh ko
Huzoor
Aaya-e-rehmat mein le gaye mujh ko

Kaha
Huzoor ne, ae andaleeb-e-bagh-e-Hijaaz!
Kali
Kali hai teri garmi-e-nawa se gudaaz
Hamesha
Sarkhush-e-jaam-e-wila hai dil tera
Fitadgi
Hai teri ghairat-e-sujood-e-niyaaz
Ura
Jo pasti-e-dunya se tu soo-e-gardoon
Sikhayi
Tujh ko mala'ik ne rif'at-e-parwaaz
Nikal
Ke bagh-e-jahan se ba-rang-e-boo aaya
Hamare
Waaste kya tohfa le ke tu aaya?
''

Huzoor!
Dehr mein aasoodgi nahin milti
Talaash
Jis ki hai woh zindagi nahin milti
Hazaron
Lala-o-gul hain riyaaz-e-hasti mein
Wafa
Ki jis mein ho boo, woh kali nahin milti
Magar
Main nazr ko ek aabgeena laya hoon
Jo
Cheez is mein hai, jannat mein bhi nahin milti
Jhalakti
Hai teri ummat ki aabroo is mein
Tarablus
Ke shaheedon ka hai lahu is mein
''
