Ek haji Madinay ke raastay mein
(Ek Haji)

Qafla
Luta gaya sehra mein aur manzil hai duur
Iss
bayabaan yaani behr-e-khushk ka saahil hai duur
Hum
safar mere shikaar-e-dashna-e-rehzan hue
Bach
gaye jo, ho ke be-dil su-e-baitullah phirey
Iss
Bukhari naujawan ne kis khushi se jaan di
!
Maut
ke zehraab mein paayi hai uss ne zindagi
Khanjar
rehzan usey goya hilal-e-eid tha
'
Haaye
Yasrab' dil mein, lab par naara-e-tauheed tha
Khauf
kehta hai ke Yasrab ki taraf tanha na chal
Shauq
kehta hai ke tu Muslim hai, be-baakana chal
Be-
ziyarat su-e-baitullah phir jaaon ga kya
Aashiqon
ko roz-e-mehshar munh na dikhlaaoon ga kya
Khauf
jaan rakhta nahin kuch dasht-e-paima-e-Hijaz
Hijrat
madfoon-e-Yasrab mein yehi makhfi hai raaz
Go
salamat mehmil-e-Shami ki humraahi mein hai
Ishq
ki lazzat magar khatron ke jaan-kaahi mein hai
Aah
!
Yeh aqal-e-ziyaan-andesh kya chaalaak hai
Aur
ta'assur aadmi ka kis qadar be-baak hai
