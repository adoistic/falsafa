Shama aur Shair
(Shama aur Shair)

Dosh
mi goftam be shama-e manzil-e veeran-e khwesh
Gesu-e
to az par-e parwana darad shana-e
Darjahan
misl-e charagh-e lala-e sahrastam
Ne
naseeb-e mehfile ne qismat-e kashana-e
Muddate
manind-e to man hum nafas mi-sokhtam
Dar
tawaf-e sho'la-am baale na zad parwana-e
Mi
tapad sad jalwa dar jaan-e amal farsoodam na
Bar
nami-kheezad az-een mehfil dil-e deewana-e
Az
kuja een aatish-e aalam-faroz andokhti
Kirmak-e
be-maaya ra soz-e Kaleem aamokhti
