Kal
ek shoreeda khwab-gah-e-Nabi pe ro ro ke keh raha tha
ke
Misr-o-Hindustan ke Muslim banaye millat mita rahe hain
yeh
zaairan-e-hareem-e-maghrib hazaar rahbar banein hamare
humein
bhala un se waasta kya jo tujh se na-aashna rahe hain
ghazab
hain yeh 'murshidan-e-khud-been', Khuda teri qaum ko bachaye
!
bigaad
kar tere Muslimon ko yeh apni izzat bana rahe hain
sune
ga Iqbal kaun inko, yeh anjuman hi badal gayi hai
naye
zamaane mein aap hum ko purani baatein suna rahe hain
!
