Two Stars

Two stars, in close conjunction bright,
One spoke into the other's light:

"What bliss, a perpetual union,
And our long wandering's end begun;

If but the heavens would be kind,
And our two lights as one could bind."

But this deep wish, this hopeful art,
Was a prophecy that we must part.

For motion is a star's decree,
Each on a path of destiny.

A dream of lasting union's plea,
For parting is the cosmos's decree.
