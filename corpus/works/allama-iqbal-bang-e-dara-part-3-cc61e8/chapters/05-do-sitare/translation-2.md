Do Sitare
Jo qiraan mein do sitare
kehne
laga ek, doosre se
Wasl-e-mudaam ho to kya khoob
Anjaam
khiraam ho to kya khoob
Thoda
sa jo meherbaan falak ho
dono ki ek hi chamak ho
Lekin
yeh wisaal ki tamanna
Paighaam
firaaq thi sarapa
Gardish
taaron ka hai muqaddar
ek ki raah hai muqarrar
Khwaab-e-sabaat aashnaai
Aaeen
jahan ka hai judaai
