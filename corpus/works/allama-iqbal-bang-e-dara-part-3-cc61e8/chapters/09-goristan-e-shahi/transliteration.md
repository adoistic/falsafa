Goristan-e-Shahi
(Goristan-e-Shahi)

Goristan
Shahi
Samaan
, badal ka pehne khirqa-e-dereena hai
Kuch
mukaddar sa jabeen-e-maah ka aaina hai
Chandni
pheeki hai is nazara-e-khamosh mein
Subah
sadiq so rahi hai raat ki aaghosh mein
Qadr ashjaar ki hairat faza hai khamoshi
Barbat
qudrat ki dheemi si nawa hai khamoshi
Baatin
har zarra-e-aalam sarapa dard hai
Aur
khamoshi lab-e-hasti pe aah-e-sard hai
Jaulaan-gah-e-aalam geer yaani woh hisaar
Dosh
par apne uthaye saikdon sadiyon ka baar
Zindagi
se tha kabhi maamoor, ab sunsaan hai
Khamoshi uske hangamon ka goristan hai
Apne
sukkaan-e-kuhan ki khaak ka dildada hai
Koh
ke sar par misaal-e-pasban istada hai
Abr
ke rozan se woh baala-e-baam-e-asman
Naazir
aalam hai najm-e-sabz faam-e-asman
Khaak
baazi wus'at-e-dunya ka hai manzar usey
Dastaan
nakami-e-insaan ki hai azbar usey
Azal se yeh musafir soo-e-manzil ja raha
Aasmaan
se inqilabon ka tamasha dekhta
Sukoon mumkin nahin aalam mein akhtar ke liye
Fatiha
khwani ko yeh thehra hai dam bhar ke liye
Rang
o aab-e-zindagi se gul badaman hai zameen
Saikdon
khoon-gashta tehzeebon ka madfan hai zameen
Khwaab
gah-e-shahon ki hai yeh manzil-e-hasrat faza
Deeda-e
ibrat! khiraj-e-ashk-e-gulgoon kar ada
Tu goristan magar yeh khaak-e-gardoon paya hai
Ik bargashta qismat qaum ka sarmaya hai
Maqbaron
ki shaan hairat-afreen hai is qadar
Jumbish
mizhgaan se hai chashm-e-tamasha ko hazar
Kaifiyat
aisi hai nakami ki is tasveer mein
utar sakti nahin aaina-e-tehreer mein
Sote
hain khamosh, aabadi ke hangamon se door
Muztarib
rakhti thi jin ko arzoo-e-nasaboor
Qabr
ki zulmat mein hai un aaftabon ki chamak
Ke darwazon pe rehta tha jabeen-gustar falak
Kya
yahi hai un shehenshahon ki azmat ka maal
ki tadbeer-e-jahan baani se darta tha zawaal
Roab
faghfoori ho dunya mein keh shaan-e-qaiseri
nahin sakti ghaneem-e-maut ki yorish kabhi
Badshahon
ki bhi kisht-e-umr ka hasil hai gor
Jaada-e
azmat ki goya aakhri manzil hai gor
Shorish
bazm-e-tarab kya, ood ki taqreer kya
Dard
mandan-e-jahan ka nala-e-shab-geer kya
Arsa-e
paikaar mein hangama-e-shamsheer kya
Khoon
ko garmane wala naara-e-takbeer kya
Koi aawaz soton ko jaga sakti nahin
Seena-e
veeran mein jaan-e-rafta aa sakti nahin
Rooh
, musht-e-khaak mein zehmat-kash-e-bedaad hai
Koocha
gard ne hui jis dam nafas, faryad hai
Zindagi
insaan ki hai manind-e-murgh-e-khush-nawa
Shaakh
par baitha, koi dam chehchahaya, ud gaya
Kya aaye riyaz-e-dehr mein hum, kya gaye
Zindagi
ki shaakh se phoote, khile, murjha gaye
Maut
har shah-o-gada ke khwaab ki taabeer hai
Sitam-gar ka sitam insaaf ki tasveer hai
Silsila
hasti ka hai ik behr-e-na-paida-kinaar
Aur
is darya-e-be-payan ke maujein hain mazaar
Hawas! khoon ro keh hai yeh zindagi be-aitbaar
Sharare ka tabassum, yeh khas-e-aatish-sawar
Chaand
, jo soorat-gar-e-hasti ka ik ijaz hai
Pehne
seemabi qaba mehv-e-khiram-e-naaz hai
Charkh
be-anjum ki dehshat-naak wus'at mein magar
Kisi uss ki koi dekhe zara waqt-e-seher
Zara sa abr ka tukda hai, jo mehtaab tha
Aakhri
aansu tapak jaane mein ho jis ki fana
Zindagi
aqwaam ki bhi hai yunhi be-aitbaar
Rang
haaye rafta ki tasveer hai unki bahaar
Ziyan khaane mein koi millat gardoon waqar
Nahin sakti abad tak bar-e-dosh-e-rozgar
Qadar qaumon ki barbadi se hai kho-gar jahan
Dekhta
be-aitnai se hai yeh manzar jahan
Ek
soorat par nahin rehta kisi shai ko qarar
Zauq
jiddat se hai tarkeeb-e-mizaj-e-rozgar
Hai nageen-e-dehr ki zeenat hamesha naam-e-nau
Maadar
geeti rahi aabistan-e-aqwaam-e-nau
Hain hazaron qafilon se aashna yeh reh-guzar
Chashm
Koh-e-Noor ne dekhe hain kitne taajwar
Misr
o Babil mit gaye, baqi nishan tak bhi nahin
Daftar
hasti mein unki daastan tak bhi nahin
Dabaya mehr-e-Iran ko ajal ki shaam ne
Azmat
Yunan-o-Roma loot li ayyam ne
Muslim bhi zamane se yunhi rukhsat hua
Aasmaan
se abr-e-aazari utha, barsa, gaya
rag-e-gul subah ke ashkon se moti ki ladi
Koi
sooraj ki kiran shabnam mein hai uljhi hui
Seena-e
darya shua'on ke liye gehwara hai
qadar pyara lab-e-joo mehr ka nazara hai
Mehv
zeenat hai sanobar, joo-e-baar aaina hai
Ghuncha-e
gul ke liye baad-e-bahaar aaina hai
Naara
zan rehti hai koyal bagh ke kaashaane mein
Chashm
insaan se nihan, patton ke uzlat-khaane mein
Aur
bulbul, mutrib-e-rangeen nawa-e-gulistan
Jis ke dam se zinda hai goya hawa-e-gulistan
ishq ke hangamon ki udti hui tasveer hai
Khaama-e
qudrat ki kaisi shokh yeh tehreer hai
Is bagh mein khamosh jalse gulistan-zadon ke hain
Wadi-e
koh-saar mein naare shaban-zadon ke hain
Zindagi
se yeh purana khaak-daan maamoor hai
Maut
mein bhi zindagani ki tadap mastoor hai
Patiyan
phoolon ki girti hain khizan mein is tarah
dast-e-tifl-e-khufta se rangeen khilone jis tarah
Yeh nishat-abad mein go aish be-andaza hai
Ek
gham, yaani gham-e-millat hamesha taaza hai
Dil hamare yaad-e-ahd-e-rafta se khali nahin
Apne
shahon ko yeh ummat bhoolne wali nahin
Ashk
baari ke bahane hain yeh ujde baam-o-dar
Girya-e
paiham se beena hai hamari chashm-e-tar
Dehr
ko dete hain moti deeda-e-giryan ke hum
Aakhri
badal hain ik guzre hue toofan ke hum
Hain
abhi sad-ha guhar uss abr ki aaghosh mein
Barq
abhi baqi hai uske seena-e-khamosh mein
Wadi-e
gul, khaak-e-sehra ko bana sakta hai yeh
Khwaab
se umeed-e-dehqan ko jaga sakta hai yeh
Mit chuka go qaum ki shaan-e-jalali ka zahoor
Hai magar baqi abhi shaan-e-jamali ka zahoor
