Wataniyat
(Wataniyat)

Is daur mein may aur hai, jaam aur hai Jam aur
Saqi ne bana ki ravish lutf-o-sitam aur
Muslim ne bhi taameer kiya apna haram aur
Tehzeeb ke Aazar ne tarshwaaye sanam aur
In taaza khudaon mein bada sab se watan hai
Jo pairahan is ka hai, woh mazhab ka kafan hai

Yeh butt keh taraasheeda-e-tehzeeb-e-naui hai
Ghaarat-gar-e-kaashaana-e-deen-e-Nabawi hai
Baazu tera Tauheed ki quwwat se qavi hai
Islam tera des hai, tu Mustafavi hai
Nazzaara-e-dereena zamaane ko dikha de
Aye Mustafavi khaak mein is butt ko mila de!

Ho qaid-e-maqaami to nateeja hai tabaahi
Reh behr mein azaad-e-watan soorat-e-maahi
Hai tark-e-watan sunnat-e-Mehboob-e-Ilaahi
De tu bhi Nabuwwat ki sadaaqat pe gawaahi
Guftaar-e-siyaasat mein watan aur hi kuch hai
Irshaad-e-Nabuwwat mein watan aur hi kuch hai

Aqwaam-e-jahaan mein hai raqaabat to isi se
Taskheer hai maqsood-e-tijaarat to isi se
Khaali hai sadaaqat se siyaasat to isi se
Kamzor ka ghar hota hai ghaarat to isi se
Aqwaam mein makhlooq-e-Khuda bat-ti hai is se
Qaumiyat-e-Islam ki jadd kat-ti hai is se
