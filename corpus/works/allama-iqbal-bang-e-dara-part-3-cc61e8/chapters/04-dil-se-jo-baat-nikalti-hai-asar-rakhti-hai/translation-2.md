Dil se jo baat nikalti hai asar rakhti hai
Par nahin, taaqat-e-parwaaz magar rakhti hai
Qudsi-ul-asal hai, rif'at pe nazar rakhti hai
Khaak se uthti hai, gardoon pe guzar rakhti hai
Ishq tha fitna-gar-o-sarkash-o-chaalaak mera
Aasmaan cheer gaya naala-e-bebaak mera

Peer-e-gardoon ne kaha sun ke, kahin hai koi
Bole sayyare, sar-e-arsh-e-bareen hai koi
Chaand kehta tha, nahin! ahl-e-zameen hai koi
Kehkashaan kehti thi, posheeda yahin hai koi
Kuch jo samjha mere shikwe ko toh Rizwaan samjha
Mujhe jannat se nikaala hua insaan samjha

Thi farishton ko bhi hairat ke yeh awaaz hai kya
Arsh waalon pe bhi khulta nahin yeh raaz hai kya
Ta sar-e-arsh bhi insaan ki tag-o-taaz hai kya!
Aa gayi khaak ki chutki ko bhi parwaaz hai kya!
Ghaafil aadaab se sukkaan-e-zameen kaise hain
Shokh-o-gustaakh yeh pasti ke makeen kaise hain!

Is qadar shokh ke Allah se bhi barham hai
Tha jo masjood-e-malaa'ik, yeh wohi Aadam hai!
Aalim-e-kaif hai, daana-e-rumooz-e-kam hai
Haan magar ijz ke asraar se na-mehram hai
Naaz hai taaqat-e-guftaar pe insaanon ko
Baat karne ka saliqa nahin naadaanon ko

Aayi aawaaz, gham-angez hai afsaana tera
Ashk-e-betaab se labrez hai paimaana tera
Aasmaan-geer hua naara-e-mastaana tera
Kis qadar shokh-zubaan hai dil-e-deewana tera
Shukr shikwe ko kiya husn-e-adaa se tu ne
Hum-sukhan kar diya bandon ko Khuda se tu ne

Hum toh maa'il ba-karam hain, koi saa'il hi nahin
Raah dikhlaayein kise, rehraw-e-manzil hi nahin
Tarbiyat aam toh hai, jauhar-e-qaabil hi nahin
Jis se taameer ho Aadam ki, yeh woh gil hi nahin
Koi qaabil ho toh hum shaan-e-kai dete hain
Dhoondne waalon ko duniya bhi nayi dete hain

Haath be-zor hain, ilhaad se dil khoogar hain
Ummati baa'is-e-ruswaai-e-paighambar hain
But-shikan uth gaye, baaqi jo rahe but-gar hain
Tha Ibrahim pidar aur pisar Aazar hain
Baada-aashaam naye, baada naya, khum bhi naye
Haram-e-Kaaba naya, but bhi naye, tum bhi naye

Woh bhi din the ke yahi maaya-e-ra'naai tha
Naazish-e-mausam-e-gul laala-e-sahraai tha
Jo Musalmaan tha, Allah ka saudaai tha
Kabhi mehboob tumhara yahi harjaai tha
Kisi yakjaai se ab ahd-e-ghulaami kar lo
Millat-e-Ahmad-e-Mursal ko maqaami kar lo!

Kis qadar tum pe garaan subah ki bedaari hai
Hum se kab pyaar hai! Haan neend tumhein pyaari hai
Taba-e-aazaad pe qaid-e-Ramzaan bhaari hai
Tumhi keh do yahi aaeen-e-wafaadari hai?
Qaum mazhab se hai, mazhab jo nahin, tum bhi nahin
Jazb-e-baaham jo nahin, mehfil-e-anjum bhi nahin

Jin ko aata nahin duniya mein koi fan, tum ho
Nahin jis qaum ko parwaa-e-nasheman, tum ho
Bijliyaan jis mein hon aasoodah, woh khirman tum ho
Bech khaate hain jo aslaaf ke madfan, tum ho
Ho niko-naam jo qabron ki tijaarat karke
Kya na bechoge jo mil jaayein sanam patthar ke?

Safha-e-dehr se baatil ko mitaaya kis ne?
Nau-e-insaan ko ghulaami se chhudaaya kis ne?
Mere Kaabe ko jabeenon se basaaya kis ne?
Mere Qur'an ko seenon se lagaaya kis ne?
The toh aaba woh tumhaare hi, magar tum kya ho
Haath par haath dhare muntazir-e-farda ho!

Kya kaha! Bahr-e-Musalmaan hai faqat waada-e-hoor
Shikwa-e-beja bhi kare koi toh laazim hai sha'oor
Adl hai faatir-e-hasti ka azal se dastoor
Muslim-aa'een hua kaafir toh mile hoor-o-qusoor
Tum mein hooron ka koi chaahne waala hi nahin
Jalwa-e-Toor toh maujood hai, Moosa hi nahin

Manfa'at ek hai is qaum ki, nuqsaan bhi ek
Ek hi sab ka Nabi, Deen bhi, Imaan bhi ek
Haram-e-Paak bhi, Allah bhi, Qur'an bhi ek
Kuch badi baat thi hote jo Musalmaan bhi ek?
Firqa-bandi hai kahin aur kahin zaatein hain
Kya zamaane mein panapne ki yahi baatein hain?

Kaun hai taarik-e-aaeen-e-Rasool-e-Mukhtaar?
Maslihat waqt ki hai kis ke amal ka me'yaar?
Kis ki aankhon mein samaaya hai shi'aar-e-aghyaar?
Ho gayi kis ki nigeh tarz-e-salaf se bezaar?
Qalb mein soz nahin, rooh mein ehsaas nahin
Kuch bhi paighaam-e-Muhammad ka tumhein paas nahin

Jaake hote hain masaajid mein saf-aara, toh ghareeb
Zehmat-e-roza jo karte hain gawaara, toh ghareeb
Naam leta hai agar koi hamara, toh ghareeb
Parda rakhta hai agar koi tumhara, toh ghareeb
Umara nasha-e-daulat mein hain ghaafil hum se
Zinda hai millat-e-baiza ghareebon ke dam se

Waa'iz-e-qaum ki woh pukhta-khayaali na rahi
Barq-tab'ee na rahi, sho'la-maqaali na rahi
Reh gayi rasm-e-azaan, rooh-e-Bilaali na rahi
Falsafa reh gaya, talqeen-e-Ghazaali na rahi
Masjidein marsiya-khwaan hain ke namaazi na rahe
Yaani woh saahib-e-ausaaf-e-Hijaazi na rahe

Shor hai, ho gaye duniya se Musalmaan naabood
Hum yeh kehte hain ke the bhi kahin Muslim maujood!
Waza mein tum ho Nasaara toh tamaddun mein Hunood
Yeh Musalmaan hain! Jinhein dekh ke sharmaayein Yahood
Yoon toh Syed bhi ho, Mirza bhi ho, Afghan bhi ho
Tum sabhi kuch ho, batao toh Musalmaan bhi ho!

Dam-e-taqreer thi Muslim ki sadaaqat be-baak
Adl uska tha qawi, laus-e-maraa'aat se paak
Shajar-e-fitrat-e-Muslim tha haya se nam-naak
Tha shujaa'at mein woh ek hasti-e-fauq-ul-idraak
Khud-gudaazi nam-e-kaifiyat-e-sehbaayash bood
Khaali az khwesh shudan soorat-e-meenaayash bood

Har Musalmaan rag-e-baatil ke liye nashtar tha
Us ke aaina-e-hasti mein amal jauhar tha
Jo bharosa tha use quwwat-e-baazu par tha
Hai tumhein maut ka dar, us ko Khuda ka dar tha
Baap ka ilm na bete ko agar azbar ho
Phir pisar qaabil-e-meeraas-e-pidar kyun-kar ho!

Har koi mast-e-mai-e-zauq-e-tan-aasaani hai
Tum Musalmaan ho! Yeh andaaz-e-Musulmaani hai!
Haidari faqr hai ne daulat-e-Usmaani hai
Tum ko aslaaf se kya nisbat-e-roohaani hai?
Woh zamaane mein mo'azzaz the Musalmaan ho kar
Aur tum khwaar hue taarik-e-Qur'an ho kar

Tum ho aapas mein ghazab-naak, woh aapas mein raheem
Tum khata-kaar-o-khata-been, woh khata-posh-o-kareem
Chaahte sab hain ke hon auj-e-surayya pe muqeem
Pehle waisa koi paida toh kare qalb-e-saleem
Takht-e-Faghfoor bhi un ka tha, sareer-e-Kay bhi
Yoon hi baatein hain ke tum mein woh hamiyyat hai bhi?

Khud-kushi shewa tumhara, woh ghayoor-o-khud-daar
Tum ukhuwwat se gurezaan, woh ukhuwwat pe nisaar
Tum ho guftaar saraapa, woh saraapa kirdaar
Tum taraste ho kali ko, woh gulistaan ba-kinaar
Ab talak yaad hai qaumon ko hikaayat un ki
Naqsh hai safha-e-hasti pe sadaaqat un ki

Misl-e-anjum ufaq-e-qaum pe raushan bhi hue
But-e-Hindi ki mohabbat mein Barahman bhi hue
Shauq-e-parwaaz mein mehjoor-e-nasheman bhi hue
Be-amal the hi jawaan, deen se bad-zan bhi hue
Un ko tehzeeb ne har band se aazaad kiya
Laa ke Kaabe se sanam-khaane mein aabaad kiya

Qais zehmat-kash-e-tanhaai-e-sehra na rahe
Shehr ki khaaye hawa, baadiya-paimaa na rahe
Woh toh deewana hai, basti mein rahe ya na rahe
Yeh zaroori hai hijaab-e-rukh-e-Laila na rahe!
Gila-e-jaur na ho, shikwa-e-bedaad na ho
Ishq aazaad hai, kyun husn bhi aazaad na ho!

Ahd-e-nau barq hai, aatish-zan-e-har khirman hai
Aiman is se koi sehra na koi gulshan hai
Is nayi aag ka aqwaam-e-kuhan eendhan hai
Millat-e-Khatm-e-Rusul sho'la ba-pairaahan hai
Aaj bhi ho jo Ibrahim ka imaan paida
Aag kar sakti hai andaaz-e-gulistaan paida

Dekh kar rang-e-chaman ho na pareshaan maali
Kaukab-e-ghuncha se shaakhein hain chamakne waali
Khas-o-khaashaak se hota hai gulistaan khaali
Gul bar-andaaz hai khoon-e-shuhada ki laali
Rang gardoon ka zara dekh toh unnaabi hai
Yeh nikalte hue suraj ki ufaq-taabi hai

Ummatein gulshan-e-hasti mein samar-cheeda bhi hain
Aur mehroom-e-samar bhi hain, khizaan-deeda bhi hain
Sainkron nakhl hain, kaaheeda bhi, baaleeda bhi hain
Sainkron batn-e-chaman mein abhi posheeda bhi hain
Nakhl-e-Islam namoona hai baroomandi ka
Phal hai yeh sainkron sadiyon ki chaman-bandi ka

Paak hai gard-e-watan se sar-e-daamaan tera
Tu woh Yusuf hai ke har Misr hai Kan'aan tera
Qaafila ho na sakega kabhi veeraan tera
Ghair-e-yak baang-e-dara kuch nahin saamaan tera
Nakhl-e-shama asti-o-dar sho'la dood resha-e-tu
Aaqibat-soz bawad saaya-e-andesha-e-tu

Tu na mit jaayega Iran ke mit jaane se
Nasha-e-mai ko ta'alluq nahin paimaane se
Hai ayaan yurish-e-Tataar ke afsaane se
Paasbaan mil gaye Kaabe ko sanam-khaane se
Kashti-e-haq ka zamaane mein sahaara tu hai
Asr-e-nau raat hai, dhundla sa sitaara tu hai

Hai jo hungama bapa yurish-e-Bulghaari ka
Ghafilon ke liye paighaam hai bedaari ka
Tu samajhta hai yeh saamaan hai dil-aazaari ka
Imtihaan hai tere isaar ka, khud-daari ka
Kyun haraasaan hai saheel-e-faras-e-a'ada se
Noor-e-haq bujh na sakega nafas-e-a'ada se

Chashm-e-aqwaam se makhfi hai haqeeqat teri
Hai abhi mehfil-e-hasti ko zaroorat teri
Zinda rakhti hai zamaane ko haraarat teri
Kaukab-e-qismat-e-imkaan hai khilaafat teri
Waqt-e-fursat hai kahaan, kaam abhi baaqi hai
Noor-e-Tauheed ka itmaam abhi baaqi hai

Misl-e-boo qaid hai ghunche mein, pareshaan hoja
Rakht-bar-dosh-e-hawaa-e-chamanistaan hoja
Hai tanak-maaya toh zarre se bayaabaan hoja
Naghma-e-mauj se hungaama-e-toofaan hoja!
Quwwat-e-ishq se har past ko baala kar de
Dehr mein ism-e-Muhammad se ujaala kar de

Ho na yeh phool toh bulbul ka tarannum bhi na ho
Chaman-e-dehr mein kaliyon ka tabassum bhi na ho
Yeh na saaqi ho toh phir mai bhi na ho, khum bhi na ho
Bazm-e-Tauheed bhi duniya mein na ho, tum bhi na ho
Khaima aflaak ka istaada isi naam se hai
Nabz-e-hasti tapish-aamaada isi naam se hai

Dasht mein, daaman-e-kohsaar mein, maidaan mein hai
Behr mein, mauj ki aaghosh mein, toofaan mein hai
Cheen ke shehr, Maraakash ke bayaabaan mein hai
Aur posheeda Musalmaan ke imaan mein hai
Chashm-e-aqwaam yeh nazaara abad tak dekhe
Rif'at-e-shaan 'Rafa'naa laka zikrak' dekhe

Murdum-e-chashm-e-zameen ya'ni woh kaali duniya
Woh tumhaare shuhada paalne waali duniya
Garmi-e-mehr ki parwarda 'Hilaali' duniya
Ishq waale jise kehte hain 'Bilaali' duniya
Tapish-andoz hai is naam se paare ki tarah
Ghota-zan noor mein hai aankh ke taare ki tarah

Aql hai teri sipar, ishq hai shamsheer teri
Mere darwesh! Khilaafat hai jahaan-geer teri
Ma-siwa Allah ke liye aag hai Takbeer teri
Tu Musalmaan ho toh taqdeer hai tadbeer teri
Ki Muhammad se wafa tu ne toh hum tere hain
Yeh jahaan cheez hai kya, Lauh-o-Qalam tere hain
