Hwaet! We thaet hyrdon          haeleth eahtian,
deman daedhwate,          thaette in dagum gelamp
Maximianes,          se yeond middangeard,
arleas chyning,          eahtnysse ahof,
cwealde cristne men,          chirchan fylde,
yeat on graeswong godheryendra,
haethen hildfruma,          haligra blod,
ryhtfremmendra.          Waes his riche brad,
wid ond weorthlic          ofer wertheode,
lytesna ofer ealne          yrmenne grund.
Foron aefter burgum,          swa he biboden haefde,
thegnas thrythfulle. Oft hi thraece raerdon,
daedum yedwolene,          tha the dryhtnes ae
feodon thurh firencraeft.          Feondshype raerdon,
