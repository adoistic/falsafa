## Key passage (TextRank #1)
> [p-80f110] The Civil Code defines property as the right to enjoy and dispose of things in the most absolute manner, provided one does not make a use of them prohibited by laws or by regulations. It declares, moreover, that no one can be constrained to cede his property, except for cause of public utility, and by means of a just and prior indemnity [^315].
