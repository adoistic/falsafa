siyahi jaise gir jaaye dam-e-tahreer kaghaz par
meri qismat mein yun tasveer hai shab-haaye-hijraan ki

kahun kya garm-joshi mai-kashi mein shola-ruyan ki
ke shama-e-khana-e-dil aatish-e-mai se ferozan ki

hamesha mujh ko tifli mein bhi mashq-e-teera-rozi thi
siyahi hai mere ayyam mein lauh-e-dabistan ki

daregh aah-e-seher-gah kaar-e-baad-e-subah karti hai
ke hoti hai zyada sard-mehri shama-ruyan ki

mujhe apne junoon ki be-takalluf parda-daari thi
valekin kya karun aave jo ruswai garebaan ki

hunar paida kiya hai maine hairat-aazmai mein
ke jauhar aaine ka har palak hai chashm-e-hairaan ki

khudaya kis qadar ehl-e-nazar ne khaak chhaani hai
ke hain sad-rakhna jun-e-ghirbaal deewaren gulistan ki

hua sharm-e-tahi-dasti se woh bhi sar-nigun aakhir
bas ae zakhm-e-jigar ab dekh le shorish namak-daan ki

ba-yaad-e-garmi-e-suhbat ba-rang-e-shola dehke hai
chhipaun kyunkar Ghalib sozishen daagh-e-numayan ki
