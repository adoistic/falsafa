Dhamki mein mar gaya jo na baab-e-nabard tha
Ishq-e-nabard-pesha talab-gaar-e-mard tha

Tha zindagi mein marg ka khatka laga hua
Udne se peshtar bhi mera rang zard tha

Taaleef nuskha-haaye-wafa kar raha tha main
Majmua-e-khayal abhi fard fard tha

Dil ta jigar ke sahil-e-dariya-e-khoon hai ab
Us reh-guzar mein jalwa-e-gul aage gard tha

Jaati hai koi kashmakash andoh-e-ishq ki
Dil bhi agar gaya toh wohi dil ka dard tha

Ahbaab chaara-saazi-e-wehshat na kar sake
Zindaan mein bhi khayal bayabaan-navard tha

Yeh laash-e-be-kafan Asad-e-khasta-jaan ki hai
Haq maghfirat kare ajab azaad mard tha
