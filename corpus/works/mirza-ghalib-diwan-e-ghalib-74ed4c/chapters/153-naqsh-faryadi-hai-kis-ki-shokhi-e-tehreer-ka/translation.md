Naqsh faryadi hai kis ki shokhi-e-tehreer ka
Kaaghazi hai pairahan har paikar-e-tasveer ka

Kaav kaav-e-sakht-jaani haaye-tanhaai na pooch
Subah karna shaam ka, laana hai joo-e-sheer ka

Jazba-e-be-ikhtiyaar-e-shauq dekha chahiye
Seena-e-shamsheer se baahar hai dam shamsheer ka

Aagahi daam-e-shunidan jis qadar chaahe bichhaaye
Mudda'a anqa hai apne aalam-e-taqreer ka

Bas-ke hoon Ghalib aseeri mein bhi aatish-zer-paa
Moo-e-aatish deeda hai halqa meri zanjeer ka

Aatishin-paa hoon gudaaz-e-vahshat-e-zindaan na pooch
Moo-e-aatish deeda hai har halqa yaan zanjeer ka

Shokhi-e-nairang said-e-vahshat-e-taoos hai
Daam-e-sabza mein hai parvaaz-e-chaman taskheer ka

Lazzat-e-ijaad-e-naaz afsoon-e-arz-e-zauq-e-qatl
Na'l aatish mein hai tegh-e-yaar se nakhcheer ka

Khisht pusht-e-dast-e-ijz o qaalib aaghosh-e-vidaa
Pur hua hai sail se paimaana kis ta'ameer ka

Vahshat-e-khwaab-e-adam shor-e-tamasha hai Asad
Jo maza jauhar nahin aaina-e-ta'beer ka
