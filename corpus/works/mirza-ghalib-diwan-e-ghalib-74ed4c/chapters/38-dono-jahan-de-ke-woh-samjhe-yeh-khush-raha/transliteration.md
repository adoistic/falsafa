Dono jahan de ke woh samjhe yeh khush raha
Yaan aa padi yeh sharm ke takraar kya karein

Thak thak ke har maqaam pe do chaar reh gaye
Tera pata na paayein to naachaar kya karein

Kya shama ke nahin hain hawa-khwah bazm mein
Ho gham hi jaan-gudaaz to gham-khwaar kya karein
