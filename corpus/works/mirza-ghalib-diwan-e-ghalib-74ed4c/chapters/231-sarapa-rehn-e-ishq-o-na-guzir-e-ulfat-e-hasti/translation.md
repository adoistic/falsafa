sarapa rehn-e-ishq o na-guzir-e-ulfat-e-hasti
ibadat barq ki karta hoon aur afsos haasil ka

ba-qadr-e-zarf hai saqi khumar-e-tishna-kaami bhi
jo tu darya-e-mai hai toh main khamyaza hoon sahil ka

ze-bas khoon-gashta-e-rashk-e-wafa tha weham bismil ka
churaya zakhm-haaye-dil ne paani tegh-e-qatil ka

nigah-e-chashm-e-haasid vaam le ae zauq-e-khud-beeni
tamashai hoon wahdat-khana-e-aaina-e-dil ka

sharar-fursat nigah saaman-e-yak-aalam charaghan hai
ba-qadr-e-rang yaan gardish mein hai paimana mehfil ka

sarasar takhtan ko shash-jehat yak-arsa jaulan tha
hua waamandagi se reh-rawan ki farq manzil ka

mujhe raah-e-sukhan mein khauf-e-gum-raahi nahi Ghalib
asa-e-khizr-e-sahra-e-sukhan hai khama-e-bedil ka
