Masjid ke zer-e-saaya kharabaat chahiye
Bhon paas aankh qibla-e-haajaat chahiye
Aashiq hue hain aap bhi ek aur shakhs par
Aakhir sitam ki kuch toh makafaat chahiye
De daad ae falak dil-e-hasrat-parast ki
Haan kuch na kuch talafi-e-mafaat chahiye
Seekhe hain meh-rukhon ke liye hum musawwari
Taqreeb kuch toh behr-e-mulaqaat chahiye
Mai se gharaz nishaat hai kis roo-siyah ko
Ek-goona bekhudi mujhe din raat chahiye
Nashonuma hai asl se Ghalib furoo ko
Khaamoshi hi se nikle hai jo baat chahiye
Hai rang-e-lala-o-gul-o-nasreen juda juda
Har rang mein bahaar ka asbaat chahiye
Sar paaye-khum pe chahiye hangaam-e-bekhudi
Roo soo-e-qibla waqt-e-munaajaat chahiye
Yaani ba-hasb-e-gardish-e-paimana-e-sifaat
Aarif hamesha mast-e-mai-e-zaat chahiye
