Tapish se meri waqf-e-kashmakash har taar-e-bistar hai
Mera sar ranj-e-baaleen hai mera tan baar-e-bistar hai

Sarishk-e-sar ba-sahra daada noor-ul-ain-e-daaman hai
Dil-e-be-dast-o-pa uftaada bar-khurdaar-e-bistar hai

Khusha iqbal-e-ranjuri, iyaadat ko tum aaye ho
Farogh-e-shama-e-baaleen taale'-e-bedaar-e-bistar hai

Ba-toofaan-gaah-e-josh-e-iztiraab-e-shaam-e-tanhaai
Shua-e-aaftaab-e-subh-e-mahshar taar-e-bistar hai

Abhi aati hai bu baalish se us ki zulf-e-mushkeen ki
Hamaari deed ko khwaab-e-Zulekha aar-e-bistar hai

Kahun kya dil ki kya haalat hai hijr-e-yaar mein Ghalib
Ke betaabi se har-yak taar-e-bistar khaar-e-bistar hai
