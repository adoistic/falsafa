Zikr us pari-vash ka aur phir bayaan apna
Ban gaya raqeeb aakhir tha jo raazdaan apna

May woh kyun bahut peete bazm-e-ghair mein ya rab
Aaj hi hua manzoor un ko imtihaan apna

Manzar ek bulandi par aur hum bana sakte
Arsh se udhar hota kaash ke makaan apna

De woh jis qadar zillat hum hansi mein taalein-ge
Baare aashna nikla unka paasbaan apna

Dard-e-dil likhoon kab tak jaaun unko dikhla doon
Ungliyaan fagaar apni khaama khoonchakaan apna

Ghiste ghiste mit jaata aap ne abas badla
Nang-e-sajda se mere sang-e-aastaan apna

Ta kare na ghammaazi kar liya hai dushman ko
Dost ki shikaayat mein humne hum-zubaan apna

Hum kahaan ke daana the kis hunar mein yakta the
Be-sabab hua Ghalib dushman aasmaan apna
