Bask-e dushwaar hai har kaam ka aasaan hona
Aadmi ko bhi mayassar nahi insaan hona

Giriya chaahe hai kharaabi mere kaashaane ki
Dar-o-deewaar se tapke hai bayaabaan hona

Waaye deewangi-e-shauq ke har dam mujh ko
Aap jaana udhar aur aap hi hairaan hona

Jalwa az-bas-ke taqaaza-e-nigah karta hai
Jauhar-e-aaina bhi chaahe hai mizhgaan hona

Ishrat-e-qatl-gah-e-ahl-e-tamanna mat poochh
Eid-e-nazzaara hai shamsheer ka uryaan hona

Le gaye khaak mein hum daagh-e-tamanna-e-nishaat
Tu ho aur aap ba-sad-rang-e-gulistaan hona

Ishrat-e-paara-e-dil, zakhm-e-tamanna khaana
Lazzat-e-reesh-e-jigar, gharq-e-namakdaan hona

Ki mere qatl ke baad uss ne jafa se tauba
Haaye uss zood-e-pashemaan ka pashemaan hona

Haif uss chaar girah kapde ki qismat Ghalib
Jis ki qismat mein ho aashiq ka garebaan hona
