Kisi ko de ke dil koi nawasanj-e-fughan kyun ho
Na ho jab dil hi seene mein toh phir munh mein zubaan kyun ho

Woh apni khoo na chhorrein-ge hum apni waza kyun chhorrein
Subuk-sar ban ke kya poochein ke hum se sargiraan kyun ho

Kiya gham-khwaar ne ruswa, lage aag is mohabbat ko
Na laawe taab jo gham ki woh mera raazdaan kyun ho

Wafa kaisi kahan ka ishq, jab sar phorna thehra
Toh phir ae sang-dil tera hi sang-e-aastaan kyun ho

Qafas mein mujh se roodaad-e-chaman kehte na dar humdum
Giri hai jis pe kal bijli woh mera aashiyaan kyun ho

Yeh keh sakte ho hum dil mein nahi hain par yeh batlao
Ke jab dil mein tumhi tum ho toh aankhon se nihan kyun ho

Ghalat hai jazb-e-dil ka shikwa, dekho jurm kis ka hai
Na kheencho gar tum apne ko kashakash darmiyaan kyun ho

Yeh fitna aadmi ki khaana-weerani ko kya kam hai
Huye tum dost jis ke dushman uss ka aasmaan kyun ho

Yahi hai aazmaana toh sataana kis ko kehte hain
Adoo ke ho liye jab tum toh mera imtihaan kyun ho

Kaha tum ne ke kyun ho ghair ke milne mein ruswai
Baja kehte ho, sach kehte ho, phir kehiyo ke haan kyun ho

Nikaalna chahta hai kaam kya taanon se tu Ghalib
Tere be-mehr kehne se woh tujh par meherbaan kyun ho
