Ek ek qatre ka mujhe dena pada hisaab
Khoon-e-jigar vadeeyat-e-mizhgaan-e-yaar tha

Ab main hoon aur maatam-e-yak-sheher-e-aarzu
Toda jo tu ne aaina timsaal-daar tha

Galiyon mein meri naash ko kheenche phiro ke main
Jaan-daad-e-hawa-e-sar-e-rahguzaar tha

Mauj-e-saraab-e-dasht-e-wafa ka na pooch haal
Har zarra misl-e-jauhar-e-tegh aab-daar tha

Kam jaante the hum bhi gham-e-ishq ko par ab
Dekha toh kam hue pe gham-e-rozgaar tha

Kis ka junoon-e-deed tamanna-shikaar tha
Aaina-khaana vaadi-e-jauhar-ghubaar tha

Kis ka khayaal aaina-e-intezaar tha
Har barg-e-gul ke parde mein dil be-qaraar tha

Jyun ghuncha-o-gul aafat-e-faal-e-nazar na pooch
Paikaan se tere jalwa-e-zakhm aashkaar tha

Dekhi wafa-e-fursat-e-ranj-o-nishaat-e-dehr
Khamyaaza yak-daraazi-e-umr-e-khumaar tha

Subh-e-qayaamat ek dam-e-gurg thi 'Asad'
Jis dasht mein woh shokh-e-do-aalam shikaar tha
