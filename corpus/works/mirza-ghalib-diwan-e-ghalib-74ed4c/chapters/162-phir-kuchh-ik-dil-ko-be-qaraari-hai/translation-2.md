Phir kuchh ik dil ko be-qaraari hai
Seena joya-e-zakhm-e-kaari hai

Phir jigar khodne laga naakhun
Aamad-e-fasl-e-laala-kaari hai

Qibla-e-maqsad-e-nigaah-e-niyaaz
Phir wohi parda-e-amaari hai

Chashm dallaal-e-jins-e-ruswaai
Dil kharidaar-e-zauq-e-khwaari hai

Wohi sad-rang naala-farsaai
Wohi sad-goona ashk-baari hai

Dil hawa-e-khiraam-e-naaz se phir
Mehsharistaan-e-beqaraari hai

Jalwa phir arz-e-naaz karta hai
Roz bazaar-e-jaan-sipaari hai

Phir usi be-wafa pe marte hain
Phir wohi zindagi hamaari hai

Phir khula hai dar-e-adaalat-e-naaz
Garm-bazaar-e-faujdaari hai

Ho raha hai jahaan mein andher
Zulf ki phir sarishta-daari hai

Phir diya paara-e-jigar ne sawaal
Ek faryaad o aah-o-zaari hai

Phir hue hain gawaah-e-ishq talab
Ashk-baari ka hukm-jaari hai

Dil o mizhgaan ka jo muqaddama tha
Aaj phir uss ki roobkaari hai

Be-khudi be-sabab nahin Ghalib
Kuchh to hai jis ki parda-daari hai
