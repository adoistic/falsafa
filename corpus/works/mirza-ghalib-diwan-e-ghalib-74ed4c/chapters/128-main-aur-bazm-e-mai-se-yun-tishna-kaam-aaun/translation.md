Main aur bazm-e-mai se yun tishna-kaam aaun
Gar main ne ki thi tauba saaqi ko kya hua tha

Hai ek teer jis mein donon chhide pade hain
Woh din gaye ke apna dil se jigar juda tha

Darmaandagi mein Ghalib kuch ban pade to jaanuun
Jab rishta be-girah tha naakhun girah-kusha tha
