The rival’s sweet speech has banished every care;
A thought of our love, the silent, is not there.

True self-control is freedom, light as air;
A mirrored form, a trace of wetness is not there.

To shatter joyful gatherings brings despair;
In broken glass, aught but a dagger's point is not there.

To speak the heart is to open a door, be aware;
The pen's soft scratch, aught but the door's creak, is not there.
