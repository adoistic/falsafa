Ho gayi hai ghair ki sheereen-bayani kaargar
Ishq ka us ko gumaan hum be-zubaanon par nahin

Zabt se matlab bajuz waarastagi deegar nahin
Daaman-e-timsaal aab-e-aaina se tar nahin

Baais-e-eeza hai barham-khurdan-e-bazm-e-suroor
Lakht-lakht-e-sheesha-e-bashikasta juz nashtar nahin

Dil ko izhaar-e-sukhan andaaz-e-fatah-ul-baab hai
Yaan sareer-e-khaama ghair-az-istakaak-e-dar nahin
