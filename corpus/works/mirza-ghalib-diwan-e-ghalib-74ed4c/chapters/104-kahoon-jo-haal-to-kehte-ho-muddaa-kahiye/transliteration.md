Kahoon jo haal to kehte ho mudda'a kahiye
Tumheen kaho ke jo tum yun kaho to kya kahiye

Na kahiyo ta'an se phir tum ke hum sitam-gar hain
Mujhe to khoo hai ke jo kuch kaho baja kahiye

Voh neshtar sahi par dil mein jab utar jaave
Nigaah-e-naaz ko phir kyun na aashna kahiye

Nahin zariya-e-raahat jaraahat-e-paikaan
Voh zakhm-e-tegh hai jis ko ke dil-kusha kahiye

Jo mudda'i bane us ke na mudda'i baniye
Jo na-saza kahe us ko na na-saza kahiye

Kaheen haqeeqat-e-jaan-kaahi-e-marz likhiye
Kaheen museebat-e-na-saazi-e-dawa kahiye

Kabhi shikayat-e-ranj-e-giraan-nisheen keeje
Kabhi hikayat-e-sabr-e-garez-pa kahiye

Rahe na jaan to qaatil ko khoon-baha deeje
Kate zubaan to khanjar ko marhaba kahiye

Nahin nigaar ko ulfat na ho nigaar to hai
Ravaani-e-ravish o masti-e-ada kahiye

Nahin bahaar ko fursat na ho bahaar to hai
Taraavat-e-chaman o khoobi-e-hawa kahiye

Safeena jab ke kinaare pe aa laga Ghalib
Khuda se kya sitam-o-jaur-e-na-khuda kahiye
