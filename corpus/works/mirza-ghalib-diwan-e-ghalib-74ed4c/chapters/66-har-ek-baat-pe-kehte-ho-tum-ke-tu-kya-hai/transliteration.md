Har ek baat pe kehte ho tum ke tu kya hai
Tumhein kaho ke yeh andaaz-e-guftugu kya hai
Na shole mein yeh karishma na barq mein yeh ada
Koi batao ke woh shokh-e-tund-khu kya hai
Yeh rashk hai ke woh hota hai hum-sukhan tum se
Wagarna khauf-e-bad-amozi-e-adoo kya hai
Chipak raha hai badan par lahu se pairahan
Hamare jaib ko ab haajat-e-rafu kya hai
Jala hai jism jahan dil bhi jal gaya hoga
Kuredte ho jo ab raakh justuju kya hai
Ragon mein daudte phirne ke hum nahin qaail
Jab aankh hi se na tapka toh phir lahu kya hai
Woh cheez jis ke liye hum ko ho behisht azeez
Siwaaye baada-e-gulfaam-e-mushk-boo kya hai
Piyun sharab agar khum bhi dekh loon do-chaar
Yeh sheesha-o-qadah-o-kooza-o-suboo kya hai
Rahi na taaqat-e-guftaar aur agar ho bhi
Toh kis ummeed pe kahiye ke aarzoo kya hai
Hua hai sheh ka musahib phire hai itraata
Wagarna sheher mein Ghalib ki aabru kya hai
