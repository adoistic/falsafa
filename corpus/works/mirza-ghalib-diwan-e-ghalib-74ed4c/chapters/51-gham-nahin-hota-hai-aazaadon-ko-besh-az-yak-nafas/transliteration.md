Gham nahin hota hai aazaadon ko besh az-yak-nafas
Barq se karte hain roshan shama-e-maatam-khana hum

Mehfilein barham kare hai ganjafa-baaz-e-khayal
Hain warq-gardani-e-nairang-e-yak-but-khana hum

Bawajood-e-yak-jahan hungama paidaai nahin
Hain charaghan-e-shabistan-e-dil-e-parwana hum

Zoaf se hai ne qanaat se yeh tark-e-justujoo
Hain wabaal-e-takia-gaah-e-himmat-e-mardana hum

Daaim-ul-habs is mein hain laakhon tamannayein Asad
Jaante hain seena-e-pur-khoon ko zindaan-khana hum

Baske hain bad-mast-e-bishkan bishkan-e-mai-khana hum
Moo-e-sheesha ko samajhte hain khat-e-paimana hum

Baske har-yak-moo-e-zulf-afshaan se hai taar-e-shuaa
Panja-e-khursheed ko samjhe hain dast-e-shaana hum

Mashq-e-az-khud-raftagi se hain ba-gulzar-e-khayal
Aashna taabeer-e-khwab-e-sabza-e-begana hum

Fart-e-be-khwaabi se hain shab-haaye-hijr-e-yaar mein
Joon zabaan-e-shama daagh-e-garmi-e-afsana hum

Shaam-e-gham mein soz-e-ishq-e-aatish-rukhsar se
Par-fishaan-e-sokhtan hain soorat-e-parwana hum

Hasrat-e-arz-e-tamanna yaan se samjha chahiye
Do-jahan hashr-e-zabaan-e-khushk hain joon shaana hum

Kishti-e-aalam ba-toofaan-e-taghaful de ke hain
Aalam-e-aab-e-gudaaz-e-jauhar-e-afsana hum

Wehshat-e-be-rabti-e-pech-o-kham-e-hasti na poochh
Nang-e-baalidan hain joon moo-e-sar-e-deewana hum
