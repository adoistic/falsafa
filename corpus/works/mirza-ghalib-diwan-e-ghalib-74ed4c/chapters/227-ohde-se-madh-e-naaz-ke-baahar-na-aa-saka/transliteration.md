ohde se madh-e-naaz ke baahar na aa saka
gar ik adaa ho to usey apni qazaa kahoon

halqe hain chashm-haaye-kushaada ba-suu-e-dil
har taar-e-zulf ko nigaah-e-surma-sa kahoon

main aur sad-hazaar nava-e-jigar-kharaash
tu aur ek woh na-shunidan keh kya kahoon

zaalim mere gumaan se mujhe munfa'il na chaah
hai hai khuda-na-karda tujhe bewafa kahoon
