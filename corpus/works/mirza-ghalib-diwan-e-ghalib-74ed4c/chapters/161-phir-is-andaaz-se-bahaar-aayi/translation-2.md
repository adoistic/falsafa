Phir is andaaz se bahaar aayi
Ke hue mehr-o-mah tamaashai

Dekho ae saakinan-e-khitta-e-khaak
Is ko kehte hain aalam-aarai

Ke zameen ho gayi hai sar-ta-sar
Rukash-e-satah-e-charkh-e-meenai

Sabzah ko jab kahin jagah na mili
Ban gaya ru-e-aab par kaai

Sabzah o gul ke dekhne ke liye
Chashm-e-nargis ko di hai beenai

Hai hawa mein sharaab ki taaseer
Baada-noshi hai baada-paimai

Kyun na duniya ko ho khushi Ghalib
Shah-e-deen-daar ne shifa paai
