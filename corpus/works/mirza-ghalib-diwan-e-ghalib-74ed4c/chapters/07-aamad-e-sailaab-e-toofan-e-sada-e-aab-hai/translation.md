The flood's approach is in the water's thundering sound,
The path is deafened by the footprint on the ground, from this.

Whose drunken eyes have made this feast a waste profound?
A fairy's pulse in wine's deep, swirling current's wound, from this.

My wild desire, a passion roaring and unbound,
Reads my disgrace in tears that on the sands are found, from this.

A trap, if hidden in the green, with beauty crowned,
Becomes a peacock, by spring's magic spell unwound, from this.

Now Layla's tent is black, Majnun's home a barren mound,
Of ruin, from a love whose wound was world-renowned, from this.

This life, Asad, is a great spectacle profound,
We see through eyes from sleep of nothingness still bound, from this.
