Aamad-e-sailaab-e-toofan-e-sada-e-aab hai
Naqsh-e-paa jo kaan mein rakhta hai ungli jaadah se

Bazm-e-mai vehshat-kadah hai kis ki chashm-e-mast ka
Sheeshe mein nabz-e-pari pinhaan hai mauj-e-baadah se

Dekhta hoon vehshat-e-shauq-e-khurosh-aamadah se
Faal-e-ruswaai sirishk-e-sar-ba-sahra-daadah se

Daam gar sabze mein pinhaan keejiye taa'oos ho
Josh-e-nairang-e-bahaar arz-e-sahra-daadah se

Khaima-e-Laila siyaah o khaana-e-Majnoon kharaab
Josh-e-veeraani hai ishq-e-daagh-e-beroon-daadah se

Bazm-e-hasti woh tamasha hai ke jiss ko hum Asad
Dekhte hain chashm-az-khwaab-e-adam-nakushaadah se
