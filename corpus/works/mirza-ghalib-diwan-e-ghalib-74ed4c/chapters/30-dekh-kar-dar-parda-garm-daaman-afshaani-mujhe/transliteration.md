Dekh kar dar-parda garm-daaman-afshaani mujhe
Kar gayi waabasta-e-tan meri uryaani mujhe

Ban gaya tegh-e-nigaah-e-yaar ka sang-e-fasaan
Marhaba main kya mubarak hai giraan-jaani mujhe

Kyun na ho be-iltifaati us ki khaatir jama hai
Jaanta hai mehv-e-pursish-haaye-pinhaani mujhe

Mere gham-khaane ki qismat jab raqam hone lagi
Likh diya minjumla-e-asbaab-e-veeraani mujhe

Bad-gumaan hota hai woh kaafir na hota kaash ke
Is qadar zauq-e-nawa-e-murgh-e-bustaani mujhe

Waaye waan bhi shor-e-mehshar ne na dam lene diya
Le gaya tha gor mein zauq-e-tan-aasaani mujhe

Waada aane ka wafa keeje yeh kya andaaz hai
Tum ne kyun saunpi hai mere ghar ki darbaani mujhe

Haan nishaat-e-aamad-e-fasl-e-bahaari waah waah
Phir hua hai taaza sauda-e-ghazal-khwaani mujhe

Di mere bhai ko Haq ne az_sar_e_nau zindagi
Mirza Yusuf hai Ghalib Yusuf-e-saani mujhe
