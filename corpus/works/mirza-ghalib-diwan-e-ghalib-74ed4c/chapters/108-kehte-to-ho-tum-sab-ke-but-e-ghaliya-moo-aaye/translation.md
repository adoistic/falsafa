Kehte to ho tum sab ke but-e-ghaliya-moo aaye
Yak martaba ghabra ke kaho koi ke woh aaye

Hoon kashmakash-e-naza mein haan jazb-e-mohabbat
Kuch keh na sakoon par woh mere poochne ko aaye

Hai sa'iqa o shola o seemaab ka aalam
Aana hi samajh mein meri aata nahi go aaye

Zahir hai ke ghabra ke na bhaagein-ge nakirein
Haan munh se magar baada-e-dosheena ki boo aaye

Jallad se darte hain na waaiz se jhagadte
Hum samjhe hue hain usey jis bhes mein jo aaye

Haan ahl-e-talab kaun sune taana-e-nayaaft
Dekha ke woh milta nahi apne hi ko kho aaye

Apna nahi yeh sheva ke aaraam se baithein
Us dar pe nahi baar toh Kaaba hi ko ho aaye

Ki ham-nafason ne asar-e-girya mein taqreer
Achhe rahe aap is se magar mujh ko dubo aaye

Is anjuman-e-naaz ki kya baat hai Ghalib
Hum bhi gaye waan aur teri taqdeer ko ro aaye
