Bahut sahi gham-e-geeti sharaab kam kya hai
Ghulam-e-saaqi-e-kausar hoon mujh ko gham kya hai

Tumhaari tarz-o-ravish jaante hain hum kya hai
Raqeeb par hai agar lutf to sitam kya hai

Sukhan mein khaama-e-Ghalib ki aatish-afshaani
Yaqeen hai hum ko bhi lekin ab us mein dam kya hai

Kate to shab kaheen kaate to saanp kehlaawe
Koi batao ke woh zulf-e-kham-ba-kham kya hai

Likha kare koi ehkaam-e-taala-e-maulood
Kise khabar hai ke waan jumbish-e-qalam kya hai

Na hashr-o-nashr ka qaayil na kaish-o-millat ka
Khuda ke waaste aise ki phir qasam kya hai

Woh daad-o-deed giraan-maaya shart hai hum-dum
Wagarna mohr-e-Suleiman-o-jaam-e-Jam kya hai
