bala se hain jo yeh pesh-e-nazar dar-o-deewar
nigah-e-shauq ko hain baal-o-par dar-o-deewar

wufoor-e-ashk ne kaashaane ka kiya yeh rang
ke ho gaye mere deewar-o-dar dar-o-deewar

nahin hai saaya ke sun kar naveed-e-maqdam-e-yaar
gaye hain chand qadam peshtar dar-o-deewar

hui hai kis qadar arzaani-e-mai-e-jalwa
ke mast hai tere kooche mein har dar-o-deewar

jo hai tujhe sar-e-sauda-e-intezaar toh aa
ke hain dukaan-e-mata-e-nazar dar-o-deewar

hujoom-e-girya ka saamaan kab kiya main ne
ke gir pade na mere paaon par dar-o-deewar

woh aa raha mere hamsaaye mein toh saaye se
huye fida dar-o-deewar par dar-o-deewar

nazar mein khatke hai bin tere ghar ki aabadi
hamesha rote hain hum dekh kar dar-o-deewar

na pooch be-khudi-e-aish-e-maqdam-e-sailaab
ke naachte hain pade sar-ba-sar dar-o-deewar

na keh kisi se ke Ghalib nahin zamaane mein
hareef-e-raaz-e-mohabbat magar dar-o-deewar
