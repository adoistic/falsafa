Shab keh barq-e-soz-e-dil se zehra-e-abr aab tha
Shola-e-jawwala har yak halqa-e-girdaab tha

Waan karam ko uzr-e-baarish tha inaan-geer-e-khiraam
Girya se yaan pamba-e-baalish kaf-e-sailaab tha

Waan khud-aaraai ko tha moti pirone ka khayaal
Yaan hujoom-e-ashk mein taar-e-nigah naayaab tha

Jalwa-e-gul ne kiya tha waan charaghaan aab-e-joo
Yaan rawaan mizhgaan-e-chashm-e-tar se khoon-e-naab tha

Yaan sar-e-pur-shor be-khwaabi se tha deewaar-joo
Waan woh farq-e-naaz mehv-e-baalish-e-kamkhwaab tha

Yaan nafas karta tha roshan shama-e-bazm-e-be-khudi
Jalwa-e-gul waan bisaat-e-sohbat-e-ahbaab tha

Farsh se ta arsh waan toofaan tha mauj-e-rang ka
Yaan zameen se aasmaan tak sokhtan ka baab tha

Naagahaan is rang se khoon-naaba tapkaane laga
Dil keh zauq-e-kaawish-e-naakhun se lazzat-yaab tha
