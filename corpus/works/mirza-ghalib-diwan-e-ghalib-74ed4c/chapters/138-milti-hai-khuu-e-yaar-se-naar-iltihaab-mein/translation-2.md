Milti hai khuu-e-yaar se naar iltihaab mein
Kaafir hoon gar na milti ho raahat azaab mein

Kab se hoon kya bataaun jahaan-e-kharaab mein
Shab-haaye-hijr ko bhi rakhoon gar hisaab mein

Taa phir na intezaar mein neend aaye umr bhar
Aane ka ahd kar gaye aaye jo khwaab mein

Qaasid ke aate aate khat ik aur likh rakhoon
Main jaanta hoon jo woh likhenge jawaab mein

Mujh tak kab unki bazm mein aata tha daur-e-jaam
Saaqi ne kuch mila na diya ho sharaab mein

Jo munkir-e-wafa ho fareb us pe kya chale
Kyun bad-gumaan hoon dost se dushman ke baab mein

Main muztarib hoon vasl mein khauf-e-raqeeb se
Daala hai tum ko waham ne kis pech-o-taab mein

Main aur hazz-e-vasl khuda-saaz baat hai
Jaan nazr deni bhool gaya iztiraab mein

Hai tevari chadhi hui andar naqaab ke
Hai ik shikan padi hui tarf-e-naqaab mein

Laakhon lagaao ek churaana nigaah ka
Laakhon banaao ek bigadna ataab mein

Woh naala dil mein khas ke baraabar jagah na paaye
Jis naale se shigaaf pade aaftaab mein

Woh sehar mudda'a-talabi mein na kaam aaye
Jis sehar se safeena ravaan ho saraab mein

Ghalib chuti sharaab par ab bhi kabhi kabhi
Peeta hoon roz-e-abr o shab-e-maahtaab mein
