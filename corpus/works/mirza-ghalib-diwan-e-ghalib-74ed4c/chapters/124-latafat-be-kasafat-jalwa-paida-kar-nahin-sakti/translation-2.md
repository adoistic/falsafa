Latafat be-kasafat jalwa paida kar nahin sakti
Chaman zangaar hai aaina-e-baad-e-bahaari ka

Hareef-e-joshish-e-dariya nahin khuddari-e-sahil
Jahaan saqi ho to baatil hai da'wa hoshiyari ka

Bahaar-e-rang-e-khoon-e-gul hai saamaan ashk-baari ka
Junoon-e-barq nashtar hai rag-e-abr-e-bahaari ka

Baraaye-hal-e-mushkil hoon za-paa uftaada-e-hasrat
Bandha hai uqda-e-khaatir se paimaan khaaksaari ka

Ba-waqt-e-sarnigooni hai tasavvur intezaaristaan
Nigah ko aablon se shughl hai akhtar-shumaari ka

Asad saaghar-kash-e-tasleem ho gardish se gardoon ki
Ke nang-e-fahm-e-mastaan hai gila bad-rozgaari ka
