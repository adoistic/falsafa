Aa ke meri jaan ko qarar nahin hai
Taaqat-e-bidaad-e-intezar nahin hai

Dete hain jannat hayat-e-dahr ke badle
Nasha ba-andaza-e-khumar nahin hai

Giriya nikaale hai teri bazm se mujh ko
Haaye ke rone pe ikhtiyar nahin hai

Hum se abas hai gumaan-e-ranjish-e-khatir
Khaak mein ashaaq ki ghubaar nahin hai

Dil se utha lutf-e-jalwa-haaye-maani
Ghair-e-gul aaina-e-bahaar nahin hai

Qatl ka mere kiya hai ahd to baare
Waaye agar ahd ustuwaar nahin hai

Tu ne qasam mai-kashi ki khaayi hai Ghalib
Teri qasam ka kuch aitbaar nahin hai
