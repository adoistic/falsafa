Huzoor-e-shah mein ahl-e-sukhan ki aazmaish hai
Chaman mein khush-nawaayan-e-chaman ki aazmaish hai

Qad o gesu mein Qais o Koh-kan ki aazmaish hai
Jahan hum hain wahan daar-o-rasan ki aazmaish hai

Karenge Koh-kan ke hausle ka imtihaan aakhir
Abhi is khasta ke neero-e-tan ki aazmaish hai

Naseem-e-Misr ko kya Peer-e-Kanaan ki hawa-khwahi
Use Yusuf ki boo-e-pairahan ki aazmaish hai

Woh aaya bazm mein dekho, na kahiyo phir ke ghafil thay
Shakeeb-o-sabr-e-ahl-e-anjuman ki aazmaish hai

Rahe dil hi mein teer achha, jigar ke paar ho behtar
Gharaz shast-e-but-e-naawak-figan ki aazmaish hai

Nahin kuch subha-o-zunnar ke phanday mein geeraai
Wafadari mein Sheikh o Barahman ki aazmaish hai

Pada reh ae dil-e-wabasta betaabi se kya haasil
Magar phir taab-e-zulf-e-pur-shikan ki aazmaish hai

Rag-o-pai mein jab utre zehr-e-gham tab dekhiye kya ho
Abhi toh talkhi-e-kaam-o-dahan ki aazmaish hai

Woh aawenge mere ghar, vaada kaisa, dekhna Ghalib
Naye fitnon mein ab charkh-e-kuhan ki aazmaish hai
