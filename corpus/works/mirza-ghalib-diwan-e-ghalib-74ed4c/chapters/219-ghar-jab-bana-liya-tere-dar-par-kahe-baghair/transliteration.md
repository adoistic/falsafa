ghar jab bana liya tere dar par kahe baghair
jaanega ab bhi tu na mera ghar kahe baghair

kehte hain jab rahi na mujhe taaqat-e-sukhan
jaanoon kisi ke dil ki main kyonkar kahe baghair

kaam us se aa pada hai ke jis ka jahaan mein
leve na koi naam sitam-gar kahe baghair

jee mein hi kuch nahi hai hamare wagarna hum
sar jaaye ya rahe na rahein par kahe baghair

chhodunga main na us but-e-kaafir ka poojna
chhode na khalq go mujhe kaafir kahe baghair

maqsad hai naaz-o-ghamza wale guftugu mein kaam
chalta nahi hai dushna-o-khanjar kahe baghair

har chand ho mushaahida-e-haq ki guftugu
banti nahi hai baada-o-saaghar kahe baghair

behra hoon main to chaahiye doona ho iltifaat
sunta nahi hoon baat mukarrar kahe baghair

Ghalib na kar huzoor mein tu baar baar arz
zaahir hai tera haal sab un par kahe baghair
