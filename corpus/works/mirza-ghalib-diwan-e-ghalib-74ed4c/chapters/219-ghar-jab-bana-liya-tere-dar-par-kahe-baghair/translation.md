I’ve made my home right at your very door, without a single word;
Will you not know this house is mine, without a single word?

They say I’ve lost the strength to speak, my voice a hazy blur;
How can I know another’s heart, without a single word?

My fate is tied to one whose name the world will not endure,
Unless they call her ‘tyrant’ first, without a single word.

There's nothing in my heart to hide, of that you can be sure;
Or I'd have spoken, head or not, without a single word.

I won't give up the worship of that idol I adore,
Though all the world may call me faithless, without a single word.

Her aim is charm and coquetry, a glance and nothing more,
But talk soon turns to the dagger’s edge, without a single word.

Though we may speak of seeing Truth, and mystic paths explore,
The talk is lost without the wine-cup, without a single word.

I'm deaf, my love, so your attention I implore;
I only hear what's said twice over, without a single word.

Oh Ghalib, do not plead your case before her anymore;
Your state is clear for her to see, without a single word.
