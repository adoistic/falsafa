Main unhein chherun aur kuch na kahein
Chal nikalte jo mai piye hote

Qehar ho ya bala ho jo kuch ho
Kaash ke tum mere liye hote

Meri qismat mein gham gar itna tha
Dil bhi ya rab kayi diye hote

Aa hi jaata woh raah par Ghalib
Koi din aur bhi jiye hote
