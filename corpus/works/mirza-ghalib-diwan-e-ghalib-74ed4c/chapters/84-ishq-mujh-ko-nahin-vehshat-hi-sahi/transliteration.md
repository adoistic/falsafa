Ishq mujh ko nahin vehshat hi sahi
Meri vehshat teri shohrat hi sahi

Qata keeje na ta'alluq hum se
Kuch nahin hai to adaavat hi sahi

Mere hone mein hai kya rusvaai
Ae woh majlis nahin khalvat hi sahi

Hum bhi dushman toh nahin hain apne
Ghair ko tujh se mohabbat hi sahi

Apni hasti hi se ho jo kuch ho
Aagahi gar nahin ghaflat hi sahi

Umr har-chand ke hai barq-khiraam
Dil ke khoon karne ki fursat hi sahi

Hum koi tark-e-wafa karte hain
Na sahi ishq museebat hi sahi

Kuch toh de ae falak-e-na-insaaf
Aah-o-faryaad ki rukhsat hi sahi

Hum bhi tasleem ki khoo daalein-ge
Be-niyaazi teri aadat hi sahi

Yaar se chhed chali jaaye 'Asad'
Gar nahin wasl toh hasrat hi sahi
