Ishq taasir se na-umeed nahin
Jaan-supaari shajar-e-bed nahin

Saltanat dast-ba-dast aayi hai
Jaam-e-mai khaatam-e-jamshed nahin

Hai tajalli teri saamaan-e-wajood
Zarra be-partav-e-khursheed nahin

Raaz-e-maashooq na ruswa ho jaaye
Warna mar jaane mein kuch bhed nahin

Gardish-e-rang-e-tarab se dar hai
Gham-e-mehroomi-e-javed nahin

Kehte hain jeete hain ummeed pe log
Hum ko jeene ki bhi ummeed nahin
