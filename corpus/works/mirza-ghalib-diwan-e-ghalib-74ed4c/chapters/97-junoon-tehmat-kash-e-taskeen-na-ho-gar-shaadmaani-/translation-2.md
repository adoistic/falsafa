Junoon tehmat-kash-e-taskeen na ho gar shaadmaani ki
Namak-paash-e-kharaash-e-dil hai lazzat zindagani ki

Kashakash-haaye-hasti se kare kya sa'i-e-aazaadi
Hui zanjeer-e-mauj-e-aab ko fursat rawaani ki

Pas-az-murdan bhi deewana ziyarat-gaah-e-tiflaan hai
Sharaar-e-sang ne turbat pe meri gul-fishaani ki
