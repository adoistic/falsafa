Hum par jafa se tark-e-wafa ka gumaan nahi
Ik chhed hai wagarna muraad imtihaan nahi

Kis munh se shukr kijiye is lutf-e-khaas ka
Pursish hai aur paaye-sukhan darmiyaan nahi

Hum ko sitam azeez sitam-gar ko hum azeez
Na-mehrbaan nahi hai agar mehrbaan nahi

Bosa nahi na dijiye dushnaam hi sahi
Aakhir zabaan toh rakhte ho tum gar dahaan nahi

Har-chand jaan-gudaazi-e-qehr-o-itaab hai
Har-chand pusht-garmi-e-taab-o-tawaan nahi

Jaan mutrib-e-taraana-e-hal min mazeed hai
Lab pardah-sanj-e-zamzama-e-al-amaan nahi

Khanjar se cheer seena agar dil na ho do-neem
Dil mein chhuri chubho mizha gar khoon-chakaan nahi

Hai nang-e-seena dil agar aatish-kada na ho
Hai aar-e-dil nafas agar aazar-fishaan nahi

Nuqsaan nahi junoon mein bala se ho ghar kharaab
Sau gaz zameen ke badle bayaabaan giraan nahi

Kehte ho kya likha hai teri sar-nawisht mein
Goya jabeen pe sajda-e-but ka nishaan nahi

Paata hoon is se daad kuch apne kalaam ki
Rooh-ul-qudus agarche mera hum-zabaan nahi

Jaan hai bahaaye-bosa walay kyun kahe abhi
Ghalib ko jaanta hai ke woh neem-jaan nahi

Jis jaa ke paaye-sail-e-bala darmiyaan nahi
Deewaanagaan ko waan hawas-e-khaanmaan nahi

Gul ghunchagi mein gharqa-e-dariya-e-rang hai
Aye aagahi fareb-e-tamaasha kahaan nahi

Kis jurm se hai chashm tujhe hasrat qubool
Barg-e-hina magar mizha-e-khoon-fishaan nahi

Har rang-e-gardish aayina eejaad-e-dard hai
Ashk-e-sahaab juz ba-widaa-e-khizaan nahi

Juz ijz kya karoon ba-tamanna-e-be-khudi
Taaqat hareef-e-sakhti-e-khwaab-e-giraan nahi

Ibrat se poochh dard-e-pareshaani-e-nigaah
Yeh gard-e-weham juz basar-e-imtihaan nahi

Barq-e-bajaan-e-hausla aatish-figan Asad
Aye dil-fasurda taaqat-e-zabt-e-faghaan nahi
