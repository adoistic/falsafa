Aaina dekh apna sa munh le ke reh gaye
Sahab ko dil na dene pe kitna ghuroor tha

Qaasid ko apne haath se gardan na maariye
Us ki khata nahi hai yeh mera qusoor tha

Zof-e-junoon ko waqt-e-tapish dar bhi door tha
Ik ghar mein mukhtasar sa bayabaan zaroor tha

Ae waaye-ghaflat-nigah-e-shauq warna yaan
Har paara-e-sang lakht-e-dil-e-Koh-e-Toor tha

Dars-e-tapish hai barq ko ab jis ke naam se
Woh dil hai yeh ke jis ka takhallus saboor tha

Har rang mein jala Asad-e-fitna-e-intezaar
Parwana-e-tajalli-e-shama-e-zahoor tha

Shayad ke mar gaya tere rukhsaar dekh kar
Paimana raat maah ka labrez-e-noor tha

Jannat hai teri tegh ke kushton ki muntazir
Jauhar sawad-e-jalwa-e-mizhgan-e-hoor tha
