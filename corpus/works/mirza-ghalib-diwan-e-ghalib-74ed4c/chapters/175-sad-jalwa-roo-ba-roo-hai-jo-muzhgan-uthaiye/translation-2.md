Sad jalwa roo-ba-roo hai jo muzhgan uthaiye
Taqat kahan ke deed ka ehsaan uthaiye

Hai sang par baraat-e-maash-e-junoon-e-ishq
Yaani hanoz minnat-e-tiflaan uthaiye

Deewar baar-e-minnat-e-mazdoor se hai kham
Aye khanumaan-kharaab na ehsaan uthaiye

Ya mere zakhm-e-rashk ko ruswa na kijiye
Ya parda-e-tabassum-e-pinhaan uthaiye

Hasti fareb-naama-e-mauj-e-saraab hai
Yak umr naaz-e-shokhi-e-unwaan uthaiye
