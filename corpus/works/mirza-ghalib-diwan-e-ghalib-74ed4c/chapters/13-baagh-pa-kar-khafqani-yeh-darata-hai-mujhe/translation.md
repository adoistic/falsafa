Baagh pa kar khafqani yeh darata hai mujhe
Saya-e-shaakh-e-gul af'ii nazar aata hai mujhe

Jauhar-e-taigh ba-sar-e-chashma-e-deegar maloom
Hoon main woh sabza ke zehr-aab ugata hai mujhe

Mudda'a mahv-e-tamasha-e-shikast-e-dil hai
Aaina-khaane mein koi liye jaata hai mujhe

Nala sarmaya-e-yak-aalam o aalam kaf-e-khaak
Aasmaan baiza-e-qumri nazar aata hai mujhe

Zindagi mein toh woh mehfil se utha dete the
Dekhoon ab mar gaye par kaun uthata hai mujhe

Baagh tujh bin gul-e-nargis se darata hai mujhe
Chahoon gar sair-e-chaman aankh dikhata hai mujhe

Shor-e-timsaal hai kis rashk-e-chaman ka ya rab
Aaina baiza-e-bulbul nazar aata hai mujhe

Hairat-e-aaina anjaam-e-junoon hoon jiyoon shama
Kis qadar daagh-e-jigar shola uthata hai mujhe

Main hoon aur hairat-e-javed magar zauq-e-khayaal
Ba-fusoon-e-nigah-e-naaz satata hai mujhe

Hairat-e-fikr-e-sukhan saaz-e-salamat hai Asad
Dil pas-e-zaanu-e-aaina bithata hai mujhe
