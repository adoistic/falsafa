Gayi woh baat ke ho guftugu to kyun kar ho
Kahe se kuch na hua phir kaho to kyun kar ho

Hamare zehen mein is fikr ka hai naam visaal
Ke gar na ho to kahan jaayein, ho to kyun kar ho

Adab hai aur yahi kashmakash to kya keeje
Haya hai aur yahi go-mago to kyun kar ho

Tumhin kaho ke guzara sanam-paraston ka
Buton ki ho agar aisi hi khoo to kyun kar ho

Uljhate ho tum agar dekhte ho aaina
Jo tum se sheher mein hon ek do to kyun kar ho

Jise naseeb ho roz-e-siyah mera sa
Woh shakhs din na kahe raat ko to kyun kar ho

Hamein phir un se ummeed aur unhein hamari qadr
Hamari baat hi poochein na woh to kyun kar ho

Ghalat na tha hamein khat par gumaan tasalli ka
Na maane deeda-e-deedar joo to kyun kar ho

Batao us mizha ko dekh kar ke mujh ko qaraar
Woh nesh ho rag-e-jaan mein faro to kyun kar ho

Mujhe junoon nahin Ghalib walay baqaul-e-huzoor
Firaaq-e-yaar mein taskeen ho to kyun kar ho
