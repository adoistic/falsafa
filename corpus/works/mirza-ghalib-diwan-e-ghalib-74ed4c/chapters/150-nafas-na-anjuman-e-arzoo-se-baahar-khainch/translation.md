Nafas na anjuman-e-arzoo se baahar khainch
Agar sharaab nahin, intezaar-e-saaghar khainch

Kamaal-e-garmi-e-sa'i-e-talaash-e-deed na poochh
Ba-rang-e-khaar mere aaina se jauhar khainch

Tujhe bahaana-e-raahat hai intezaar ae dil
Kiya hai kis ne ishaara ke naaz-e-bistar khainch

Teri taraf hai ba-hasrat nazaara-e-nargis
Ba-kori-e-dil-o-chashm-e-raqeeb saaghar khainch

Ba-neem-ghamza adaa kar haq-e-vadeeat-e-naaz
Niyaam-e-parda-e-zakhm-e-jigar se khanjar khainch

Mere qadah mein hai sehba-e-aatish-e-pinhaan
Ba-roo-e-sufra kabaab-e-dil-e-samandar khainch

Na keh ke taaqat-e-ruswaai-e-visaal nahin
Agar yahi arq-e-fitna hai, mukarrar khainch

Junoon-e-aaina mushtaaq-e-yak-tamaasha hai
Humaare safhe pe baal-e-pari se mistar khainch

Khumaar-e-minnat-e-saaqi agar yahi hai 'Asad'
Dil-e-gudaakhta ke maikade mein saaghar khainch
