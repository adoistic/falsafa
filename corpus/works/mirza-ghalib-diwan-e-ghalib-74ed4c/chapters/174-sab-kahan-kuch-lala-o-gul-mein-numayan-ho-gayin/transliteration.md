Sab kahan kuch lala-o-gul mein numayan ho gayin
Khaak mein kya suratein hon-gi ke pinhan ho gayin

Yaad theen hum ko bhi ranga-rang bazm-araiyan
Lekin ab naqsh-o-nigaar-e-taaq-e-nisiyan ho gayin

Theen banat-un-naash-e-gardoon din ko parde mein nihan
Shab ko un ke jee mein kya aayi ke uriyan ho gayin

Qaid mein Yaqoob ne li go na Yusuf ki khabar
Lekin aankhein rozan-e-deewar-e-zindaan ho gayin

Sab raqeebon se hon na-khush par zanan-e-Misr se
Hai Zulekha khush ke mehv-e-mah-e-Kinaan ho gayin

Joo-e-khoon aankhon se behne do ke hai sham-e-firaaq
Main yeh samjhun-ga ke sham'ein do farozan ho gayin

In pari-zaadon se len-ge khuld mein hum inteqaam
Qudrat-e-haq se yahi hoorein agar waan ho gayin

Neend us ki hai dimaagh us ka hai raatein us ki hain
Teri zulfein jis ke baazu par pareshaan ho gayin

Main chaman mein kya gaya goya dabistaan khul gaya
Bulbulein sun kar mere naale ghazal-khwaan ho gayin

Woh nigahein kyun hui jaati hain ya Rab dil ke paar
Jo meri kotahi-e-qismat se mizhgaan ho gayin

Bas ke roka maine aur seene mein ubhreen pay-ba-pay
Meri aahein bakhiya-e-chaak-e-gareebaan ho gayin

Waan gaya bhi main toh un ki gaaliyon ka kya jawaab
Yaad theen jitni duayein sarf-e-darbaan ho gayin

Jaan-faza hai baada jis ke haath mein jaam aa gaya
Sab lakeerein haath ki goya rag-e-jaan ho gayin

Hum muwahhid hain hamara kaish hai tark-e-rusoom
Millatein jab mit gayin ajzaa-e-eemaan ho gayin

Ranj se khoogar hua insaan toh mit jaata hai ranj
Mushkilein mujh par padeen itni ke aasaan ho gayin

Yoon hi gar rota raha 'Ghalib' toh aye ehl-e-jahan
Dekhna in bastiyon ko tum ke veeraan ho gayin
