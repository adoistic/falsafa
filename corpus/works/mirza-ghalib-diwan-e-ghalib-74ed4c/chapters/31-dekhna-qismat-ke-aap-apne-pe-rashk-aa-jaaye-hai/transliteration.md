Dekhna qismat ke aap apne pe rashk aa jaaye hai
Main usey dekhun bhala kab mujh se dekha jaaye hai

Haath dho dil se yahi garmi gar andeshe mein hai
Aabgeena tundi-e-sahba se pighla jaaye hai

Ghair ko ya Rab woh kyun kar mana-e-gustakhi kare
Gar haya bhi uss ko aati hai toh sharma jaaye hai

Shauq ko yeh latt ke har dam naala kheenche jaaiye
Dil ki woh haalat ke dam lene se ghabra jaaye hai

Door chashm-e-bad teri bazm-e-tarab se waah waah
Naghma ho jaata hai waan gar naala mera jaaye hai

Garchay hai tarz-e-taghaful parda-daar-e-raaz-e-ishq
Par hum aise khoye jaate hain ke woh paa jaaye hai

Uss ki bazm-aaraaiyan sun kar dil-e-ranjoor yaan
Misl-e-naqsh-e-mudda'a-e-ghair baitha jaaye hai

Ho ke aashiq woh pari-rukh aur naazuk ban gaya
Rang khilta jaaye hai jitna ke udta jaaye hai

Naqsh ko uss ke musavvir par bhi kya kya naaz hain
Kheenchta hai jis qadar utna hi khinchta jaaye hai

Saaya mera mujh se misl-e-dood bhaage hai Asad
Paas mujh aatish-bajaan ke kis se thehra jaaye hai
