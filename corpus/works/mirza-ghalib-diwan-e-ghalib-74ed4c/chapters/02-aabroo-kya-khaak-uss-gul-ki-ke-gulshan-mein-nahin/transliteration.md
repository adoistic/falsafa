Aabroo kya khaak uss gul ki ke gulshan mein nahin
Hai garebaan nang-e-pairaahan jo daaman mein nahin

Zof se ae girya kuch baaqi mere tan mein nahin
Rang ho kar udd gaya jo khoon ke daaman mein nahin

Ho gaye hain jama ajzaa-e-nigaah-e-aaftaab
Zarre uss ke ghar ki deewaron ke rauzan mein nahin

Kya kahun taariki-e-zindaan-e-gham andher hai
Pamba-e-noor-e-subah se kam jiss ke rauzan mein nahin

Raunaq-e-hasti hai ishq-e-khaana veeraan saaz se
Anjuman be-shama hai gar barq khirman mein nahin

Zakhm silwaane se mujh par chaara-joi ka hai taan
Ghair samjha hai ke lazzat zakhm-e-sozan mein nahin

Baskeh hain hum ek bahaar-e-naaz ke maare hue
Jalwa-e-gul ke siwa gard apne madfan mein nahin

Qatra qatra ek hayoola hai naye naasoor ka
Khoon bhi zauq-e-dard se faarigh mere tan mein nahin

Le gayi saaqi ki nakhwat qulzum-aashaami meri
Mauj-e-mai ki aaj rag-e-meena ki gardan mein nahin

Ho fishaar-e-zof mein kya na-tawaani ki namood
Qad ke jhukne ki bhi gunjaish mere tan mein nahin

Thi watan mein shaan kya Ghalib ke ho ghurbat mein qadr
Be-takalluf hoon woh musht-e-khas ke gul-khan mein nahin
