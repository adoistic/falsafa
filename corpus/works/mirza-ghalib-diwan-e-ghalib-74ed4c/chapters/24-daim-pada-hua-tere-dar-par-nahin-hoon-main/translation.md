Daim pada hua tere dar par nahin hoon main
Khaak aisi zindagi pe ke patthar nahin hoon main

Kyun gardish-e-mudaam se ghabra na jaaye dil
Insaan hoon, pyaala-o-saaghar nahin hoon main

Ya Rab, zamaana mujh ko mitaata hai kis liye
Lauh-e-jahaan pe harf-e-mukarrar nahin hoon main

Had chaahiye saza mein uqubat ke waaste
Aakhir gunahgaar hoon, kaafir nahin hoon main

Kis waaste aziz nahin jaante mujhe
Laal o zumurrad o zar o gohar nahin hoon main

Rakhte ho tum qadam meri aankhon se kyun daregh
Rutbe mein mehr-o-maah se kam-tar nahin hoon main

Karte ho mujh ko mana-e-qadam-bos kis liye
Kya aasmaan ke bhi baraabar nahin hoon main

Ghalib, wazifa-khwaar ho, do shah ko dua
Woh din gaye ke kehte the naukar nahin hoon main
