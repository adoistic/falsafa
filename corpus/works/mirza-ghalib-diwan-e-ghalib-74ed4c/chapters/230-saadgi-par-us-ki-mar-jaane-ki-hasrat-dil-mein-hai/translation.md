saadgi par us ki mar jaane ki hasrat dil mein hai
bas nahin chalta ke phir khanjar kaf-e-qatil mein hai

dekhna taqreer ki lazzat ke jo us ne kaha
main ne yeh jaana ke goya yeh bhi mere dil mein hai

garchay hai kis kis buraai se walay ba-in-hama
zikr mera mujh se behtar hai ke us mehfil mein hai

bas hujoom-e-na-umeedi khaak mein mil jaayegi
yeh jo ik lazzat hamari sa'i-e-be-hasil mein hai

ranj-e-rah kyun kheenchiye waamandagi ko ishq hai
uth nahin sakta hamara jo qadam manzil mein hai

jalwa-zaar-e-aatish-e-dozakh hamara dil sahi
fitna-e-shor-e-qiyamat kis ke aab-o-gul mein hai

hai dil-e-shoreeda-e-Ghalib tilism-e-pech-o-taab
rehm kar apni tamanna par ke kis mushkil mein hai
