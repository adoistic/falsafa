hawas ko hai nishaat-e-kaar kya kya
na ho marna toh jeenay ka maza kya

tajahul-peshgi se mudda'a kya
kahan tak ae saraapa naaz kya kya

nawazish-haaye-be-ja dekhta hoon
shikayat-haaye-rangeen ka gila kya

nigaah-e-be-muhaba chahta hoon
taghaful-haaye-tamkeen-aazma kya

furogh-e-shoala-e-khas yak-nafas hai
hawas ko paas-e-naamoos-e-wafa kya

nafas mauj-e-muheet-e-be-khudi hai
taghaful-haaye-saqi ka gila kya

dimaagh-e-itr-e-pairaahan nahin hai
gham-e-aawargi-haaye-saba kya

dil-e-har-qatra hai saaz-e-ana-al-bahr
hum uss ke hain hamara poochna kya

muhaba kya hai main zaamin idhar dekh
shaheedan-e-nigah ka khoon-baha kya

sun ae ghaarat-gar-e-jins-e-wafa sun
shikast-e-sheesha-e-dil ki sada kya

kiya kis ne jigar-daari ka da'wa
shakeb-e-khaatir-e-aashiq bhala kya

yeh qaatil waada-e-sabr-aazma kyun
yeh kaafir fitna-e-taaqat-ruba kya

balaaye-jaan hai Ghalib uss ki har baat
ibaarat kya ishaarat kya ada kya
