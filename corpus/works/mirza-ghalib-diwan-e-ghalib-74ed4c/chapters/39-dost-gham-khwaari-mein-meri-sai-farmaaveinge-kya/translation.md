Dost gham-khwaari mein meri sa'i farmaaveinge kya
Zakhm ke bharte talak naakhun na badh jaaveinge kya

Be-niyaazi had se guzri banda-parwar kab talak
Hum kaheinge haal-e-dil aur aap farmaaveinge kya

Hazrat-e-naaseh gar aavein deeda-o-dil farsh-e-raah
Koi mujh ko yeh toh samjha do ke samjhaaveinge kya

Aaj waan tegh-o-kafan baandhe huey jaata hoon main
Uzr mere qatl karne mein woh ab laaveinge kya

Gar kiya naaseh ne hum ko qaid achha yoon sahi
Yeh junoon-e-ishq ke andaaz chhut jaaveinge kya

Khaana-zaad-e-zulf hain zanjeer se bhaageinge kyun
Hain giraftaar-e-wafa zindaan se ghabraaveinge kya

Hai ab is ma'moore mein qeht-e-gham-e-ulfat Asad
Hum ne yeh maana ke Dilli mein rahein khaaveinge kya
