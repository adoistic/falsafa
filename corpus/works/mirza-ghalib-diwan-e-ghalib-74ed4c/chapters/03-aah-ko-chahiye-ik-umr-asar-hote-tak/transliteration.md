Aah ko chahiye ik umr asar hote tak
Kaun jeeta hai tiri zulf ke sar hote tak

Daam-e-har-mauj mein hai halqa-e-sad-kaam-e-nahang
Dekhein kya guzre hai qatre pe gauhar hote tak

Aashiqui sabr-talab aur tamanna betaab
Dil ka kya rang karun khoon-e-jigar hote tak

Ta-qayamat shab-e-furqat mein guzar jaayegi umr
Saat din hum pe bhi bhaari hain seher hote tak

Hum ne maana ke taghaful na karoge lekin
Khaak ho jaayenge hum tum ko khabar hote tak

Partau-e-khur se hai shabnam ko fana ki taaleem
Main bhi hoon ek inaayat ki nazar hote tak

Yak nazar besh nahin fursat-e-hasti ghaafil
Garmi-e-bazm hai ik raqs-e-sharar hote tak

Gham-e-hasti ka Asad kis se ho juz marg ilaaj
Shama har rang mein jalti hai seher hote tak
