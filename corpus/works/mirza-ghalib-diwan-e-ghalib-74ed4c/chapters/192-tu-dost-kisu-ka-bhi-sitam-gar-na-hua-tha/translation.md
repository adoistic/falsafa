Tu dost kisu ka bhi sitam-gar na hua tha
Auron pe hai woh zulm ke mujh par na hua tha

Chhoda mah-e-nakhshab ki tarah dast-e-qaza ne
Khursheed hanoz us ke barabar na hua tha

Taufeeq ba-andaza-e-himmat hai azal se
Aankhon mein hai woh qatra ke gohar na hua tha

Jab tak ke na dekha tha qad-e-yaar ka aalam
Main mo'taqid-e-fitna-e-mahshar na hua tha

Main saada-dil aazurdagi-e-yaar se khush hoon
Yaani sabaq-e-shauq mukarrar na hua tha

Darya-e-maasi tunak-aabi se hua khushk
Mera sar-e-daaman bhi abhi tar na hua tha

Jaari thi Asad daagh-e-jigar se meri tehseel
Aatish-kada jageer-e-samandar na hua tha
