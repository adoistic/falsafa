Woh firaaq aur woh visaal kahan
Woh shab-o-roz o maah-o-saal kahan

Fursat-e-kaarobaar-e-shauq kisey
Zauq-e-nazzara-e-jamaal kahan

Dil toh dil woh dimaagh bhi na raha
Shor-e-sauda-e-khatt-o-khaal kahan

Thi woh ek shakhs ke tasavvur se
Ab woh ra'naai-e-khayal kahan

Aisa aasaan nahi lahu rona
Dil mein taaqat jigar mein haal kahan

Hum se chhoota qimaar-khana-e-ishq
Waan jo jaavein girah mein maal kahan

Fikr-e-duniya mein sar khapata hoon
Main kahan aur yeh wabaal kahan

Muzmahil ho gaye quwa Ghalib
Woh anaasir mein e'tidaal kahan

Bosa mein woh muzaaiqa na kare
Par mujhe taaqat-e-sawaal kahan

Falak-e-sifla be-muhaaba hai
Iss sitamgar ko infi'aal kahan
