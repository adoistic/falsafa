Zamana sakht kam-azaar hai ba-jaan-e-Asad
Wagarna hum toh tavaqqo zyaada rakhte hain

Tan-ba-band-e-havas dar nadaada rakhte hain
Dil-ze-kaar-e-jahaan uftaada rakhte hain

Tameez-e-zishti-o-neki mein laakh baatein hain
Ba-aks-e-aaina yak-fard-e-saada rakhte hain

Ba-rang-e-saaya hamein bandagi mein hai tasleem
Ke daagh-e-dil ba-jabeen-kushaada rakhte hain

Ba-zaahidaan rag-e-gardan hai rishta-e-zunnar
Sar-ba-paaye-but-na-nihaada rakhte hain

Maaf-e-behooda-goi hain naasihaan-e-aziz
Dil-ba-dast-e-nigaare nadaada rakhte hain

Ba-rang-e-sabza azeezan-e-bad-zubaan yak-dast
Hazaar tegh-ba-zehr-aab-daada rakhte hain

Adab ne saunpi hamein surma-saai-e-hairat
Zubaan-basta-o-chashm-kushaada rakhte hain
