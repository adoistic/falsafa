Afsos ke dandaan ka kiya rizq falak ne
Jin logon ki thi dar-khur-e-aqd-e-guhar angusht

Kaafi hai nishaani tera chhal'le ka na dena
Khaali mujhe dikhla ke ba-waqt-e-safar angusht

Likhta hoon Asad sozish-e-dil se sukhan-e-garm
Taa rakh na sake koi mere harf par angusht
