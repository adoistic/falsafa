Ek ja harf-e-wafa likha tha so bhi मिट gaya
Zaahiran kaaghaz tere khat ka ghalat-bardaar hai

Jee jale zauq-e-fana ki na-tamami par na kyun
Hum nahin jalte nafas har chand aatish-baar hai

Aag se paani mein bujhte waqt uthti hai sada
Har koi darmaandagi mein naale se naachaar hai

Hai wohi bad-masti-e-har-zarra ka khud uzr-khwaah
Jis ke jalwe se zameen ta aasmaan sarshaar hai

Mujh se mat keh tu humein kehta tha apni zindagi
Zindagi se bhi mera jee in dinon bezaar hai

Aankh ki tasveer sar-naame pe kheenchi hai ke ta
Tujh pe khul jaave ke iss ko hasrat-e-deedar hai
