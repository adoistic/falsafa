Rone se aur ishq mein be-baak ho gaye
Dhoye gaye hum itne ke bas paak ho gaye

Sarf-e-baha-e-may hue aalaat-e-maikashi
The yehi do hisaab so yun paak ho gaye

Rusva-e-dahr go hue aawargi se tum
Baare tabiyaton ke to chaalaak ho gaye

Kehta hai kaun naala-e-bulbul ko be-asar
Parde mein gul ke laakh jigar chaak ho gaye

Poochhe hai kya wujood o adam ahl-e-shauq ka
Aap apni aag ke khas-o-khashaak ho gaye

Karne gaye the us se taghaful ka hum gila
Ki ek hi nigaah ke bas khaak ho gaye

Is rang se uthaai kal us ne 'Asad' ki naash
Dushman bhi jis ko dekh ke gham-naak ho gaye
