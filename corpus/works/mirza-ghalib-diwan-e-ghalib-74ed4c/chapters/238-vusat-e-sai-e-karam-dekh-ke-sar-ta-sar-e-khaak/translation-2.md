vus'at-e-sa'i-e-karam dekh ke sar-ta-sar-e-khaak
guzre hai aabla-paa abr-e-gauharbaar hanoz

yak-qalam kaaghaz-e-aatish-zada hai safha-e-dasht
naqsh-e-paa mein hai tab-e-garmi-e-raftaar hanoz

daagh-e-atfaal hai deewana ba-kohsaar hanoz
khilvat-e-sang mein hai naala talabgaar hanoz

khaana hai sail se khoo-karda-e-deedaar hanoz
doorbeen dar-zada hai rakhna-e-deewaar hanoz

aayi yak-umr se ma'zoor-e-tamaasha nargis
chashm-e-shabnam mein na toota mizha-e-khaar hanoz

kyun hua tha taraf-e-aabla-e-paa ya rab
jaada hai waa-shudan-e-pechish-e-toomaar hanoz

hoon khaamoshi-e-chaman hasrat-e-deedaar Asad
mizha hai shaana-kash-e-turra-e-guftaar hanoz
