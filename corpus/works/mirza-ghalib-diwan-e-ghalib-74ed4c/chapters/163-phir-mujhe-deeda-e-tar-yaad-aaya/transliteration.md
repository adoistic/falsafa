Phir mujhe deeda-e-tar yaad aaya
Dil jigar tishna-e-faryad aaya

Dam liya tha na qayamat ne hanoz
Phir tera waqt-e-safar yaad aaya

Saadgi-haaye-tamanna yaani
Phir woh nairang-e-nazar yaad aaya

Uzr-e-waamandagi ae hasrat-e-dil
Naala karta tha jigar yaad aaya

Zindagi yoon bhi guzar hi jaati
Kyun tera raah-guzar yaad aaya

Kya hi Rizwan se ladaai hogi
Ghar tera khuld mein gar yaad aaya

Aah woh jurrat-e-faryad kahan
Dil se tang aa ke jigar yaad aaya

Phir tere koocha ko jaata hai khayal
Dil-e-gum-gashta magar yaad aaya

Koi veerani si veerani hai
Dasht ko dekh ke ghar yaad aaya

Main ne Majnun pe ladakpan mein 'Asad'
Sang uthaya tha ke sar yaad aaya

Vasl mein hijr ka darr yaad aaya
Ain jannat mein saqar yaad aaya
