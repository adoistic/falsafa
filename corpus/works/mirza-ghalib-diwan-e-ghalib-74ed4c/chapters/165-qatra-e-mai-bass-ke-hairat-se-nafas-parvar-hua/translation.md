Qatra-e-mai bass ke hairat se nafas-parvar hua
Khat-e-jaam-e-mai sarasar rishta-e-gauhar hua

Aitbaar-e-ishq ki khaana-kharaabi dekhna
Ghair ne ki aah lekin woh khafa mujh par hua

Garmi-e-daulat hui aatish-zan-e-naam-e-neko
Khaana-e-khatim mein yaaqoot-e-nageen akhtar hua

Nashe mein gum-kardah-raah aaya woh mast-e-fitna-khu
Aaj rang-e-rafta daur-e-gardish-e-saaghar hua

Dard se dar-parda di mizhgaan-e-siyaahaan ne shikast
Reza reza istikhwaan ka post mein nishtar hua

Zohd gardeedan hai gird-e-khaana-haaye-mun'imaan
Daana-e-tasbeeh se main mohra-dar-shashdar hua

Ae ba-zabt-e-haal-e-na-afsurdagaan josh-e-junoon
Nasha-e-mai hai agar yak-parda naazuk-tar hua

Is chaman mein resha-daari jis ne sar kheencha Asad
Tar zabaan-e-lafz-e-aam-e-Saqi-e-Kausar hua
