Nala juz husn-e-talab ae sitam-ejaad nahin
Hai taqaza-e-jafa shikwa-e-bedaad nahin

Ishq-o-mazdoori-e-ishrat-gah-e-Khusrau kya khoob
Hum ko tasleem niko-naami-e-Farhad nahin

Kam nahin woh bhi kharabi mein peh vus'at maloom
Dasht mein hai mujhe woh aish ke ghar yaad nahin

Ahl-e-beenish ko hai toofan-e-hawadis maktab
Latma-e-mauj kam-az sili-e-ustaad nahin

Waaye mehroomi-e-tasleem-o-bada haal-e-wafa
Jaanta hai ke hamein taaqat-e-faryaad nahin

Rang-e-tamkeen-e-gul-o-lala pareshaan kyun hai
Gar charaghan-e-sar-e-rah-guzar-e-baad nahin

Sabad-e-gul ke talay band kare hai gul-cheen
Muzhda ae murgh ke gulzaar mein sayyaad nahin

Nafi se karti hai isbaat taravish goya
Di hai jaa-e-dahan uss ko dam-e-ejaad nahin

Kam nahin jalwa-gari mein tere kooche se bahisht
Yehi naqsha hai walay iss qadar aabaad nahin

Karte kis munh se ho ghurbat ki shikayat Ghalib
Tum ko be-mehri-e-yaaran-e-watan yaad nahin
