Husn ghamze ki kashakash se chhoota mere baad
Baare aaraam se hain ahl-e-jafa mere baad

Mansab-e-sheftagi ke koi qaabil na raha
Hui maazooli-e-andaaz-o-ada mere baad

Shama bujhti hai to uss mein se dhuaan uthta hai
Shola-e-ishq siyah-posh hua mere baad

Khoon hai dil khaak mein ahvaal-e-butaan par yaani
Un ke naakhun hue mohtaaj-e-hina mere baad

Dar-khur-e-arz nahin jauhar-e-bedaad ko jaa
Nigah-e-naaz hai surme se khafa mere baad

Hai junoon ahl-e-junoon ke liye aaghosh-e-vidaa
Chaak hota hai garebaan se juda mere baad

Kaun hota hai hareef-e-mai-e-mard-afgan-e-ishq
Hai mukarrar lab-e-saaqi pe sala mere baad

Gham se marta hoon ke itna nahin duniya mein koi
Ke kare taaziyat-e-mehr-o-wafa mere baad

Aaye hai bekasi-e-ishq pe rona Ghalib
Kis ke ghar jaaye ga sailaab-e-bala mere baad

Thi nigah meri nihaan-khaana-e-dil ki naqaab
Be-khatar jeete hain arbaab-e-riya mere baad

Tha main guldasta-e-ahbaab ki bandish ki giyaah
Mutafarriq hue mere rufaqa mere baad
