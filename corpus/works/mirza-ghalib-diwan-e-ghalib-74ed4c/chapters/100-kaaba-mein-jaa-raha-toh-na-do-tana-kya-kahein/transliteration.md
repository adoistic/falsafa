Kaaba mein jaa raha toh na do ta'na kya kahein
Bhoola hoon haq-e-sohbat-e-ahl-e-kunisht ko

Ta'at mein taa rahe na may-o-angabeen ki laag
Dozakh mein daal do koi le kar bahisht ko

Hoon munharif na kyun rah-o-rasm-e-sawaab se
Tedha laga hai qat qalam-e-sarnavisht ko

Ghalib kuchh apni sa'i se lehna nahin mujhe
Khirman jale agar na malakh khaaye kisht ko
