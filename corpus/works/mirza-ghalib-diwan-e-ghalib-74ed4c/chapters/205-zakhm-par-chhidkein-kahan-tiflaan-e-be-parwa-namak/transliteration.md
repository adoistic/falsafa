Zakhm par chhidkein kahan tiflaan-e-be-parwa namak
Kya maza hota agar patthar mein bhi hota namak

Gard-e-raah-e-yaar hai saamaan-e-naaz-e-zakhm-e-dil
Warna hota hai jahan mein kis qadar paida namak

Mujh ko arzaani rahe tujh ko mubarak hojiyo
Naala-e-bulbul ka dard aur khanda-e-gul ka namak

Shor-e-jaulaan tha kinaar-e-bahr par kis ka ke aaj
Gard-e-saahil hai ba-zakhm-e-mauja-e-dariya namak

Daad deta hai mere zakhm-e-jigar ki waah waah
Yaad karta hai mujhe, dekhe hai woh jis jaa namak

Chhod kar jaana tan-e-majrooh-e-aashiq haif hai
Dil talab karta hai zakhm aur maange hain aaza namak

Ghair ki minnat na kheenchunga pa-e-taufeer-e-dard
Zakhm misl-e-khanda-e-qaatil hai sar-ta-pa namak

Yaad hain Ghalib tujhe woh din ke wajd-e-zauq mein
Zakhm se girta toh main palkon se chunta tha namak

Is amal mein aish ki lazzat nahi milti Asad
Zor nisbat mai se rakhta hai izaara ka namak
