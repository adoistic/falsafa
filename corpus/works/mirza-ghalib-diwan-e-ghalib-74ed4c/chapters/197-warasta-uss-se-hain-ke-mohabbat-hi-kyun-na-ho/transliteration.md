Warasta uss se hain ke mohabbat hi kyun na ho
Keeje hamare saath adawat hi kyun na ho

Chhora na mujh mein zoaf ne rang-e-ikhtilat ka
Hai dil pe baar naqsh-e-mohabbat hi kyun na ho

Hai mujh ko tujh se tazkira-e-ghair ka gila
Har-chand bar-sabeel-e-shikayat hi kyun na ho

Paida hui hai kehte hain har dard ki dawa
Yun ho toh chaara-e-gham-e-ulfat hi kyun na ho

Dala na be-kasi ne kisi se maamla
Apne se kheenchta hoon khajalat hi kyun na ho

Hai aadmi bajaye khud ek mahshar-e-khayal
Hum anjuman samajhte hain khilwat hi kyun na ho

Hangaama-e-zabooni-e-himmat hai infi'aal
Haasil na keeje dehr se ibrat hi kyun na ho

Warastagi bahana-e-begaangi nahin
Apne se kar na ghair se wehshat hi kyun na ho

Mitta hai faut-e-fursat-e-hasti ka gham koi
Umr-e-aziz sarf-e-ibadat hi kyun na ho

Is fitna-khoo ke dar se ab uthte nahin Asad
Iss mein hamare sar pe qayamat hi kyun na ho
