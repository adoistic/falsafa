Tere tausan ko saba baandhte hain
Hum bhi mazmoon ki hawa baandhte hain

Aah ka kis ne asar dekha hai
Hum bhi ek apni hawa baandhte hain

Teri fursat ke muqabil ae umr
Barq ko pa-ba-hina baandhte hain

Qaid-e-hasti se rihaai maloom
Ashk ko be-sar-o-pa baandhte hain

Nasha-e-rang se hai waashud-e-gul
Mast kab band-e-qaba baandhte hain

Ghalati-haaye-mazaameen mat pooch
Log naale ko rasa baandhte hain

Ahl-e-tadbeer ki waamaandagiyaan
Aablon par bhi hina baandhte hain

Saada purkaar hain khoobaan Ghalib
Hum se paimaan-e-wafa baandhte hain

Paaon mein jab woh hina baandhte hain
Mere haathon ko juda baandhte hain

Husn-e-afsurda-dili-ha-e-rangeen
Shauq ko pa-ba-hina baandhte hain

Qaid mein bhi hai aseeri aazaad
Chashm-e-zanjeer ko wa baandhte hain

Shaikh-ji Kaabe ka jaana maloom
Aap masjid mein gadha baandhte hain

Kis ka dil zulf se bhaaga ke Asad
Dast-e-shaana ba-qazaa baandhte hain

Tere beemaar pe hain faryaadi
Woh jo kaaghaz mein dawa baandhte hain
