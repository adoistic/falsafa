Chashm-e-khoobaan khaamoshi mein bhi nawa-pardaaz hai
Surma to kehve ke dood-e-sho'la-e-aawaaz hai

Paikar-e-ushaaq saaz-e-taale'-e-na-saaz hai
Naala goya gardish-e-sayyaara ki aawaaz hai

Dast-gaah-e-deeda-e-khoon-baar-e-majnoon dekhna
Yak-bayaabaan jalwa-e-gul farsh-e-paa-andaaz hai

Chashm-e-khoobaan mai-farosh-e-nasha-zaar-e-naaz hai
Surma goya mauj-e-dood-e-sho'la-e-aawaaz hai

Hai sareer-e-khaama rezish-haaye-istaqbaal-e-naaz
Naama khud paighaam ko baal-o-par-e-parwaaz hai

Sarnawisht-e-iztiraab-anjaami-e-ulfat na poochh
Naal-e-khaama khaar-khaar-e-khaatir-e-aaghaaz hai

Shokhi-e-izhaar ghair-az-wehshat-e-majnoon naheen
Laila-e-ma'ni Asad mehmil-nasheen-e-raaz hai

Naghmah hai kaanon mein us ke naala-e-murgh-e-aseer
Rishta-e-paa yaan nawa-saamaan-e-band-e-saaz hai

Sharm hai tarz-e-talaash-e-intikhaab-e-yak-nigaah
Iztiraab-e-chashm barpaa dokhta-e-ghammaz hai
