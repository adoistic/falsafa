Hui taakhir to kuch baais-e-taakhir bhi tha
Aap aate the magar koi inaan-geer bhi tha

Tum se be-ja hai mujhe apni tabaahi ka gila
Us mein kuch shaaiba-e-khoobi-e-taqdeer bhi tha

Tu mujhe bhool gaya ho to pata batla doon
Kabhi fitraak mein tere koi nakhcheer bhi tha

Qaid mein hai tere vehshi ko wahi zulf ki yaad
Haan kuch ik ranj-e-giraan-baari-e-zanjeer bhi tha

Bijli ik kaund gayi aankhon ke aage to kya
Baat karte ke main lab-tishna-e-taqreer bhi tha

Yusuf us ko kahun aur kuch na kahe khair hui
Gar bigad baithe to main laaiq-e-taazeer bhi tha

Dekh kar ghair ko ho kyun na kaleja thanda
Naala karta tha valay taalib-e-taaseer bhi tha

Peshe mein aib nahi rakhiye na Farhad ko naam
Hum hi aashufta-saron mein woh jawaan-e-meer bhi tha

Hum the marne ko khade paas na aaya na sahi
Aakhir us shokh ke tarkash mein koi teer bhi tha

Pakde jaate hain farishton ke likhe par na-haq
Aadmi koi hamara dam-e-tehreer bhi tha

Rekhte ke tumheen ustaad nahin ho Ghalib
Kehte hain agle zamaane mein koi Mir bhi tha
