Kab woh sunta hai kahani meri
Aur phir woh bhi zabani meri

Khalish-e-ghamza-e-khoon-rez na poochh
Dekh khoonaba-fishaani meri

Kya bayaan kar ke mera royein-ge yaar
Magar aashufta-bayaani meri

Hoon za-khud rafta-e-baida-e-khayal
Bhool jaana hai nishani meri

Mutaqabil hai muqabil mera
Ruk gaya dekh ravaani meri

Qadr-e-sang-e-sar-e-rah rakhta hoon
Sakht arzaan hai giraani meri

Gard-e-baad-e-rah-e-betaabi hoon
Sarsar-e-shauq hai baani meri

Dahan us ka jo na maloom hua
Khul gayi hech-madaani meri

Kar diya zo'f ne aajiz Ghalib
Nang-e-peeri hai jawaani meri
