Yak zarra-e-zameen nahin be-kaar baagh ka
Yaan jaada bhi fateela hai laale ke daagh ka

Be-mai kise hai taaqat-e-aashob-e-aagahi
Kheencha hai ajz-e-hausla ne khat-e-ayaagh ka

Bulbul ke kaarobaar pe hain khanda-haaye-gul
Kehte hain jis ko ishq khalal hai dimaagh ka

Taaza nahin hai nasha-e-fikr-e-sukhan mujhe
Tiryaaki-e-qadeem hoon dood-e-charaagh ka

Sau baar band-e-ishq se aazaad hum hue
Par kya karein ke dil hi adu hai faraagh ka

Be-khoon-e-dil hai chashm mein mauj-e-nigah ghubaar
Yeh mai-kada kharaab hai mai ke suraagh ka

Baagh-e-shagufta tera bisaat-e-nishaat-e-dil
Abr-e-bahaar khum-kada kis ke dimaagh ka?

Josh-e-bahaar-e-kulfat-e-nazaara hai 'Asad'
Hai abr pamba rozan-e-deewar-e-baagh ka
