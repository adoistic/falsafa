Shab ke woh majlis-furoz-e-khalwat-e-namoos tha
Rishta-e-har shama khaar-e-kiswat-e-fanoos tha

Mashhad-e-aashiq se koson tak jo ugti hai hina
Kis qadar ya rab halaak-e-hasrat-e-paa-bos tha

Haasil-e-ulfat na dekha juz shikast-e-aarzu
Dil-ba-dil paiwasta goya yak lab-e-afsos tha

Kya kahun beemari-e-gham ki faraghat ka bayaan
Jo ke khaya khoon-e-dil be-minnat-e-kaimoos tha

But-parasti hai bahaar-e-naqsh-bandihaaye dehr
Har sareer-e-khaama mein yak naala-e-naaqoos tha

Tab'a ki waashud ne rang-e-yak gulistaan gul kiya
Yeh dil-e-waabasta goya baiza-e-taoos tha

Kal Asad ko hum ne dekha gosha-e-gham-khaana mein
Dast bar-sar, sar ba-zaanu-e-dil-e-mayoos tha
