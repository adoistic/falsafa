Koi din gar zindagani aur hai
Apne jee mein hum ne thaani aur hai

Aatish-e-dozakh mein yeh garmi kahan
Soz-e-gham-haaye-nihani aur hai

Baarha dekhi hain un ki ranjishein
Par kuch ab ke sargirani aur hai

De ke khat munh dekhta hai naama-bar
Kuch toh paigham-e-zabani aur hai

Qaati-e-a'maar hai aksar nujoom
Woh bala-e-aasmani aur hai

Ho chukeen Ghalib balaayein sab tamaam
Ek marg-e-nagahani aur hai
