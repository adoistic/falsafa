Kyun na ho chashm-e-butaan mahv-e-taghaful kyun na ho
Yaani is beemar ko nazaare se parhez hai

Marte marte dekhne ki aarzoo reh jaayegi
Waaye naakaami ke is kaafir ka khanjar tez hai

Aariz-e-gul dekh roo-e-yaar yaad aaya Asad
Joshish-e-fasl-e-bahaari ishtiyaaq-angez hai
