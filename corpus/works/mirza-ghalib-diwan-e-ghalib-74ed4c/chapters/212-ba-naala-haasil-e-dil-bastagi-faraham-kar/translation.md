From sighs, compound the interest that a bound heart brings;
The treasure of the house of chains is but a cry, becomes clear.

The light that pours is measured by the strength of love;
Without it, this whole house of glass is empty sky, becomes clear.

Asad prefers her chosen, artful cruelty;
For promises of faith are just a well-known lie, becomes clear.
