marte hain wale un ki tamanna nahi karte

dar-parda unhein ghair se hai rabt-e-nahani
zahir ka yeh parda hai ke parda nahi karte

yeh bais-e-naumeedi-e-arbab-e-havas hai
Ghalib ko bura kehte hain achha nahi karte
