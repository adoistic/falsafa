Ze-baske mashq-e-tamasha junoon-alamat hai
Kushad-o-bast-e-mizha saili-e-nadamat hai

Na janoon kyonkar mitey taan-e-bad-ehdi
Tujhe ke aaina bhi varta-e-malamat hai

Ba-pech-o-taab-e-havas silk-e-aafiyat mat tod
Nigah-e-ijz sar-rishta-e-salamat hai

Wafa muqabil o daawa-e-ishq be-bunyaad
Junoon-e-saakhta o fasl-e-gul qayamat hai
