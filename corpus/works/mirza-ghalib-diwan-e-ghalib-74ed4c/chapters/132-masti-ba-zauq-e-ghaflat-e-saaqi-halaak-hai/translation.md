Masti ba-zauq-e-ghaflat-e-saaqi halaak hai
Mauj-e-sharaab yak-muzha-e-khwaab-naak hai

Juz zakhm-e-tegh-e-naaz nahin dil mein aarzu
Jaib-e-khayal bhi tere haathon se chaak hai

Josh-e-junoon se kuch nazar aata nahin Asad
Sahra hamari aankh mein yak-musht-e-khaak hai
