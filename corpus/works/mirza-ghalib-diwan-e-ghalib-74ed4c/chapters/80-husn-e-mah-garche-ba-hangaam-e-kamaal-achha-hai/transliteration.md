Husn-e-mah garche ba-hangaam-e-kamaal achha hai
Us se mera mah-e-khurshid-jamaal achha hai

Bosa dete nahin aur dil pe hai har lehza nigaah
Jee mein kehte hain ke muft aaye toh maal achha hai

Aur bazaar se le aaye agar toot gaya
Saaghar-e-jam se mera jaam-e-sifaal achha hai

Be-talab dein toh maza uss mein siwa milta hai
Woh gada jisko na ho khoo-e-sawaal achha hai

Unke dekhe se jo aa jaati hai munh par raunaq
Woh samajhte hain ke beemaar ka haal achha hai

Dekhiye paate hain ushshaaq buton se kya faiz
Ik brahman ne kaha hai ke yeh saal achha hai

Hum-sukhan teshe ne Farhad ko Shireen se kiya
Jis tarah ka ke kisi mein ho kamaal achha hai

Qatra dariya mein jo mil jaaye toh dariya ho jaaye
Kaam achha hai woh jiska ke ma'aal achha hai

Khizr-e-sultaan ko rakhe khaaliq-e-akbar sarsabz
Shah ke baagh mein yeh taaza nihaal achha hai

Hum ko maaloom hai jannat ki haqeeqat lekin
Dil ke khush rakhne ko Ghalib yeh khayaal achha hai
