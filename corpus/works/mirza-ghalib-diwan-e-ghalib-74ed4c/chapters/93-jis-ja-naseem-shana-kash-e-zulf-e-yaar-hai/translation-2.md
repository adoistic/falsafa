Jis ja naseem shana-kash-e-zulf-e-yaar hai
Naafa dimaagh-e-aahu-e-dasht-e-tataar hai

Kis ka suraagh-e-jalwa hai hairat ko ae khuda
Aaina farsh-e-shash-jehat-e-intezaar hai

Hai zarra zarra tangi-e-ja se ghubaar-e-shauq
Gar daam yeh hai vus'at-e-sehra shikaar hai

Dil mudda'i o deeda bana mudda'a-alaih
Nazzare ka muqaddama phir roo-bakaar hai

Chhidke hai shabnam aaina-e-barg-e-gul par aab
Ae andaleeb waqt-e-vidaa-e-bahaar hai

Pach aa padi hai waada-e-dildar ki mujhe
Woh aaye ya na aaye pe yaan intezaar hai

Be-parda su-e-vaadi-e-majnoon guzar na kar
Har zarre ke niqaab mein dil be-qaraar hai

Ae andaleeb yak-kaf-e-khas behr-e-aashiyaan
Toofaan-e-aamad aamad-e-fasl-e-bahaar hai

Dil mat ganwa khabar na sahi sair hi sahi
Ae be-dimaagh aaina timsaal-daar hai

Ghaflat kafeel-e-umr o Asad zaamin-e-nishaat
Ae marg-e-naagahaan tujhe kya intezaar hai
