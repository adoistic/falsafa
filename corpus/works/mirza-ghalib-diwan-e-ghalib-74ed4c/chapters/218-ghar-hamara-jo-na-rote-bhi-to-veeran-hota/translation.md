ghar hamara jo na rote bhi to veeran hota
behr gar behr na hota to biyaban hota

tangi-e-dil ka gila kya yeh woh kaafir-dil hai
ke agar tang na hota to pareshaan hota

baad yak-umr-e-wara baar to deta baare
kaash Rizwan hi dar-e-yaar ka darbaan hota
