shab-e-akhtar qadah-e-aish ne mehmil baandha
ba-yak qaafila-e-aabla manzil baandha

subha-e-waamandagi-e-shauq-o-tamaasha manzoor
jaada par zewar-e-sad aaina manzil baandha

zabt-e-girya gohar-e-aabla laaya aakhir
paa-e-sad mauj toofaan-kada-e-dil baandha

haif ae nang-e-tamanna ke pay-e-arz-e-haya
yak-araq aaina wajh-e-saail baandha

husn-e-aashuftagi-e-jalwa se arz-e-ejaaz
dast-e-moo se ba-sar-e-daawa-e-baatil baandha

tapish-e-aaina pardaaz-e-tamanna laai
naama-e-shauq ba-baal-e-par-e-bismil baandha

deeda ta dil hai yak aaina charaaghaan kis ne
khalwat-e-naaz pe pairaaya-e-mehfil baandha

na-umeedi ne ba-taqreeb-e-mazaamin-e-khumaar
koocha-e-mauj ko khamyaaza-e-saahil baandha

mutrib-e-dil ne mere taar-e-nafas se Ghalib
saaz par rishta pay-e-naghma-e-Bedil baandha
