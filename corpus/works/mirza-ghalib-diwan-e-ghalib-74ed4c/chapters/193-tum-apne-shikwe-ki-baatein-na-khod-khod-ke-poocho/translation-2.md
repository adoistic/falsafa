Tum apne shikwe ki baatein na khod khod ke poocho
Hazar karo mere dil se ke is mein aag dabi hai

Dila yeh dard-o-alam bhi to mughtanam hai ke aakhir
Na girya-e-sehri hai na aah-e-neem-shabi hai

Nazar ba-nuqs-e-gadayaan kamaal-e-be-adabi hai
Ke khaar-e-khushk ko bhi daawa-e-chaman-nasabi hai

Hua wisaal se shauq-e-dil-e-harees ziyaada
Lab-e-qadah pe kaf-e-baada josh-e-tishna-labi hai

Khusha woh dil ke saraapa tilism-e-be-khabri ho
Junoon o yaas o alam rizq-e-mudda'a-talabi hai

Chaman mein kis ke yeh barham hui hai bazm-e-tamasha
Ke barg barg-e-saman sheesha reza-e-halabi hai

Imam-e-zahir-o-baatin ameer-e-soorat-o-ma'ani
Ali Wali Asadullah jaanasheen-e-Nabi hai
