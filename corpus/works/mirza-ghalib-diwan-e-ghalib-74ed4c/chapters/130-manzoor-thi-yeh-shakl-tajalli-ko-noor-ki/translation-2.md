Manzoor thi yeh shakl tajalli ko noor ki
Qismat khuli tere qad-o-rukh se zahoor ki

Ik khoonchakaan kafan mein karoron banao hain
Padti hai aankh tere shaheedon pe hoor ki

Waaiz na tum piyo na kisi ko pila sako
Kya baat hai tumhari sharaab-e-tahoor ki

Ladta hai mujh se hashr mein qaatil ke kyun utha
Goya abhi suni nahin aawaaz soor ki

Aamad bahaar ki hai jo bulbul hai naghma-sanj
Udti si ik khabar hai zabaani tayoor ki

Go waan nahin pe waan ke nikaale hue toh hain
Kaabe se in buton ko bhi nisbat hai door ki

Kya farz hai ke sab ko mile ek sa jawaab
Aao na hum bhi sair karein koh-e-toor ki

Garmi sahi kalaam mein lekin na is qadar
Ki jis se baat uss ne shikaayat zaroor ki

Ghalib gar is safar mein mujhe saath le chalein
Hajj ka sawaab nazr karun-ga huzoor ki
