Meherbaan ho ke bula lo mujhe chaaho jis waqt
Main gaya waqt nahin hoon ke phir aa bhi na sakoon

Zoaf mein taana-e-aghyar ka shikwa kya hai
Baat kuch sar toh nahin hai ke utha bhi na sakoon

Zeher milta hi nahin mujh ko sitam-gar warna
Kya qasam hai tere milne ki ke kha bhi na sakoon

Is qadar zabt kahan hai kabhi aa bhi na sakoon
Sitam itna toh na keeje ke utha bhi na sakoon

Lag gayi aag agar ghar ko toh andesha kya
Shola-e-dil toh nahin hai ke bujha bhi na sakoon

Tum na aao-ge toh marne ki hain sau tadbeerein
Maut kuch tum toh nahin ho ke bula bhi na sakoon

Hans ke bulwaiye mit jaaye-ga sab dil ka gila
Kya tasawwur hai tumhara ke mita bhi na sakoon
