Meri hasti fazaaye hairat-abaad-e-tamanna hai
Jise kehte hain naala woh isi aalam ka anqa hai

Khizaan kya fasl-e-gul kehte hain kis ko koi mausam ho
Wohi hum hain qafas hai aur maatam baal-o-par ka hai

Wafa-e-dilbaraan hai ittefaaqi warna ae humdum
Asar faryaad-e-dil-haaye-hazin ka kis ne dekha hai

Na laayi shokhi-e-andesha taab-e-ranj-e-naumeedi
Kaf-e-afsos milna ahd-e-tajdeed-e-tamanna hai

Na sowe aablon mein gar sirishk-e-deeda-e-num se
Ba-jaulaan-gaah-e-naumeedi nigaah-e-aajizaan pa hai

Ba-sakhti-haaye-qaid-e-zindagi maloom azaadi
Sharar bhi said-e-daam-e-rishta-e-rag-haaye-khaara hai

Taghaaful-mashrabi se na-tamaami baske paida hai
Nigaah-e-naaz chashm-e-yaar mein zunnaar-e-meena hai

Tasarruf vehshiyon mein hai tasavvur-haaye-majnoon ka
Sawaad-e-chashm-e-aahu aks-e-khaal-e-ru-e-laila hai

Mohabbat tarz-e-paiwand-e-nihaal-e-dosti jaane
Daveedan resha-saan muft-e-rag-e-khwaab-e-zulekha hai

Kiya yak-sar gudaaz-e-dil niyaaz-e-joshish-e-hasrat
Suwaida nuskha-e-teh-bandi-e-daagh-e-tamanna hai

Hujoom-e-rezish-e-khoon ke sabab rang udh nahin sakta
Hinaa-e-panja-e-sayyad murgh-e-rishta bar-paa hai

Asar soz-e-mohabbat ka qayaamat be-mahaaba hai
Ke rag se sang mein tukhm-e-sharar ka resha paida hai

Nihaan hai gauhar-e-maqsood jaib-e-khud-shanaasi mein
Ke yaan ghawwaas hai timsaal aur aaina darya hai

Azeezo zikr-e-vasl-e-ghair se mujh ko na behlaao
Ke yaan afsoon-e-khwaab afsaana-e-khwaab-e-zulekha hai

Tasavvur behr-e-taskeen-e-tapeendan-haaye-tifl-e-dil
Ba-baagh-e-rang-haaye-rafta gul-cheen-e-tamaasha hai

Ba-sa'i-e-ghair hai qata-e-libaas-e-khaana-veeraani
Ke naaz-e-jaada-e-reh rishta-e-daamaan-e-sehra hai
