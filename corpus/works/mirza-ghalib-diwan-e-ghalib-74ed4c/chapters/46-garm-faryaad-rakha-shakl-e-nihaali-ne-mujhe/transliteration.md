Garm-faryaad rakha shakl-e-nihaali ne mujhe
Tab amaan hijr mein di burd-e-layaali ne mujhe

Nasiya-o-naqd-e-do-aalam ki haqeeqat maloom
Le liya mujh se meri himmat-e-aali ne mujhe

Kasrat-aaraai-e-wahdat hai parastaari-e-wahm
Kar diya kaafir inn asnaam-e-khayaali ne mujhe

Hawas-e-gul ke tasawwur mein bhi khatka na raha
Ajab aaraam diya be-par-o-baali ne mujhe

Zindagi mein bhi raha zauq-e-fana ka maara
Nasha bakhsha ghazab is saaghar-e-khaali ne mujhe

Bas-ke thi fasl-e-khizaan-e-chamanistaan-e-sukhan
Rang-e-shohrat na diya taaza-khayaali ne mujhe

Jalwa-e-khur se fana hoti hai shabnam 'Ghalib'
Kho diya satwat-e-asmaa-e-jalaali ne mujhe
