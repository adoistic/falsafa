Seemab-pusht garmi-e-aaina de hai hum
Hairaan kiye hue hain dil-e-beqaraar ke

Aaghosh-e-gul kushooda baraaye-vidaa hai
Ae andaleeb chal ke chale din bahaar ke

Yoon baad-e-zabt-e-ashk phiroon gird-e-yaar ke
Paani piye kisu pe koi jaise vaar ke

Baad-az-vidaa-e-yaar ba-khoon dar tapeeda hain
Naqsh-e-qadam hain hum kaf-e-paa-e-nigaar ke

Hum mashq-e-fikr-e-vasl-o-gham-e-hijr se Asad
Laaiq nahin rahe hain gham-e-rozgaar ke
