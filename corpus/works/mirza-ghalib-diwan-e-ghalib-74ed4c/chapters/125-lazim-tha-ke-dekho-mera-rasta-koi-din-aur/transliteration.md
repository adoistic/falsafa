Lazim tha ke dekho mera rasta koi din aur
Tanha gaye kyun ab raho tanha koi din aur

Mit jaayega sar gar tera patthar na ghise ga
Hoon dar pe tere naasiya-farsa koi din aur

Aaye ho kal aur aaj hi kehte ho ke jaaun
Maana ke hamesha nahin achha koi din aur

Jaate hue kehte ho qayamat ko milenge
Kya khoob qayamat ka hai goya koi din aur

Haan ae falak-e-peer jawaan tha abhi Arif
Kya tera bigadta jo na marta koi din aur

Tum maah-e-shab-e-chaar-dahum the mere ghar ke
Phir kyun na raha ghar ka woh naqsha koi din aur

Tum kaun se the aise khare daad-o-sitad ke
Karta malik-ul-maut taqaza koi din aur

Mujh se tumhein nafrat sahi Nayyar se ladaai
Bachchon ka bhi dekha na tamasha koi din aur

Guzri na ba-har-haal yeh muddat khush-o-na-khush
Karna tha jawaan-marg guzaara koi din aur

Naadaan ho jo kehte ho ke kyun jeete hain Ghalib
Qismat mein hai marne ki tamanna koi din aur
