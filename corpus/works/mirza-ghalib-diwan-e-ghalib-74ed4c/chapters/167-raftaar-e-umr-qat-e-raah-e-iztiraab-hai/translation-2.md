Raftaar-e-umr qat-e-raah-e-iztiraab hai
Is saal ke hisaab ko barq aaftaab hai

Meena-e-may hai sarv-e-nishaat-e-bahaar se
Baal-e-tazarv jalwa-e-mauj-e-sharaab hai

Zakhmi hua hai paashna-e-paa-e-sabaat ka
Ne bhaagne ki goo'n na iqaamat ki taab hai

Jaadaad-e-baada-noshi-e-rindaan hai shash-jihat
Ghaafil gumaan kare hai ke geeti kharaab hai

Nazaara kya hareef ho us barq-e-husn ka
Josh-e-bahaar jalwe ko jiske naqaab hai

Main na-muraad dil ki tasalli ko kya karun
Maana ke tere rukh se nigeh kaamyaab hai

Guzra Asad musarrat-e-paighaam-e-yaar se
Qaasid pe mujh ko rashk-e-sawaal-o-jawaab hai

Zaahir hai tarz-e-qaid se sayyaad ki gharz
Jo daana daam mein hai so ashk-e-kabaab hai

Be-chashm-e-dil na kar hawas-e-sair-e-laala-zaar
Yaani yeh har warq warq-e-intikhaab hai
