Zikr mera ba-badi bhi usey manzoor nahin
Ghair ki baat bigad jaaye toh kuchh door nahin

Waada-e-sair-e-gulistan hai khusha taale-e-shauq
Muzhda-e-qatl muqaddar hai jo mazkoor nahin

Shaahid-e-hasti-e-mutlaq ki kamar hai aalam
Log kehte hain ke hai par hamein manzoor nahin

Qatra apna bhi haqeeqat mein hai dariya lekin
Hum ko taqleed-e-tunuk-zarfi-e-Mansoor nahin

Hasrat ae zauq-e-kharaabi ke woh taaqat na rahi
Ishq-par-arbada ki goon tan-e-ranjoor nahin

Main jo kehta hoon ke hum lenge qayamat mein tumhein
Kis raunat se woh kehte hain ke hum hoor nahin

Zulm kar zulm agar lutf dareegh aata ho
Tu taghaaful mein kisi rang se maazoor nahin

Saaf durdi-kash-e-paimana-e-Jam hain hum log
Waaye woh baada ke afshurda-e-angoor nahin

Hoon Zahoori ke muqaabil mein Khafai Ghalib
Mere daawe pe yeh hujjat hai ke mash-hoor nahin
