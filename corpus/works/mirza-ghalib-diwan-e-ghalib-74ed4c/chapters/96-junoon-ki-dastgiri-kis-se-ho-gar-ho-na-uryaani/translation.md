Junoon ki dastgiri kis se ho gar ho na uryaani
Garebaan-chaak ka haq ho gaya hai meri gardan par

Ba-rang-e-kaaghaz-e-aatish-zada nairang-e-betaabi
Hazaar aaina dil baandhe hai baal-e-yak-tapeendan par

Falak se hum ko aish-e-rafta ka kya kya taqaaza hai
Mataa-e-burda ko samjhe hue hain qarz-e-rahzan par

Hum aur woh be-sabab ranj-aashna dushman ke rakhta hai
Shuaa-e-mehr se tohmat nigah ki chashm-e-rozen par

Fana ko saunp gar mushtaaq hai apni haqeeqat ka
Farogh-e-taala-e-khaashaak hai mauqoof-e-gulkhan par

Asad bismil hai kis andaaz ka qaatil se kehta hai
Ke mashq-e-naaz kar khoon-e-do-aalam meri gardan par

Fusoon-e-yak-dili hai lazzat-e-bedaad dushman par
Ke wajd-e-barq joon parwana baal-afshaan hai khirman par

Takalluf khaar-khaar-e-iltimaas-e-be-qaraari hai
Ke rishta baandhta hai pairahan angusht-e-sozan par

Yeh kya wehshat hai ae deewane pesh-az-marg waawela
Rakhi beja bina-e-khaana-e-zanjeer-e-shewan par
