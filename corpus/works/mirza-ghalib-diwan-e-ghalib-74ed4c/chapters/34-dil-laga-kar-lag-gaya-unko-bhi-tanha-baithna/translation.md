Dil laga kar lag gaya unko bhi tanha baithna
Baare apni bekasi ki hum ne paayi daad yaan

Hain zawaal-aamada ajza aafreenish ke tamaam
Mehr-e-gardoon hai charaagh-e-rah-guzaar-e-baad yaan

Hai tarahhum-aafreen aaraaish-e-bedaad yaan
Ashk-e-chashm-e-daam hai har daana-e-sayyaad yaan

Hai gudaaz-e-mom andaaz-e-chakeedan-haaye-khoon
Neesh-e-zanboor-asl hai nishtar-e-fassad yaan

Na-gawaara hai hamein ehsaan-e-saahib-daulataan
Hai zar-e-gul bhi nazar mein jauhar-e-faulaad yaan

Jumbish-e-dil se hue hain uqda-haaye-kaar waa
Kamtareen mazdoor-e-sangeen-dast hai Farhaad yaan

Qatra-haaye-khoon-e-bismil zeb-e-daamaan hain Asad
Hai tamaasha-e-kardani gul-cheeni-e-jallaad yaan
