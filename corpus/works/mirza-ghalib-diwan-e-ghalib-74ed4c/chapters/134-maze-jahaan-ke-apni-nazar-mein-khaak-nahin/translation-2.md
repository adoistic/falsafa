Maze jahaan ke apni nazar mein khaak nahin
Siwaaye khoon-e-jigar so jigar mein khaak nahin

Magar ghubaar hue par hawa udaa le jaaye
Vagarna taab-o-tawaan baal-o-par mein khaak nahin

Yeh kis behisht-shamaail ki aamad aamad hai
Ke ghair-e-jalwa-e-gul rehguzar mein khaak nahin

Bhala usey na sahi kuch mujhi ko rehm aata
Asar mere nafs-e-be-asar mein khaak nahin

Khayaal-e-jalwa-e-gul se kharaab hain maikash
Sharaab-khaane ke deewar-o-dar mein khaak nahin

Hua hoon ishq ki ghaarat-gari se sharminda
Siwaaye hasrat-e-taameer ghar mein khaak nahin

Hamaare she'r hain ab sirf dil-lagi ke Asad
Khula ke faaida arz-e-hunar mein khaak nahin
