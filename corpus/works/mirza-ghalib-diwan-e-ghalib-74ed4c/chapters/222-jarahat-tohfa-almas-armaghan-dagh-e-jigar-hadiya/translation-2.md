jarahat tohfa, almas armaghan, dagh-e-jigar hadiya
mubarakbaad Asad, gham-khwaar-e-jaan-e-daradmand aaya

junoon garm-e-intezaar o naala-e-betaabi kamand aaya
suwaida ta ba-lab zanjeeri-e-dood-e-sipand aaya

mah-e-akhtar-fishaan ki behr-e-istaqbaal aankhon se
tamasha kishwar-e-aaina mein aaina-band aaya

taghaful, bad-gumaani, balkeh meri sakht-jaani se
nigaah-e-be-hijaab-e-naaz ko beem-e-gazand aaya

faza-e-khanda-e-gul tang o zauq-e-aish be-parwa
faraghat-gaah-e-aaghosh-e-wida-e-dil-pasand aaya

adam hai khair-khwah-e-jalwa ko zindaan-e-betaabi
khiraam-e-naaz barq-e-khirman-e-sa'i-e-sipand aaya
