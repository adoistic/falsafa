Kyun jal gaya na taab-e-rukh-e-yaar dekh kar
Jalta hoon apni taaqat-e-deedaar dekh kar

Aatish-parast kehte hain ahl-e-jahan mujhe
Sargarm-e-naala-haaye-shararbaar dekh kar

Kya aabru-e-ishq jahan aam ho jafa
Rukta hoon tum ko be-sabab aazaar dekh kar

Aata hai mere qatl ko par josh-e-rashk se
Marta hoon us ke haath mein talwaar dekh kar

Saabit hua hai gardan-e-meena peh khoon-e-khalq
Larze hai mauj-e-mai teri raftaar dekh kar

Wa-hasrata ke yaar ne khencha sitam se haath
Hum ko harees-e-lazzat-e-aazaar dekh kar

Bik jaate hain hum aap mataa-e-sukhan ke saath
Lekin ayaar-e-taba-e-kharidaar dekh kar

Zunnar baandh subha-e-sad-daana tod daal
Rah-rau chale hai raah ko hamvaar dekh kar

In aablon se paaon ke ghabra gaya tha main
Jee khush hua hai raah ko pur-khaar dekh kar

Kya bad-gumaan hai mujh se ke aaiine mein mere
Tooti ka aks samjhe hai zangaar dekh kar

Girni thi hum peh barq-e-tajalli na Toor par
Dete hain baada zarf-e-qadah-khwaar dekh kar

Sar phodna woh Ghalib-e-shoreeda haal ka
Yaad aa gaya mujhe teri deewaar dekh kar
