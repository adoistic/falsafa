Chahiye achhon ko jitna chahiye
Yeh agar chahein to phir kya chahiye

Sohbat-e-rindaan se wajib hai hazar
Jaye-maye apne ko kheenchha chahiye

Chahne ko tere kya samjha tha dil
Baaray ab iss se bhi samjha chahiye

Chaak mat kar jaib be-ayaam-e-gul
Kuch idhar ka bhi ishaara chahiye

Dosti ka parda hai begaanagi
Munh chhupana hum se chhora chahiye

Dushmani ne meri khoya ghair ko
Kis qadar dushman hai dekha chahiye

Apni ruswai mein kya chalti hai sa'i
Yaar hi hangama-aara chahiye

Munhasir marne pe ho jis ki ummeed
Na-umeedi uss ki dekha chahiye

Ghaafil in meh-tal'aton ke waaste
Chahne waala bhi achha chahiye

Chahte hain khoob-ruyon ko Asad
Aap ki soorat to dekha chahiye
