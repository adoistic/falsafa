Humse khul jaao ba-waqt-e-mai-parasti ek din
Varna hum chhedenge rakh kar uzr-e-masti ek din

Ghurra-e-auj-e-bina-e-aalam-e-imkaan na ho
Iss bulandi ke naseebon mein hai pasti ek din

Qarz ki peete the mai lekin samajhte the ke haan
Rang laavegi hamari faaqa-masti ek din

Naghma-haaye-gham ko bhi ae dil ghaneemat jaaniye
Be-sada ho jaayega yeh saaz-e-hasti ek din

Dhaul-dhappa uss sarapa-naaz ka sheva nahin
Hum hi kar baithe the Ghalib pesh-dasti ek din
