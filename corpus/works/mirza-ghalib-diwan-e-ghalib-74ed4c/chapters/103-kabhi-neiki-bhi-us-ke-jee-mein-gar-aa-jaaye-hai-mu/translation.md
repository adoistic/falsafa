Kabhi neiki bhi us ke jee mein gar aa jaaye hai mujh se
Jafaayein kar ke apni yaad sharma jaaye hai mujh se

Khudaaya jazba-e-dil ki magar taseer ulti hai
Ke jitna kheenchta hoon aur khinchta jaaye hai mujh se

Woh bad-khoo aur meri daastaan-e-ishq toolaani
Ibaarat mukhtasar qaasid bhi ghabra jaaye hai mujh se

Idhar woh bad-gumaani hai idhar yeh na-tawaani hai
Na poochha jaaye hai us se na bola jaaye hai mujh se

Sambhalne de mujhe ae na-umeedi kya qayamat hai
Ke daaman-e-khayal-e-yaar chhoota jaaye hai mujh se

Takalluf bar-taraf nazzargi mein bhi sahi lekin
Woh dekha jaaye kab yeh zulm dekha jaaye hai mujh se

Huye hain paaon hi pehle nabard-e-ishq mein zakhmi
Na bhaaga jaaye hai mujh se na thehra jaaye hai mujh se

Qayamat hai ke hove muddai ka hum-safar Ghalib
Woh kaafir jo Khuda ko bhi na saunpa jaaye hai mujh se
