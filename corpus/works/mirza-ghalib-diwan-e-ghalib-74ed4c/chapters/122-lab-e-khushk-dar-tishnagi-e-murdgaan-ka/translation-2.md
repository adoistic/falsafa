Lab-e-khushk dar-tishnagi-e-murdgaan ka
Ziarat-kadah hoon dil-azardagaan ka

Hama na-umeedi, hama bad-gumaani
Main dil hoon fareb-e-wafa-khordagaan ka

Shaguftan kameen-gaah-e-taqreeb-joi
Tasavvur hoon be-mojib azardagaan ka

Ghareeb-e-sitam-deeda-e-baaz-gashtan
Sukhan hoon sukhan bar lab-awardagaan ka

Sarapa yak aaina-daar-e-shikastan
Iraada hoon yak-aalam-e-afsurdagaan ka

Ba-soorat takalluf, ba-maani ta'assuf
Asad main tabassum hoon pazhmurdagaan ka
