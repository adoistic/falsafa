Faarigh mujhe na jaan ke maanind-e-subh-o-mehr
Hai daagh-e-ishq zeenat-e-jeeb-e-kafan hanoz

Hai naaz-e-muflisaan zar-e-az-dast-rafta par
Hoon gul-farosh-e-shokhi-e-daagh-e-kohan hanoz

Mai-khaana-e-jigar mein yahan khaak bhi nahin
Khamyaaza kheenche hai but-e-be-daad-fan hanoz

Joon jaada sar-ba-koo-e-tamanna-e-be-dili
Zanjeer-e-paa hai rishta-e-hubb-ul-watan hanoz
