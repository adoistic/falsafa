Dard minnat-kash-e-dawa na hua
Main na achha hua, bura na hua

Jama karte ho kyun raqeebon ko
Ik tamasha hua, gila na hua

Hum kahan qismat aazmane jaayen
Tu hi jab khanjar-aazma na hua

Kitne shirin hain tere lab ke raqeeb
Gaaliyan kha ke be-maza na hua

Hai khabar garam un ke aane ki
Aaj hi ghar mein boriya na hua

Kya woh Namrood ki khudaai thi
Bandagi mein mera bhala na hua

Jaan di, di hui usi ki thi
Haq toh yun hai ke haq adaa na hua

Zakhm gar dab gaya lahu na thama
Kaam gar ruk gaya rawa na hua

Rehzani hai ke dil-sitaani hai
Le ke dil, dil-sitaan rawana hua

Kuch toh padhiye ke log kehte hain
Aaj 'Ghalib' ghazal-sara na hua
