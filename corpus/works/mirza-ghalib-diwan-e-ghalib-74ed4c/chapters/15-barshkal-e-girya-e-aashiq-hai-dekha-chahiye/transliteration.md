Barshkal-e-girya-e-aashiq hai dekha chahiye
Khul gayi manind-e-gul sau ja se deewar-e-chaman

Ulfat-e-gul se ghalat hai daawa-e-warastagi
Sarv hai ba-wasf-e-azadi giraftar-e-chaman

Saaf hai az bas-ke aks-e-gul se gulzar-e-chaman
Jaanasheen-e-jauhar-e-aaina hai khaar-e-chaman

Hai nazakat bas ke fasl-e-gul mein me'maar-e-chaman
Qaalib-e-gul mein dhali hai khisht-e-deewar-e-chaman

Teri aaraish ka istiqbal karti hai bahaar
Jauhar-e-aaina hai yaan naqsh-e-ehzaar-e-chaman

Bas ke paayi yaar ki rangeen-adai se shikast
Hai kulah-e-naaz-e-gul bar taaq-e-deewar-e-chaman

Waqt hai gar bulbul-e-miskeen zulaikhai kare
Yusuf-e-gul jalwa-farma hai ba-bazaar-e-chaman

Vehshat-afza girya-ha mauqoof-e-fasl-e-gul Asad
Chashm-e-daryaa-rez hai meezab-e-sarkar-e-chaman
