Shumaar-e-subha marghoob-e-but-e-mushkil pasand aaya
Tamasha-e-ba-yak-kaf burdan-e-sad-dil pasand aaya

Ba-faiz-e-be-dili naumeedi-e-javed aasaan hai
Kushaish ko hamara uqda-e-mushkil pasand aaya

Hawa-e-sair-e-gul aaina-e-be-mehri-e-qaatil
Ke andaaz-e-ba-khoon ghalteedan-e-bismil pasand aaya

Rawani-haaye-mauj-e-khoon-e-bismil se tapakta hai
Ke lutf-e-be-tahasha raftan-e-qaatil pasand aaya

Asad har-ja sukhan ne tarah-e-baagh-e-taaza daali hai
Mujhe rang-e-bahaar-e-ijaadi-e-Bedil pasand aaya
