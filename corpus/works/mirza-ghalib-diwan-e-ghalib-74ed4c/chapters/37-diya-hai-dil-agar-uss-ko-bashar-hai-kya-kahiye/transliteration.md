Diya hai dil agar uss ko, bashar hai kya kahiye
Hua raqeeb to ho, naama-bar hai kya kahiye

Yeh zid ke aaj na aave aur aaye bin na rahe
Qaza se shikwa hamein kis qadar hai, kya kahiye

Rahe hai yun gah-o-be-gah ke koo-e-dost ko ab
Agar na kahiye ke dushman ka ghar hai, kya kahiye

Zahe karishma ke yun de rakha hai hum ko fareb
Ke bin kahe hi unhein sab khabar hai, kya kahiye

Samajh ke karte hain bazaar mein woh pursish-e-haal
Ke yeh kahe ke sar-e-rahguzar hai, kya kahiye

Tumhein nahin hai sar-rishta-e-wafa ka khayal
Hamare haath mein kuch hai, magar hai kya kahiye

Unhein sawaal pe zaum-e-junoon hai, kyun lariye
Hamein jawaab se qata-e-nazar hai, kya kahiye

Hasad saza-e-kamal-e-sukhan hai, kya kijiye
Sitam baha-e-mata-e-hunar hai, kya kahiye

Kaha hai kis ne ke Ghalib bura nahin lekin
Siwaaye is ke ke aashifta-sar hai, kya kahiye
