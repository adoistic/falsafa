Hareef-e-matlab-e-mushkil nahin fusoon-e-niyaaz
Dua qubool ho ya Rab ke umr-e-Khizr daraaz
Na ho ba-harzah bayaabaan-navard-e-wahm-e-wujood
Hanoz tere tasavvur mein hai nasheb-o-faraaz
Wisaal-e-jalwa tamasha hai par dimaagh kahan
Ke deeje aaina-e-intezar ko pardaaz
Har ek zarra-e-aashiq hai aaftaab-parast
Gayi na khaak hue par hawaa-e-jalwa-e-naaz
Na poochh vus'at-e-mai-khaana-e-junoon Ghalib
Jahan yeh kaasa-e-gardoon hai ek khaak-andaaz
Fareb-e-san'at-e-eejaad ka tamasha dekh
Nigaah aks-farosh o khayaal aaina-saaz
Ze-baski jalwa-e-sayyad hairat-aara hai
Udi hai safha-e-khaatir se soorat-e-parwaaz
Hujoom-e-fikr se dil misl-e-mauj larze hai
Ke sheesha naazuk o sahbaa-e-aabgeena-gudaaz
Asad se tark-e-wafa ka gumaan voh ma'ni hai
Ke kheenchiye par-e-taair se soorat-e-parwaaz
Hanoz ae asar-e-deed nang-e-ruswaai
Nigaah fitna-khiraam o dar-do-aalam baaz
