Gar khamoshi se faida ikhfa-e-haal hai
Khush hoon keh meri baat samajhni muhaal hai

Kis ko sunaun hasrat-e-izhaar ka gila
Dil fard-e-jama-o-kharch zabaan-haaye-laal hai

Kis pardeh mein hai aaina-pardaaz ae Khuda
Rahmat keh uzr-khwah-e-lab-e-be-sawaal hai

Haye haye khuda-nakhwasta woh aur dushmani
Ae shauq-e-munfa'il yeh tujhe kya khayal hai

Mishkeen libaas-e-Kaaba Ali ke qadam se jaan
Naaf-e-zameen hai na keh naaf-e-ghazaal hai

Wehshat pe meri arsa-e-aafaq tang tha
Dariya zameen ko arq-e-infa'aal hai

Hasti ke mat fareb mein aa jaaiyo Asad
Aalam tamaam halqa-e-daam-e-khayal hai

Pehlu-tehi na kar gham-o-andoh se Asad
Dil waqf-e-dard kar keh faqeeron ka maal hai
