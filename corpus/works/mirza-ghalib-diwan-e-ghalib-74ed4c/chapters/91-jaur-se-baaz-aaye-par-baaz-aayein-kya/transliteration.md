Jaur se baaz aaye par baaz aayein kya
Kehte hain hum tujh ko munh dikhlayein kya

Raat din gardish mein hain saat aasmaan
Ho rahega kuch na kuch ghabrayein kya

Laag ho toh uss ko hum samjhein lagaav
Jab na ho kuch bhi toh dhoka khayein kya

Ho liye kyun naama-bar ke saath saath
Ya Rab apne khat ko hum pahunchayein kya

Mauj-e-khoon sar se guzar hi kyun na jaaye
Aastaan-e-yaar se uth jaayein kya

Umr bhar dekha kiya marne ki raah
Mar gaye par dekhiye dikhlayein kya

Poochte hain woh ke 'Ghalib' kaun hai
Koi batlao ke hum batlayein kya
