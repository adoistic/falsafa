Ajab nishaat se jallad ke chale hain hum aage
Ke apne saaye se sar paanv se hai do qadam aage

Qaza ne tha mujhe chaha kharaab-e-baada-e-ulfat
Faqat kharaab likha bas na chal saka qalam aage

Gham-e-zamana ne jhaadi nishaat-e-ishq ki masti
Wagarna hum bhi uthaate the lazzat-e-alam aage

Khuda ke waaste daad is junoon-e-shauq ki dena
Ke uske dar pe pahunchte hain naama-bar se hum aage

Yeh umr bhar jo pareshaaniyan uthaai hain humne
Tumhaare aaiyo ae turra-haaye-kham-ba-kham aage

Dil o jigar mein par-afshaan jo ek mauja-e-khoon hai
Hum apne zo'm mein samjhe hue the is ko dam aage

Qasam janaaze pe aane ki mere khaate hain Ghalib
Hamesha khaate the jo meri jaan ki qasam aage
