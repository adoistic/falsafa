Az mehr ta-ba-zarra dil-o-dil hai aaina
Tuti ko shash-jehat se muqabil hai aaina

Hairat hujum-e-lazzat-e-ghaltani-e-tapish
Seemab-balish o kamar-e-dil hai aaina

Ghaflat ba-baal-e-jauhar-e-shamshir parfashaan
Yaa'n pusht-e-chashm-e-shokhi-e-qatil hai aaina

Hairat-nigah-e-barq-e-tamasha bahaar-e-shokh
Dar pardah-e-hawa par-e-bismil hai aaina

Yaa'n reh gaye hain naakhun-e-tadbeer toot kar
Jauhar-e-tilism-e-uqda-e-mushkil hai aaina

Ham-zaanu-e-ta'ammul o ham-jalwa-gaah-e-gul
Aaina-band-e-khalvat-o-mehfil hai aaina

Dil kaar-gaah-e-fikr o Asad be-nawa-e-dil
Yaa'n sang-e-aastaana-e-Bedil hai aaina
