Jo na naqd-e-daagh-e-dil ki kare shola paasbaani
Toh fusurdagi nihaan hai ba-kameen-e-bezabaani

Mujhe us se kya tawaqqo ba-zamaana-e-jawani
Kabhi koodaki mein jis ne na suni miri kahani

Yoon hi dukh kisi ko dena nahin khoob warna kehta
Ke mirey adoo ko Ya Rab mile meri zindagani
