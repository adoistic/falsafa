Kyunkar us but se rakkhun jaan aziz
Kya nahin hai mujhe imaan aziz

Dil se nikla peh na nikla dil se
Hai tere teer ka paikaan aziz

Taab laaye hi banegi Ghalib
Waaqia sakht hai aur jaan aziz
