Before this idol, life is prized no more, dear,
Is not the faith I live for what I adore, dear?

The shaft was pulled, but something was not freed;
Your arrow's tip remains within my core, dear.

This fate, O Ghalib, you have but to endure;
The trial is harsh, and life is worth much more, dear.
