Jab ba-taqreeb-e-safar yaar ne mehmil baandha
Tapish-e-shauq ne har zarre pe ik dil baandha

Ahl-e-beenish ne ba-hairat-kada-e-shokhi-e-naaz
Jauhar-e-aaina ko tooti-e-bismil baandha

Yaas o ummeed ne yak-arbada maidaan maanga
Ajz-e-himmat ne tilism-e-dil-e-saail baandha

Na bandhe tashnagi-e-zauq ke mazmoon Ghalib
Garche dil khol ke dariya ko bhi saahil baandha

Istilahaat-e-aseeran-e-taghaful mat poochh
Jo girah aap na kholi use mushkil baandha

Yaar ne tashnagi-e-shauq ke mazmoon chaahe
Hum ne dil khol ke dariya ko bhi saahil baandha

Tapish-e-aaina parwaaz-e-tamanna laayi
Naama-e-shauq ba-haal-e-dil-e-bismil baandha

Deeda ta-dil hai yak-aaina charaaghaan kis ne
Khalwat-e-naaz pe pairaaya-e-mehfil baandha

Na-umeedi ne ba-taqreeb-e-mazaamin-e-khumaar
Koocha-e-mauj ko khamyaaza-e-saahil baandha

Mutrib-e-dil ne mere taar-e-nafas se Ghalib
Saaz par rishta paye naghma-e-Bedil baandha

Na-tawaani hai tamashaayi-e-umr-e-rafta
Rang ne aaina aankhon ke muqaabil baandha

Nok-e-har-khaar se tha bas-ke sar-e-duzdi-e-zakhm
Chun namad hum ne kaf-e-pa pe Asad dil baandha
