Naqsh-e-naaz-e-but-e-tannaz be-aaghosh-e-raqeeb
Paaye-taaoos paye-khaama-e-maani maange

Tu woh bad-khu ke tahayyur ko tamaasha jaane
Gham woh afsaana ke aashufta-bayaani maange

Woh tap-e-ishq-e-tamanna hai ke phir soorat-e-shama
Shola ta-nabz-e-jigar resha-dawaani maange

Tishna-e-khoon-e-tamaasha jo woh paani maange
Aaina rukhsat-e-andaaz-e-rawaani maange

Rang ne gul se dam-e-arz-e-pareshaani-e-bazm
Barg-e-gul reza-e-meena ki nishaani maange

Zulf tehreer-e-pareshaan-e-taqaaza hai magar
Shaana-saan moo-ba-zubaan khaama-e-maani maange

Aamad-e-khat se na kar khanda-e-sheereen ke mubaad
Chashm-e-mor aaina-e-dil nigraani maange

Hoon giraftaar-e-kameen-gaah-e-taghaaful ke jahaan
Khwaab sayyaad se parwaaz-e-giraani maange

Chashm parwaaz-o-nafs khufta magar zo'f-e-ummeed
Shehpar-e-kaah paye-muzhda-rasaani maange

Wehshat-e-shor-e-tamaasha hai ke joon nikhat-e-gul
Namak-e-zakhm-e-jigar baal-fishaani maange

Gar mile Hazrat-e-Bedil ka khat-e-lauh-e-mazaar
Asad aaina-e-parwaaz-e-ma'ani maange

Rang ne gul se dam-e-arz-e-pareshaani-e-bazm
Barg-e-gul reza-e-meena ki nishaani maange
