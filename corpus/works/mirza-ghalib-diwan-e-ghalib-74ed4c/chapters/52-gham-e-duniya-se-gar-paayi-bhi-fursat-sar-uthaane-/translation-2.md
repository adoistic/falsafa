Gham-e-duniya se gar paayi bhi fursat sar uthaane ki
Falak ka dekhna taqreeb tere yaad aane ki

Khulega kis tarah mazmoon mere maktoob ka ya Rab
Qasam khaayi hai us kaafir ne kaaghaz ke jalaane ki

Lipatna parniyaan mein shola-e-aatish ka pinhaan hai
Walay mushkil hai hikmat dil mein soz-e-gham chhupaane ki

Unhein manzoor apne zakhmiyon ka dekh aana tha
Uthe the sair-e-gul ko, dekhna shokhi bahaane ki

Hamari saadgi thi iltifaat-e-naaz par marna
Tera aana na tha zaalim magar tamheed jaane ki

Lakad-kob-e-hawadis ka tahammul kar nahi sakti
Meri taaqat ke zaamin thi buton ki naaz uthaane ki

Kahun kya khoobi-e-auzaa-e-abna-e-zamaan, Ghalib
Badi ki usne jis se hum ne ki thi baarha neki
