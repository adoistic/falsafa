Na gul-naghma hoon na parda-e-saaz
Main hoon apni shikast ki aawaaz

Tu aur aaraish-e-kham-e-kaakul
Main aur andesha-haaye-door-daraaz

Laaf-e-tamkeen fareb-e-saada-dili
Hum hain aur raaz-haaye-seena-gudaaz

Hoon giraftaar-e-ulfat-e-sayyaad
Varna baaqi hai taaqat-e-parvaaz

Woh bhi din ho ke us sitam-gar se
Naaz kheenchun bajaaye hasrat-e-naaz

Nahin dil mein mere woh qatra-e-khoon
Jis se mizhgaan hui na ho gulbaaz

Aye tera ghamza yak-qalam-angez
Aye tera zulm sar-ba-sar andaaz

Tu hua jalwa-gar mubarak ho
Rezish-e-sajda-e-jabeen-e-niyaaz

Mujh ko poochha toh kuch ghazab na hua
Main ghareeb aur tu ghareeb-nawaaz

Asadullah Khan tamaam hua
Aye dareegha woh rind-e-shaahid-baaz
