Mumkin nahin ke bhool ke bhi aarmeeda hoon
Main dasht-e-gham mein aahu-e-sayyad-deeda hoon

Hoon dard-mand jabr ho ya ikhtiyaar ho
Geh naala-e-kasheeda geh ashk-e-chasheeda hoon

Ne subha se ilaqa ne saaghar se waasta
Main maaraz-e-misaal mein dast-e-bureeda hoon

Hargiz kisi ke dil mein nahin hai meri jagah
Yaani kalaam-e-naghz walay na-shuneeda hoon

Ehl-e-wara ke halqe mein har-chand hoon zaleel
Par aasiyon ke zumre mein main barguzeeda hoon

Hoon garmi-e-nishaat-e-tasavvur se naghma-sanj
Main andaleeb-e-gulshan-e-na-aafreeda hoon

Main chashm-e-wa-kushaada aur gulshan nazar-fareb
Lekin abas ke shabnam-e-khursheed-deeda hoon

Paani se sag-gazida dare jis tarah Asad
Darta hoon aaina se ke mardum-gazida hoon
