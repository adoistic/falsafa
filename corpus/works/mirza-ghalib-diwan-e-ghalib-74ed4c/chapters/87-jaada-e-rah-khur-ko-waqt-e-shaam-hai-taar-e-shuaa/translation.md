Jaada-e-rah khur ko waqt-e-shaam hai taar-e-shuaa
Charkh vaa karta hai maah-e-nau se aaghosh-e-vidaa

Shama se hai bazm-e-angusht-e-tahayyur dar dahan
Shola-e-aawaz-e-khoobaan par ba-hangaam-e-samaa

Jyun par-e-taaoos jauhar takhta-e-mashq-e-rang hai
Bas ke hai woh qibla-e-aaina mehv-e-ikhtiraa

Ranjish-e-hairat-sirishtaan seena-saafi peshkash
Jauhar-e-aaina hai yaan gard-e-maidan-e-nizaa

Chaar soo-e-dehr mein bazaar-e-ghaflat garam hai
Aql ke nuqsaan se uthta hai khayal-e-intefaa

Aashna Ghalib nahin hain dard-e-dil ke aashna
Varna kis ko mere afsaane ki taab-e-istemaa
