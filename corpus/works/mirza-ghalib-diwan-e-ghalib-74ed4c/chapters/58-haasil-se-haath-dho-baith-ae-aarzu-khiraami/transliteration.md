Haasil se haath dho baith ae aarzu-khiraami
Dil josh-e-girya mein hai doobi hui asaami

Is shama ki tarah se jis ko koi bujha de
Main bhi jale-huon mein hoon daagh-e-na-tamaami

Karte ho shikwa kis ka tum aur bewafai
Sar peet'te hain apna hum aur nek-naami

Sad-rang-e-gul katarna dar-parda qatl karna
Tegh-e-adaa nahin hai paaband-e-be-niyaami

Tarf-e-sukhan nahin hai mujh se Khuda-na-karda
Hai naama-bar ko us se daawa-e-hum-kalaami

Taaqat fasaana-e-baad andesha shola-ijaad
Ae gham hanoz aatish ae dil hanoz khaami

Har chand umr guzri aazurdagi mein lekin
Hai sharh-e-shauq ko bhi joon shikwa na-tamaami

Hai yaas mein Asad ko saaqi se bhi faraaghat
Darya se khushk guzre maston ki tishna-kaami
