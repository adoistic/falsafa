Nikohish hai saza faryadi-e-bedaad-e-dilbar ki
Mubada khanda-e-dandaan-numa ho subah-e-mahshar ki

Rag-e-Laila ko khaak-e-dasht-e-Majnun reshgi bakhshe
Agar bowe bajaaye-daana dehqaan nok-e-nishtar ki

Par-e-parwana shayad baadbaan-e-kashti-e-may tha
Hui majlis ki garmi se rawani daur-e-saaghar ki

Karun bedaad-e-zauq-e-par-fishaani arz kya qudrat
Ke taaqat ud gayi udne se pehle mere sheh-par ki

Kahan tak roun us ke khaime ke peechhe qayamat hai
Miri qismat mein ya Rabb kya na thi deewar patthar ki

Bajuz deewangi hota na anjaam-e-khud-aaraai
Agar paida na karta aaina zanjeer-e-jauhar ki

Ghurur-e-lutf-e-saqi nasha-e-bebaaki-e-mastaan
Nam-e-daaman-e-isyaan hai tarawat mauj-e-kausar ki

Mera dil maangte hain aariyat ahl-e-hawas shayad
Yeh jaana chahte hain aaj daawat mein samandar ki

Asad juz aab-bakhshidan ze-dariya Khizr ko kya tha
Dubota chashma-e-haiwaan mein gar kashti Sikandar ki
