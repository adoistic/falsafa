Na hui gar mere marne se tasalli na sahi
Imtihaan aur bhi baaqi ho toh yeh bhi na sahi

Khaar khaar-e-alam-e-hasrat-e-deedar toh hai
Shauq gulcheen-e-gulistan-e-tasalli na sahi

Mai-parastaan khum-e-mai munh se lagaaye hi bane
Ek din gar na hua bazm mein saaqi na sahi

Nafas-e-Qais ke hai chashm-o-charagh-e-sehra
Gar nahin shama-e-siyaah-khaana-e-Laila na sahi

Ek hangama pe mauqoof hai ghar ki raunaq
Noha-e-gham hi sahi naghma-e-shaadi na sahi

Na sataaish ki tamanna na silay ki parwaah
Gar nahin hain mere ashaar mein maani na sahi

Ishrat-e-sohbat-e-khoobaan hi ghanimat samjho
Na hui Ghalib agar umr-e-taba'ee na sahi
