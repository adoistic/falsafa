Sargashtagi mein aalam-e-hasti se yaas hai
Taskeen ko de naveed ke marne ki aas hai
Leta nahin mere dil-e-aawaara ki khabar
Ab tak woh jaanta hai ke mere hi paas hai
Kijiye bayaan suroor-e-tap-e-gham kahaan talak
Har moo mere badan pe zabaan-e-sipaas hai
Hai woh ghuroor-e-husn se begaana-e-wafa
Harchand uss ke paas dil-e-haq-shanaas hai
Pee jis qadar mile shab-e-mahtaab mein sharaab
Is balghami-mizaaj ko garmi hi raas hai
Har ek makaan ko hai makeen se sharaf Asad
Majnoon jo mar gaya hai toh jangal udaas hai
Kya gham hai uss ko jiska Ali sa imam ho
Itna bhi ae falak-zada kyun bad-hawaas hai
