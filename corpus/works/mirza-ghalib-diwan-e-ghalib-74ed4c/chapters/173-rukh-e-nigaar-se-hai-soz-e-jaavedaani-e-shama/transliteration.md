Rukh-e-nigaar se hai soz-e-jaavedaani-e-shama
Hui hai aatish-e-gul aab-e-zindagaani-e-shama

Zubaan-e-ahl-e-zubaan mein hai marg-e-khaamoshi
Yeh baat bazm mein raushan hui zubaani-e-shama

Kare hai sarf ba-imaa-e-shola qissa tamaam
Ba-tarz-e-ahl-e-fana hai fasaana-khwaani-e-shama

Gham usko hasrat-e-parwaana ka hai aye sholay
Tere larazne se zaahir hai na-tawaani-e-shama

Tere khayaal se rooh ehtizaaz karti hai
Ba-jalva-rezi-e-baad o ba-par-fishaani-e-shama

Nishaat-e-daagh-e-gham-e-ishq ki bahaar na poochh
Shaguftagi hai shaheed-e-gul-e-khizaani-e-shama

Jale hai dekh ke baalin-e-yaar par mujh ko
Na kyon ho dil pe mere daagh-e-bad-gumaani-e-shama
