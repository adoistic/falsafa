Jis zakhm ki ho sakti ho tadbeer rafu ki
Likh deejiyo ya Rabb usey qismat mein adu ki

Achha hai sar-angusht-e-hinaai ka tasavvur
Dil mein nazar aati to hai ik boond lahu ki

Kyun darte ho ushaaq ki be-hauslagi se
Yaan to koi sunta nahi faryaad kisu ki

Dashne ne kabhi munh na lagaya ho jigar ko
Khanjar ne kabhi baat na poochhi ho galu ki

Sad-haif woh naakaam ke ik umr se Ghalib
Hasrat mein rahe ek but-e-arbada-jo ki

Go zindagi-e-zahid-e-be-chara abas hai
Itna hai ke rehti to hai tadbeer wuzu ki

Ab be-khabaraan mere lab-e-zakhm-e-jigar par
Bakhiya jise kehte ho shikayat hai rafu ki
