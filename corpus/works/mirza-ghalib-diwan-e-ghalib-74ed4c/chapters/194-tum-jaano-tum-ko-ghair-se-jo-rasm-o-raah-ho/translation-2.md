Tum jaano tum ko ghair se jo rasm-o-raah ho
Mujh ko bhi poochte raho toh kya gunaah ho

Bachte nahin muaakhiza-e-roz-e-hashr se
Qaatil agar raqeeb hai toh tum gawaah ho

Kya woh bhi be-gunah-kash o haq-naashanaas hain
Maana ke tum bashar nahin khursheed o maah ho

Ubhra hua naqaab mein hai unke ek taar
Marta hoon main ke yeh na kisi ki nigaah ho

Jab mai-kada chhoota toh phir ab kya jagah ki qaid
Masjid ho, madrasa ho, koi khanqah ho

Sunte hain jo bahisht ki taareef, sab durust
Lekin Khuda kare woh tera jalwa-gaah ho

Ghalib bhi gar na ho toh kuch aisa zarar nahin
Duniya ho Ya Rab aur mera baadshah ho
