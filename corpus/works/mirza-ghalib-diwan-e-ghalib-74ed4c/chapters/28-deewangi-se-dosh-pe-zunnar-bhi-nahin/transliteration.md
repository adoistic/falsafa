Deewangi se dosh pe zunnar bhi nahin
Yaani hamare jeb mein ik taar bhi nahin

Dil ko niyaz-e-hasrat-e-deedar kar chuke
Dekha toh hum mein taaqat-e-deedar bhi nahin

Milna tera agar nahin aasaan toh sehel hai
Dushwaar toh yahi hai ke dushwaar bhi nahin

Be-ishq umr kat nahin sakti hai aur yaan
Taaqat ba-qadr-e-lazzat-e-aazaar bhi nahin

Shoreedagi ke haath se hai sar vabaal-e-dosh
Sehra mein ae Khuda koi deewar bhi nahin

Gunjaish-e-adawat-e-aghyar yak taraf
Yaan dil mein zoaf se hawas-e-yaar bhi nahin

Dar naala-haaye-zaar se mere Khuda ko maan
Aakhir nawa-e-murgh-e-giraftaar bhi nahin

Dil mein hai yaar ki saf-e-mizhgaan se rokashi
Haalanke taaqat-e-khalish-e-khaar bhi nahin

Is saadgi pe kaun na mar jaaye ae Khuda
Ladte hain aur haath mein talwaar bhi nahin

Dekha Asad ko khalwat-o-jalwat mein baarha
Deewana gar nahin hai toh hoshiyaar bhi nahin
