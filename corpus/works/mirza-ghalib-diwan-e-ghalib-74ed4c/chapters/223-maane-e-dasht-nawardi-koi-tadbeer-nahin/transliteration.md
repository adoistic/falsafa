maane-e-dasht-nawardi koi tadbeer nahin
ek chakkar hai mere paanv mein zanjeer nahin
shauq is dasht mein daudaaye hai mujh ko ke jahan
jaada ghair az nigah-e-deeda-e-tasveer nahin
hasrat-e-lazzat-e-aazaar rahi jaati hai
jaada-e-raah-e-wafa juz dam-e-shamsheer nahin
ranj-e-naumeedi-e-javed gawara rahiyo
khush hoon gar naala-e-zabooni kash-e-taaseer nahin
sar khujata hai jahan zakhm-e-sar achha ho jaaye
lazzat-e-sang ba-andaza-e-taqreer nahin
jab karam rukhsat-e-bebaaki-o-gustaakhi de
koi taqseer bajuz khijlat-e-taqseer nahin
Ghalib apna yeh aqeeda hai ba-qaul-e-Naasikh
aap be-behra hai jo mo'taqid-e-Meer nahin
Meer ke she'r ka ahwaal kahoon kya Ghalib
jis ka deewan kam-az-gulshan-e-Kashmir nahin
aaina daam ko parde mein chhupata hai abas
ke pari-zaad-e-nazar qaabil-e-taskheer nahin
misl-e-gul zakhm hai mera bhi sinan se tu'am
tera tarkash hi kuch aabistani-e-teer nahin
