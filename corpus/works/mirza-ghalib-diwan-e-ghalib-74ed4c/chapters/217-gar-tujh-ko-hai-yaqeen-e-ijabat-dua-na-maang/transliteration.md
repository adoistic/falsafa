gar tujh ko hai yaqeen-e-ijabat dua na maang
yaani baghair-e-yak-dil-e-be-mudda'a na maang

aata hai daagh-e-hasrat-e-dil ka shumaar yaad
mujh se mere gunah ka hisaab ae khuda na maang

ae aarzoo shaheed-e-wafa khoon-baha na maang
juz behr-e-dast-o-baazu-e-qaatil dua na maang

barham hai bazm-e-ghuncha ba-yak-jumbish-e-nishaat
kaashaana bas ke tang hai ghaafil hawa na maang

main door gard-e-arz-e-rusoom-e-niyaaz hoon
dushman samajh walay nigah-e-aashna na maang

yak-bakht auj nazr-e-subuk-baari-e-Asad
sar par wabaal-e-saaya-e-baal-e-huma na maang

gustaakhi-e-wisaal hai mashaata-e-niyaaz
yaani dua bajuz kham-e-zulf-e-dota na maang

Isa tilism-e-husn-e-taghaaful hai zeenhaar
juz pusht-e-chashm nuskha-e-arz-e-dawa na maang

nazaara deegar o dil-e-khooneen nafas digar
aaina dekh jauhar-e-barg-e-hina na maang
