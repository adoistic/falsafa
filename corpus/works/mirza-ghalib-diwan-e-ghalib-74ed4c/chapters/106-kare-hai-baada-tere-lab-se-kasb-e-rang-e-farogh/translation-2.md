Kare hai baada tere lab se kasb-e-rang-e-farogh
Khat-e-pyaala saraasar nigaah-e-gul-cheen hai

Kabhi toh is dil-e-shoreeda ki bhi daad mile
Ke ek umr se hasrat-parast-e-baaleen hai

Baja hai gar na sune naala-haaye-bulbul-e-zaar
Ke gosh-e-gul nam-e-shabnam se pamba-aageen hai

Asad hai naza mein chal be-wafa baraaye-Khuda
Maqaam-e-tark-e-hijaab o widaa-e-tamkeen hai
