dil mira soz-e-nihaan se be-muhaaba jal gaya
aatish-e-khaamosh ki maanind goya jal gaya

dil mein zauq-e-vasl o yaad-e-yaar tak baaqi nahin
aag is ghar mein lagi aisi ke jo tha jal gaya

main adam se bhi parey hoon warna ghaafil baarha
meri aah-e-aatisheen se baal-e-anqa jal gaya

arz keeje jauhar-e-andesha ki garmi kahaan
kuchh khayaal aaya tha vehshat ka ke sehra jal gaya

dil nahin tujh ko dikhaata warna daaghon ki bahaar
is chiraaghaan ka karoon kya kaar-farma jal gaya

main hoon aur afsurdagi ki aarzoo Ghalib ke dil
dekh kar tarz-e-tapaak-e-ahl-e-duniya jal gaya

khaanmaan-e-aashiqaan dukaan-e-aatish-baaz hai
shola-roo jab ho gaye garm-e-tamaasha jal gaya

ta kuja afsos-e-garmi-haaye-sohbat ae khayaal
dil ba-soz-e-aatish-e-daagh-e-tamanna jal gaya

hai Asad begaana-e-afsurdagi ae bekasi
dil ze-andaaz-e-tapaak-e-ahl-e-duniya jal gaya

dood mera sunbulistaan se kare hai ham-sari
baskeh shauq-e-aatish-e-gul se saraapa jal gaya

shama-rooyaan ki sar-angusht-e-hinaai dekh kar
ghuncha-e-gul par-fishaan parwaana-aasa jal gaya
