Muddat hui hai yaar ko mehmaan kiye hue
Josh-e-qadah se bazm charaghaan kiye hue

Karta hoon jama phir jigar-e-lakht-lakht ko
Arsa hua hai daawat-e-mizhgaan kiye hue

Phir waz-e-ehtiyaat se rukne laga hai dam
Barson hue hain chaak-e-garebaan kiye hue

Phir garm-naala-haaye-shararbaar hai nafas
Muddat hui hai sair-e-charaghaan kiye hue

Phir pursish-e-jaraahat-e-dil ko chala hai ishq
Saamaan-e-sad-hazaar namakdaan kiye hue

Phir bhar raha hoon khaama-e-mizhgaan ba-khoon-e-dil
Saaz-e-chaman taraazi-e-daamaan kiye hue

Baaham-digar hue hain dil o deeda phir raqeeb
Nazaara o khayaal ka saamaan kiye hue

Dil phir tawaaf-e-koo-e-malaamat ko jaaye hai
Pindaar ka sanam-kada veeraan kiye hue

Phir shauq kar raha hai khareedaar ki talab
Arz-e-mataa-e-aql-o-dil-o-jaan kiye hue

Daude hai phir har ek gul-o-laala par khayaal
Sad-gulistaan nigaah ka saamaan kiye hue

Phir chaahta hoon naama-e-dil-daar kholna
Jaan nazr-e-dil-farebi-e-unwaan kiye hue

Maange hai phir kisi ko lab-e-baam par hawas
Zulf-e-siyaah rukh pe pareshaan kiye hue

Chaahe hai phir kisi ko muqaabil mein aarzoo
Surme se tez dashna-e-mizhgaan kiye hue

Ik nau-bahaar-e-naaz ko taake hai phir nigaah
Chehra farogh-e-mai se gulistaan kiye hue

Phir jee mein hai ke dar pe kisi ke pade rahein
Sar zer-e-baar-e-minnat-e-darbaan kiye hue

Jee dhoondta hai phir wahi fursat ke raat din
Baithe rahein tasavvur-e-jaanaan kiye hue

Ghalib humein na chhed ke phir josh-e-ashk se
Baithe hain hum tahayya-e-toofaan kiye hue
