Taskeen ko hum na royein jo zauq-e-nazar mile
Hooran-e-khuld mein teri soorat magar mile

Apni gali mein mujh ko na kar dafn baad-e-qatl
Mere pate se khalq ko kyun tera ghar mile

Saaqi-gari ki sharm karo aaj warna hum
Har shab piya hi karte hain mai jis qadar mile

Tujh se toh kuch kalaam nahin lekin ae nadeem
Mera salaam kehiyo agar naama-bar mile

Tum ko bhi hum dikhayein ke Majnoon ne kya kiya
Fursat kashakash-e-gham-e-pinhaan se gar mile

Laazim nahin ke Khizr ki hum pairavi karein
Jaana ke ik buzurg humein hum-safar mile

Ae saakinan-e-koocha-e-dil-daar dekhna
Tum ko kahin jo Ghalib-e-ashufta-sar mile
