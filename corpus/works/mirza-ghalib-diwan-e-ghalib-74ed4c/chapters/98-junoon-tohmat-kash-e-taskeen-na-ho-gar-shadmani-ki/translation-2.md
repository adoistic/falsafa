Junoon tohmat-kash-e-taskeen na ho gar shadmani ki
Namak-paash-e-kharaash-e-dil hai lazzat-e-zindagani ki

Kashakash-haaye-hasti se kare kya saii-e-aazaadi
Hui zanjeer-e-mauj-e-aab ko fursat rawaani ki

Pas-az-murdan bhi deewana ziarat-gah-e-tiflaan hai
Sharaar-e-sang ne turbat pe meri gul-fishaani ki

Na kheench ae saii-e-dast-e-na-rasa zulf-e-tamanna ko
Pareshaan-tar hai moo-e-khaama se tadbeer-e-maani ki

Kahaan hum bhi rag-o-pay rakhte hain, insaaf behtar hai
Na kheenche taaqat-e-khamiyaza tohmat-e-naatwaani ki

Takalluf bartaraf Farhad aur itni subuk-dasti
Khayaal aasaan tha lekin khwaab-e-Khusro ne giraani ki

Asad ko boriye mein dhar ke phoonka mauj-e-hasti ne
Faqeeri mein bhi baaqi hai sharaarat naujawaani ki
