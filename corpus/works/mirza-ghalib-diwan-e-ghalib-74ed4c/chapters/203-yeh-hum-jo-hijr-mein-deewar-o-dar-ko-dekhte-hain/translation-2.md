Yeh hum jo hijr mein deewar-o-dar ko dekhte hain
Kabhi saba ko kabhi naama-bar ko dekhte hain

Woh aaye ghar mein hamare khuda ki qudrat hai
Kabhi hum un ko kabhi apne ghar ko dekhte hain

Nazar lage na kahin uss ke dast-o-baazu ko
Yeh log kyun merey zakhm-e-jigar ko dekhte hain

Tere jawaahir-e-tarf-e-kulah ko kya dekhein
Hum auj-e-taala-e-laal-o-guhar ko dekhte hain
