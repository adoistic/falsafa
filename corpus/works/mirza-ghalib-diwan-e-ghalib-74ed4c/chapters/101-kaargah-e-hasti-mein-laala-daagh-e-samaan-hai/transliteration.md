Kaargah-e-hasti mein laala daagh-e-samaan hai
Barq-e-kharman-e-raahat khoon-e-garm-e-dehqaan hai

Ghunicha ta shaguftan-ha barg-e-aafiyat maloom
Ba-wajood-e-dil-jamee khwaab-e-gul pareshaan hai

Hum se ranj-e-betaabi kis tarah uthaya jaaye
Daagh pusht-e-dast-e-ijz shola khas-ba-dandaan hai

Ishq ke taghaaful se harza-gard hai aalam
Roo-e-shash-jehat-e-aafaaq pusht-e-chashm-e-zindaan hai

Hairat tapidan ha khoon bahaa-e-deedan ha
Rang-e-gul ke parde mein aayina par-afshaan hai

Vehshat-e-anjuman hai gul dekh laale ka aalam
Misl-e-dood-e-mijmar ke daagh baal-afshaan hai

Aye karam na ho ghaafil warna hai Asad be-dil
Be-ghar sadaf goya pusht-e-chashm-e-nesaan hai
