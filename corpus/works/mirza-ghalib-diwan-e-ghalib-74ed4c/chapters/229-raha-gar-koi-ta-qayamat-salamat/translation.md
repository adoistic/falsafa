raha gar koi ta-qayamat salamat
phir ik roz marna hai hazrat-salamat

jigar ko mere ishq-e-khoon-naaba-mashrab
likhe hai khudawand-e-nemat salamat

ala-ar-raghm-e-dushman shaheed-e-wafa hoon
mubarak mubarak salamat salamat

nahin gar sar-o-barg-e-idraak-e-maani
tamasha-e-nairang-e-soorat salamat

do-aalam ki hasti pe khat-e-fana kheench
dil-o-dast-e-arbab-e-himmat salamat

nahin gar ba-kaam-e-dil-e-khasta gardoon
jigar-khai-e-josh-e-hasrat salamat

na auron ki sunta na kehta hoon apni
sar-e-khasta dushwar-e-wehshat salamat

wufoor-e-bala hai hujoom-e-wafa hai
salamat malamat malamat salamat

na fikr-e-salamat na beem-e-malamat
ze-khud-raftagi-haaye-hairat salamat

rahe Ghalib-e-khasta maghloob-e-gardoon
yeh kya be-niyazi hai hazrat-salamat
