Aamad-e-khat se hua hai sard jo bazaar-e-dost
Dood-e-shama-e-kushta tha shayad khat-e-rukhsaar-e-dost

Ae dil-e-na'aaqibat-andesh zabt-e-shauq kar
Kaun la sakta hai taab-e-jalwa-e-deedaar-e-dost

Khaana-veeraan-saazi-e-hairat tamaasha kijiye
Soorat-e-naqsh-e-qadam hoon rafta-e-raftaar-e-dost

Ishq mein bedaad-e-rashk-e-ghair ne maara mujhe
Kushta-e-dushman hoon aakhir garche tha beemaar-e-dost

Chashm-e-ma raushan ke us be-dard ka dil shaad hai
Deeda-e-pur-khoon hamara saaghar-e-sarshaar-e-dost

Ghair yoon karta hai meri pursish us ke hijr mein
Be-takalluf dost ho jaise koi gham-khwaar-e-dost

Taake main jaanoon ke hai us ki rasaai waan talak
Mujh ko deta hai payaam-e-waada-e-deedaar-e-dost

Jab ke main karta hoon apna shikwa-e-zoaf-e-dimaagh
Sar kare hai woh hadees-e-zulf-e-ambar-baar-e-dost

Chupke chupke mujh ko rote dekh paata hai agar
Hans ke karta hai bayaan-e-shokhi-e-guftaar-e-dost

Mehrbaani-haaye-dushman ki shikaayat kijiye
Ta bayaan kije sipaas-e-lazzat-e-aazaar-e-dost

Yeh ghazal apni mujhe jee se pasand aati hai aap
Hai radeef-e-sher mein 'Ghalib' za-bas takraar-e-dost

Chashm-band-e-khalq juz timsaal-e-khud-beeni nahin
Aaina hai qaalib-e-khisht-e-dar-o-deewaar-e-dost

Barq-e-khirman-zaar-e-gauhar hai nigaah-e-tez yaan
Ashk ho jaate hain khushk az-garmi-e-raftaar-e-dost

Hai sawa neze pe us ke qaamat-e-nau-khez se
Aaftab-e-subh-e-mehshar hai gul-e-dastaar-e-dost

Ae adoo-e-maslehat chand ba-zabt afsurda reh
Kardani hai jama' taab-e-shokhi-e-deedaar-e-dost

Laghzish-e-mastaana o josh-e-tamaasha hai 'Asad'
Aatish-e-mai se bahaar-e-garmi-e-bazaar-e-dost
