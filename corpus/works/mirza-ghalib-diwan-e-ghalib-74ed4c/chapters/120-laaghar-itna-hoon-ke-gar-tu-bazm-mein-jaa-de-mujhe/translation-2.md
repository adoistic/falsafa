Laaghar itna hoon ke gar tu bazm mein jaa de mujhe
Mera zimma dekh kar gar koi batla de mujhe

Kya ta'ajjub hai jo uss ko dekh kar aa jaaye rehm
Waan talak koi kisi heele se pahuncha de mujhe

Munh na dikhlawe na dikhla par ba-andaaz-e-itaab
Khol kar parda zara aankhein hi dikhla de mujhe

Yaan talak meri giraftaari se woh khush hai ke main
Zulf gar ban jaaun to shaane mein uljha de mujhe
