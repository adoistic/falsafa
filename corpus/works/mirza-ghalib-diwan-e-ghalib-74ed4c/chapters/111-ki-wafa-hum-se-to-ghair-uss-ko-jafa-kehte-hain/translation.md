Ki wafa hum se to ghair uss ko jafa kehte hain
Hoti aayi hai ke achhon ko bura kehte hain

Aaj hum apni pareshaani-e-khatir un se
Kehne jaate to hain par dekhiye kya kehte hain

Agle waqton ke hain yeh log inhein kuch na kaho
Jo mai-o-naghma ko andoh-ruba kehte hain

Dil mein aa jaaye hai hoti hai jo fursat ghash se
Aur phir kaun se naale ko rasaa kehte hain

Hai pare-sarhad-e-idraak se apna masjood
Qible ko ehl-e-nazar qibla-numa kehte hain

Paaye-afgaar pe jab se tujhe reham aaya hai
Khaar-e-rah ko tere hum mehr-e-giya kehte hain

Ik sharar dil mein hai uss se koi ghabraayega kya
Aag matloob hai hum ko jo hawa kehte hain

Dekhiye laati hai uss shokh ki nakhwat kya rang
Uss ki har baat pe hum naam-e-khuda kehte hain

Vahshat-o-Shefta ab marsiya kahwein shaayad
Mar gaya Ghalib-e-aashufta-nawa kehte hain
