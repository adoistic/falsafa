Khamoshiyon mein tamasha adaa nikalti hai
Nigaah dil se tere surma-sa nikalti hai

Fishaar-e-tangi-e-khalwat se banti hai shabnam
Saba jo ghunche ke parde mein jaa nikalti hai

Na poochh seena-e-aashiq se aab-e-tegh-e-nigaah
Ke zakhm-e-rauzan-e-dar se hawa nikalti hai

Ba-rang-e-sheesha hoon yak-gosha-e-dil-e-khaali
Kabhi pari meri khalwat mein aa nikalti hai

Ba-halqa-e-kham-e-gesu hai raasti aamoz
Dahan-e-maar se goya saba nikalti hai

Asad ko hasrat-e-arz-e-niyaaz thi dam-e-qatl
Hanoz yak sukhan be-sada nikalti hai
