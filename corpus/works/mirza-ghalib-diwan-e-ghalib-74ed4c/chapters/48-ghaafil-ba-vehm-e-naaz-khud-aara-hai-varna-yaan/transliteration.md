Ghaafil ba-vehm-e-naaz khud-aara hai varna yaan
Be-shaana-e-saba nahi turra-e-giyaah ka

Bazm-e-qadah se aish-e-tamanna na rakh ke rang
Said-ze-daam-jasta hai is daam-gaah ka

Rahmat agar qubool kare kya ba'eed hai
Sharmindagi se uzr na karna gunaah ka

Maqtal ko kis nishaat se jaata hoon main ke hai
Pur-gul khayal-e-zakhm se daaman-e-nigaah ka

Jaan dar-hawaa-e-yak-nigah-e-garm hai Asad
Parwaana hai vakeel tere daad-khwaah ka

Uzlat-gazeen-e-bazm hain waamandgaan-e-deed
Meena-e-mai hai aabla-e-paa-e-nigaah ka

Har gaam aablay se hai dil dar-tah-e-qadam
Kya beem ahl-e-dard ko sakhti-e-raah ka

Taa'oos-dar-rikaab hai har zarra aah ka
Yaarab nafas ghubaar hai kis jalwa-gaah ka

Jaib-e-niyaaz-e-ishq nishaan-daar-e-naaz hai
Aaina hoon shikastan-e-tarf-e-kulaah ka
