naveed-e-aman hai bedaad-e-dost jaan ke liye
rahi na tarz-e-sitam koi aasmaan ke liye

bala se gar mizha-e-yaar tishna-e-khoon hai
rakkhoon kuch apni bhi mizhgaan-e-khoon-fishaan ke liye

woh zinda hum hain ke hain roshnaas-e-khalq ae Khizr
na tum ke chor bane umr-e-jaavedaan ke liye

raha bala mein bhi main mubtilaa-e-aafat-e-rashk
balaaye-jaan hai ada teri ik jahaan ke liye

falak na door rakh iss se mujhe ke main hi nahin
daraaz-dasti-e-qaatil ke imtihaan ke liye

misaal yeh meri koshish ki hai ke murgh-e-aseer
kare qafas mein faraaham khas aashiyaan ke liye

gada samajh ke woh chup tha meri jo shaamat aayi
utha aur uth ke qadam maine paasbaan ke liye

ba-qadr-e-shauq nahin zarf-e-tangnaaye-ghazal
kuch aur chahiye vus'at mere bayaan ke liye

diya hai khalq ko bhi ta use nazar na lage
bana hai aish Tajammul Husain Khan ke liye

zubaan pe baar-Khudaya yeh kis ka naam aaya
ke mere nutq ne bose meri zubaan ke liye

Naseer-ud-daulat-o-deen aur Moin-ul-millat-o-mulk
bana hai charkh-e-bareen jiss ke aastaan ke liye

zamaana ehd mein uss ke hai mahv-e-aaraaish
banein ge aur sitaare ab aasmaan ke liye

waraq tamaam hua aur madh baaqi hai
safeena chahiye iss behr-e-bekaraan ke liye

adaa-e-khaas se Ghalib hua hai nukta-sara
salaa-e-aam hai yaaraan-e-nukta-daan ke liye
