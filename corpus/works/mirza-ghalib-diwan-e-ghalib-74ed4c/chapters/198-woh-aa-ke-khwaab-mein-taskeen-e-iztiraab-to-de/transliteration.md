Woh aa ke khwaab mein taskeen-e-iztiraab to de
Walay mujhe tapish-e-dil majaal-e-khwaab to de

Karay hai qatl lagaawat mein tera ro dena
Teri tarah koi tegh-e-nigah ko aab to de

Dikha ke jumbish-e-lab hi tamaam kar hum ko
Na de jo bosa toh munh se kahin jawaab to de

Pila de oak se saaqi jo hum se nafrat hai
Piyaala gar nahi deta na de sharaab to de

Asad khushi se mere haath paaon phool gaye
Kaha jo uss ne zara mere paaon daab to de

Yeh kaun kehve hai aabaad kar humein lekin
Kabhi zamaana muraad-e-dil-kharaab to de
