Hai vasl hijr aalam-e-tamkeen-o-zabt mein
Maashooq-e-shokh o aashiq-e-deewana chahiye

Is lab se mil hi jaayega bosa kabhi to haan
Shauq-e-fuzool o jurrat-e-rindana chahiye

Aashiq naqaab-e-jalwa-e-jaanaana chahiye
Fanoos-e-shama ko par-e-parwana chahiye
