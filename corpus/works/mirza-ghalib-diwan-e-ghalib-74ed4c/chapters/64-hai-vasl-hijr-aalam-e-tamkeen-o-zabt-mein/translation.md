The lover needs a veil for the beloved's sacred fire;
what love demands is a moth's wing to feed its own desire.

When union, absence, are but poise and cold command,
what love demands is a wild heart and a teasing hand.

A kiss from those red lips will one day be possessed;
what love demands is reckless nerve and a pointless quest.
