Hai sabza-zaar har dar-o-deewar-e-gham-kada
Jis ki bahaar yeh ho phir us ki khizaan na poochh

Naachaar bekasi ki bhi hasrat uthaaiye
Dushwaari-e-rah-o-sitam-e-ham-rahaan na poochh

Juz dil suraagh-e-dard ba-dil-khuftagaan na poochh
Aaina arz kar khat-o-khaal-e-bayaan na poochh

Hindustan saaya-e-gul paaye-takht tha
Jaah-o-jalaal-e-ehd-e-wisaal-e-butaan na poochh

Ghaflat-mataa-e-kafa-e-mizaan-e-adl hoon
Ya rab hisaab-e-sakhti-e-khwaab-e-giraan na poochh

Har daagh-e-taaza yak-dil-e-daagh-e-intezaar hai
Arz-e-fazaaye-seena-e-dard-e-imtehaan na poochh

Kehta tha kal woh mahram-e-raaz apne se ke aah
Dard-e-judaai-e-Asadullah Khan na poochh

Parwaaz-e-yak-tap-e-gham-e-taskheer-e-naala hai
Garmi-e-nabz-e-khaar-o-khas-e-aashiyaan na poochh

Tu mashq-baaz kar dil-e-parwana hai bahaar
Betaabi-e-tajalli-e-aatish-ba-jaan na poochh
