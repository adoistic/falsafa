Koh ke hon baar-e-khatir gar sada ho jaiye
Be-takalluf ae sharaar-e-jasta kya ho jaiye

Baiza-aasa nang-e-baal-o-par hai yeh kunj-e-qafas
Az-sar-e-nau zindagi ho gar raha ho jaiye

Vus'at-e-mashrab niyaaz-e-kulfat-e-vahshat Asad
Yak-bayabaan saaya-e-baal-e-huma ho jaiye

Yaad rakhiye naaz haaye iltifaat-e-awwaleen
Aashiyaan-e-taair-e-rang-e-hina ho jaiye

Lutf-e-ishq har ek andaaz-e-digar dikhlayiyega
Be-takalluf yak nigaah-e-aashna ho jaiye

Daad az dast-e-jafa-e-sadma-e-zarb-ul-masal
Gar hama uftaadgi joon naqsh-e-paa ho jaiye
