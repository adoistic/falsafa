Jahan tera naqsh-e-qadam dekhte hain
Khayaban khayaban iram dekhte hain

Dil-ashuftagaan khaal-e-kunj-e-dahan ke
Suwaida mein sair-e-adam dekhte hain

Tere sarv-e-qaamat se ik qadd-e-adam
Qayamat ke fitne ko kam dekhte hain

Tamasha ke ae mehv-e-aaina-daari
Tujhe kis tamanna se hum dekhte hain

Suraagh-e-taf-e-naala le daagh-e-dil se
Ke shab-rau ka naqsh-e-qadam dekhte hain

Bana kar faqeeron ka hum bhes Ghalib
Tamasha-e-ahl-e-karam dekhte hain

Kisu ko z-khud rasta kam dekhte hain
Ke aahu ko paaband-e-ram dekhte hain

Khat-e-lakht-e-dil yak-qalam dekhte hain
Mizha ko jawahar raqam dekhte hain
