Shab khumaar-e-shauq-e-saaqi rastakhez-andaaza tha
Ta-muheet-e-baada surat khaana-e-khumiyaza tha
Yak qadam vehshat se dars-e-daftar-e-imkaan khula
Jaada ajzaa-e-do-aalam dasht ka sheeraaza tha
Maane-e-vehshat-khiraami-haaye-Laila kaun hai
Khaana-e-Majnoon-e-sehra-gard be-darwaaza tha
Poochh mat ruswaai-e-andaaz-e-istighna-e-husn
Dast marhoon-e-hina rukhsaar rehn-e-ghaaza tha
Naala-e-dil ne diye auraaq-e-lakht-e-dil ba-baad
Yaadgaar-e-naala ik deewan-e-be-sheeraaza tha
Hoon charaaghaan-e-havas joon kaaghaz-e-aatish-zada
Daagh garm-e-koshish-e-eejaad-e-daagh-e-taaza tha
Be-nawaai tar sadaa-e-naghma-e-shohrat Asad
Boriya yak neestaan-aalam buland aawaza tha
