Muzhda ae zauq-e-aseeri ke nazar aata hai
Daam-e-khaali qafas-e-murgh-e-giraftaar ke paas

Jigar-e-tishna-e-aazaar tasalli na hua
Joo-e-khoon hum ne bahai bun-e-har khaar ke paas

Mund gayin kholte hi kholte aankhein hai hai
Khoob waqt aaye tum is aashiq-e-beemar ke paas

Main bhi ruk ruk ke na marta jo zabaan ke badle
Dashna ik tez sa hota mere gham-khwaar ke paas

Dahan-e-sher mein ja baithe lekin ae dil
Na khade hojiye khoobaan-e-dil-aazaar ke paas

Dekh kar tujh ko chaman baske namu karta hai
Khud-ba-khud pahunche hai gul gosha-e-dastaar ke paas

Mar gaya phod ke sar Ghalib-e-vehshi hai hai
Baithna us ka woh aa kar teri deewar ke paas
