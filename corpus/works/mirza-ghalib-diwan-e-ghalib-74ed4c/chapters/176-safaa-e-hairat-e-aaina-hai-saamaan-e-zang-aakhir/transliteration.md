Safaa-e-hairat-e-aaina hai saamaan-e-zang aakhir
Taghayyur aab-e-bar-ja-maandah ka paata hai rang aakhir
Na ki saamaan-e-aish-o-jaah ne tadbeer-e-wehshat ki
Hua jaam-e-zumurrud bhi mujhe daagh-e-palang aakhir
Khat-e-naukhez neel-e-chashm-e-zakhm-e-saafi-e-aariz
Liya aaina ne hirz-par-e-toti bachang aakhir
Hilaal-aasa tahi reh gar kushaadan-haaye-dil chaahe
Hua mah kasrat-e-sarmaaya-andozi se tang aakhir
Tadap kar mar gaya woh said-e-baal-afshaan ke muztar tha
Hua naasoor-e-chashm-e-taaziyat chashm-e-khadang aakhir
Likhi yaaron ki bad-masti ne maikhaane ki paamaali
Hui qatra-fishaani-haaye-mai baaraan-e-sang aakhir
Asad parde mein bhi aahang-e-shauq-e-yaar qaayam hai
Nahin hai naghme se khaali khameedan-haaye-chang aakhir
