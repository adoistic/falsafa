Nukta-cheen hai gham-e-dil uss ko sunaye na bane
Kya bane baat jahan baat banaye na bane

Main bulata toh hoon uss ko magar ae jazba-e-dil
Uss pe ban jaaye kuch aisi ke bin aaye na bane

Khel samjha hai kahin chhod na de bhool na jaaye
Kaash yoon bhi ho ke bin mere sataye na bane

Ghair phirta hai liye yoon tere khat ko ke agar
Koi poochhe ke yeh kya hai toh chhupaye na bane

Uss nazakat ka bura ho woh bhale hain toh kya
Haath aawein toh unhein haath lagaye na bane

Keh sake kaun ke yeh jalwa-gari kis ki hai
Parda chhora hai woh uss ne ke uthaye na bane

Maut ki raah na dekhoon ke bin aaye na rahe
Tum ko chahoon ke na aao toh bulaye na bane

Bojh woh sar se gira hai ke uthaye na uthe
Kaam woh aan pada hai ke banaye na bane

Ishq par zor nahin hai yeh woh aatish Ghalib
Ke lagaye na lage aur bujhaye na bane
