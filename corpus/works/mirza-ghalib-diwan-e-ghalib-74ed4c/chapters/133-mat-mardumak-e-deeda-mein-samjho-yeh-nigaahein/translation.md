Mat mardumak-e-deeda mein samjho yeh nigaahein
Hain jama suwayda-e-dil-e-chashm mein aahein

Kis dil pe hai azm-e-saf-e-mizhgaan-e-khud-aara
Aaiine ke paayaab se utri hain sipaahein

Dair-o-haram aaiina-e-takrar-e-tamanna
Waamaandagi-e-shauq taraashe hai panaahein

Joon mardumak-e-chashm se hon jama nigaahein
Khwaabeeda ba-hairat-kada-e-daagh hain aahein

Phir halqa-e-kaakul mein parhin deed ki raahein
Joon dood faraaham hueen rozan mein nigaahein

Paaya sar-e-har-zarra jigar-gosha-e-wehshat
Hain daagh se ma'moor shaqaiq ki kulaahein

Yeh matla' Asad jauhar-e-afsoon-e-sukhan ho
Gar arz-e-tapaak-e-jigar-sokhta chaahein

Hairat-kash-e-yak-jalwa-e-ma'ani hain nigaahein
Kheenchun hoon suwayda-e-dil-e-chashm se aahein
