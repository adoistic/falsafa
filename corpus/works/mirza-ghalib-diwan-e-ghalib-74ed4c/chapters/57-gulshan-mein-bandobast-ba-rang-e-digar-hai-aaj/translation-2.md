Gulshan mein bandobast ba-rang-e-digar hai aaj
Qumri ka tauq halqa-e-bairoon-e-dar hai aaj

Aata hai ek paara-e-dil har fighaan ke saath
Taar-e-nafas kamand-e-shikaar-e-asar hai aaj

Ae aafiyat kinara kar ae intezaam chal
Sailaab-e-girya darpai-e-deewar-o-dar hai aaj

Maazooli-e-tapish hui ifraat-e-intezaar
Chashm-e-kushaada halqa-e-bairoon-e-dar hai aaj

Hairat-farosh-e-sad-nigraani hai iztiraar
Sar-rishta chaak-e-jaib ka taar-e-nazar hai aaj

Hoon daagh-e-neem-rangi-e-shaam-e-wisaal-e-yaar
Noor-e-chiraagh-e-bazm se josh-e-seher hai aaj

Karti hai aajizi-e-safar sokhtan tamaam
Pairaahan-e-khasak mein ghubaar-e-sharar hai aaj

Taa-subah hai ba-manzil-e-maqsad raseedani
Dood-e-chiraagh-e-khaana ghubaar-e-safar hai aaj

Door-uftaada-e-chaman-e-fikr hai Asad
Murgh-e-khayaal bulbul-e-be-baal-o-par hai aaj
