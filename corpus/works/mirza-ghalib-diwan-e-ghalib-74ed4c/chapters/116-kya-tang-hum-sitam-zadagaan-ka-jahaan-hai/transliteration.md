Kya tang hum sitam-zadagaan ka jahaan hai
Jis mein ke ek baiza-e-mor aasmaan hai

Hai kaaynaat ko harkat tere zauq se
Partau se aaftaab ke zarre mein jaan hai

Haalaanke hai yeh seli-e-khaara se laala rang
Ghaafil ko mere sheeshe pe mai ka gumaan hai

Ki us ne garm seena-e-ahl-e-hawas mein jaa
Aave na kyun pasand ke thanda makaan hai

Kya khoob tum ne ghair ko bosa nahi diya
Bas chup raho hamare bhi munh mein zabaan hai

Baitha hai jo ke saaya-e-deewar-e-yaar mein
Farmaan-rawaa-e-kishwar-e-Hindustan hai

Hasti ka aitbaar bhi gham ne mita diya
Kis se kahun ke daagh jigar ka nishaan hai

Hai baare aitmaad-e-wafa-daari is qadar
Ghalib hum is mein khush hain ke na-meherbaan hai

Delhi ke rehne waalo Asad ko satao mat
Be-chaara chand roz ka yaan mehmaan hai
