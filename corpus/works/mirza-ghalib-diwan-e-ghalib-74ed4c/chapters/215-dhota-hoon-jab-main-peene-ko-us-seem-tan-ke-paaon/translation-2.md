dhota hoon jab main peene ko us seem-tan ke paaon
rakhta hai zid se kheench ke baahar lagan ke paaon

di saadgi se jaan padun koh-kan ke paaon
haihaat kyun na toot gaye peer-zan ke paaon

bhaage the hum bahut so usi ki saza hai yeh
ho kar aseer daabte hain raahzan ke paaon

marham ki justuju mein phira hoon jo door door
tan se siwa figaar hain us khasta-tan ke paaon

Allah-re zauq-e-dasht-nawardi ke baad-e-marg
hilte hain khud-ba-khud mere andar kafan ke paaon

hai josh-e-gul bahaar mein yaan tak ke har taraf
udte hue ulajhte hain murgh-e-chaman ke paaon

shab ko kisi ke khwaab mein aaya na ho kahin
dukhte hain aaj us but-e-naazuk-badan ke paaon

Ghalib mere kalaam mein kyun-kar maza na ho
peeta hoon dhoke Khusrau-e-sheereen-sukhan ke paaon
