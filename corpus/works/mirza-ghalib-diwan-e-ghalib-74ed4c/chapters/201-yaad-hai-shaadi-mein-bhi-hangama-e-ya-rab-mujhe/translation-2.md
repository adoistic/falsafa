Yaad hai shaadi mein bhi hangama-e-ya-rab mujhe
Subha-e-zahid hua hai khanda-e-zer-e-lab mujhe
Hai kushaad-e-khatir-e-wabasta dar rehn-e-sukhan
Tha tilism-e-qufl-e-abjad khaana-e-maktab mujhe
Ya rab is aashuftagi ki daad kis se chahiye
Rashk aasaish pe hai zindaniyon ki ab mujhe
Taba hai mushtaq-e-lazzat-haaye-hasrat kya karun
Aarzu se hai shikast-e-aarzu matlab mujhe
Dil laga kar aap bhi Ghalib mujhi se ho gaye
Ishq se aate the maane Mirza saahab mujhe
