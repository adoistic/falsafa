Hai aarmidagi mein nekohish baja mujhe
Subh-e-watan hai khanda-e-dandaan-numa mujhe

Dhoonde hai us mughanni-e-aatish-nafas ko jee
Jis ki sada ho jalwa-e-barq-e-fana mujhe

Mastaana tay karoon hoon rah-e-waadi-e-khayal
Ta baaz-gasht se na rahe mudda'a mujhe

Karta hai bas-ke baagh mein tu be-hijaabiyan
Aane lagi hai nikhat-e-gul se haya mujhe

Khulta kisi pe kyun mere dil ka maamla
Sheron ke intekhaab ne ruswa kiya mujhe

Waan rang-ha ba-parda-e-tadbeer hain hanoz
Yaan shola-e-charaagh hai barg-e-hina mujhe

Parwaaz-ha niyaaz-e-tamasha-e-husn-e-dost
Baal-kushada hai nigah-e-aashna mujhe

Az-khud-guzashtagi mein khamoshi pe harf hai
Mauj-e-ghubaar-e-surma hui hai sada mujhe

Ta chand past-fitrati-e-taba-e-aarzoo
Ya Rab mile bulandi-e-dast-e-dua mujhe

Main ne junoon se ki jo 'Asad' iltemas-e-rang
Khoon-e-jigar mein ek hi ghota diya mujhe

Hai pech-o-taab rishta-e-shama-e-sahar-gahi
Khijlat gudaazi-e-nafas-e-na-rasa mujhe

Yaan aab-o-daana mausam-e-gul mein haraam hai
Zunnaar-wa-gusasta hai mauj-e-saba mujhe

Yak-baar imtihaan-e-hawas bhi zaroor hai
Ae josh-e-ishq baada-e-mard-aazma mujhe
