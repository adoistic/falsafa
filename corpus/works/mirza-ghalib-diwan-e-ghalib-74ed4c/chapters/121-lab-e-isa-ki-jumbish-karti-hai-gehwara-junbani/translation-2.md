The stir of Jesus’ lips can only rock a cradle;
For one by ruby lips slain, the Last Day is a sleep of stone.

Past the desert of seeking, Ghalib, is annihilation’s plain;
The sweat from courage’s stallion is a flood that leaves the saddle overthrown.
