Reham kar zaalim ke kya bood-e-charaagh-e-kushta hai
Nabz-e-bimaar-e-wafaa dood-e-charaagh-e-kushta hai

Dil-lagi ki aarzoo be-chain rakhti hai hamein
Warna yaan be-raunaqi sood-e-charaagh-e-kushta hai

Nasha-e-mai be-chaman dood-e-charaagh-e-kushta hai
Jaam daagh-e-shola-andood-e-charaagh-e-kushta hai

Daagh-e-rabt-e-baham hain ahl-e-baagh gar gul ho shaheed
Laala chashm-e-hasrat-aalood-e-charaagh-e-kushta hai

Shor hai kis bazm ki arz-e-jaraahat-khaana ka
Subah yak-bazm-e-namak sood-e-charaagh-e-kushta hai
