Dil se teri nigaah jigar tak utar gayi
Dono ko ik adaa mein razaamand kar gayi

Shaq ho gaya hai seena khusha lazzat-e-faraagh
Takleef-e-parda-daari-e-zakhm-e-jigar gayi

Woh baada-e-shabaana ki sarmastiyaan kahaan
Uthiye bas ab ke lazzat-e-khwaab-e-sahar gayi

Udti phire hai khaak meri koo-e-yaar mein
Baare ab ae hawa havas-e-baal-o-par gayi

Dekho toh dil-farebi-e-andaaz-e-naqsh-e-paa
Mauj-e-khiraam-e-yaar bhi kya gul katar gayi

Har bul-hawas ne husn-parasti she'aar ki
Ab aabroo-e-sheva-e-ahl-e-nazar gayi

Nazaare ne bhi kaam kiya waan niqaab ka
Masti se har nigeh tere rukh par bikhar gayi

Farda o di ka tafarraqa yak baar mit gaya
Kal tum gaye ke hum pe qayamat guzar gayi

Maara zamaane ne Asadullah Khan tumhein
Woh walwale kahaan woh jawaani kidhar gayi
