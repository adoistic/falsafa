Shumar-e-subha marghoob-e-but-e-mushkil-pasand aaya
Tamasha-e-ba-yak-kaf burdan-e-sad-dil-pasand aaya

Ba-faiz-e-be-dili naumeedi-e-javed aasaan hai
Kushayish ko hamara uqda-e-mushkil-pasand aaya

Hawa-e-sair-e-gul aaina-e-be-mehri-e-qatil
Keh andaaz-e-ba-khoon-ghaltidan-e-bismil-pasand aaya

Rawani-haaye-mauj-e-khoon-e-bismil se tapakta hai
Keh lutf-e-be-tahasha-raftan-e-qatil-pasand aaya

Asad har ja sukhan ne tarah-e-bagh-e-taza daali hai
Mujhe rang-e-bahaar-e-ejaadi-e-Bedil-pasand aaya

Sawad-e-chashm-e-bismil intekhab-e-nuqta-aarayi
Khiraam-e-naaz be-parwaai-e-qatil pasand aaya

Hui jis ko bahaar-e-fursat-e-hasti se aagahi
Ba-rang-e-lala jaam-e-baada par mahmil pasand aaya
