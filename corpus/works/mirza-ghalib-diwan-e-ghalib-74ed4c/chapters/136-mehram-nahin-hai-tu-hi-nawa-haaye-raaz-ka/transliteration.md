Mehram nahin hai tu hi nawa-haaye-raaz ka
Yaan warna jo hijaab hai parda hai saaz ka

Rang-e-shikasta subh-e-bahaar-e-nazaara hai
Yeh waqt hai shaguftan-e-gul-haaye-naaz ka

Tu aur su-e-ghair nazar-haaye-tez tez
Main aur dukh teri mizha-haaye-daraaz ka

Sarfa hai zabt-e-aah mein mera wagarna main
Tu'ma hoon ek hi nafas-e-jaan-gudaaz ka

Hain baski josh-e-baada se sheeshe uchhal rahe
Har gosha-e-bisaat hai sar sheesha-baaz ka

Kaavish ka dil kare hai taqaaza ke hai hanoz
Naakhun pe qarz us girah-e-neem-baaz ka

Taaraaj-e-kaavish-e-gham-e-hijraan hua Asad
Seena ke tha dafeena gohar-haaye-raaz ka
