Nahin ke mujh ko qayamat ka aiteqad nahin
Shab-e-firaaq se roz-e-jaza ziyaad nahin

Koi kahe ke shab-e-mah mein kya burai hai
Bala se aaj agar din ko abr-o-baad nahin

Jo aaun saamne un ke to marhaba na kahein
Jo jaaun waan se kahin ko to khairbad nahin

Kabhi jo yaad bhi aata hoon main to kehte hain
Ke aaj bazm mein kuchh fitna-o-fasaad nahin

Alaawa eid ke milti hai aur din bhi sharaab
Gada-e-koocha-e-mai-khana na-murad nahin

Jahan mein ho gham-e-shaadi baham hamein kya kaam
Diya hai hum ko Khuda ne woh dil ke shaad nahin

Tum unke waade ka zikr un se kyun karo Ghalib
Yeh kya ke tum kaho aur woh kahein ke yaad nahin
