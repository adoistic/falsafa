Is bazm mein mujhe nahin banti haya kiye
Baitha raha agarche ishaare hua kiye

Dil hi toh hai siyasat-e-darbaan se dar gaya
Main aur jaaun dar se tere bin sada kiye

Rakhta phirun hoon khirqa-o-sajjada rehn-e-mai
Muddat hui hai daawat-e-aab-o-hava kiye

Be-sarfa hi guzarti hai ho garche umr-e-Khizr
Hazrat bhi kal kahenge ke hum kya kiya kiye

Maqdoor ho toh khaak se poochun ke ae laeem
Tu ne woh ganj-haaye-giraan-maaya kya kiye

Kis roz tuhmatein na taraasha kiye adoo
Kis din hamare sar pe na aaray chala kiye

Sohbat mein ghair ki na padi ho kahin yeh khoo
Dene laga hai bosa baghair iltija kiye

Zid ki hai aur baat magar khoo buri nahin
Bhoole se usne saikdon waade wafa kiye

Ghalib tumhin kaho ke milega jawaab kya
Maana ke tum kaha kiye aur woh suna kiye
