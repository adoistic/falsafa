Arz-e naaz-e shokhi-e dandaan baraaye khanda hai
Daawa-e jamiyyat-e ahbaab jaa-e khanda hai

Hai adam mein ghuncha mehv-e ibrat-e anjaam-e gul
Yak jahaan zaanu ta'ammul dar qafa-e khanda hai

Kulfat-e afsurdagi ko aish-e betaabi haraam
Warna dandaan dar dil afshurdan binaa-e khanda hai

Shorish-e baatin ke hain ahbaab munkir warna yaan
Dil muheet-e girya o lab aashna-e khanda hai

Khud-faroshi haaye hasti bas-ki jaa-e khanda hai
Har shikast-e qeemat-e dil mein sadaa-e khanda hai

Naqsh-e ibrat dar nazar ya naqd-e ishrat dar bisaat
Do jahaan vus'at ba-qadr-e yak fazaa-e khanda hai

Jaa-e istehza hai ishrat-koshi-e hasti, Asad
Subh o shabnam fursat-e nashv-o-numaa-e khanda hai
