Be-e'tedaaliyon se subuk sab mein hum huay
Jitne zyaada ho gaye utne hi kam huay

Pinhan tha daam sakht qareeb aashiyaan ke
Udne na paaye thay ke giraftaar hum huay

Hasti hamari apni fana par daleel hai
Yaan tak mite ke aap hum apni qasam huay

Sakhti-kashaan-e-ishq ki poochhe hai kya khabar
Woh log rafta rafta saraapa alam huay

Teri wafa se kya ho talaafi ke dehr mein
Tere siwa bhi hum pe bohot se sitam huay

Likhte rahe junoon ki hikaayaat-e-khoon-chakaan
Har-chand us mein haath hamare qalam huay

Allah re teri tundi-e-khoo jiss ke beem se
Ajzaa-e-naala dil mein mere rizq-e-hum huay

Ahl-e-hawas ki fateh hai tark-e-nabard-e-ishq
Jo paanv uth gaye wohi un ke alam huay

Naale adam mein chand hamare supurd thay
Jo waan na khinch sake so woh yaan aa ke dam huay

Chhori Asad na hum ne gadaai mein dil-lagi
Saail huay toh aashiq-e-ahl-e-karam huay
