Kehte ho na denge hum dil agar pada paaya
Dil kahan ke gum keeje hum ne mudda'a paaya

Ishq se tabiyat ne zeest ka maza paaya
Dard ki dawa paayi dard-e-be-dawa paaya

Dost-daar-e-dushman hai aitimaad-e-dil maloom
Aah be-asar dekhi naala na-rasa paaya

Saadgi o purkaari, be-khudi o hoshiyari
Husn ko taghaful mein jurrat-aazma paaya

Ghuncha phir laga khilne aaj hum ne apna dil
Khoon kiya hua dekha, gum kiya hua paaya

Haal-e-dil nahi maloom lekin is qadar yaani
Hum ne baarha dhoonda, tum ne baarha paaya

Shor-e-pand-e-naaseh ne zakhm par namak chhidka
Aap se koi pooche tum ne kya maza paaya

Hai kahan tamanna ka doosra qadam Ya Rab
Hum ne dasht-e-imkaan ko ek naqsh-e-paa paaya

Be-dimaagh-e-khijlat hoon rashk-e-imtehaan ta-kay
Ek bekasi tujh ko aalam-aashna paaya

Khaak-baazi-e-ummeed kaarkhana-e-tifli
Yaas ko do-aalam se lab-ba-khanda wa paaya

Kyun na vehshat-e-Ghalib baaj-khwah-e-taskeen ho
Kushta-e-taghaful ko khasm-e-khoon-baha paaya

Fikr-e-naala mein goya halqa hoon za-sar-ta-paa
Uzw uzw joon zanjeer yak-dil-sada paaya

Shab nazaara-parwar tha khwaab mein khayaal us ka
Subah mauja-e-gul ko naqsh-e-boriya paaya

Jis qadar jigar khoon ho, koocha daadan-e-gul hai
Zakhm-e-tegh-e-qaatil ko turfa dil-kusha paaya

Hai makeen ki paa-daari naam-e-saahib-khana
Hum se tere kooche ne naqsh-e-mudda'a paaya

Ne Asad jafa-saail ne sitam junoon-maail
Tujh ko jis qadar dhoonda ulfat-aazma paaya
