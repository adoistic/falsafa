Hai bazm-e-butaan mein sukhan aazurda-labon se
Tang aaye hain hum aise khushamad-talabon se

Hai daur-e-qadah wajah-e-pareshani-e-sahba
Yak-baar laga do khum-e-mai mere labon se

Rindaan-e-dar-e-maikada gustaakh hain zaahid
Zinhaar na hona taraf in be-adabon se

Bedaad-e-wafa dekh ke jaati rahi aakhir
Har-chand meri jaan ko tha rabt labon se

Kya poochhe hai bar-khud ghalatihaaye-azeezaan
Khwaari ko bhi ik aar hai aali-nasabon se

Go tum ko raza-joi-e-aghyar hai lekin
Jaati hai mulaqaat kab aise sababon se

Mat poochh Asad waada-e-kam-fursati-e-zeest
Do din bhi jo kaate to qayamat ta'abon se
