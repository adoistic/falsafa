Chaak ki khwahish agar wehshat ba-uryaani kare
Subah ke maanind zakhm-e-dil girebaani kare

Jalway ka tere woh aalam hai ke gar keeje khayal
Deeda-e-dil ko ziyarat-gaah-e-hairaani kare

Hai shikastan se bhi dil naumeed ya Rab kab talak
Aabgeena koh par arz-e-giraan-jaani kare

Mai-kada gar chashm-e-mast-e-naaz se paave shikast
Moo-e-sheesha deeda-e-saaghar ki mizhgaani kare

Khatt-e-aariz se likha hai zulf ko ulfat ne ahd
Yak-qalam manzoor hai jo kuch pareshani kare
