Ghair lein mehfil mein bose jaam ke
Hum rahein yun tishna-lab paighaam ke

Khastagi ka tum se kya shikwa ke yeh
Hathkande hain charkh-e-neeli-faam ke

Khat likhenge garche matlab kuch na ho
Hum toh aashiq hain tumhare naam ke

Raat pee zamzam pe mai aur subah-dam
Dhoye dhabbe jaama-e-ehraam ke

Dil ko aankhon ne phansaya kya magar
Yeh bhi halqe hain tumhare daam ke

Shaah ke hai ghusl-e-sehat ki khabar
Dekhiye kab din phiren hamaam ke

Ishq ne Ghalib nikamma kar diya
Warna hum bhi aadmi the kaam ke
