pa-ba-daaman ho raha hoon baske main sahra-navard
khaar-e-pa hain jauhar-e-aaina-e-zaanu mujhe

dekhna haalat mere dil ki ham-aaghoshi ke waqt
hai nigaah-aashna tera sar-e-har-moo mujhe

hoon saraapa saaz-e-aahang-e-shikayat kuchh na poochh
hai yahi behtar ke logon mein na chhede tu mujhe

baais-e-waamaandagi hai umr-e-fursat-jo mujhe
kar diya hai pa-ba-zanjeer-e-ram-e-aahu mujhe

khaak-e-fursat bar-sar-e-zauq-e-fana ae intezaar
hai ghubaar-e-sheesha-e-sa'at ram-e-aahu mujhe

ham-zubaan aaya nazar fikr-e-sukhan mein tu mujhe
mardumak hai toti-e-aaina-e-zaanu mujhe

yaad-e-mizhgaan mein ba-nishtar-zaar-e-sauda-e-khayaal
chaahiye waqt-e-tapish yak-dast sad-pehlu mujhe

iztiraab-e-umr be-matlab nahin aakhir ke hai
justujoo-e-fursat-e-rabt-e-sar-e-zaanu mujhe

chaahiye darmaan-e-resh-e-dil bhi tegh-e-yaar se
marham-e-zangaar hai woh wasma-e-abru mujhe

kasrat-e-jaur-o-sitam se ho gaya hoon be-dimaagh
khoob-ruyon ne banaaya Ghalib-e-bad-khoo mujhe

fursat-e-aaraam-e-ghash hasti hai bohraan-e-adam
hai shikast-e-rang-e-imkaan gardish-e-pehlu mujhe

saaz-e-eema-e-fana hai aalam-e-peeri Asad
qaamat-e-kham se hai haasil shokhi-e-abru mujhe
