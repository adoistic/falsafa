Shabnam ba-gul-e-laala na khaali z-adaa hai
Daagh-e-dil-e-be-dard nazar-gaah-e-haya hai
Dil khoon-shuda-e-kashmakash-e-hasrat-e-deedar
Aaina ba-dast-e-but-e-bad-mast hina hai
Sholay se na hoti hawas-e-shola ne jo ki
Jee kis qadar afsurdagi-e-dil pe jala hai
Timsaal mein teri hai woh shokhi ke ba-sad-zauq
Aaina ba-andaaz-e-gul aaghosh-kusha hai
Qumri kaf-e-khaakastar o bulbul qafas-e-rang
Ae naala nishaan-e-jigar-e-sokhta kya hai
Khoo ne teri afsurda kiya vehshat-e-dil ko
Maashooqi o be-hauslagi turfa balaa hai
Majboori o daawaa-e-giriftaari-e-ulfat
Dast-e-tah-e-sang-aamada paimaan-e-wafaa hai
Maaloom hua haal-e-shaheedan-e-guzishta
Tegh-e-sitam aaina-e-tasveer-numa hai
Ae partav-e-khursheed-e-jahan-taab idhar bhi
Saaye ki tarah hum pe ajab waqt pada hai
Na-karda gunaahon ki bhi hasrat ki mile daad
Ya Rab agar un karda gunaahon ki sazaa hai
Begaanagi-e-khalq se be-dil na ho Ghalib
Koi nahin tera toh meri jaan Khuda hai
