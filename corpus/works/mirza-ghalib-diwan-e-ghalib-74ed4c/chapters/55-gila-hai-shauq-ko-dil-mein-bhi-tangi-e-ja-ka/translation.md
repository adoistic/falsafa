Gila hai shauq ko dil mein bhi tangi-e-ja ka
Gauhar mein mahv hua iztiraab dariya ka

Yeh jaanta hoon ke tu aur paasakh-e-maktoob
Magar sitam-zada hoon zauq-e-khaama-farsa ka

Hina-e-paa-e-khizaan hai bahaar agar hai yahi
Dawaam-e-kulfat-e-khaatir hai aish-e-duniya ka

Gham-e-firaaq mein takleef-e-sair-e-baagh na do
Mujhe dimaagh nahin khanda-haaye-beja ka

Hanooz mehrami-e-husn ko tarasta hoon
Kare hai har bun-e-mu kaam chashm-e-beena ka

Dil uss ko pehle hi naaz-o-adaa se de baithe
Humein dimaagh kahan husn ke taqaaza ka

Na keh ke girya ba-miqdaar-e-hasrat-e-dil hai
Miri nigaah mein hai jama-o-kharch dariya ka

Falak ko dekh ke karta hoon uss ko yaad Asad
Jafa mein uss ki hai andaaz kaar-farma ka

Mera shumool har ik dil ke paich-o-taab mein hai
Main mudda'a hoon tapish-naama-e-tamanna ka

Mili na vus'at-e-jaulaan yak junoon hum ko
Adam ko le gaye dil mein ghubaar sehra ka
