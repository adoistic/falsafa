Gham khaane mein boda dil-e-nakaam bahut hai
Yeh ranj ke kam hai mai-e-gulfaam bahut hai

Kehte hue saaqi se haya aati hai warna
Hai yoon ke mujhe dard-e-tah-e-jaam bahut hai

Ne teer kamaan mein hai na sayyad kameen mein
Goshe mein qafas ke mujhe aaram bahut hai

Kya zohd ko maanoon ke na ho garche riyaai
Paadaash-e-amal ki tama-e-khaam bahut hai

Hain ahl-e-khirad kis rawish-e-khaas pe naazaan
Paabastagi-e-rasm-o-rah-e-aam bahut hai

Zamzam hi pe chhoro mujhe kya tauf-e-haram se
Aalooda-ba-mai jaama-e-ehraam bahut hai

Hai qehar gar ab bhi na bane baat ke un ko
Inkaar nahin aur mujhe ibraam bahut hai

Khoon ho ke jigar aankh se tapka nahin ae marg
Rehne de mujhe yaan ke abhi kaam bahut hai

Hoga koi aisa bhi ke Ghalib ko na jaane
Shayar toh woh achha hai pe badnaam bahut hai
