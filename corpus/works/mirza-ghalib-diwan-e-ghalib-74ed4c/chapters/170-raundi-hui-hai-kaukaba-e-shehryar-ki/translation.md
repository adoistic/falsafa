Raundi hui hai kaukaba-e-shehryar ki
Itraaye kyun na khaak sar-e-rah-guzar ki

Jab uske dekhne ke liye aayein badshah
Logon mein kyun numood na ho lala-zaar ki

Bhooke nahin hain sair-e-gulistan ke hum walay
Kyun kar na khaiye ke hawa hai bahaar ki
