Aaina kyun na doon ke tamasha kahein jise
Aisa kahan se laaun ke tujh sa kahein jise

Hasrat ne la rakha teri bazm-e-khayal mein
Guldasta-e-nigah suwaida kahein jise

Phoonka hai kis ne gosh-e-mohabbat mein aye Khuda
Afsun-e-intezar tamanna kahein jise

Sar par hujoom-e-dard-e-ghareebi se daaliye
Woh ek musht-e-khaak ke sehra kahein jise

Hai chashm-e-tar mein hasrat-e-deedar se nihaan
Shauq-e-inaan gusikhta darya kahein jise

Darkaar hai shaguftan-e-gul-haaye-aish ko
Subh-e-bahaar pamba-e-meena kahein jise

Ghalib bura na maan jo waa'iz bura kahe
Aisa bhi koi hai ke sab achha kahein jise

Ya Rab humein toh khwaab mein bhi mat dikhaiyo
Yeh mehshar-e-khayal ke duniya kahein jise

Hai intezar se sharar-abaad rastakhez
Mizhgan-e-kohkan rag-e-khara kahein jise

Kis fursat-e-visaal pe hai gul ko andaleeb
Zakhm-e-firaaq khanda-e-beja kahein jise
