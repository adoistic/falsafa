Naala-e-dil mein shab andaaz-e-asar naayaab tha
Tha sipand-e-bazm-e-vasl-e-ghair go betaab tha

Dekhte the hum ba-chashm-e-khud woh toofan-e-bala
Aasmaan-e-sifla jis mein yak-kaf-e-sailaab tha

Mauj se paida hue pairahan-e-dariya mein khaar
Giriya-e-vahshat be-qaraar-e-jalwa-e-mahtaab tha

Josh-e-takleef-e-tamasha mahshar-aabaad nigaah
Fitna-e-khwaabeeda ko aaina musht-e-aab tha

Be-khabar mat keh humein be-dard khud-beeni se poochh
Qulzam-e-zauq-e-nazar mein aaina paayaab tha

Be-dili haaye Asad afsurdagi aahang-tar
Yaad-e-ayyaam ke zauq-e-suhbat-e-ahbaab tha

Maqdam-e-sailaab se dil kya nishaat-aahang hai
Khaana-e-aashiq magar saaz-e-sadaa-e-aab tha

Naazish-e-ayyaam-e-khaakastar-nasheeni kya kahun
Pehlu-e-andesha waqf-e-bistar-e-sanjaab tha

Kuchh na ki apni junoon-e-na-rasa ne varna yaan
Zarra zarra ru-kash-e-khursheed-e-aalam-taab tha

Aaj kyun parwa nahin apne aseeron ki tujhe
Kal talak tera bhi dil mehr-o-wafa ka baab tha

Yaad kar woh din ke har yak halqa tere daam ka
Intezaar-e-said mein ik deeda-e-be-khwaab tha

Main ne roka raat Ghalib ko wagarna dekhte
Us ke sail-e-giriya mein gardoon kaf-e-sailaab tha
