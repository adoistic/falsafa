surma-e-muft-e-nazar hoon meri qeemat yeh hai
ke rahe chashm-e-kharidaar pe ehsaan mera

rukhsat-e-naala mujhe de ke mubaada zaalim
tere chehre se ho zaahir gham-e-pinhaan mera

khalvat-e-aabla-e-paa mein hai jaulaan mera
khoon hai dil tangi-e-vehshat se bayaabaan mera

hasrat-e-nasha-e-vehshat na ba-sa'i-e-dil hai
arz-e-khamyaaza-e-majnoon hai gareebaan mera

fehm zanjeeri-e-be-rabti-e-dil hai ya-rab
kis zubaan mein hai laqab khwaab-e-pareshaan mera
