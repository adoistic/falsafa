Gulshan ko teri sohbat az-bas-keh khush aayi hai
Har ghunche ka gul hona aaghosh-kushai hai

Waan kangar-e-istaghna har-dam hai bulandi par
Yaan naale ko aur ulta daawa-e-rasaai hai

Az-bas-keh sikhata hai gham zabt ke andaaze
Jo daagh nazar aaya ik chashm-numai hai
