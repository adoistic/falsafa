Loon waam bakht-e-khufta se yak-khwaab-e-khush walay
Ghalib yeh khauf hai ke kahan se ada karoon

Khush wehshatay ke arz-e-junoon-e-fana karoon
Joon gard-e-raah jaama-e-hasti qaba karoon

Aa ae bahaar-e-naaz ke tere khiraam se
Dastaar gard-e-shaakh-e-gul-e-naqsh-e-pa karoon

Khush uftaadgi ke ba-sehraa-e-intezaar
Joon jaada gard-e-rah se nigeh surma-saa karoon

Sabr aur yeh ada ke dil aaway aseer-e-chaak
Dard aur yeh kameen ke rah-e-naala waa karoon

Woh be-dimaagh-e-minnat-e-iqbaal hoon ke main
Wehshat ba-daagh-e-saaya-e-baal-e-huma karoon

Woh iltimaas-e-lazzat-e-be-daad hoon ke main
Tegh-e-sitam ko pusht-e-kham-e-iltija karoon

Woh raaz-e-naala hoon ke ba-sharh-e-nigaah-e-ijz
Afshaan ghubaar-e-surma se fard-e-sada karoon
