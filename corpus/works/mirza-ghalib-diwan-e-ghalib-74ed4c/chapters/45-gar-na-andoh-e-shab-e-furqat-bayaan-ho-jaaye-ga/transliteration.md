Gar na andoh-e-shab-e-furqat bayaan ho jaaye-ga
Be-takalluf daagh-e-mah mehr-e-dahan ho jaaye-ga

Zohra gar aisa hi shaam-e-hijr mein hota hai aab
Partav-e-mahtaab sail-e-khanmaan ho jaaye-ga

Le to loon sote mein us ke paanv ka bosa magar
Aisi baaton se woh kaafir bad-gumaan ho jaaye-ga

Dil ko hum sarf-e-wafa samjhe the kya maaloom tha
Yaani yeh pehle hi nazr-e-imtehaan ho jaaye-ga

Sab ke dil mein hai jagah teri jo tu raazi hua
Mujh pe goya ik zamaana meherbaan ho jaaye-ga

Gar nigaah-e-garm farmaati rahi taaleem-e-zabt
Shola khas mein jaise khoon rag mein nihaan ho jaaye-ga

Baagh mein mujh ko na le ja warna mere haal par
Har gul-e-tar ek chashm-e-khoon-fishaan ho jaaye-ga

Waaye gar mera tera insaaf mehshar mein na ho
Ab talak to yeh tavaqqo hai ke waan ho jaaye-ga

Faayda kya soch aakhir tu bhi daana hai 'Asad'
Dosti naadaan ki hai jee ka ziyaan ho jaaye-ga

Gar woh mast-e-naaz deve ga salaaye-arz-e-haal
Khaar-e-gul behr-e-dahan-e-gul zabaan ho jayega

Gar shahaadat aarzoo hai nashe mein gustaakh ho
Baal sheeshe ka rag-e-sang-e-fasaan ho jayega
