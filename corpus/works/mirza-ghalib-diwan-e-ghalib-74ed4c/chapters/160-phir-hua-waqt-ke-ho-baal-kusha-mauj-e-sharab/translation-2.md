Phir hua waqt ke ho baal-kusha mauj-e-sharab
De bat-e-mai ko dil-o-dast-e-shana mauj-e-sharab

Poochh mat wajah-e-siyah-masti-e-arbaab-e-chaman
Saaya-e-taak mein hoti hai hawa mauj-e-sharab

Jo hua gharq-e-mai bakht-e-rasa rakhta hai
Sar se guzre pe bhi hai baal-e-huma mauj-e-sharab

Hai yeh barsaat woh mausam ke ajab kya hai agar
Mauj-e-hasti ko kare faiz-e-hawa mauj-e-sharab

Chaar mauj uthti hai toofan-e-tarab se har su
Mauj-e-gul, mauj-e-shafaq, mauj-e-saba, mauj-e-sharab

Jis qadar rooh-e-nabati hai jigar tishna-e-naaz
De hai taskeen ba-dam-e-aab-e-baqa mauj-e-sharab

Baske daude hai rag-e-taak mein khoon ho ho kar
Shehpar-e-rang se hai baal-kusha mauj-e-sharab

Mauja-e-gul se charaghan hai guzargaah-e-khayal
Hai tasavvur mein ze-bas jalwa-numa mauj-e-sharab

Nasha ke parde mein hai mehv-e-tamasha-e-dimaagh
Baske rakhti hai sar-e-nashv-o-numa mauj-e-sharab

Ek aalam pe hain toofani-kaifiyat-e-fasl
Mauja-e-sabza-e-nau-khez se ta mauj-e-sharab

Sharh-e-hangama-e-hasti hai zehe mausam-e-gul
Rehbar-e-qatra-ba-dariya hai khusha mauj-e-sharab

Hosh udte hain mere jalwa-e-gul dekh Asad
Phir hua waqt ke ho baal-kusha mauj-e-sharab
