Dar-khur-e-qahr-o-ghazab jab koi hum sa na hua
Phir ghalat kya hai ke hum sa koi paida na hua

Bandagi mein bhi woh azaada o khudbeen hain ke hum
Ulte phir aaye dar-e-Kaaba agar waa na hua

Sab ko maqbool hai daawa teri yaktaai ka
Ru-ba-ru koi but-e-aaina-seema na hua

Kam nahin naazish-e-ham-naami-e-chashm-e-khoobaan
Tera beemar bura kya hai gar achha na hua

Seene ka daagh hai woh naala ke lab tak na gaya
Khaak ka rizq hai woh qatra ke dariya na hua

Naam ka mere hai jo dukh ke kisi ko na mila
Kaam mein mere hai jo fitna ke barpa na hua

Har bun-e-moo se dam-e-zikr na tapke khoon-e-naab
Hamza ka qissa hua, ishq ka charcha na hua

Qatre mein Dajla dikhaai na de aur juzv mein kul
Khel ladkon ka hua, deeda-e-beena na hua

Thi khabar garm ke Ghalib ke udenge purze
Dekhne hum bhi gaye the, pe tamasha na hua
